# Ruby Rabbit's Clean-Up Adventure

Ruby Rabbit hopped down the wooden steps to her basement lab. *Thump, thump, thump* went her fluffy feet on each step. The basement smelled like old books and dust, and Ruby wrinkled her pink nose.

"Oh my!" Ruby gasped when she reached the bottom. Her lab was very messy! Test tubes were scattered everywhere, papers covered the floor like fallen leaves, and sticky spills made everything look yucky.

Just then, Ruby heard a tiny voice crying. "Help! Help!"

Ruby's long ears perked up. She followed the sound to a corner where she found three little field mice trapped under a fallen box of beakers.

"Don't worry, little friends," Ruby said gently. "I'll help you!"

The smallest mouse sniffled. "We came looking for food, but everything fell on us!"

Ruby's heart felt warm with kindness. Even though her lab was messy and she had lots of work to do, helping others was more important.

"Let me think," Ruby said, tapping her furry chin. She needed a strategy to lift the heavy box safely.

Ruby remembered her science lessons. "I know! I'll use a lever!"

She found a long wooden ruler and placed it under the box. Then she pushed down on the other end. *Creak!* The box lifted up slowly.

"Quick! Crawl out!" Ruby called.

The three mice scurried to safety, their tiny claws clicking on the concrete floor.

"Thank you, Ruby!" squeaked the mama mouse. "You're so kind!"

But Ruby noticed something else. The mice looked very dirty from all the dust and spills.

"You need to get clean," Ruby said with a caring smile. "Staying clean keeps us healthy and happy!"

Ruby filled a small bowl with warm, soapy water. The sweet smell of lavender soap filled the air.

"Washing is fun!" Ruby showed them, splashing gently. "We wash our paws, our faces, and behind our ears!"

The mice giggled as they took turns washing. Soon they were sparkling clean and felt much better.

"Now," said Ruby, "let's clean up this lab together! Many paws make light work!"

The mice were so grateful for Ruby's kindness that they wanted to help too. Ruby gave each mouse a small job they could do safely.

They worked as a team, singing a happy cleaning song:

"Clean up, clean up, everybody everywhere!
Clean up, clean up, everybody do your share!"

Ruby carefully put away the glass beakers where they belonged. The mice helped sort papers into neat piles. Together, they wiped up all the sticky spills until the lab sparkled.

When they finished, the basement lab looked wonderful! Everything was organized, clean, and safe.

"Ruby," said the papa mouse, "you were so kind to help us, even when you were busy. How can we thank you?"

Ruby's whiskers twitched happily. "Helping friends is what makes me happiest! And now we're all clean and safe."

The mice promised to visit Ruby's lab often – but only through the special mouse door Ruby made for them, so nothing would fall again.

As Ruby looked around her clean, organized lab, she felt proud. By being kind and helping others first, everything had worked out perfectly. Her lab was cleaner than ever, and she had made three wonderful new friends.

"Kindness always makes everything better," Ruby said with a happy hop.

The End.
