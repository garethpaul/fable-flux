# Polly's Musical Basement Band

In a busy basement filled with old boxes and forgotten treasures, lived a bright green parrot named Polly. She had beautiful red and blue feathers on her wings and loved to make music more than anything else in the whole world.

"Tweet, tweet! What a lovely day to sing!" chirped Polly as she fluttered around the basement. The basement was always busy with sounds - *drip, drop* went the pipes, *creak, creak* went the old wooden stairs, and *rustle, rustle* went the mice in the corners.

One sunny morning, Polly discovered something wonderful. Behind a dusty box, she found old musical instruments! There was a tiny drum, a small bell, some wooden blocks, and even a little harmonica.

"Oh my! These would make beautiful music!" Polly squawked excitedly. She tried to play all the instruments at once, but it was very hard. The drum was too big for her tiny feet, and she couldn't hold the harmonica and ring the bell at the same time.

*Thump, clang, jingle!* The sounds were all mixed up and didn't sound pretty at all.

"This isn't working," sighed Polly, her colorful feathers drooping sadly. "I wish I could make beautiful music, but I can't play everything by myself."

Just then, she heard a small voice say, "Maybe we can help!"

Polly looked around and saw three little mice peeking out from behind a box. There was Squeaky, a gray mouse with big ears, Whiskers, a brown mouse with a long tail, and Tiny, the smallest mouse with bright black eyes.

"You want to help me make music?" asked Polly, tilting her head curiously.

"Yes!" squeaked Squeaky. "I love to tap my feet to rhythms!"

"And I can shake things really well!" added Whiskers, wiggling his whiskers.

"I'm small, but I can make big sounds!" piped up Tiny in his squeaky voice.

Polly's eyes sparkled with excitement. "That's a wonderful idea! But we need to be fair. Everyone should get to play something they like."

So they worked together to share the instruments fairly. Squeaky chose the drum because he loved to tap. *Tap, tap, tap!* His little paws made perfect rhythms.

Whiskers picked up the bell and shook it gently. *Ring, ring, ring!* The sound was clear and sweet.

Tiny grabbed the wooden blocks and clicked them together. *Click, click, click!* Even though he was small, he made lovely sounds.

And Polly sang beautiful songs while keeping time with her wings. "La, la, la! Music is the best when we share!"

Together, they created the most wonderful music the basement had ever heard. The *tap, tap* of the drum mixed with the *ring, ring* of the bell, the *click, click* of the blocks, and Polly's sweet singing.

"Listen!" whispered Polly. "When we work together, our music sounds so much better than when I tried to do it all alone!"

The four friends practiced every day, taking turns with different instruments and always making sure everyone felt included. Soon, even the other basement creatures - the spiders, the crickets, and the friendly old toad - came to listen to their beautiful concerts.

"Thank you for teaching me about teamwork," Polly told her friends. "Music is so much more fun when we share it together!"

From that day on, Polly's basement became the happiest, most musical place in the whole neighborhood, filled with friendship, fairness, and beautiful songs.

The End.
