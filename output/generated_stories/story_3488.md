# Splash the Dolphin's Sharing Adventure

Splash the dolphin loved bath time! Every evening, he would swim into the big, warm bathtub and play with his colorful toys. The bathroom was his favorite place to have fun and get squeaky clean.

"Wheee!" Splash squeaked happily as he dove under the bubbly water. His shiny gray skin sparkled with soap bubbles, and his friendly black eyes twinkled with joy.

One day, Splash heard a tiny voice coming from the corner of the bathtub. "Hello? Is anyone there?"

Splash poked his head up above the bubbles. "Who said that?" he asked, looking around with curiosity.

"Down here!" called the voice. Splash looked down and saw a small toy boat bobbing near his tail. On the boat stood a tiny figure with dark hair and beautiful, colorful clothes.

"Hi! I'm Maria," said the little figure. "I'm from Mexico, and this is my special boat. I've been waiting so long for someone to play with!"

Splash's eyes grew wide with excitement. "I'm Splash! I love making new friends! Would you like to play with my toys?"

Maria's face lit up. "Really? You would share with me?"

"Of course!" Splash said cheerfully. He swam over to his collection of bath toys - rubber ducks, floating rings, and spinning wheels. "Look at all these fun things we can play with together!"

But then Splash noticed something. Maria looked a little sad. "What's wrong?" he asked gently.

"Well," Maria said softly, "I only have my little boat. I don't have anything special to share with you."

Splash thought for a moment, then smiled his biggest dolphin smile. "But Maria, you DO have something special to share! Will you tell me about Mexico? I've never heard about other places before!"

Maria's eyes sparkled. "Oh yes! In Mexico, we have beautiful music and yummy food called tacos! And we celebrate with colorful piñatas and dance together!"

"That sounds amazing!" Splash clapped his flippers together. "Will you teach me a Mexican song?"

Maria began to sing a sweet lullaby in Spanish: "Estrellita, donde estas..." Her voice was like tiny silver bells ringing in the bathroom.

Splash listened carefully, then tried to sing along. Even though the words were new to him, the melody made him feel warm and happy inside.

"Now let me share something with you!" Splash said. He taught Maria how dolphins click and whistle to talk to each other. "Click-click-whistle means 'hello friend' in dolphin!"

Soon, Splash and Maria were working together as a team. Maria steered her boat while Splash pushed it gently with his nose, creating the most wonderful water adventures. They pretended to sail to Mexico, where they shared tacos made of soap bubbles and danced to Maria's beautiful songs.

"This is the best bath time ever!" Splash laughed, splashing gently so Maria wouldn't fall off her boat.

"It's so much more fun when we share and play together!" Maria agreed, clapping her tiny hands.

As the bath water began to cool down, Splash and Maria hugged goodbye. "Thank you for sharing your toys and for listening to my stories," Maria said.

"Thank you for teaching me about Mexico and your pretty songs!" Splash replied. "Sharing makes everything better, doesn't it?"

From that day on, Splash always remembered that sharing wasn't just about toys - it was about sharing stories, songs, and friendship too. And whenever he met someone new, he was excited to learn about their special culture and share his own dolphin world with them.

The End.
