# Nutkin's Big Helper Day

The morning sunshine peeked through the bathroom window. Nutkin the squirrel sat on the fluffy bath mat, his bushy tail twitching with excitement. Today was special - Mama Squirrel had asked him to help clean the bathroom!

"Remember, Nutkin," Mama Squirrel said gently, "follow each step carefully. First, put all the toys in the basket."

Nutkin nodded eagerly. His brown eyes sparkled. He loved helping Mama! But as he looked around the bathroom, his tail drooped a little. There were so many bath toys scattered everywhere - rubber ducks floating in puddles, colorful boats on the sink counter, and foam letters stuck to the mirror.

"This looks really hard," Nutkin whispered, his small paws fidgeting.

"You can do it, sweetie," Mama encouraged. "Just take it one step at a time."

Nutkin took a deep breath. The air smelled fresh and clean, like lavender soap. He scampered over to the first rubber duck. As he picked it up, it squeaked happily - *squeak, squeak!*

"Into the basket you go!" Nutkin giggled, carefully placing the duck inside the wicker toy basket.

But then Nutkin spotted a shiny bottle on the counter. It looked so interesting! He started to climb up to explore it.

"Wait, Nutkin," Mama called softly. "Remember what comes first?"

Nutkin paused, his tiny claws gripping the counter edge. "Oh! The toys in the basket first," he remembered. His whiskers twitched as he thought carefully.

He scurried back down and continued collecting toys. One by one, he gathered the boats - *splash* went the water as he lifted them. The foam letters made soft *pop* sounds as he peeled them from the mirror.

"I'm following the steps!" Nutkin chirped proudly.

Next, Mama showed him how to wipe the sink with a soft cloth. "Gentle circles, just like this," she demonstrated, her paw moving in smooth, round motions.

Nutkin copied her movements. The cloth felt warm and damp in his small paws. He made careful circles, watching the water droplets disappear. "Look, Mama! The sink is sparkling!"

"Beautiful work!" Mama smiled. "Now, what's the last step?"

Nutkin thought hard, his nose twitching. "Put the towels in the hamper!"

He gathered the damp towels, their soft fabric tickling his fur. Even though they were bigger than him, Nutkin was determined. He dragged them one by one to the hamper, his little heart beating fast with effort.

*Thump!* went the first towel into the hamper. *Thump!* went the second one.

Just then, Baby Chipmunk peeked around the corner. She looked sad, her tiny eyes filled with tears.

"What's wrong?" Nutkin asked kindly.

"I made a mess in my room," Baby Chipmunk sniffled. "I don't know how to clean it up."

Nutkin's chest puffed up with pride. "I can help you! I learned how to follow cleaning steps today!"

He took Baby Chipmunk's paw gently. "First, we put toys in their basket. Then we wipe surfaces clean. Last, we put dirty clothes in the hamper. One step at a time!"

Baby Chipmunk's face brightened like sunshine. "Really? You'll teach me?"

"Of course!" Nutkin beamed. "Following directions makes everything easier!"

Together, they scampered off to Baby Chipmunk's room. Nutkin felt warm and happy inside, his tail swishing with joy. He had learned that following directions step-by-step made big jobs feel small, and now he could help others too!

Mama Squirrel watched them go, her heart full of love and pride.

The End.
