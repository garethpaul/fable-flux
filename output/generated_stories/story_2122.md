# Luna's Calm Bath Time

Luna was a clever orange cat with bright green eyes and soft, fluffy fur. She lived with her loving family in a cozy house. Every evening, Luna watched her little human friend, Maya, take a bath in the warm, steamy bathroom.

The bathroom had white tiles that sparkled like stars. The bathtub was big and round, filled with warm, bubbly water that smelled like lavender flowers. Luna always sat on the soft blue rug, watching carefully.

But tonight was different. Maya was crying and splashing loudly in the tub.

"No bath! No bath!" Maya shouted. Water splashed everywhere. Her face was red and tears rolled down her cheeks.

Maya's mama looked tired and worried. "Please, Maya. Bath time can be fun," she said gently.

Luna's ears twitched. She knew she had to help. The clever cat remembered something special her own mama cat had taught her long ago.

"Meow," Luna said softly. She walked slowly to the edge of the bathtub.

Maya stopped crying and looked at Luna with big, curious eyes.

Luna began to purr. It was a deep, gentle sound that filled the bathroom like a warm hug. "Purrr-purrr-purrr," she sang.

"What's Luna doing?" Maya asked, wiping her tears.

"I think she's showing you something special," Mama said with a smile.

Luna sat very still. She closed her green eyes and breathed slowly. In and out. In and out. Her purring became a peaceful song.

"My mama cat taught me this," Luna seemed to say with her gentle purrs. "When the water feels scary, we breathe like the ocean waves. Slow and calm."

Maya watched Luna carefully. The clever cat's breathing was so peaceful. Maya tried to copy her.

"In... and out," Maya whispered. Her breathing became slower, just like Luna's.

Luna opened one green eye and winked at Maya. Then she began to purr a special tune her grandmother cat had taught her. It sounded like a lullaby from far away.

"That sounds like music," Maya said softly. "What language is Luna speaking?"

"Cats have their own special language," Mama explained. "Luna is sharing something her family taught her. It's like a tradition passed down from cat to cat."

Maya listened to Luna's gentle purr-song. The bathroom felt warm and safe now. The water didn't seem scary anymore.

"Can you teach me Luna's calm breathing?" Maya asked her mama.

Together, Maya and Mama breathed slowly like Luna. In and out. In and out. The bathroom filled with peaceful, quiet sounds.

Luna purred louder, happy that her tradition helped her friend feel better.

Maya giggled softly. "Luna's purr-language is beautiful. Thank you for sharing it with me."

The clever cat rubbed against Maya's hand with her soft fur. She was proud to share her family's tradition of staying calm and peaceful.

From that night on, every bath time became special. Maya would breathe slowly like Luna taught her. Luna would sit nearby, purring her grandmother's gentle songs.

The bathroom became a place of calmness and friendship, where different languages of love were shared.

Maya learned that when things feel scary or hard, breathing slowly and staying calm helps everything feel better. And Luna learned that sharing family traditions makes friendships even stronger.

The End.
