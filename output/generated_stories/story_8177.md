# Benny's Big Dam Dream

Benny the beaver loved working in the sunny backyard. The warm sunshine made his brown fur feel cozy. Green grass sparkled with morning dew. Pretty butterflies fluttered from flower to flower.

Every day, Benny collected sticks and stones. He wanted to build the most amazing dam ever! A big dam for all his friends to enjoy.

But today felt different. Benny looked at his pile of sticks. So small! He looked at his pile of stones. So tiny! His dam dream seemed too big for just one little beaver.

"This will take forever!" Benny said sadly. His flat tail slapped the ground. His little paws felt tired already.

Just then, Ruby the rabbit hopped by. Her pink nose twitched as she sniffed the sweet flowers.

"Why do you look so sad, Benny?" Ruby asked kindly.

"I want to build a wonderful dam," Benny sighed. "But I don't have enough sticks or stones. It's too much work for me alone!"

Ruby's ears perked up. "Maybe I can help! I know where lots of good sticks are hiding."

Soon, Ruby returned with her arms full of perfect building sticks. "Here you go, Benny!"

"Wow! Thank you so much!" Benny's eyes sparkled with joy.

Then Sam the squirrel scampered down from his tree. His bushy tail swished happily. "What are you two doing?"

"We're helping Benny build his dream dam!" Ruby explained.

"I love helping friends!" Sam chattered excitedly. "I can gather the smoothest stones from the garden path!"

Sam quickly collected a pile of beautiful round stones. Each one was perfect for building.

Next, Olivia the owl flew down with a gentle whoosh of her feathers. "Hoot! What's all this excitement about?"

"We're working together to build Benny's dam!" Sam squeaked.

"How wonderful!" Olivia hooted. "I can see the whole backyard from up high. I know exactly where to find the strongest branches!"

Soon, all four friends were working side by side. Ruby sorted the sticks by size. Sam arranged the stones in neat rows. Olivia flew overhead, spotting the best building spots. And Benny used his special beaver skills to fit everything together perfectly.

"This stick goes here!" Ruby called out.

"This stone fits right there!" Sam added cheerfully.

"Move that branch a little to the left!" Olivia hooted helpfully.

As they worked, they sang happy songs. They shared yummy snacks. They laughed and played together.

By afternoon, something magical had happened. Where once there was just an empty spot, now stood the most beautiful dam anyone had ever seen! It was strong and sturdy, with smooth stones and perfectly placed sticks.

"We did it!" Benny cheered, dancing around with joy. "But wait... we all did it together!"

"That's right!" Ruby giggled. "Working together made everything easier and more fun!"

"And faster too!" Sam added, doing a little flip.

"When friends help each other, amazing things can happen!" Olivia said wisely.

Benny looked at his wonderful friends. His heart felt warm and full of love. "Thank you all so much. This dam is even better than I dreamed because we built it together!"

From that day on, all the backyard animals came to play at Benny's beautiful dam. And whenever someone needed help with a big job, they remembered Benny's lesson: working together makes everything possible!

The End.
