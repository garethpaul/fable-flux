# Nutkin's Big Bathroom Helper Job

Nutkin was a busy little squirrel who lived in the big oak tree outside the Johnson family's house. Every morning, he watched through the bathroom window as the family got ready for their day.

One Tuesday morning, Nutkin heard crying from inside. Little Emma Johnson was standing by the sink, tears rolling down her cheeks.

"I can't reach the soap!" Emma sniffled. "And I can't brush my teeth by myself!"

Nutkin's fluffy tail twitched with worry. He wanted to help, but how could a small squirrel help with big bathroom problems?

"I know!" Nutkin chittered excitedly. "I can be a bathroom helper!"

Nutkin scampered down the tree and squeezed through the tiny bathroom window. The white tiles felt cool under his paws, and he could smell the fresh mint toothpaste.

"Hello!" Nutkin said cheerfully. "I'm here to help!"

Emma's eyes grew wide. "A talking squirrel! But... you're so little. How can you help me?"

Nutkin looked around the bright bathroom. The soap was too high. The toothbrush was too big for his tiny paws. Everything seemed impossible.

But Nutkin didn't give up. "Let me think," he said, tapping his small paw on his chin.

First, Nutkin tried to push the soap down. It was too heavy. Then he tried to carry the toothbrush. It was too big. Nutkin felt frustrated, but he took a deep breath.

"Emma," Nutkin said, "maybe we can work together!"

Emma wiped her tears. "How?"

"You can lift me up to reach things, and I can help you remember what to do!" Nutkin suggested. "We can be a team!"

Emma smiled for the first time that morning. "That sounds fun!"

Emma gently picked up Nutkin and held him near the soap dispenser. Nutkin pressed the button with both tiny paws. Sweet-smelling soap squirted into Emma's hands.

"Now rub your hands together!" Nutkin instructed, demonstrating with his own small paws. "Make lots of bubbles!"

Emma giggled as the bubbles tickled her fingers. The bathroom filled with the fresh scent of lavender soap.

Next, Emma lifted Nutkin to the toothbrush holder. Nutkin couldn't carry the big toothbrush, but he could point to it.

"Remember to brush up and down," Nutkin said, moving his paws to show her. "And don't forget the back teeth!"

Emma brushed carefully, following Nutkin's directions. "This is much more fun with a friend!" she said with a foamy smile.

When they finished, Nutkin helped Emma remember to put everything back in its place. The soap went in the holder, the toothbrush went in the cup, and the towel got hung up neatly.

"We did it!" Emma cheered, giving Nutkin a gentle hug.

"You were very responsible," Nutkin said proudly. "And I learned that even small helpers can make a big difference when we work together!"

From that day on, Nutkin visited Emma every morning. He couldn't do everything by himself, but by listening to Emma's needs and finding creative ways to help, they made the perfect bathroom team.

Emma learned to be responsible for her morning routine, and Nutkin learned that helping others feels wonderful, even when you have to be creative about how you do it.

"Thank you for being my bathroom helper," Emma said each morning.

And Nutkin always replied, "Thank you for teaching me that the best helpers listen first and never give up!"

The End.
