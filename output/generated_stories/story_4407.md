# Nutkin's Safe Art Studio

The morning sun peeked through the soft blue curtains of Emma's cozy bedroom. Little Nutkin the squirrel sat on the windowsill, his fluffy gray tail twitching with excitement. Today felt like a perfect day for creating something beautiful!

"Good morning, sunshine!" Nutkin chirped to himself, looking around the room. Emma was still sleeping peacefully in her bed, cuddling her teddy bear. Nutkin had discovered this wonderful bedroom last week and loved visiting when it was quiet and safe.

Nutkin's bright eyes spotted Emma's art supplies on the little wooden desk. Colorful crayons, safety scissors with round tips, and sheets of paper waited for creative hands. "Oh my!" whispered Nutkin. "I could make something special for Emma!"

But first, Nutkin remembered what his mama had taught him about being safe. "Always look before you leap, check for dangers, and be gentle with everything," he said quietly, repeating her wise words.

Nutkin carefully climbed down to the soft carpet. The fluffy rug felt warm and cozy under his tiny paws. He looked all around - no sharp corners nearby, no small pieces he might trip over. "Safety first!" he squeaked softly.

On the desk, Nutkin found fallen leaves that Emma had collected yesterday. Golden yellow, bright red, and warm orange leaves lay scattered about. "These would be perfect for my art project!" he thought.

Very carefully, Nutkin arranged the leaves on a piece of white paper. He used his tiny paws to create a beautiful pattern - a tree shape made entirely of real leaves! The red leaves became apples, the yellow ones were like sunshine, and the orange leaves looked like little birds.

"This needs something special," Nutkin whispered. He found a brown crayon and very gently held it in his paws. Drawing was tricky with little squirrel hands, but Nutkin was patient. Slowly, carefully, he drew a trunk for his leaf tree.

The crayon felt smooth and waxy. It smelled like art and creativity. Nutkin took his time, making sure each line was just right. "Patience makes everything better," he remembered his mama saying.

As Nutkin worked, he thought about how the leaves had once helped clean the air for everyone to breathe. "Even in art, we can show love for nature," he said proudly.

Just then, Emma began to wake up. Nutkin quickly and quietly gathered his art supplies, making sure everything was tidy and safe. He placed his beautiful leaf artwork where Emma would see it first thing.

"Oh my goodness!" Emma gasped when she saw the magical leaf picture on her desk. "How did this beautiful art get here?"

Nutkin smiled from his hiding spot behind the curtain. He felt warm and happy inside. He had created something beautiful, stayed safe the whole time, used nature's gifts respectfully, and been patient with himself as he learned.

Emma carefully picked up the artwork, her eyes sparkling with wonder. "This is the most beautiful nature art I've ever seen!" she said. "I'll keep it forever!"

From his cozy spot by the window, Nutkin felt proud. He had learned that being safe, patient, and caring for nature made his creative adventures even more special.

"Tomorrow I'll make another surprise," Nutkin thought happily. "But I'll always remember to be safe and kind to everything around me."

The End.
