# Rocco's Musical Mix-Up

In a bright musical practice room, a little raccoon named Rocco sat at his shiny piano. His tiny paws pressed the white and black keys. *Plink, plink, plink* went the happy sounds.

Rocco loved making music! His striped tail wiggled when he played. His black mask made him look like a musical superhero.

"La, la, la!" sang Rocco as he played his favorite song.

*CRASH!* went the cymbals next door.

Rocco stopped playing. His ears twitched. "What was that loud noise?"

*BOOM, BOOM, BOOM!* came the drums from another room.

"I can't hear my pretty piano music!" Rocco said, covering his ears with his paws.

The sounds got louder and louder. A trumpet went *TOOT, TOOT!* A violin squeaked *SCREECH!* 

Rocco felt angry. His fur stood up. "They're too loud! They're ruining my music!"

He marched to the first room. Inside, a little bear was playing drums with a big smile.

"Stop that noise!" Rocco said, crossing his arms.

The bear looked sad. "But I love playing drums. The beat makes me happy!"

"Well, it makes me unhappy!" Rocco huffed.

Next, he went to the trumpet room. A young fox was practicing scales.

"Your trumpet is too loud!" Rocco complained.

The fox's ears drooped. "I'm learning a song for my grandma. She taught me this music from her country."

Rocco felt a little funny in his tummy. But he was still upset about his piano time.

He stomped back to his room. But now the practice room felt quiet and lonely. Rocco pressed the piano keys, but the music didn't sound as beautiful as before.

*Tap, tap, tap* came a gentle knock.

"Come in," Rocco said softly.

The bear and fox peeked inside. They looked nervous.

"We're sorry we were loud," said the bear. "We didn't know you were trying to play piano."

"We love music too," said the fox. "Maybe we can find a way to play together?"

Rocco's heart felt warm. He remembered how sad they looked when he was mean to them.

"I'm sorry I was grumpy," Rocco said. "I love music so much, I forgot that you love it too."

The fox smiled. "My grandma's song is very gentle. Want to hear it?"

The fox played a soft, sweet melody. It sounded like golden honey.

"That's beautiful!" Rocco said. "It sounds like sunshine!"

"Can you play it on piano?" asked the bear.

Rocco tried the melody on his keys. It sounded magical! The bear tapped a quiet rhythm with his drumsticks. *Tap, tap, tap* - soft like raindrops.

"We sound wonderful together!" laughed Rocco.

From that day on, the three friends made music together. They learned songs from different places. The bear taught them rhythms from his family. The fox shared melodies from her grandma's country. Rocco played piano harmonies that made everything sound perfect.

The practice room filled with the most beautiful music anyone had ever heard. And the best part was sharing it with friends.

"Music is better when we make it together," Rocco said, his tail wiggling with joy.

And they all agreed!

The End.
