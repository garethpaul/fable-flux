# Leo's Kitchen Discovery

Leo the lion cub stretched his tiny paws and yawned as the morning sun peeked through the kitchen window. The warm kitchen smelled like cinnamon and honey, making his little tummy rumble with hunger.

"Good morning, Leo!" called Mama Lion from across the kitchen. She was stirring something that smelled absolutely wonderful in a big, shiny pot. "Are you ready for breakfast?"

Leo nodded eagerly, but as he looked around the bright kitchen, something caught his golden eyes. On the counter sat a basket full of colorful fruits he had never seen before. There were round orange ones, bumpy green ones, and long yellow ones that curved like smiles.

"Mama, what are those?" Leo asked, pointing with his small paw toward the mysterious basket.

Mama Lion smiled warmly. "Those are fruits from different places around the world, little one. Would you like to discover what they are?"

Leo's eyes sparkled with excitement. "Yes, please! Can I touch them?"

"Of course," Mama said, lifting Leo up so he could see better. "But first, let's wash our paws. That's how we take care of ourselves before we handle food."

At the sink, Leo felt the warm, soapy water tickle his paws. "The water feels nice and bubbly!" he giggled, scrubbing between his tiny claws just like Mama showed him.

With clean paws, Leo began his discovery adventure. He picked up the orange fruit first. "It feels bumpy like a basketball!" he said, rolling it gently in his paws.

"That's an orange," Mama explained. "It's sweet and juicy inside."

Next, Leo touched the green, bumpy fruit. "This one feels like it has little mountains all over it!"

"Very good observation! That's an avocado. It's creamy and smooth inside, perfect for growing cubs like you."

The curved yellow fruit made Leo laugh. "This one looks like it's smiling at me!"

"That's a banana," Mama chuckled. "And it is happy to meet you! Bananas give us energy to play and grow strong."

Leo wanted to try everything at once, but his tummy was getting very rumbly. "Mama, I want to eat them all right now!"

Mama Lion sat down beside Leo. "I understand you're excited, sweetheart. But our bodies work best when we eat just the right amount. How about we make a fair plan? We can try a little bit of each fruit with our breakfast, and save the rest for snacks later."

Leo thought about this. It seemed like a good compromise. "That sounds fair! And then I can discover how each one tastes!"

Together, they prepared a beautiful breakfast. Leo helped wash the fruits, feeling proud as he took care of each one carefully. The orange was indeed sweet and juicy, making Leo's whiskers twitch with delight. The avocado was creamy and mild, and the banana was soft and sweet.

"I discovered that I love trying new things!" Leo announced happily, his golden fur glistening in the warm kitchen light.

"And I discovered that my little cub is becoming very wise about taking care of himself," Mama said, nuzzling Leo's head. "You learned to wash your paws, try new foods, and make fair choices about eating."

Leo purred contentedly, his tummy happy and full. He had started the morning curious about strange fruits and ended it feeling proud of all his discoveries. The warm kitchen felt even cozier knowing he had learned something new about the world and about taking good care of himself.

The End.
