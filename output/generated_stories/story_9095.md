# Melody's Office Adventure

In a cozy home office with tall bookshelves and a big wooden desk, lived a cheerful little bird named Melody. She had bright yellow feathers that sparkled like sunshine and a voice as sweet as honey.

Every morning, Melody would perch on the windowsill and sing beautiful songs. "Tweet-tweet-trill!" she would chirp happily, watching the people work at their computers below.

But one day, Melody felt very hungry. Her little tummy was making grumbly sounds. "Rumble, rumble," it went. She looked around the office for something to eat, but all she could see were papers, pens, and computer screens.

"Oh dear," Melody sighed. "I'm so hungry, but I don't know where to find the right food for a little bird like me."

Just then, she heard a gentle voice from below. "Hello there, little bird! Are you okay?" It was Sarah, who worked at the big desk.

Melody flew down carefully and perched on the edge of Sarah's desk. "Tweet-tweet," she sang softly. "I'm very hungry, but I don't want to take food that isn't meant for me."

Sarah smiled warmly. "You're such a polite little bird! I can see you're hungry, but you're being very respectful by asking. That's wonderful!"

"But what can I eat?" Melody asked, tilting her tiny head.

"Well," said Sarah thoughtfully, "I have some crackers in my desk, but those aren't the best food for birds. Birds need special foods like seeds and berries to stay healthy and strong."

Melody's bright eyes lit up. "Really? I didn't know that!"

Sarah nodded. "Yes! Just like how I need vegetables and fruits to stay healthy, you need foods that are just right for your little bird body."

Then Sarah had an idea. "Would you like to work together to solve this problem?"

"Oh yes!" chirped Melody excitedly, flapping her tiny wings.

Sarah opened her desk drawer and pulled out her phone. "Let me call my friend Tom who works downstairs. He loves birds and always keeps bird seeds in his office."

A few minutes later, Tom came upstairs carrying a small bag of colorful seeds. "These are perfect for our feathered friend!" he said with a big smile.

Tom poured some seeds into a small dish. The seeds were brown, black, and striped - they looked like tiny treasures! "These sunflower seeds will give you lots of energy," he explained to Melody.

Melody pecked at the seeds delicately. "Mmm! These taste wonderful! Much better than crackers would have been."

"See?" said Sarah. "When we work together and respect each other's needs, everyone feels better!"

Tom added, "And it's great that you asked for help instead of just taking food that wasn't right for you. That shows you have good boundaries!"

As Melody ate her healthy breakfast, she felt grateful and happy. Her tummy stopped rumbling, and she felt strong enough to sing her most beautiful song yet.

"Tweet-trill-tweet!" she sang joyfully. "Thank you for helping me and teaching me about healthy bird food!"

Sarah and Tom clapped their hands. "Your song is so beautiful, Melody! We're different - you're a bird and we're people - but we can still be wonderful friends and help each other."

From that day on, Melody, Sarah, and Tom worked together as a team. Melody would sing cheerful songs to make their workday pleasant, while Sarah and Tom made sure she always had the right foods to stay healthy and strong.

The End.
