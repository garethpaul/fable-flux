# Benny's Big Helper Day

Benny the beaver lived in a cozy playful living room. Soft cushions sat everywhere like fluffy clouds. Colorful blocks scattered across the carpet. A big toy chest stood in the corner, overflowing with fun things.

Benny loved to build with his strong teeth and busy paws. He made towers with blocks. He stacked books high. Building made him feel happy and proud.

But today, something was different. Benny heard soft crying sounds coming from behind the big toy chest.

"Hello?" Benny called out. "Is someone there?"

A tiny voice answered. "Yes... I'm stuck!"

Benny waddled over on his short legs. Behind the toy chest, he found Luna the little lamb. Her fluffy white wool was caught on a loose nail.

"Oh no!" said Benny. "You're really stuck!"

Luna's eyes filled with tears. "I was trying to get my ball. It rolled back here. Now I can't move!"

Benny's heart felt heavy seeing Luna so sad. He wanted to help, but the space was very tight. His big beaver body might not fit.

"I'm too small to help," Benny said quietly. "Maybe I should find a grown-up."

"Please don't leave me!" Luna cried. "I'm scared!"

Benny took a deep breath. His whiskers twitched as he thought hard. "You know what? I AM big enough to help! I just need to be brave."

Benny squeezed his round body into the narrow space. It was a tight fit! His brown fur brushed against the wall. But he kept going.

"I can reach the nail!" Benny said. His sharp teeth were perfect for this job. Carefully, he nibbled at the threads holding Luna's wool.

"Almost there!" Benny huffed and puffed. His strong teeth worked and worked.

POP! The wool came free!

"I'm loose!" Luna jumped up and down with joy. "Thank you, Benny! You saved me!"

But they still had another problem. Luna's red ball was way in the back corner.

"I can get that too!" said Benny. He used his flat tail like a paddle. Swish, swish! He pushed the ball toward Luna.

"Here's your ball!" Benny smiled big, showing his front teeth.

Luna hugged the ball tight. "You're the best helper ever, Benny!"

As they crawled out from behind the toy chest, more friends appeared. Rosie the rabbit and Max the mouse had heard the commotion.

"What happened?" asked Rosie, her pink nose twitching.

"Benny rescued me!" Luna told everyone. "He was so brave and helpful!"

Max's tiny voice squeaked with excitement. "Wow, Benny! You're a hero!"

Benny's chest puffed up with pride. He felt warm and fuzzy inside. Helping Luna made him happier than building any tower.

"I learned something today," Benny said thoughtfully. "When someone needs help, I shouldn't worry about being too big or too small. I should just try my best!"

Luna nodded. "And I learned to ask for help when I need it!"

All the friends spent the rest of the day playing together in the living room. They built block towers as a team. They shared Luna's ball. And whenever someone needed help, they all worked together.

That night, as Benny curled up in his cozy corner, he felt proud and happy. Tomorrow, he would look for more ways to help his friends.

"Helping others feels even better than building," Benny whispered to himself as he drifted off to sleep.

The End.
