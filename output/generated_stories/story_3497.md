# Bubbles Chooses the Beat

Bubbles was a playful dolphin who loved to make sounds. Click, whistle, squeak! Today, Bubbles flapped her tail and swam into a bright musical practice room. The room was full of soft lights, shiny drums, tiny bells, and a big piano that went plink-plonk when you touched the keys.

“Hello!” Bubbles chirped, making happy bubbles rise. “I am ready to make music!”

Her friends were there too. Mei the panda tapped a drum with gentle paws. Tiko the parrot ruffled green and red feathers and sang, “La-la-la!” Samir the camel hummed low and warm, “Hmmmm.” Amina the girl smiled and waved a sparkly ribbon like a river. They all came from different places, and they liked to learn new things together.

A sign on the wall said, “Today’s Plan: Pick a beat, tell a story, play together.”

Amina clapped. “We will make a song about a brave journey! But first, we must choose the beat.”

Bubbles wiggled with joy. “I want to go fast!” she squeaked. “Zip-zip-zoom!”

Mei tapped slow. “I like slow. Tap… tap… tap,” she said softly.

Tiko squawked, “Fast is fun! But I can sing slow, too. Laaa!”

Samir blinked his long lashes. “A medium beat feels comfy,” he said. “Not too fast, not too slow.”

Bubbles looked around. The room smelled like fresh wood and a little like sweet orange from a cleaner. The piano keys felt cool under her fin. She made a plink. Her tummy felt fluttery. So many choices!

Amina smiled. “Let’s make a good choice together. First, we think. Then, we try. Then, we decide.”

Bubbles nodded. “Think, try, decide,” she whispered. “I can do that.”

“Think,” Amina said. “What do we want our music to feel like?”

Bubbles closed her eyes. She pictured a big blue sea and a small boat. She heard wind shhh and waves whoosh. “I want our music to feel brave,” Bubbles said. “Brave and kind.”

Mei hugged her drum. “Brave can be slow and strong.”

Tiko swayed. “Brave can be fast and bright!”

Samir smiled. “Brave can be steady and calm.”

“Now we try,” Amina said. “We will try three beats. We will listen with our ears and with our hearts.”

First, they tried a fast beat. Tiko sang quick notes, “La-la-la-la!” Mei tippity-tapped like raindrops. Bubbles clicked fast, click-click-click! The room buzzed, and Bubbles felt her tail swish-swish with excitement. But Mei’s face looked a little worried.

Next, they tried a slow beat. Mei tapped, “Thoom… thoom…” Samir hummed low and long. Bubbles made slow whistles, whooooo. The sound was big and brave, like a tall mountain. But Tiko’s wings drooped. “It is hard for me to stay slow,” he said softly.

Last, they tried a medium beat. Amina clapped steady. Samir hummed “Hmm-hmm,” like a camel walking. Mei tapped soft-steady. Tiko sang, “La-la,” with bright notes on top. Bubbles clicked and whistled in a happy pattern, like waves that roll and roll.

Bubbles listened. Her ears felt warm. Her heart felt calm and brave at the same time. She saw Mei relax. She saw Tiko smile. She felt the room’s air move with the steady beat.

Amina asked, “What did we learn?”

Bubbles took a deep breath. “We thought. We tried. Now we decide,” she said. “I choose the medium beat.”

“Why?” asked Amina kindly.

“It helps all of us,” Bubbles said. “Mei likes steady. Tiko can sing bright. Samir feels comfy. I can click and whistle a brave story. The medium beat is best for our team.”

Mei clapped her paws. “Good choice!”

Tiko flapped. “Great choice!”

Samir nodded. “Wise choice.”

Amina smiled. “Now we tell our story with music.”

They began. Amina whispered, “Once, friends from many places met by the sea.” She waved her ribbon like a path.

Mei’s drum became footsteps. Tap tap, steady and strong.

Samir’s hum became a warm desert wind. Hmmmm, like sand singing.

Tiko’s voice became bright birds in the sky. La-la! La-la!

Bubbles’ clicks and whistles became the sea talking. Click, whoosh, wheee!

The room filled with sounds and feelings. Bubbles felt the story grow. “Our friends see a storm,” she whistled low. The drum grew louder, thoom-thoom. The hum grew deeper. Tiko sang a brave, “La!”

Bubbles clicked in the medium beat and called, “We will stay together.” The sounds joined hands, like friends. The storm passed. The music became sunny and light again. The ribbon danced like sunshine on water.

They ended with a soft tap, a gentle hum, a sweet la, and a happy click.

Amina said, “What did we do well?”

Bubbles beamed. “We listened. We tried. We chose the beat that helped everyone. We told a brave story together.”

Mei patted her drum. “We are different, and that makes our music rich.”

Tiko preened. “My quick notes fit on top!”

Samir smiled. “My calm hum made a safe place.”

Bubbles splashed a tiny splash. “I learned to make a good choice. Think. Try. Decide. Choose what helps the team.”

Amina nodded. “That is smart decision-making. And we celebrated our differences.”

Bubbles felt proud and peaceful. The room smelled warm and sweet. The piano keys still felt cool. Her heart beat steady, like their song.

“Friends from many places can make one beautiful sound,” Bubbles said.

“Yes,” the friends said together. “We can.”

The End.
