# Splash the Dolphin's Garden Adventure

Once upon a time, there lived a playful dolphin named Splash who had a very special secret. Unlike other dolphins who lived in the ocean, Splash lived in a magical garden shed behind Mrs. Lily's house. The shed was filled with colorful watering cans, soft garden gloves, and the sweet smell of fresh flowers.

Every morning, Splash would swim through the air around the shed, doing happy flips and spins. "Wheee!" he would squeak with joy. His shiny gray skin sparkled in the sunlight that peeked through the wooden windows.

One sunny day, Splash heard tiny voices coming from outside the shed. "We're so thirsty!" squeaked the voices. "The sun is so hot, and we need water!"

Splash peeked out and saw three little rabbits sitting sadly by the dried-up garden. Their fluffy ears drooped, and their pink noses twitched with worry.

"Oh no!" said Splash, his kind heart feeling heavy. "I want to help, but I've never shared my special shed before. What if they don't like it? What if they take all my space?"

Splash swam in worried circles around his shed, making soft clicking sounds. The watering cans jingled gently as he passed by them.

Then Splash remembered what his mama had taught him: "When we share what we have, we make the world brighter for everyone."

Taking a deep breath of the flowery air, Splash called out, "Little rabbits! Would you like to come into my shed? I have lots of fresh, cool water here!"

The rabbits' eyes grew wide with surprise and joy. "Really?" asked the smallest rabbit. "You would share with us?"

"Of course!" said Splash, doing a happy somersault. "But I need you to be gentle with my things, okay? This is my special home."

The rabbits nodded eagerly. "We promise to be careful and respectful!"

Splash opened the shed door wide. The rabbits hopped inside, their soft paws pattering on the wooden floor. "Wow!" they gasped, looking around at the magical shed.

Splash showed them to a large, shallow pan filled with crystal-clear water. "Here you go, friends! Drink as much as you need."

As the rabbits drank happily, Splash noticed something wonderful. Sharing his space didn't make him feel crowded – it made him feel warm and happy inside! The shed felt more alive with friends in it.

"Thank you, Splash!" said the rabbits, their bellies full of cool water. "You're so kind and generous!"

After the rabbits left, Splash did excited loops around his shed. "That felt amazing!" he squeaked. "I helped my friends, and I still have my special space!"

From that day on, Splash learned that being generous didn't mean giving away everything he had. It meant sharing thoughtfully while still taking care of himself. He set up a special visiting time each day when woodland friends could come for water and a chat.

Splash also started doing morning exercises with his new friends – stretching, jumping, and swimming through the air together. The shed filled with laughter and joy every single day.

And whenever young animals in the garden needed help, they knew they could count on Splash the generous dolphin, who had learned that sharing with others makes everyone's heart grow bigger and brighter.

The End.
