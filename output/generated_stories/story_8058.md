# Ollie's Rooftop Garden Adventure

High up on a tall building in the city, a playful otter named Ollie poked his whiskered nose over the edge of a rooftop. His dark eyes sparkled with curiosity as he looked around at the gray concrete space.

"Oh my!" Ollie said, his voice full of wonder. "This rooftop is so... empty. It needs something special!"

Ollie's wet paws made little puddles as he explored the flat roof. The sun felt warm on his brown fur, and the wind tickled his whiskers. But something made him feel a little sad.

"I miss the green plants from my river home," Ollie sighed. "This gray space feels lonely."

Just then, he spotted something colorful in the corner - old flower pots, some with dried soil, and a few forgotten seeds scattered on the ground. Ollie's tail began to wiggle with excitement.

"I have an idea!" he chirped. "I can make this rooftop beautiful again!"

Ollie carefully gathered the pots with his paws. Some were cracked, but he thought they looked interesting that way. He found an old watering can and filled it with fresh rainwater from a puddle.

"Every plant needs water to grow," Ollie said to himself, remembering what his mama had taught him. "Just like how I need clean water in my river!"

As Ollie worked, he noticed a small sparrow watching him from nearby. The bird looked worried.

"What are you doing, otter?" asked the sparrow. "This isn't your home!"

Ollie paused and thought carefully about his feelings. He felt excited about his idea, but he also understood why the bird might be concerned.

"You're right, little sparrow," Ollie said kindly. "This isn't my river home. But I want to help make this place beautiful for everyone - including you! Would you like to help me plant a garden?"

The sparrow's eyes lit up. "A garden? With flowers and places for birds to rest?"

"Yes!" Ollie clapped his paws together. "We can work together!"

Ollie and the sparrow became a wonderful team. Ollie used his strong paws to fill the pots with soil, while the sparrow used her tiny beak to carefully place seeds in each pot. They arranged the pots in a colorful circle.

"This pot can grow tall sunflowers," said Ollie, patting the soil gently.

"And this one can have tiny daisies for butterflies!" added the sparrow.

As they worked, more city animals came to see what was happening. A friendly pigeon brought more seeds, and a curious squirrel helped arrange small stones around the pots.

When they finished, Ollie stepped back and looked at their creation. The rooftop now had a beautiful circle of planted pots, ready to grow into a wonderful garden.

"We did this together," Ollie said, feeling proud and happy. "When we use our creativity and work as a team, we can make any place more beautiful!"

The sparrow chirped happily. "And we're helping the environment too! Plants clean the air and give homes to insects and birds."

Ollie nodded, his whiskers twitching with joy. "Even though I missed my river, I found a way to bring nature here. And I made new friends too!"

As the sun began to set, painting the sky in pretty colors, Ollie felt grateful for his adventure. He had learned that creativity and kindness could turn any space into something wonderful.

"Tomorrow, I'll come back to water our garden," Ollie promised his new friends.

The End.
