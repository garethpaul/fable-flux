# Ollie's Tidy Time Adventure

In a cozy closet hideaway behind the winter coats, lived a playful otter named Ollie. His soft brown fur sparkled in the dim light, and his whiskers twitched with excitement every morning. But today, Ollie's special hideaway looked very messy!

"Oh my!" squeaked Ollie, looking around at the scattered toys, crumpled papers, and dirty socks everywhere. "How did my hideaway get so untidy?"

Ollie's tummy rumbled. "I'm hungry, but I also want to play, and I should clean up too," he said, scratching his furry head. "What should I do first?"

Just then, Ollie remembered what Mama Otter had taught him. "When you have lots to do, make a plan!" she had said. "Think about what's most important first."

Ollie sat down on a pile of soft towels and thought carefully. "Let me see... I need to eat breakfast, brush my teeth, and clean my hideaway. But which one first?"

His tummy rumbled again, louder this time. "Breakfast first!" he decided. "I can't think well when I'm hungry."

After munching on his favorite fish crackers, Ollie felt much better. "Now I can think clearly," he said, patting his full belly. "Next, I should brush my teeth so they stay clean and healthy."

Ollie grabbed his tiny toothbrush and brushed his sharp little teeth until they sparkled. "Swish, swish, brush, brush!" he sang happily. "Clean teeth make me feel fresh!"

Now it was time for the big job - cleaning his hideaway. But Ollie had a clever idea! He noticed some empty boxes in the corner.

"I can use these old boxes to organize my things!" Ollie said excitedly. "That's recycling AND organizing!"

He labeled one box "TOYS" with colorful crayons, another "BOOKS," and a third "CLOTHES." As he sorted his belongings, Ollie discovered things he had forgotten about - a bouncy ball, his favorite story about sea adventures, and a cozy scarf.

"This is fun!" Ollie giggled as he placed each item in its proper box. "Everything has a home now!"

But then Ollie found something that made him wrinkle his nose. "Eww! These socks are stinky!" He quickly put them in a special bag for washing. "Clean socks are much better than smelly ones!"

As Ollie worked, he sang a little song: "First things first, that's the way! Plan my time throughout the day! Clean and tidy, fresh and neat, makes my hideaway complete!"

When he finished, Ollie looked around his transformed hideaway. The boxes were neatly arranged, his bed was made with soft blankets, and everything smelled fresh and clean.

"Wow!" Ollie clapped his paws together. "When I made a plan and did things in the right order, everything got done! And I still have time to play!"

Ollie curled up with his favorite book, feeling proud and happy. His hideaway was now a perfect place to read, play, and rest.

"Tomorrow, I'll remember to plan my time again," Ollie said with a big yawn. "First breakfast, then brush teeth, then tidy up. That way, I'll have more time for fun!"

As the sun set through the closet window, Ollie snuggled into his clean, cozy hideaway, dreaming of tomorrow's adventures.

The End.
