# Bella's Rooftop Building Day

Bella was a busy beaver with bright brown fur and a big flat tail. She lived in a cozy den, but today she had a special job to do on the rooftop of the tall apartment building.

"Tap, tap, tap!" went Bella's strong teeth as she worked on fixing the wooden deck. The morning sun felt warm on her fuzzy back.

"This will be the most wonderful rooftop garden!" Bella said happily. "Everyone will love playing here!"

But then she heard a tiny voice. "Excuse me, Miss Beaver?"

Bella looked around and saw a small gray pigeon sitting on the edge of the roof. The pigeon looked worried.

"Hello there, little friend," Bella said gently. "What's wrong?"

"My name is Pip," chirped the pigeon. "This is where my family builds our nest every spring. We've lived here for three years. Are you going to make us move away?"

Bella stopped working and sat down. She could see that Pip was scared. His little feathers were all ruffled up.

"Oh my!" Bella said softly. "I didn't know this was your home. I'm so sorry! I want everyone to be happy here."

"But where will we live?" Pip asked sadly. "My babies will hatch soon."

Bella thought carefully. She wanted to finish her garden, but she also wanted to help Pip's family. There had to be a way for everyone to be happy together.

"Pip, would you like to help me make a plan?" Bella asked kindly. "Maybe we can work together!"

Pip's eyes lit up. "Really? You want my help?"

"Of course!" Bella smiled. "You know this rooftop better than anyone. Can you show me where your nest is?"

Pip flew to a quiet corner where some old boards made a cozy shelter. "Here's our home," he cooed proudly.

Bella looked around thoughtfully. "What if we build the garden on this side, and make your corner even more beautiful? We could add some pretty flowers and a little bird bath!"

"That sounds wonderful!" Pip chirped excitedly. "And I could help you pick the best spots for plants. I know where the sun shines brightest!"

Soon, more friends came to help. A friendly cat named Whiskers brought some flower seeds. A helpful rabbit named Rosie carried water in her cheeks. Even a wise old owl named Oliver gave advice about the best places for different plants.

"This is amazing!" Bella laughed as everyone worked together. "When we talk nicely to each other and share our ideas, we can solve any problem!"

Pip flew in happy circles. "And when we listen to each other, everyone feels important!"

By sunset, the rooftop looked magical. There was a beautiful garden with colorful flowers, a cozy corner for Pip's family with a tiny bird bath, and plenty of space for everyone to play.

"Thank you for listening to me, Bella," Pip said gratefully. "You helped me feel safe and happy."

"Thank you for sharing your ideas, Pip!" Bella replied. "This garden is much better because we worked together!"

All the friends sat together watching the stars appear. They learned that when you use peaceful words and kind listening, you can solve problems and make everyone happy.

The End.
