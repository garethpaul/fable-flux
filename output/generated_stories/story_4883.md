# Gigi the Giraffe's Safe Space Mission

Gigi the giraffe loved her special closet hideaway. It was cozy and quiet, with soft blankets and her favorite books. The closet smelled like lavender from mama's sachets. Gigi felt safe and happy there.

One morning, Gigi heard crying from outside her closet. She poked her long neck out and saw her little brother Pip sitting on the floor.

"What's wrong, Pip?" Gigi asked gently.

"The big kids won't let me play," Pip sniffled. "They say I'm too little. I need somewhere safe to go."

Gigi's heart felt warm and caring. "Would you like to see my special hideaway?"

Pip's eyes lit up. "Really? You'd share it with me?"

"Of course!" Gigi said. "But first, we need to make it safe for both of us."

Gigi showed Pip her closet. It was perfect for one giraffe, but two giraffes would need more room.

"We need to negotiate with Mama," Gigi explained. "That means we talk nicely and ask for help."

They found Mama in the kitchen. She was making banana pancakes that smelled sweet and delicious.

"Mama," Gigi said politely, "Pip needs a safe space too. Can we make our closet bigger?"

Mama smiled. "That's very thoughtful, Gigi. But we can't make the closet bigger. What else could we do?"

Gigi thought hard. She didn't give up easily. "Maybe we could take turns? Or find another safe space?"

"Those are good ideas," Mama said. "What about the reading nook under the stairs?"

Pip clapped his hooves. "That sounds perfect!"

But when they looked, the space was full of old boxes and spider webs. It didn't look safe at all.

"Don't worry," Gigi said with determination. "We can fix this together!"

Gigi and Pip worked as a team. They moved the boxes carefully, one by one. Gigi used her long neck to reach the high spider webs with a soft broom. Pip swept the floor with a small brush.

"Safety first!" Gigi reminded Pip. "We check for sharp edges and make sure everything is clean."

They found soft cushions and arranged them carefully. Gigi brought some of her books to share. Pip added his favorite stuffed elephant.

"Now we both have safe spaces!" Gigi said proudly.

Mama came to see their work. "You two did something wonderful. You helped each other and made sure everyone felt safe and included."

"It's only fair that everyone has a special place," Gigi said. "And it's more fun when we help each other!"

That afternoon, when the big kids were being too loud and rough, Pip had his own cozy hideaway. Gigi visited him there, and they read stories together.

"Thank you for not giving up on me," Pip whispered.

"That's what big sisters do," Gigi said, giving him a gentle hug. "We keep trying until everyone feels safe and happy."

From that day on, Gigi and Pip took turns in both safe spaces. Sometimes they played together, sometimes apart. But they always made sure the other felt included and cared for.

Gigi learned that the best part of having a safe space wasn't keeping it to herself - it was making sure everyone in her family felt safe and loved too.

The End.
