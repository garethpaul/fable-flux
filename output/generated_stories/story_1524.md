# Toby's Big Lab Adventure

Toby the tortoise wore tiny safety goggles on his wise old head. His green shell gleamed under the bright lab lights. He was the leader of the basement science lab, and today felt very important.

The lab smelled like fresh soap and had shiny metal tables everywhere. Colorful bottles lined the tall shelves. Everything had its special place.

"Good morning, everyone!" called Toby in his slow, gentle voice. "Today we will make rainbow bubbles!"

Three little mice scurried into the lab. Pip, Pop, and Puff squeaked with excitement. Their tiny paws patted the smooth floor as they ran around.

"Can we start now? Can we? Can we?" squeaked Pip, jumping up and down.

"First, we must follow the safety rules," said Toby firmly but kindly. "Put on your goggles, please."

Pop groaned loudly. "But Toby, that's boring! We want bubbles NOW!"

Puff nodded fast. "Yeah! Let's just mix everything together!"

Toby took a deep breath. Being a good leader was sometimes hard work. He needed to keep everyone safe and happy.

"I understand you're excited," said Toby. "But what if we get soap in our eyes? That would hurt and ruin our fun."

The mice looked at each other. They didn't want hurt eyes.

"How about this," suggested Toby. "If you put on your goggles first, you can each pick one special color for our bubble mix."

"Really?" asked Pip, her whiskers twitching with joy.

"Really," smiled Toby. "Good leaders listen to their team."

The mice quickly put on their tiny goggles. They looked like little scientists now!

"I pick blue!" said Pip.

"I want red!" squeaked Pop.

"Yellow for me!" giggled Puff.

Toby nodded proudly. "Perfect! Now, let's follow the recipe step by step."

He pointed to a big chart on the wall with colorful pictures. "First, we measure the soap. Then we add water. Last comes the special color powder."

The mice watched carefully as Toby showed them how to measure. His slow, steady movements made everything clear and safe.

"Can I pour the soap?" asked Pop politely.

"May I add the water?" said Pip, raising her tiny paw.

"What about me?" wondered Puff.

Toby's eyes twinkled. "You can all help! We'll take turns. That's what good teammates do."

One by one, each mouse carefully added their part. Pop poured the soap slowly. Pip added the warm water drop by drop. Puff sprinkled in the magical color powder.

The mixture began to shimmer and sparkle. Beautiful swirls of blue, red, and yellow danced together.

"Wow!" gasped all three mice together.

Toby dipped a special bubble wand into the mixture. He blew gently, and amazing rainbow bubbles floated through the air! They popped with tiny musical sounds.

"We did it!" cheered the mice, clapping their little paws.

"Yes, we did," said Toby warmly. "Because we worked together, followed directions, and kept everyone safe."

Pop looked up at Toby with big, grateful eyes. "Thank you for teaching us the right way."

"And for listening to our ideas too," added Pip.

Puff nodded. "You're the best leader ever, Toby!"

As rainbow bubbles filled the basement lab with magic, Toby felt proud and happy. He had learned that good leaders keep everyone safe, listen to others, and find ways to make everyone feel special.

The lab sparkled with joy, friendship, and the most beautiful bubbles anyone had ever seen.

The End.
