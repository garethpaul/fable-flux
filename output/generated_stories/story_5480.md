# Penny Panda's Healthy Car Trip

Penny Panda was so excited! Today, her family was going on a long car trip to visit Grandma Bear. She bounced up and down in her car seat as Mama Panda started the engine.

"Vroom, vroom!" went the car as they drove down the sunny road.

After just ten minutes, Penny's tummy started to rumble. "Mama, I'm hungry!" she said, patting her fluffy black and white belly.

"Oh my," said Mama Panda, looking worried. "I forgot to pack our healthy snacks! All we have are these sugary cookies from yesterday."

Penny looked at the bright package of cookies. They smelled sweet and looked yummy, but she remembered what Papa Panda had taught her about healthy eating. "Mama, cookies will make my tummy hurt on our long trip. Can we find something better?"

Mama Panda smiled proudly. "You're absolutely right, my smart little panda. But what can we do? We're already on the road."

Penny put her paw to her chin and thought hard. She looked out the window and saw a colorful farmers market with a big red sign. "Look, Mama! Can we stop there?"

"That's a wonderful idea!" said Mama Panda, turning into the parking lot.

At the farmers market, Penny's nose filled with the fresh smell of fruits and vegetables. She saw bright red apples that gleamed in the sunshine, orange carrots that crunched when the farmer tapped them, and purple grapes that looked like tiny jewels.

"Let's build the perfect healthy snack pack!" said Penny excitedly.

First, they picked sweet, crispy apple slices. "These will give me energy for our trip," said Penny, taking a small bite. The apple was juicy and made a satisfying crunch.

Next, they chose baby carrots. "Orange vegetables help my eyes stay strong," Penny said, remembering her lessons.

Then they found a container of mixed nuts. "These will help my brain think better," she added, shaking the container and listening to the nuts rattle.

Finally, they picked a bottle of cool, clear water. "Water keeps my body happy and healthy," Penny said, already feeling proud of their choices.

Back in the car, Penny carefully arranged all their healthy snacks in a special basket. "Now I'm prepared for our whole trip!" she said happily.

As they drove, Penny munched on her apple slices. The sweet juice made her smile, and she felt energetic and ready for adventure. Her tummy felt comfortable and happy.

"You know what, Penny?" said Mama Panda, looking at her daughter in the rearview mirror. "Your patience and smart thinking saved our trip. Instead of eating cookies that would make us feel yucky, we have delicious healthy food that makes us feel strong!"

Penny beamed with pride. "And I learned that when there's a problem, I can think of a solution! Planning ahead with healthy food makes everything better."

When they finally reached Grandma Bear's house, Penny still felt wonderful. She had energy to play and her tummy felt just right.

"I'm going to remember to always pack healthy snacks before our next trip," said Penny. "Being prepared with good food makes adventures even more fun!"

Grandma Bear hugged her tight. "What a wise and healthy little panda you are!"

The End.
