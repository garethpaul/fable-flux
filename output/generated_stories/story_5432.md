# Ollie's International Friendship Feast

Ollie the otter wiggled his whiskers as he peeked into Mama's big pantry. The shelves were filled with colorful jars, boxes, and bags from all around the world! There were shiny red tomatoes from Italy, golden rice from Japan, and sweet mangoes from India.

"Wow!" squeaked Ollie, his brown eyes sparkling. "Look at all these yummy foods from different countries!"

Ollie had a wonderful idea. His friend Benny Bear was feeling sad because he missed his grandmother who lived far away in Mexico. "I'll make something special to cheer him up!" Ollie said, clapping his little paws together.

He reached up high and grabbed a jar of salsa from Mexico. *Crash!* Down tumbled three more jars, spilling colorful spices all over the clean pantry floor. The sweet smell of cinnamon mixed with the spicy scent of paprika.

"Oh no!" gasped Ollie, looking at the red and yellow mess. His fluffy tail drooped with worry.

Just then, Mama Otter came into the pantry. "Ollie, what happened here?" she asked, seeing the spilled spices everywhere.

Ollie's cheeks turned pink. He wanted to hide, but he knew what was right. "I'm sorry, Mama," he said in a small voice. "I was trying to get the salsa to help my friend Benny feel better. He misses his grandma in Mexico. But I made a big mess instead."

Mama Otter's expression softened. She knelt down and gave Ollie a warm hug. "I'm proud of you for telling the truth, little one. And what a kind heart you have, wanting to help your friend!"

"But I ruined everything," Ollie sniffled.

"Not everything," smiled Mama. "Let's clean this up together, and then I'll help you make something special for Benny. We can use foods from around the world to show him that even when people live far apart, we're all connected through friendship and kindness."

Ollie's eyes lit up like twinkling stars. "Really?"

Together, they swept up the spices. Then Mama showed Ollie how to make colorful friendship flags using tortillas from Mexico, cheese from France, and herbs from Italy. They rolled them up like little scrolls.

"These represent how food brings people from different countries together," explained Mama. "Just like how friendship connects hearts across the world."

When Benny Bear arrived, his sad face immediately brightened. "These smell amazing!" he said, taking a big sniff. "They remind me of the foods my grandma makes!"

"Each ingredient comes from a different country," Ollie said proudly. "Just like how friends can come from anywhere in the world, but we all care about each other!"

Benny hugged Ollie tight. "Thank you for thinking of me. This makes me feel like my grandma is close by."

As they munched on their international treats, Ollie felt warm and happy inside. He had learned that being honest about mistakes was important, and that caring for friends from different places made the world feel smaller and more connected.

"The world is like our pantry," Ollie told Benny. "Full of different, wonderful things that all belong together!"

Mama Otter smiled proudly as she watched the two friends share their special meal, knowing that Ollie had learned valuable lessons about honesty, kindness, and global friendship.

The End.
