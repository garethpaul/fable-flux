# Benny Beaver's Big Helper Day

Benny Beaver loved to help others. He had big front teeth perfect for gnawing wood, a flat tail that went *slap-slap* on the water, and the biggest heart in the whole forest. Today, Benny woke up extra early because he had promised to help his friend Rabbit Ruby clean her bathroom.

"Good morning, sunshine!" Benny said to himself in the mirror as he brushed his teeth. *Scrub, scrub, scrub* went his toothbrush. "Clean teeth make me feel strong and ready to help!"

Benny splashed cool water on his furry face. The water felt refreshing and made his whiskers tingle. He combed his brown fur until it was neat and tidy.

"A clean beaver is a happy beaver!" he sang as he washed his paws with sweet-smelling soap. The bubbles tickled between his fingers.

After breakfast, Benny packed his special helper kit: a scrub brush made from pine needles, natural soap he made from forest berries, and soft towels woven from cattail fluff. He was very resourceful and loved making useful things from nature.

*Knock, knock, knock!* Benny tapped on Ruby's door with his tail.

"Oh, Benny! Thank you for coming!" Ruby hopped up and down. "My bathroom is such a mess, and my grandma is visiting today!"

Benny looked at the bathroom. The sink had soap spots, the mirror was foggy, and towels were scattered everywhere. But Benny wasn't worried.

"Don't worry, Ruby! We'll make this bathroom sparkle!" Benny said cheerfully. "But first, let's wash our paws so we stay healthy while we work."

Ruby giggled as they washed their paws together. The warm water felt good, and the soap smelled like fresh flowers.

"Working with clean paws keeps germs away," Benny explained. "That's how we stay strong and healthy!"

Benny showed Ruby how to clean the mirror in big, swooping circles. *Squeak, squeak* went the cloth against the glass.

"Look, Ruby! I can see my reflection clearly now!" Benny pointed with his paw. "Clean mirrors help us see how handsome we look!"

Next, they scrubbed the sink together. Benny used his pine needle brush while Ruby wiped with a soft cloth. The sink began to shine like a white pearl.

"This is fun!" Ruby laughed. "I never knew cleaning could be so enjoyable!"

"When we help others and take care of ourselves, everything feels good!" Benny said, his tail swishing happily.

They organized the towels by color and hung them neatly. Benny showed Ruby how to fold them into perfect squares.

"My grandma will be so surprised!" Ruby clapped her paws together.

Just then, Grandma Rabbit arrived. "Oh my! This bathroom is absolutely beautiful!" she exclaimed. "Ruby, you've done wonderful work!"

"Benny helped me!" Ruby said proudly. "He taught me that staying clean and helping others makes us feel great inside!"

Benny's chest puffed up with pride. "Taking care of ourselves and helping friends is the best feeling ever!"

Grandma Rabbit gave them both warm hugs. "You two are wonderful helpers. Clean spaces and clean bodies help us stay healthy and happy!"

As Benny walked home, he felt wonderful. His fur was clean, his heart was full, and he had helped a good friend. He had learned that wellness meant taking care of his body AND helping others feel good too.

"Tomorrow, I'll help someone else!" Benny said happily, already planning his next helpful adventure.

The End.
