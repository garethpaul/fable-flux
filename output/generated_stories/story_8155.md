# Gigi the Giraffe Learns to Share

Gigi the giraffe was very tall and very thoughtful. She loved to think about things while she brushed her long, yellow teeth in the bathroom every morning. The bathroom had a big mirror, white tiles that felt cool under her hooves, and a sink that was just the right height for her long neck.

One sunny morning, Gigi was taking her time in the bathroom. She was brushing her teeth very slowly, looking at herself in the shiny mirror, and humming a happy tune. "La la la," she sang softly, making bubbles with her toothpaste.

*Knock knock knock!*

"Gigi, are you almost done?" called her little brother Benny the bunny from outside the door. "I really need to brush my teeth too!"

"Just a few more minutes!" Gigi called back, continuing to brush. She was enjoying her quiet thinking time.

*Knock knock knock!* This time it was louder.

"Gigi, please! I need to get ready for school!" Benny's voice sounded worried.

Gigi looked at herself in the mirror. She had been in the bathroom for a very long time. Her teeth were already clean and sparkly, but she was having so much fun looking at her spots and practicing her smile.

*Knock knock knock knock!*

Now it was Mama Bear's voice. "Children, what's happening out there?"

"Gigi won't come out of the bathroom!" Benny said. "I'm going to be late for school!"

Gigi's ears drooped. She could hear Benny hopping from foot to foot outside the door. *Thump thump thump.* He sounded upset.

"Oh no," Gigi said to herself, looking at her reflection. "I've been thinking so much about myself that I forgot about Benny!"

Gigi remembered what Mama Bear had taught them: "Always think about how others feel too."

She quickly rinsed her mouth with cool water, *swish swish*, and put her toothbrush back in its special spot. Then she opened the door with her long neck.

"I'm so sorry, Benny!" Gigi said, bending down to look at her little brother. "I was taking too long. The bathroom is all yours!"

Benny's ears perked up. "Thank you, Gigi!" He hopped quickly into the bathroom.

But then Gigi had an even better idea. "Wait, Benny! Would you like me to help you reach the sink? I could lift you up with my long neck!"

Benny's eyes lit up like little stars. "Yes, please! That would be so helpful!"

Gigi carefully lifted Benny up so he could reach the sink easily. She held very still while he brushed his tiny teeth. *Brush brush brush.*

"This is much easier!" Benny giggled. "Thank you for helping me, Gigi!"

"You're welcome!" Gigi said. "I'm sorry I didn't think about you before. Sharing the bathroom is important."

Mama Bear peeked in and smiled. "Look at you two working together! Gigi, that was very thoughtful of you to help Benny and to apologize."

From that day on, Gigi and Benny took turns in the bathroom. Sometimes Gigi would help Benny reach high places, and sometimes Benny would help Gigi by handing her things that had fallen on the floor.

"Sharing and helping feels much better than keeping everything to myself," Gigi thought as she watched Benny hop happily to school on time.

And every morning when she looked in the mirror, Gigi remembered to think not just about herself, but about others too.

The End.
