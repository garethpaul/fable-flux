# Koko the Koala's Wellness Garden

Koko the koala loved spending time in Grandpa's old garden shed. The wooden walls smelled like fresh pine, and sunlight danced through the dusty windows. Today, Koko had a special plan.

"I want to make this shed a wellness place," Koko said softly to himself. "A place where everyone can feel calm and happy."

Just then, Koko heard a *tap-tap-tap* on the door. Three little field mice peeked inside.

"Hello, Koko!" squeaked Mia, the smallest mouse. "We heard you talking. What's a wellness place?"

Koko smiled his gentle smile. "A wellness place is somewhere we take care of our bodies and feelings. We can stretch, breathe deeply, and share kind words."

"That sounds wonderful!" said Max, the middle mouse. "But we use this shed to store our winter nuts. Where would we put them?"

Koko took a deep breath, just like he did when he needed to think clearly. "Let's talk about this peacefully. What do you need, and what do I need?"

The mice gathered in a circle with Koko on the soft hay bales. The shed felt cozy and warm.

"We need a safe, dry place for our nuts," explained Molly, the biggest mouse.

"And I need space for wellness activities," said Koko calmly. "Maybe we can work together?"

Mia's whiskers twitched with excitement. "Could we share the shed?"

"That's a great idea!" Koko clapped his fuzzy paws. "Let's make a plan together."

They spent the morning organizing. Koko showed the mice how to stack their nut boxes neatly along one wall. The mice helped Koko clear a soft area with old blankets for stretching.

"Now we need wellness activities we can all do," said Koko. "What makes you feel good?"

"I love gentle stretches," squeaked Mia, reaching her tiny paws up high.

"Deep breathing makes me feel calm," said Max, taking a slow breath that made his little belly rise and fall.

"And I like saying kind things to my friends," added Molly with a warm smile.

Koko nodded happily. "Perfect! Let's try them all together."

They started with stretches. Koko showed them how to reach like tall eucalyptus trees, then curl up small like sleeping koalas. The mice giggled as they copied his movements.

Next, they practiced deep breathing. "Smell the sweet flowers," Koko said softly. "Now breathe out slowly, like a gentle breeze."

The shed filled with peaceful quiet as they breathed together.

Finally, they shared kind words. "You're a wonderful friend, Koko," said the mice. "You're creative problem-solvers," Koko replied warmly.

As the sun set, painting the shed walls golden orange, they sat together feeling happy and relaxed.

"We did it!" cheered Mia. "We made a plan, talked peacefully, and created something wonderful together."

"And we all feel so much better," added Max, stretching his tiny arms.

Koko looked around their shared wellness space, filled with neat nut boxes and cozy exercise areas. "When we communicate kindly and work as a team, we can solve any problem. Taking care of ourselves and each other makes everything better."

The mice agreed, already planning tomorrow's wellness time together.

From that day on, the garden shed became the most peaceful, happy place in the whole garden, where friends gathered to take care of their wellness together.

The End.
