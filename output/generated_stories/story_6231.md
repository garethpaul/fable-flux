# Ruby Rabbit's Rooftop Clean-Up

High up on a sunny rooftop, Ruby Rabbit was having the most wonderful day. She loved visiting the rooftop garden where colorful flowers danced in the breeze and butterflies fluttered about like tiny rainbows.

"What a beautiful morning!" Ruby said, stretching her fluffy white paws toward the warm sun. But as she hopped around to explore, she noticed something that made her nose wrinkle.

"Oh my!" she gasped. The rooftop was covered with litter! Empty juice boxes, candy wrappers, and banana peels were scattered everywhere. The pretty flowers looked sad, and the butterflies had flown away.

Ruby's heart felt heavy. "This isn't fair to the flowers or the animals who live here," she said sadly. "Someone made this mess, but they didn't clean it up. That's not right!"

Ruby sat down and thought carefully. She could hop away and pretend she didn't see the mess, but that wouldn't help anyone. Or she could try to find who made the mess and be angry with them. But Ruby had a better idea.

"I know what I'll do!" she announced proudly. "I'll clean this up myself and show everyone how wonderful it feels to take care of our special places."

Ruby rolled up her sleeves and began picking up the trash. As she worked, she sang a happy cleaning song: "Pick it up, pick it up, make it clean and bright! When we care for where we live, everything feels right!"

Soon, she heard footsteps on the rooftop stairs. Three little mice appeared, looking embarrassed.

"Um, excuse me," squeaked the smallest mouse. "We saw you cleaning up here. That's... that's our mess from yesterday's picnic."

Ruby stopped cleaning and smiled kindly. "Hello there! Thank you for telling me the truth. That was very brave and honest of you."

The middle mouse shuffled her tiny feet. "We forgot to clean up after ourselves. We're really sorry."

"We didn't mean to hurt the flowers," added the biggest mouse. "We just got excited about our picnic and ran home when it started getting dark."

Ruby's ears perked up happily. "I understand! Sometimes we forget things when we're having fun. But you know what? We can fix this together!"

"Really?" asked all three mice.

"Of course!" Ruby said. "When we take care of our bodies by washing our hands and brushing our teeth, we feel good, right? Well, taking care of our environment is just like taking care of ourselves!"

The mice nodded eagerly and joined Ruby in cleaning. They picked up every piece of trash and put it in the proper bins. Then they used a small watering can to give the flowers a drink.

"Wow!" said the smallest mouse, washing her paws in a little puddle of rainwater. "The rooftop looks beautiful again!"

"And look!" pointed the middle mouse. "The butterflies are coming back!"

Ruby clapped her paws together joyfully. "When we use peaceful words to solve problems and take care of our world, everyone feels happy. You three used wonderful, honest words to apologize, and now we've made everything clean and beautiful together!"

The biggest mouse smiled. "From now on, we'll always clean up after ourselves. Taking care of things is important!"

"Just like taking care of our bodies!" added Ruby. "Clean hands, clean teeth, and clean spaces make everyone feel wonderful!"

As the sun set, Ruby and her new mouse friends sat together on the sparkling clean rooftop, watching the butterflies dance among the happy flowers.

The End.
