# Ruby Rabbit's Rainbow Garden

Ruby was a small white rabbit with floppy ears and bright pink eyes. She lived near a wooden garden shed that smelled like fresh soil and old tools. Ruby had a big dream - she wanted to grow the most beautiful garden ever!

Every morning, Ruby hopped to the shed. She pushed open the creaky door with her nose. "Today I'll plant my perfect garden!" she said to herself. The shed was full of seed packets, all showing the same white flowers.

Ruby grabbed a packet with her teeth. She read the label carefully. "White daisies," she said. "These will make my garden look just like me!"

She dug little holes in the soft dirt behind the shed. The earth felt cool under her paws. Ruby planted row after row of white daisy seeds. She watered them with a tiny watering can.

"My garden will be so pretty!" Ruby said, wiggling her nose happily.

But as days passed, Ruby noticed other animals had different ideas. A yellow butterfly named Sunny fluttered over.

"Hello, Ruby!" Sunny chirped. "I see you're planting flowers. May I suggest some yellow sunflowers? They smell like sunshine!"

Ruby's ears drooped. "No thank you. I only want white flowers like me."

Next, a red cardinal named Rosie landed on the shed roof. "Ruby, red roses would be lovely here! They smell so sweet!"

"No," Ruby said firmly. "Only white flowers belong in my garden."

A purple caterpillar named Violet crawled up. "What about some purple pansies? They're my favorite!"

Ruby shook her head. "White flowers only, please."

Days went by. Ruby's white daisies grew, but something felt wrong. Her garden looked... empty. It was pretty, but it seemed lonely somehow.

One morning, Ruby sat in the shed feeling sad. The wooden walls felt cold around her. Through the window, she could see other gardens nearby. They sparkled with many colors - red, yellow, purple, orange, and blue.

"Those gardens look so happy," Ruby whispered. "Mine looks... boring."

Just then, Sunny, Rosie, and Violet appeared at the shed door.

"Ruby," said Sunny gently, "we want to help make your garden special."

"But we're all different colors," added Rosie. "Would that be okay?"

Ruby looked at her new friends. Sunny was bright yellow, Rosie was deep red, and Violet was soft purple. They were all different from her white fur, but they were so kind.

"I... I thought my garden should look like me," Ruby said quietly. "But maybe..."

"Different can be beautiful too," said Violet softly.

Ruby's pink eyes lit up. "You're right! Let's work together!"

The friends made a plan. Ruby would keep her white daisies in the center. Around them, they would plant yellow sunflowers, red roses, and purple pansies.

They worked all day. Ruby dug holes while Sunny found the sunniest spots. Rosie picked the best soil, and Violet arranged the seeds in pretty patterns.

When they finished, Ruby stepped back. Her garden was a rainbow! White daisies danced with yellow sunflowers. Red roses hugged purple pansies. Every color looked beautiful together.

"It's perfect!" Ruby gasped. The garden smelled like a mix of all the flowers - sweet, fresh, and wonderful.

Sunny landed on Ruby's ear. "See? Different friends make everything more beautiful!"

"And more fun too!" added Rosie.

Ruby hugged her friends. "Thank you for teaching me that different is wonderful. My garden is so much better with all of us together!"

From that day on, Ruby's rainbow garden was the most beautiful in the whole neighborhood. Animals came from far away to see how lovely different colors could look together.

Ruby learned that the best things happen when everyone works together, no matter how different they look.

The End.
