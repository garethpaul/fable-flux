# Nutkin's Big Guest Room Adventure

Nutkin the squirrel twitched his fluffy gray tail as he peeked into Grandma Oak's cozy guest room. The morning sun streamed through the lace curtains, making everything glow golden and warm. Today, three of his woodland friends were coming for a sleepover, and Nutkin wanted everything to be perfect!

"Let me see," Nutkin said to himself, scratching behind his ear. "Rabbit loves soft things, Hedgehog needs dark corners, and Blue Jay likes high places. But there's only one comfy bed!"

Nutkin's whiskers twitched as he thought hard. This was a puzzle that needed a good strategy!

Soon, *thump thump thump* came Rabbit's gentle hops up the tree trunk stairs. "Hi, Nutkin!" she said, her pink nose wiggling. "Oh my, what a lovely soft bed! May I rest here?"

Before Nutkin could answer, *shuffle shuffle* came Hedgehog, rolling into a spiky ball. "Hello, friends! I'm so sleepy. That bed looks perfectly dark underneath for a hedgehog nap!"

Then *whoosh!* Blue Jay swooped through the open window, landing gracefully on the windowsill. His bright blue feathers sparkled in the sunlight. "Greetings! What a wonderful high perch this bed would make for my afternoon rest!"

All three friends looked at the one cozy bed with its puffy pillows and warm blanket. Nutkin saw their faces fall as they realized they all wanted the same spot.

"Oh dear," whispered Rabbit, her ears drooping.

"I suppose I could sleep on the hard floor," sighed Hedgehog.

"Maybe I should fly home," said Blue Jay sadly.

But Nutkin's bright black eyes sparkled with an idea. "Wait! I have a strategy that could help everyone!" He clapped his tiny paws together. "What if we take turns and share?"

"Share?" asked Rabbit, tilting her head.

"Yes!" Nutkin's tail swished excitedly. "Rabbit, you love soft things, so you get the bed first for your morning nap. The pillows will smell like sweet clover!"

Rabbit's ears perked up. "That sounds wonderful!"

"Hedgehog," Nutkin continued, "you like dark, cozy spaces. When Rabbit is done, you can burrow under the blanket for your afternoon nap. It'll be perfectly shadowy!"

Hedgehog uncurled with delight. "Oh, that's even better than I imagined!"

"And Blue Jay," Nutkin said with a grin, "you can have the bed for evening rest. By then, you'll have the highest, softest perch with the best view of the sunset through the window!"

Blue Jay flapped his wings happily. "What a clever plan!"

But then Rabbit asked, "What about you, Nutkin? Where will you sleep?"

Nutkin's heart felt warm and fuzzy. "I'll share with whoever wants company! Sometimes the best part of having friends over is being together."

"You're so generous!" said Hedgehog.

"And smart!" added Blue Jay.

"This is much better than fighting over the bed," said Rabbit, giving Nutkin a gentle nuzzle.

All afternoon, the friends took their turns just as planned. When evening came, they discovered something magical - the bed was big enough for everyone to snuggle together! They spent the night telling stories, sharing acorns, and giggling under the stars.

As they drifted off to sleep, Nutkin smiled. Sometimes the best strategy isn't just solving a problem - it's bringing friends closer together.

The End.
