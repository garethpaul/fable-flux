# Nutkin's Bathroom Helper Adventure

Nutkin the squirrel loved to help his family. He had soft brown fur and a big, fluffy tail that twitched when he was excited. Today, Mama Squirrel needed his help in the bathroom.

"Nutkin," called Mama from the shiny white bathroom. "I need to clean up after Baby Squirrel's bath time. Can you help me?"

Nutkin's tail twitched with excitement. "Yes, Mama! I want to help!"

When Nutkin scampered into the bathroom, he saw water puddles on the blue tile floor. Wet towels were scattered everywhere. Baby Squirrel's rubber duck floated in the bathtub, and soap bubbles sparkled like tiny rainbows.

"Oh my!" said Nutkin, looking around with his bright black eyes.

Mama Squirrel smiled. "It's quite a mess, isn't it? But if we follow my directions step by step, we can clean it up together. Are you ready to listen carefully?"

Nutkin nodded eagerly. "I'm ready, Mama!"

"First," said Mama, "please pick up all the wet towels and put them in the laundry basket."

Nutkin looked at the colorful towels - a yellow one by the sink, a green one near the toilet, and a pink one by the bathtub. He carefully picked up each soft, damp towel. They felt heavy and smelled like lavender soap.

"Good job following directions!" praised Mama. "Next, please drain the water from the bathtub and put the toys in the toy basket."

Nutkin pulled the rubber stopper with a satisfying *pop*! He watched the soapy water swirl down the drain with a gurgling sound. Then he gathered Baby Squirrel's bath toys - the yellow duck, two boats, and a floating ball.

"I'm doing it, Mama!" Nutkin chirped happily.

"Wonderful! Now comes the tricky part," said Mama. "Please wipe up the water puddles with this dry towel. Start from the far corner and work your way to the door."

Nutkin took the fluffy white towel. He thought for a moment. Which corner should he start with? He remembered Mama's words and decided to start at the corner farthest from the door, just like she said.

As he wiped, the towel soaked up the cool water. His little paws worked carefully, following Mama's directions exactly. Soon, the floor was dry and safe to walk on.

"Last step," said Mama. "Please hang up the clean towels on the towel rack."

Nutkin stretched up high to reach the silver towel rack. It was a little challenging, but he didn't give up. He hung each towel neatly, just as Mama had shown him before.

When they finished, the bathroom sparkled! The mirror gleamed, the floor was dry, and everything was in its proper place.

"Nutkin, you were such a wonderful helper!" said Mama, giving him a warm hug. "You listened to each direction and followed them perfectly. Because you helped me, we finished much faster than if I had done it alone."

Nutkin's chest puffed up with pride. "I liked following your directions, Mama. It made the job easier!"

"That's right," said Mama. "When we follow directions carefully and help others, we can accomplish amazing things together."

From that day on, Nutkin always remembered to listen carefully to directions. He discovered that following instructions helped him help others better, and it made every task more fun and successful.

The End.
