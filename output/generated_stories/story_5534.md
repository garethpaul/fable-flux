# Miko's Morning Adventure

The warm kitchen smelled like fresh bananas and sunshine. Little Miko the monkey sat at the wooden table, swinging his furry legs back and forth. His tummy rumbled loudly.

"Grumble, grumble, GRUMBLE!" went his belly.

"Oh my!" said Mama Monkey, stirring something yummy in a big pot. "Someone sounds very hungry this morning."

Miko looked around the cozy kitchen. Steam danced up from Mama's cooking, making the windows all foggy. The yellow walls felt warm and happy, just like a big hug.

"Mama, I'm SO hungry I could eat a whole tree!" Miko said, bouncing in his chair.

But Mama shook her head gently. "Not yet, little one. First we need to do our morning routine. Remember what comes before breakfast?"

Miko's shoulders drooped. He had forgotten again! Every morning was the same problem - he wanted to eat RIGHT NOW, but Mama always said they had to do other things first.

"But Mama," Miko whined, "my tummy is making monster noises!"

"I know, sweetie," Mama said with a warm smile. "But our routine helps us start each day the right way. Can you remember what we do first?"

Miko thought hard, tapping his little finger on his chin. "Um... wash our hands?"

"That's right! What a smart monkey you are!"

Miko scampered to the sink. The warm, soapy water felt bubbly and nice on his hands. He sang his washing song: "Scrub, scrub, scrub between my fingers, wash away the sleepy germs!"

"What comes next?" Mama asked.

"Exercise time!" Miko remembered, jumping up and down.

"But it's raining outside," Miko said sadly, looking at the wet window. "We can't go to the jungle gym."

Mama's eyes twinkled. "Who says we need to go outside? Let's be creative!"

She moved the kitchen chairs to make space. "Welcome to our indoor jungle!" she announced.

Miko's eyes grew wide with excitement. Mama showed him how to do monkey stretches, reaching high like tree branches and bending low like picking fruit. They marched in place like elephants - STOMP, STOMP, STOMP! They flapped their arms like colorful parrots - FLAP, FLAP, FLAP!

"This is the BEST indoor jungle ever!" Miko giggled, doing jumping jacks.

After their fun exercise, they sat quietly for two minutes, breathing deeply like sleeping sloths. Miko felt calm and ready.

"Now what?" Mama asked.

"Set the table!" Miko said proudly, remembering the next part of their routine.

He carefully placed two bright blue bowls on the table and folded the napkins into neat triangles. The kitchen looked perfect and ready.

"And NOW..." Mama said with a big smile, "it's time for breakfast!"

She served warm oatmeal with sliced bananas and a drizzle of honey. It smelled like heaven and tasted even better.

"Mmm!" Miko said, taking a big spoonful. "This tastes extra delicious!"

"Do you know why?" Mama asked.

Miko thought while he chewed. "Because we did our whole routine first?"

"Exactly! When we follow our routine, everything feels better. We're clean, our bodies are awake and strong, and our space is ready. It makes breakfast taste sweeter and our whole day brighter."

Miko nodded, understanding now. "And we can be creative with our routine too, like making an indoor jungle!"

"That's my clever little monkey," Mama said, giving him a warm hug.

From that day on, Miko loved their morning routine. Rain or shine, he knew they could always find creative ways to start their day just right.

The End.
