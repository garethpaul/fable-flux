# Splash the Dolphin's Big Dream

In a cozy bedroom with soft blue walls and fluffy white clouds painted on the ceiling, lived a very special friend named Splash. Splash was a playful dolphin who lived in a magical snow globe on Emma's nightstand. Every night, when Emma fell asleep, Splash would come to life and explore the wonderful bedroom.

One evening, Splash swam out of his snow globe with a big splash of glittery water. "Oh my!" he said, looking around the room. "I have such a big problem. Tomorrow is Show and Tell at the ocean school, and I don't know what to share!"

Splash flopped sadly on the soft, pink carpet. He had seen all the amazing things in Emma's room - her colorful books, her box of crayons, her building blocks, and her stuffed animals. "All the other dolphins will bring shiny shells or pretty seaweed," he sighed. "But I'm stuck in this bedroom. What can I possibly share?"

As Splash looked around feeling worried, his eyes landed on Emma's art supplies scattered on her little wooden desk. There were bright markers that smelled like fruit, sparkly stickers that caught the moonlight, and sheets of paper as white as ocean foam.

"Wait a minute!" Splash said, his gray skin tingling with excitement. "Maybe I don't need to find something from the ocean. Maybe I can CREATE something special!"

Splash began to think hard, his round black eyes twinkling. He remembered all the wonderful stories Emma's mommy read from the books on the shelf. He thought about the beautiful pictures Emma drew with her crayons. He remembered the tall towers Emma built with her colorful blocks.

"I know!" Splash squeaked happily. "I can make my own treasure to share!"

Using his flippers carefully, Splash picked up a blue marker. On a big piece of paper, he began to draw. He drew the cozy bedroom with its soft carpet and twinkling night light. He drew Emma sleeping peacefully in her bed with her teddy bear. He drew himself swimming happily in his snow globe home.

"This shows where I live!" Splash said proudly. "No other dolphin has a home like this!"

But Splash wasn't finished yet. He picked up silver and gold stickers and placed them all around his drawing like stars. Then he wrote in big, wobbly letters: "My Special Home."

As the first rays of sunshine peeked through the bedroom window, Splash carefully rolled up his artwork. "I created something that shows my own special world," he said with a big dolphin smile. "I learned that creativity means using your imagination to make something new and wonderful!"

Splash swam back into his snow globe just as Emma began to wake up. Through the glass, he winked at her and held up his colorful creation.

That day at ocean school, Splash proudly shared his artwork. All the other dolphins gathered around, amazed by his creative drawing. "Wow, Splash!" said his friend Bubbles. "You made something so special and unique. We never thought about creating our own treasures!"

From that day on, Splash learned that creativity was the most wonderful treasure of all. He discovered that when you use your imagination and the tools around you, you can create amazing things that are uniquely yours.

The End.
