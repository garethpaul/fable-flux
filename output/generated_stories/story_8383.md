# Benny Bear's Special Morning

High up in Grandma's house, in a cozy quiet attic, lived a friendly little bear cub named Benny. The attic was Benny's favorite place, with soft sunlight streaming through the round window and dust motes dancing like tiny fairies in the golden beams.

One morning, Benny woke up feeling a bit grumpy. His fur was messy, his tummy felt empty, and his little bear body felt stiff from sleeping.

"Grrrr," Benny grumbled, rubbing his sleepy eyes. "I don't feel good today."

But then Benny remembered what Mama Bear had taught him about taking care of himself. "When you don't feel your best, little one," she had said, "you need to show yourself some love."

Benny sat up in his soft bed of old quilts and took a deep breath. The attic smelled like lavender and old books – such a peaceful smell that made him feel a tiny bit better already.

"First things first," Benny said to himself, just like Mama taught him. "I need to stretch my sleepy body!"

Benny stood up on his hind legs and reached his little paws way up high toward the wooden ceiling beams. "Stretch, stretch, stretch!" he giggled, feeling his muscles wake up. Then he bent down to touch his toes, wobbling a little but having fun.

Next, Benny did his favorite morning dance. He wiggled his bottom, shook his fluffy ears, and spun in circles until he felt dizzy and happy. The old floorboards creaked cheerfully under his dancing paws.

"Exercise makes my body feel strong and happy!" Benny declared, feeling much more awake now.

After his dance, Benny walked to his special mirror – an old looking glass leaning against the attic wall. He looked at his reflection and smiled.

"Good morning, Benny," he said kindly to himself. "You're a good bear, and you deserve to feel happy today."

Benny gently brushed his brown fur with his favorite wooden brush until it was soft and neat. He washed his face with cool water from his little basin, patting it dry with a fluffy towel that smelled like sunshine.

Then Benny's tummy rumbled loudly. "GRROWWWL!"

"Oh my!" Benny laughed. "My tummy is asking for breakfast!"

Benny prepared his favorite healthy breakfast: sweet honey on warm bread, fresh berries that burst with juice in his mouth, and cool milk that made his whiskers tingle. He ate slowly, enjoying every delicious bite.

As Benny finished eating, he heard a tiny "chirp, chirp" from outside the attic window. A little bird sat on the windowsill, looking cold and tired.

"Oh, you poor little friend," Benny said gently. "You look like you need some care too!"

Benny shared some of his berries with the bird and gave it fresh water in a tiny dish. The bird chirped happily and fluffed its feathers.

"When we take good care of ourselves," Benny realized, "we have more love to share with others!"

The bird sang a beautiful thank-you song before flying away into the bright blue sky.

Benny felt wonderful now – his body was strong from exercise, his fur was clean and soft, his tummy was full of good food, and his heart was warm from helping his new friend.

"Taking care of myself isn't selfish," Benny said happily, settling into his favorite sunny spot by the window. "It helps me be the best bear I can be for everyone I love!"

From that day on, Benny started every morning with stretches, kind words to himself, and a healthy breakfast. And he always had plenty of love left over to share with others.

The End.
