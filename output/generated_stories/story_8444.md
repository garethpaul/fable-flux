# Robo's Garden of Many Colors

Robo the robot dog wagged his silver tail as he rolled onto the sunny balcony garden. His LED eyes sparkled blue with excitement. Today was special - his friend Maya was coming to visit!

"Beep beep! Good morning, plants!" Robo said cheerfully. He loved taking care of the garden with his built-in watering system. The tomatoes were red and round, the lettuce was crisp and green, and the sunflowers stood tall and bright yellow.

*Ding dong!* The doorbell chimed.

"Maya's here!" Robo's tail wagged faster as he rolled to greet his friend.

Maya bounced onto the balcony carrying a small pot. "Hi Robo! I brought something special from my grandmother's garden in Mexico!"

Robo tilted his metal head curiously. "What is it? It smells wonderful!"

"These are marigolds - we call them 'cempasúchil' in Spanish," Maya explained, setting down the bright orange flowers. "My abuela says they bring good luck and happiness!"

Robo's sensors detected the sweet, spicy scent. "They're beautiful! But they look different from our other flowers."

Maya giggled. "That's what makes them special! In my family, we use these flowers for celebrations. They're very important to us."

As they worked together to plant the marigolds, more friends arrived. Jin brought purple orchids from his family's Korean garden. "These represent strength and beauty," he said proudly.

Fatima carried a small olive tree cutting. "My mama says olive trees mean peace. They grow in many places, but ours came from Morocco."

Soon, Aisha arrived with colorful herbs. "These are from my Ethiopian grandmother's recipe garden. They smell amazing when you cook with them!"

Robo looked around at all the different plants. His circuits buzzed with confusion. "But... our garden was perfect before. Now everything looks so different!"

Maya knelt down next to Robo. "Different doesn't mean bad, Robo. Look how pretty all the colors are together!"

Jin nodded. "My orchids look even more beautiful next to Maya's marigolds!"

Fatima pointed to where her olive tree sat near the tomatoes. "See how the silver-green leaves make the red tomatoes look brighter?"

Aisha sprinkled some of her herbs near the lettuce. "And my herbs will help keep bugs away from all our vegetables!"

Robo's LED eyes slowly changed from blue to warm golden yellow. "Oh! I understand now. Each plant brings something special to our garden!"

"Exactly!" Maya clapped her hands. "Just like how each of us brings something special when we're together!"

The friends spent the afternoon learning about each other's plants. Robo practiced saying "cempasúchil" and learned that orchids need less water than sunflowers. He discovered that olive trees like sunny spots and that Ethiopian herbs taste wonderful in salad.

As the sun began to set, Robo looked at their beautiful, colorful garden. The orange marigolds danced next to yellow sunflowers. Purple orchids stood proudly beside red tomatoes. The silver-green olive tree swayed gently near the crisp lettuce and aromatic herbs.

"This is the most beautiful garden ever!" Robo announced, his tail wagging with joy. "Thank you for teaching me that different can be wonderful!"

Maya hugged Robo's smooth metal side. "That's what makes friendship special - we all bring different gifts to share!"

From that day on, Robo loved tending to their multicultural garden, learning new words in different languages, and celebrating how each plant - and each friend - made their world more colorful and beautiful.

The End.
