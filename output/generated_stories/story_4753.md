# Splash the Dolphin's Big Dream

In a cozy study corner by the window, little Emma was reading her favorite book about ocean animals. The colorful pictures showed whales, seahorses, and dolphins swimming in the deep blue sea. As she turned the page, something magical happened!

"Splash!" A friendly gray dolphin popped right out of the book!

"Hello there!" chirped the dolphin, spinning in a happy circle above Emma's reading chair. "I'm Splash! I live in the ocean, but I love visiting study corners to learn new things!"

Emma giggled and clapped her hands. "Wow! A real dolphin! What are you doing here, Splash?"

Splash's eyes sparkled with excitement. "I have a very big dream, Emma! I want to swim all the way around the world to visit every ocean and meet dolphins everywhere. But... it seems so far and so hard!"

The little dolphin's fins drooped a bit. "Sometimes I feel too small to do something so big."

Emma stood up and gently patted Splash's smooth, wet nose. "My mommy says big dreams start with small steps! What could be your first small step?"

Splash thought hard, tapping his fin against his chin. "Hmm... maybe I could start by swimming to the next coral reef? That's just one day away from my home!"

"That's perfect!" Emma cheered. "And what would help you get stronger for your big journey?"

"Oh! I could practice my swimming every day!" Splash began doing little flips in the air. "I could swim fast, swim slow, and swim in zigzags! Movement makes me stronger!"

Emma nodded wisely. "And what if you get tired or scared?"

Splash puffed out his chest. "Then I'll remember that dolphins are brave! If I feel scared, I can rest, take deep breaths, and try again. My dolphin friends always say, 'Never give up on your dreams!'"

"You're so smart, Splash!" Emma said. "And think about all the friends you'll make around the world!"

Splash's eyes grew wide with wonder. "Yes! I'll meet dolphins who speak different languages and know different games! I'll learn about warm tropical waters and cold arctic seas! The world is so big and full of amazing things!"

The playful dolphin started swimming in excited circles around the study corner. "Emma, you helped me see that my big dream isn't too big at all! I just need to break it into smaller pieces, like a puzzle!"

Emma pulled out her crayons and paper. "Let's make you a map! We can draw all the places you want to visit!"

Together, they drew a colorful map with blue oceans, green islands, and happy dolphins everywhere. Splash traced the path with his fin.

"First, the coral reef," Splash said proudly. "Then the next ocean, and the next! Each swim will make me stronger and braver!"

As the sun began to set outside the study corner window, Splash gave Emma a gentle, wet hug.

"Thank you for helping me believe in my dream, Emma! Remember, you can achieve anything if you set goals, practice every day, and never give up!"

With a happy "Splash!" the dolphin dove back into his book, already planning his first adventure to the coral reef.

Emma smiled and hugged her book tight. She had learned that dreams, no matter how big, could come true with small steps, practice, and a brave heart.

The End.
