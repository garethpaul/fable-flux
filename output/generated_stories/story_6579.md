# Melody's Musical Ocean Cleanup

Melody the dolphin loved swimming through the clear blue practice pool in the underwater musical room. The walls sparkled with colorful coral decorations, and gentle bubbles floated up like musical notes. But today, something was very wrong.

"Oh no!" squeaked Melody, her gray skin glistening as she swam in worried circles. The practice room was full of yucky plastic bottles and crinkly wrappers floating everywhere. The water didn't smell fresh and clean anymore.

Melody's friend Harmony, a wise old sea turtle, poked her green head through the doorway. "What's wrong, little one?" she asked gently.

"Look at all this trash!" Melody clicked sadly. "How can we practice our songs when our beautiful room is so messy? The water tastes icky, and I can't swim properly!"

Harmony's kind eyes twinkled. "You know, Melody, this is a perfect chance to help our ocean home. Would you like to learn how?"

Melody's tail flipped with excitement. "Yes, yes! But I'm just one little dolphin. How can I clean all this up?"

"Let's start small," Harmony suggested. "See that plastic bottle? Can you gently push it toward me with your nose?"

Melody swam over and carefully nudged the bottle. It felt rough and strange against her smooth skin. "Like this?" she asked, moving it slowly.

"Perfect! Now, let's make it fun. Pretend you're dancing to music while you clean!"

Melody began to spin and twirl gracefully through the water, collecting trash as she moved. Her sleek body glided like she was performing a beautiful underwater ballet. "This is actually fun!" she giggled, doing a happy flip.

Soon, other sea friends heard the splashing and came to help. Shelly the seahorse grabbed tiny pieces with her curly tail. Finn the fish formed a line to pass bigger items along. Even Ollie the octopus used all eight arms to gather trash quickly.

"Look how much we can do when we work together!" Melody sang happily, her voice making beautiful sounds through the water.

As they cleaned, Harmony taught them an important song:
"Keep our ocean clean and bright,
Every day and every night.
Trash belongs in proper places,
Not floating around our water spaces!"

After an hour of teamwork, the practice room sparkled again. The water was crystal clear, and sweet ocean scents filled the space. Melody took a deep, refreshing breath through her blowhole.

"Now we can practice our music in a healthy, clean room!" Melody announced, doing three joyful spins. "And I learned that even though I'm small, I can make a big difference!"

Harmony smiled proudly. "And when we all help together, we can keep our ocean home beautiful for everyone."

Melody began practicing her scales, her voice ringing pure and clear through the clean water. Each note sounded more beautiful than ever before. She had learned that taking care of her environment wasn't just helpful—it made everything better for everyone.

From that day on, Melody made sure to clean up any trash she found, and she taught other young sea creatures to do the same. The musical practice room stayed sparkling clean, and all the ocean friends could enjoy making beautiful music together in their healthy, happy home.

The End.
