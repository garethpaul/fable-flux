# Sunny Sings and Crafts a Cozy Nest

Sunny was a small singing bird with bright yellow feathers and a sweet, twinkly voice. One day, Sunny fluttered into the guest room. The room was quiet and soft. The curtains were light and swishy. The rug felt fluffy under Sunny’s little feet. A warm sunbeam made the dust look like tiny stars.

“Tweet-a-dee! What a nice room,” sang Sunny. “But where can I rest my wings?”

Sunny looked around. There was a big bed, a little chair, and a basket of colorful yarn on the table. There were also paper shapes, safe blunt scissors, glue sticks, buttons, and fuzzy pom-poms. In the corner sat a small plate with apple slices, carrot sticks, and a cup of cool water.

Sunny tried to sit on a pillow, but the pillow was too bouncy. “Oh no,” chirped Sunny, “I need a cozy place, just for me.” Sunny felt a little worried. The guest room was nice, but there was no bird nest.

As Sunny tapped the table, a friend walked in. It was Momo, a gentle mouse with round ears and kind eyes. Momo nibbled a carrot stick and waved. “Hi, Sunny! You look thoughtful.”

“I want a cozy nest,” said Sunny softly. “But I don’t have one here.”

Momo’s whiskers twitched. “Let’s make one! We can craft a nest right in this guest room.”

Sunny’s eyes sparkled. “Craft a nest? With these things?”

“Yes,” said Momo. “Crafting is fun. We can use soft yarn, paper rings, and a box for a base. We will be safe and careful.”

Sunny took a sip of water. The water felt cool and fresh. “Tweet! I feel better already,” Sunny sang. “Let’s make a plan.”

Step by step, they began. They set a small cardboard box on the rug. It felt sturdy. Momo said, “This is the base.” Sunny nodded and sang, “Base, base, in the right place!”

They lined the box with paper strips. The paper made a whispery sound—swish, swish. Sunny pressed the paper with gentle wings. “It sounds like the wind,” Sunny giggled.

They added loops of yarn—blue, green, and gold. The yarn was soft and tickly on Sunny’s feathers. “It feels like a hug,” Sunny said.

“Snip, snip,” went the safe scissors as Momo trimmed tape. “Sticky, sticky,” went the tape as they pressed the edges down. Sunny carefully picked up shiny buttons. “Click, click,” they went as they decorated the edge. The nest began to look warm and happy.

Sunny sang a little song as they worked:
“Craft and sing, craft and sing,
Make a nest for resting wing.
Kind and careful, take our time,
Friendship makes our work all shine.”

After a while, Sunny felt a tiny rumble in the tummy. “I am hungry,” Sunny said.

Momo smiled. “Let’s take a snack break. Crafting feels better with good food.” They shared the apple slices and carrot sticks. Crunch, crunch. The apple was sweet and juicy. The carrot was crisp and bright. Sunny drank more water. “Ahh,” Sunny sighed. “Healthy food gives me energy to craft.”

Back to work they went. Momo found soft cotton balls. “Clouds!” Sunny chirped. They tucked the cotton around the inside of the nest. It felt pillowy and light. Sunny wrapped a shiny ribbon around the outside. It looked like a rainbow hug.

Just then, Sunny tried the nest. Plop! The nest wobbled. “Oh no,” said Sunny. “The nest tips to one side.”

Momo nodded. “That is our problem. We can fix it.” Momo placed small pebbles in a tiny cloth bag and set it at the bottom of the box. “This will make it steady,” said Momo.

Sunny tested again. Plop! The nest stayed still. “It works!” Sunny sang. “We solved our problem with our plan and our friendship.”

They weren’t done yet. Sunny wanted a little roof. “In case a breeze comes,” Sunny said. Momo folded thick paper like a gentle tent and taped it to the box. The roof made a cozy shade. Sunny’s feathers fluffed with joy.

Sunny nestled inside. It was warm, soft, and calm. The room smelled like paper and apples. Sunlight made the ribbon shine. Sunny took a deep breath. “Thank you, Momo,” Sunny said. “You are a good friend.”

Momo grinned. “You are a good crafter, Sunny. You had a problem. You made a plan. You worked safely. You took a healthy snack break. And you asked for help.”

Sunny sang a happy song:
“Thank you, friend, for helping me.
Crafting shows what we can be.
Plan and try, fix and test,
Healthy snacks make crafting best.”

Sunny closed sleepy eyes. “I learned that crafting can solve problems. Healthy food helps my body. And friends make work feel easy and fun.”

Momo tucked a tiny pom-pom by Sunny’s wing. “Sweet dreams, Sunny.”

Sunny smiled inside the new nest. “Tweet-a-dee. I feel safe, loved, and proud.”

The guest room felt extra cozy now. The little nest was a symbol of care, creativity, and teamwork.

Lesson: When you have a problem, you can make a plan, craft with care, eat healthy snacks for energy, and ask friends to help. Together, you can make something wonderful.

The End.
