# Rocco's Sorry Song

In a cozy bedroom with soft blue walls, a little musical raccoon named Rocco lived in a special toy box. Rocco had fuzzy gray fur with black stripes around his tail. He wore a tiny red bow tie and carried a small silver bell that made the sweetest *ting-a-ling* sound.

Every morning, Rocco would wake up and play gentle music for his best friend, Teddy Bear. But today was different. Today, Rocco felt grumpy.

"I don't want to share my bell today," Rocco said, holding it tight against his fluffy chest. The morning sunlight streamed through the window, making dust sparkles dance in the air.

Teddy Bear waddled over with his soft brown paws. "Can we make music together, Rocco? Please?"

"No!" Rocco snapped. "This is MY bell!" He turned his back to Teddy Bear and curled up in the corner of the toy box.

Teddy Bear's button eyes looked sad. His fuzzy ears drooped down. "Oh," he whispered quietly. Then he walked away to sit by the window.

Rocco played his bell alone. *Ting-a-ling, ting-a-ling.* But the music didn't sound happy anymore. It sounded lonely and hollow. The cozy bedroom felt cold and empty.

All day long, Rocco watched Teddy Bear sitting by himself. Teddy Bear didn't play with the other toys. He just stared out the window at the fluffy white clouds.

As the afternoon sun made golden patterns on the soft carpet, Rocco's heart began to feel heavy. He missed his friend's warm laugh. He missed how Teddy Bear would clap his paws when Rocco played music.

"Maybe I was mean," Rocco whispered to himself. His little raccoon ears twitched with worry.

When evening came, Rocco took a deep breath. He picked up his precious silver bell and walked slowly across the bedroom. His tiny paws made soft *pat-pat-pat* sounds on the warm wooden floor.

"Teddy Bear?" Rocco said in a small voice. "I'm sorry I was mean to you. I was wrong to say you couldn't play with my bell."

Teddy Bear turned around. His button eyes were shiny with tears. "Really?"

"Really," said Rocco. He held out his bell. "Would you like to make music with me? We can take turns, or we can play together. I should have shared from the beginning."

Teddy Bear's whole face lit up like sunshine. "I forgive you, Rocco! I missed making music with my best friend."

Rocco felt warm and happy inside. "I missed you too. I learned that sharing makes everything more fun."

They sat together on the soft bedroom rug. Rocco rang his bell *ting-a-ling*, and Teddy Bear clapped his paws *clap-clap-clap*. Together, they made the most beautiful music the cozy bedroom had ever heard.

"Thank you for forgiving me," Rocco said, giving Teddy Bear a big fluffy hug.

"Thank you for saying sorry," Teddy Bear replied. "That's what best friends do."

As the stars twinkled outside the bedroom window, Rocco and Teddy Bear played their friendship song. The music filled the room with love and joy. Rocco learned that saying sorry and asking for forgiveness makes friendships even stronger.

From that day on, Rocco always remembered to share and be kind to his friends.

The End.
