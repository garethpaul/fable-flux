# Ollie's Rooftop Garden Adventure

Ollie the otter loved to splash and play, but today he had climbed all the way up to the tall building's rooftop. The sun felt warm on his soft, brown fur as he looked around with his bright, curious eyes.

"Wow!" Ollie whispered. "Look at all these empty flower pots!"

The rooftop was covered with big clay pots, but they were all empty and dusty. Ollie's whiskers twitched with excitement. He had a wonderful idea!

"I want to make a beautiful garden up here," Ollie said to himself. "But this looks like a really big job."

Just then, he heard tiny voices calling, "Help! Help!"

Ollie looked down and saw three little sparrows trapped under a heavy watering can that had fallen over.

"Don't worry, little friends!" Ollie called. "I'll help you!"

But when Ollie tried to lift the watering can by himself, it was too heavy. He pushed and pulled, but it wouldn't budge.

"Oh no," Ollie sighed. "I'm not strong enough alone."

"We can help!" chirped Bella, the smallest sparrow. "If you can tip it just a little, we can squeeze out!"

Ollie thought carefully. "You're right! If we work together, we can do it!"

Ollie pushed with all his might while the sparrows flapped their wings and pushed from inside. Slowly, the watering can tilted just enough for the sparrows to fly free.

"Thank you, Ollie!" sang the sparrows. "How can we help you?"

Ollie's eyes sparkled. "I want to plant a garden here, but it's such a big job. Would you like to help me?"

"Yes!" chirped all three sparrows together.

First, Ollie knew they needed a plan. "Let's think about this step by step," he said wisely. "What do we need for a garden?"

"Seeds!" said Bella.

"Soil!" said Sam, the second sparrow.

"Water!" said Sophie, the third sparrow.

"And patience to let everything grow," added Ollie with a smile.

The friends worked together all morning. The sparrows flew down to collect tiny seeds from the park below. Ollie used his strong paws to scoop fresh soil from a delivery truck parked nearby. Together, they filled each pot carefully.

When Ollie got tired, he remembered to rest in the shade and drink cool water. "Taking care of myself helps me take care of our garden," he said.

The sparrows took turns too, resting their little wings when they needed to.

As the sun reached high in the sky, they planted their last seed.

"Now we need to be patient," Ollie said gently. "Gardens take time to grow, but if we water them every day and work together, something wonderful will happen."

Every day after that, Ollie and his sparrow friends climbed to the rooftop. They watered their plants, pulled tiny weeds, and sang happy songs to help the seeds grow.

After two weeks, tiny green shoots peeked through the soil!

"Look!" squeaked Bella. "Our teamwork made something beautiful!"

Ollie clapped his paws together. "When we help each other and work together, we can do amazing things!"

Soon, the rooftop bloomed with colorful flowers that danced in the breeze. Bees hummed happily, and butterflies visited every day.

"This is the most beautiful garden ever," Ollie said, hugging his friends. "And the best part is, we made it together!"

The End.
