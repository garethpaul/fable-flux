# Benny the Beaver's Rooftop Wellness Day

Benny the beaver was always busy, busy, busy! He lived on the rooftop of a tall building in the city, where he had built the most wonderful stick house. Every day, Benny would scurry around collecting twigs, arranging his home, and working from sunrise to sunset.

"Work, work, work!" Benny would chatter to himself as he dragged heavy branches across the rooftop. His brown fur was always messy, and his big front teeth were covered in bark bits.

One morning, Benny woke up feeling very tired. His back hurt from carrying sticks, his paws were dirty from all the work, and he felt grumpy. "Ow, ow, ow," he groaned as he tried to stand up. "Why do I feel so yucky?"

Just then, Ruby the robin landed on his rooftop. She had bright red feathers that sparkled in the morning sun, and she looked fresh and happy.

"Good morning, Benny!" chirped Ruby cheerfully. "You look tired. Are you feeling okay?"

Benny rubbed his sore back. "I don't feel good, Ruby. I'm always working, but I feel worse and worse each day."

Ruby tilted her little head thoughtfully. "When was the last time you took care of yourself, Benny? I mean really took care of yourself?"

"What do you mean?" asked Benny, confused.

"Well," said Ruby, hopping closer, "do you wash yourself? Do you exercise for fun? Do you rest?"

Benny looked down at his muddy paws and messy fur. "I'm too busy for that stuff! I have work to do!"

Ruby shook her head gently. "Oh, Benny! Taking care of your body isn't extra work – it's the most important work of all! Without a healthy, clean, strong body, you can't do anything well."

"Really?" Benny's eyes grew wide.

"Yes! Let me show you," said Ruby. "First, let's get you clean."

Ruby led Benny to a small puddle of fresh rainwater on the rooftop. "Splash the cool water on your face and paws," she instructed. "Feel how refreshing it is!"

Benny dipped his paws in the water. "Ooh! It feels so cool and nice!" He splashed water on his face and began washing his fur. The dirt came off, and his brown coat began to shine.

"Now," said Ruby, "let's brush those teeth!" She showed Benny how to use a small twig to gently clean his big front teeth until they were white and sparkly.

"I feel cleaner already!" said Benny, admiring his reflection in the water.

"Next comes the fun part – exercise!" Ruby spread her wings. "Let's stretch our bodies and move!"

Ruby taught Benny to stretch his arms up high like tree branches, touch his toes like picking up sticks, and march in place like he was dancing. They did jumping jacks together on the rooftop.

"This is fun!" laughed Benny, feeling his heart beat faster in a good way. "I feel more awake!"

"And now," said Ruby, "the most important part – rest." She showed Benny how to lie down comfortably and take deep, slow breaths. "Your body needs rest to stay strong."

After their wellness time, Benny felt amazing. His body felt strong, his mind felt clear, and he was happy.

"Ruby, thank you!" said Benny. "I understand now. Taking care of my body helps me work better AND feel better!"

From that day on, Benny started each morning with washing, exercising, and planning time to rest. He discovered that when he took care of himself, he had more energy for his work and felt happy all day long.

"A healthy beaver is a happy beaver!" Benny would say with a big, clean smile.

The End.
