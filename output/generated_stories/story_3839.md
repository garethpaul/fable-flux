# Ellie's Bubble Bath Helper

In a sunny yellow bathroom lived a gentle elephant calf named Ellie. She had soft gray skin and kind brown eyes. Ellie loved taking warm bubble baths in the big white bathtub.

Every evening, Ellie would turn on the shiny faucet. The water made a happy splashing sound. She would add sweet-smelling bubbles that tickled her trunk. Bath time was her favorite time of day.

One evening, Ellie's best friend Benny Bunny was coming for a sleepover. "I can't wait to show Benny how fun bath time is!" Ellie said excitedly. She flapped her ears with joy.

But when Ellie tried to reach the bubble soap, she had a big problem. The bottle sat way up high on the bathroom shelf. Ellie stretched her trunk as far as she could. "Oh dear," she sighed. "I'm too little to reach it."

Ellie felt sad bubbles in her tummy. The bathroom felt too quiet without the happy splash of bubbles. Her trunk drooped down low.

"Maybe I should give up," Ellie whispered. But then she remembered Benny's excited voice on the phone. "He's counting on a fun bubble bath!"

Ellie took a deep breath. The air smelled like minty toothpaste. "I won't give up on my friend," she said bravely. "There must be another way!"

First, Ellie tried jumping. Her feet made soft thumping sounds on the fluffy bath mat. But she still couldn't reach the high shelf. The bottle seemed even farther away.

Next, Ellie looked around the bathroom. She spotted a small step stool hiding behind the toilet. "Maybe this will help!" she said hopefully. She pushed it with her trunk, making scraping sounds on the tile floor.

Ellie climbed onto the step stool. She stretched her trunk up, up, up. Her trunk tip barely touched the bottle. "Almost there!" she puffed.

Then Ellie had a wonderful idea. She remembered the long-handled back scrubber hanging on the wall. "This might work!" she said excitedly.

Carefully, Ellie used her trunk to lift the scrubber. She gently pushed the bubble bottle with the soft brush end. The bottle wobbled and tipped. Ellie caught it perfectly with her trunk!

"Hooray!" Ellie trumpeted happily. She did a little dance on the step stool. Her heart felt warm and proud.

Just then, the doorbell rang. "Ding-dong!" Benny had arrived!

"Hi Ellie!" called Benny from downstairs. "I brought my rubber ducky for our bath!"

"Perfect timing!" Ellie called back. She turned on the warm water and squeezed in lots of sweet bubble soap. The bubbles grew tall and fluffy like white clouds.

When Benny hopped into the bathroom, his eyes grew wide. "Wow! Look at all these amazing bubbles!" he exclaimed. "How did you make so many?"

Ellie smiled proudly. "I had to solve a big problem first," she explained. "But I didn't give up because I wanted our bath to be special."

As they splashed in the warm, bubbly water, Benny said, "Thank you for working so hard to make this perfect, Ellie. You're the best friend ever!"

Ellie felt happy tingles in her trunk. The bathroom filled with their joyful laughter and gentle splashing sounds. The bubbles sparkled like tiny rainbows in the light.

"Friends help friends have fun," Ellie said wisely. "And when we prepare with care, everything turns out wonderful!"

The End.
