# Leo's Sharing Dance

In a cozy closet hideaway behind the winter coats, lived a brave little lion cub named Leo. His golden fur was soft and fluffy, and his tiny roar sounded more like a happy purr. Leo had made the closet his special place, filled with colorful pillows and his favorite toy drum.

One sunny morning, Leo heard his tummy rumble. "Grumble, grumble, GROWL!" it said loudly. He looked at his special snack box in the corner of his hideaway. Inside were three shiny red apples, two bunches of sweet purple grapes, and a bag of crunchy orange carrots.

"Yummy!" Leo said, licking his lips. "These look so good!"

Just as he was about to take a big bite of apple, he heard a tiny voice outside his closet door.

"Sniff, sniff," came the sound. "I'm so hungry, but I can't find any food anywhere."

Leo peeked through the crack in the door. There sat Rosie, a small bunny with droopy ears and sad eyes. Her fluffy white tail wasn't bouncing like usual.

Leo's heart felt warm and tingly. He looked at his delicious snacks, then back at hungry Rosie. His brave little heart knew exactly what to do.

"Rosie!" Leo called out, opening his closet door wide. "Would you like to share my snacks with me?"

Rosie's ears perked up like two fuzzy question marks. "Really? You would share with me?"

"Of course!" Leo said with a big smile. "Sharing makes everything taste better!"

Leo brought out his snack box and placed it between them on the soft carpet. "Pick whatever looks yummy to you!"

Rosie chose a bunch of purple grapes. "Crunch, crunch!" she munched happily. "These are the sweetest grapes ever!"

Leo bit into a crispy carrot. "Crunch! You're right! Everything does taste better when we share!"

Soon, more friends appeared. Tommy the turtle smelled the delicious food, and Bella the bird heard their happy laughter.

"Can we join too?" they asked hopefully.

"The more friends, the more fun!" Leo declared bravely.

All four friends sat in a circle, sharing the healthy snacks. The apples were crisp and sweet, the grapes burst with juicy flavor, and the carrots made satisfying crunchy sounds.

"This gives me so much energy!" said Tommy, doing a little turtle wiggle.

"Me too!" chirped Bella, flapping her wings.

Leo felt so happy that his paws started tapping. "Let's dance!" he roared joyfully.

The friends began to dance together. Leo spun in circles, Rosie hopped and bounced, Tommy did his wiggle dance, and Bella twirled in the air. Their laughter filled the room like musical notes.

"Tap, tap, bounce, wiggle, spin!" they danced and giggled.

As the sun began to set, the friends felt full and happy. Their bellies were satisfied with nutritious food, and their hearts were warm with friendship.

"Thank you, Leo," said Rosie, giving him a gentle hug. "You're the most generous friend ever!"

"Sharing made this the best day!" agreed Tommy and Bella.

Leo's chest puffed up with pride, but not because he was showing off. He felt proud because he had been brave enough to share, and it made everyone happy.

"Tomorrow, let's all bring snacks to share!" Leo suggested.

"Yes!" all his friends cheered together.

That night, Leo curled up in his cozy closet hideaway, smiling as he remembered the day. He learned that being generous and sharing with friends made everything more fun, more delicious, and filled his heart with joy.

The End.
