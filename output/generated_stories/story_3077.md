# Polly's Special Hideaway

Polly was a bright green parrot with beautiful red feathers on her wings. She lived in a cozy house with her family, but her very favorite place was a special closet hideaway she had made all by herself.

The closet was tucked away behind the winter coats. Polly had decorated it with colorful ribbons she found, shiny buttons, and soft blankets. "This is my special place," Polly would say proudly, fluffing her feathers.

One rainy morning, Polly heard sniffling sounds coming from the hallway. She peeked out of her hideaway and saw Benny the bunny sitting by the window, looking very sad.

"What's wrong, Benny?" Polly asked, hopping down from her perch.

"I miss my grandma," Benny said with a sigh. "She used to tell me the most wonderful stories about our family. Now I don't remember them very well."

Polly's heart felt warm and fuzzy. She wanted to help her friend feel better. "Would you like to come to my special hideaway?" she asked. "Maybe we can think of something together."

Benny's ears perked up. "Really? You'd share your special place with me?"

"Of course!" Polly chirped happily. "Friends share special things!"

Polly led Benny to her cozy closet hideaway. Benny's eyes grew wide with wonder. "Wow, Polly! This is amazing! It smells like lavender and feels so soft and warm."

They snuggled together on the fluffy blankets. Polly had an idea. "Benny, why don't you tell me what you remember about your grandma's stories? Even little bits!"

Benny thought hard. "Well, she always started with 'Once upon a time, in a meadow full of dancing flowers...'"

"That sounds beautiful!" Polly said. "What happened next?"

"I... I don't remember," Benny said sadly.

Polly gently patted Benny's paw with her wing. "That's okay! We can make up our own story together. You can start it the way your grandma did, and we'll create new adventures!"

Benny's face lit up. "Really? We can do that?"

"Yes! And this can be our special tradition," Polly explained. "Every time it rains, we can come to my hideaway and tell stories together. That way, you'll always have special stories to remember."

Benny felt much better. "Once upon a time, in a meadow full of dancing flowers," he began, "there lived a brave little bunny and his best friend, a creative parrot..."

Together, they spent the whole rainy afternoon making up wonderful stories. They took turns adding parts, laughing and using their imaginations. The closet hideaway felt even more special now that it was filled with friendship and new traditions.

"Thank you, Polly," Benny said as the sun began to peek through the clouds. "You helped me feel better, and now we have our own special story tradition!"

"That's what friends do," Polly said with a happy squawk. "We help each other and share the things that matter most."

From that day on, whenever Benny felt sad or lonely, he knew he could count on Polly and their special hideaway. And Polly learned that sharing her favorite place made it even more wonderful.

The two friends had discovered that the best traditions are the ones you create together, and that helping a friend always makes your own heart feel full of joy.

The End.
