# Ollie Otter's Clean and Healthy Day

In a cozy nursery with soft blue walls and fluffy white clouds painted on the ceiling, lived a playful little otter named Ollie. The nursery smelled like fresh lavender, and sunshine streamed through the big window, making everything feel warm and happy.

But today, Ollie had a problem. He looked around his beautiful nursery and saw toys scattered everywhere, his water bowl was murky and dirty, and he hadn't brushed his thick, brown fur in days. His favorite stuffed fish was covered in dust, and even his soft blanket smelled funny.

"Oh no!" said Ollie, wrinkling his little black nose. "My nursery doesn't feel clean and healthy anymore. I don't feel very good either."

Ollie's tummy rumbled loudly. He realized he had been eating too many sweet fish crackers and not enough of the nutritious foods that made him strong and energetic.

"I need to make some changes," Ollie said thoughtfully, scratching behind his ear. "But how can I make everything better?"

Then Ollie had a wonderful idea! He would create a special daily routine to keep himself and his nursery healthy and clean.

First, Ollie started with his own body. He filled his basin with fresh, cool water that sparkled in the sunlight. "Splash, splash!" he giggled as he washed his paws and face. The water felt refreshing and made his whiskers tingle.

Next, he carefully brushed his soft fur with gentle strokes. "Brush, brush, brush," he sang happily. His fur became shiny and smooth, and he felt so much better.

"Now for healthy food!" Ollie declared. Instead of reaching for the sweet crackers, he chose crunchy carrots, fresh fish, and cool water. "Crunch, crunch! This tastes so good and makes my body strong," he said, feeling proud of his choice.

After eating, Ollie looked at his messy nursery. "Time to clean up!" he announced. He picked up each toy, one by one, and put them in their special places. He organized his books on the shelf and fluffed his pillows.

When he cleaned his water bowl, the fresh water sparkled like diamonds. "Thank you, clean water, for keeping me healthy," Ollie said gratefully.

Ollie even invented a fun cleaning song: "Clean up, clean up, it's the healthy way! Clean up, clean up, every single day!"

As he worked, other nursery friends came to watch. "Wow, Ollie!" said Bella Bunny. "Your nursery looks amazing! Can you teach us your healthy routine?"

"Of course!" Ollie smiled widely. "When we all stay clean and healthy together, our whole nursery community feels better!"

By evening, Ollie's nursery smelled fresh like clean cotton. His toys were organized, his water was crystal clear, and he felt energetic and happy. His tummy felt good from eating nutritious food, and his fur was soft and clean.

"I'm so grateful for my healthy habits," Ollie said, snuggling into his fresh, clean blanket. "Taking care of myself and my space makes me feel wonderful!"

From that day forward, Ollie followed his healthy routine every day. He ate good food, stayed clean, kept his nursery tidy, and even helped his friends learn healthy habits too.

And whenever little otters visited the nursery, they learned that healthy habits make everyone feel happy, strong, and grateful for their bodies and homes.

The End.
