# Benny Beaver's Big Dinner Plan

Benny Beaver was very excited. Today was his family's special dinner party! He wanted everything to be perfect in their cozy dining room.

"I have the BEST plan!" Benny said, clapping his little paws together. He looked around the warm dining room with its wooden table and soft yellow chairs.

Benny started moving things around. He pushed the big chair to the window. He moved the small chairs to the corners. He even tried to drag the heavy table to a new spot.

"There!" Benny said proudly. "Now everyone will sit exactly where I think is best!"

But when Mama Beaver came in, she looked confused. "Oh my! Benny, where should I put the soup bowls?"

"Right there!" Benny pointed to a chair by the window. But the chair was too far from the table.

Papa Beaver walked in carrying a basket of warm bread. "Where's my usual spot, son?" Papa looked around, scratching his furry head.

"I moved everything to make it perfect!" Benny said. But he started to feel worried. The room looked mixed up, not perfect.

Little Sister Beaver came in last. She was very small and couldn't reach the tall chair Benny had picked for her. "I can't climb up there," she said sadly.

Benny's tail drooped. His perfect plan wasn't working. Everyone looked uncomfortable and confused.

"Maybe," said Mama Beaver gently, "we could work together to make the dining room nice for everyone."

Benny felt his cheeks get warm. "But I wanted to make it perfect by myself," he whispered.

Papa Beaver knelt down next to Benny. "Your ideas are wonderful! But sometimes the best plans happen when we listen to everyone."

"Really?" Benny asked, looking up with hopeful eyes.

"Let's try together," suggested Little Sister. "I have an idea too!"

Benny took a deep breath. "Okay. What does everyone think?"

Mama Beaver smiled. "I need the table in the middle so I can reach everyone with the food."

"And I like my chair facing the garden," added Papa Beaver.

Little Sister pointed to a small chair. "I need the low chair so I can sit safely."

Benny listened carefully. Then he had a new idea! "What if we put the table in the middle like Mama wants, and Papa's chair facing the garden, and Sister's low chair next to Mama?"

"Where would you like to sit?" asked Mama.

Benny looked around thoughtfully. "Next to everyone! That way we can all talk and share."

Working together, the beaver family moved the furniture. Benny helped push chairs. Mama arranged the table. Papa moved the heavy pieces. Little Sister put the napkins in just the right spots.

When they finished, the dining room looked beautiful and cozy. Everyone had what they needed, and they could all see each other's smiling faces.

"This is even better than my first plan!" Benny said happily.

"That's because we all helped," said Little Sister, giving Benny a hug.

As they sat down for dinner, Benny felt proud and happy. He had learned that the best solutions come when everyone shares their ideas and works together.

"Thank you for helping me make it really perfect," Benny said to his family.

And it truly was perfect – because everyone felt comfortable, respected, and loved.

The End.
