# Leo's Rainbow Playdate

Leo the lion cub bounced excitedly on the soft, colorful rug in his living room. Today was special - his friends were coming over to play! The room smelled like fresh cookies from the kitchen, and sunlight streamed through the big windows, making everything bright and cheerful.

"Roar! I can't wait!" Leo said, his golden fur shimmering as he practiced his best lion roar. His toy chest was overflowing with blocks, stuffed animals, and his favorite red fire truck.

*Ding dong!* The doorbell chimed like a happy song.

"They're here!" Leo's mama called from the kitchen. "Remember to share and be kind, little one."

Leo rushed to the door and saw his friends arriving. There was Emma the elephant with her long, gray trunk and kind eyes. Benny the bear waddled in with his thick, brown fur that looked so fluffy. Zara the zebra clip-clopped inside with her beautiful black and white stripes that looked like a living painting.

"Hi everyone!" Leo roared happily. "Let's play with my blocks!"

They all sat in a circle on the soft carpet. Leo dumped out his colorful building blocks - red ones, blue ones, yellow ones, and green ones. They clinked and clattered as they scattered across the floor.

"I want to build a tall tower!" said Emma, reaching for the red blocks with her trunk.

"I want the red ones too!" Leo said, grabbing the same blocks. "I had them first!"

"But I called them first!" Emma's voice sounded sad and frustrated.

Leo felt his chest getting tight and warm. He wanted to roar loudly, but he took a deep breath instead, just like Mama taught him.

Benny picked up some blue blocks with his big paws. "Look! These blue ones are pretty too!"

Zara used her mouth to pick up yellow blocks. "And these yellow ones shine like the sun!"

Leo looked around at his friends. Emma's trunk was so good at picking up blocks. Benny's strong paws could stack them really high. Zara's careful mouth could place them just right. Everyone was different, and everyone was good at different things!

"Wait!" Leo said, his eyes growing bright. "What if we all build together? Emma, your trunk is amazing at reaching high places!"

Emma's eyes sparkled. "Really? And Benny, your paws are so strong for the bottom!"

"And Zara, you're so careful and gentle!" Benny added with a warm smile.

"And Leo, you have the best ideas!" Zara said, swishing her striped tail happily.

They all worked together, each using their special skills. Emma lifted blocks up high with her trunk. Benny made a strong foundation with his powerful paws. Zara placed blocks with gentle precision. Leo directed and cheered everyone on.

Soon they had built the most beautiful rainbow tower anyone had ever seen! It had red blocks from Emma, blue blocks from Benny, yellow blocks from Zara, and green blocks from Leo.

"Look what we made together!" Leo roared with joy. "It's so much better than anything I could build alone!"

"We're all different, but that makes us special," said Emma, wrapping her trunk around her friends in a group hug.

"Different is wonderful!" agreed all the friends together.

As they admired their rainbow tower, Leo felt warm and happy inside. He learned that when everyone shares their special gifts, amazing things happen. Being different wasn't something to worry about - it was something to celebrate!

The End.
