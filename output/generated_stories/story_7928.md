# Milo's Morning Routine

In a cozy, quiet attic filled with soft sunbeams and gentle dust motes dancing in the air, lived a kind little mouse named Milo. His tiny home was tucked behind an old wooden chest, where he had made the most comfortable nest from scraps of soft fabric and cotton.

Every morning, Milo loved to wake up early when the attic was peaceful and still. He would stretch his little paws, twitch his whiskers, and smell the fresh morning air that drifted through the small attic window.

But one sunny Tuesday morning, something felt different. Milo woke up feeling confused and worried.

"Oh my!" squeaked Milo, looking around his messy nest. "I can't remember what I'm supposed to do today!"

His tiny bed was unmade, his breakfast crumbs were scattered everywhere, and his favorite acorn was nowhere to be found. Milo's whiskers trembled as he felt overwhelmed.

"I need to find my morning routine," he said to himself. "But where do I start?"

Milo sat down and took three deep breaths, just like his grandmother had taught him. The sweet smell of morning air filled his tiny nose, and he began to feel calmer.

"I know!" chirped Milo. "I'll make a plan and do one thing at a time."

First, Milo decided to make his bed. He pulled the soft cotton pieces into place and fluffed his tiny pillow made from a dandelion seed. "There!" he squeaked happily. "My bed looks cozy and neat."

Next, Milo gathered all his breakfast crumbs into a small pile. He saved the biggest pieces for later and swept the tiny crumbs into his special corner. "Much better!" he said, feeling proud.

Then came the tricky part - finding his favorite acorn. Milo looked under his pillow, behind his water drop collection, and even inside his sock drawer made from a matchbox.

"Where could it be?" wondered Milo, but he didn't give up.

Finally, he spotted something round and brown peeking out from under yesterday's leaf blanket. "My acorn!" he cheered, holding it close to his furry chest.

With his space clean and organized, Milo felt much better. He decided to create a simple morning routine to help him every day.

"First, I'll make my bed," he said, counting on his tiny paws. "Then I'll eat breakfast and clean up. Next, I'll find my special acorn. And last, I'll plan something fun for the day!"

Milo wrote his routine on a small piece of birch bark with a tiny twig dipped in berry juice. He hung it near his bed where he could see it every morning.

The next day, Milo woke up feeling excited instead of worried. He looked at his routine list and smiled.

"Good morning, routine!" he squeaked cheerfully.

One by one, Milo completed each task. Make the bed - check! Eat breakfast and clean up - check! Find his acorn - check! Plan something fun - check!

"I feel so happy and ready for my day!" Milo said, doing a little dance around his tidy home.

From that day on, Milo followed his gentle morning routine. It helped him feel calm, organized, and ready for all the wonderful adventures each new day would bring.

The End.
