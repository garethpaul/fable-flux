# Squeaky's Fair Share

Squeaky the mouse lived in a cozy hole behind the bathroom sink. He had soft gray fur and tiny pink ears that wiggled when he was excited. Every morning, Squeaky loved to explore the big, white bathroom with its shiny tiles and warm sunbeams.

One sunny Tuesday morning, Squeaky peeked out of his hole. "What a beautiful day!" he squeaked happily. The bathroom smelled fresh and clean, like soap bubbles and mint toothpaste.

As Squeaky scurried across the cool tile floor, he discovered something wonderful. Near the bathtub sat a small pile of colorful bath toys - a yellow rubber duck, a red boat, and a green frog that squeaked when you pressed it.

"Oh my!" said Squeaky, his whiskers twitching with excitement. "These toys look so fun!"

Just then, he heard tiny footsteps. Around the corner came Pip, a little brown mouse who lived under the bathtub. Pip's tummy was rumbling loudly.

"Good morning, Squeaky," said Pip sadly. "I'm so hungry, but I can't find any crumbs anywhere."

Squeaky looked at the toys, then at his hungry friend. He remembered finding some delicious cookie crumbs earlier that morning. "Wait here, Pip!" Squeaky said kindly.

He scurried back to his hole and returned with half of his cookie crumbs. "Here, Pip. Let's share these together!"

Pip's eyes grew wide and happy. "Really? For me? Thank you so much, Squeaky!"

As they munched on the sweet crumbs, Pip noticed the colorful bath toys. "Wow, those toys look amazing!" Pip said wistfully.

Squeaky thought for a moment. It wouldn't be fair if he played with all the toys while Pip just watched. "Would you like to play with them together?" Squeaky asked with a warm smile.

"Could we really?" Pip asked, bouncing up and down.

"Of course! Sharing makes everything more fun," Squeaky replied.

The two friends spent the morning playing safely with the bath toys. Squeaky sailed the red boat in a puddle of water while Pip made the green frog squeak a happy tune. They took turns with the yellow duck, making it bob and splash gently.

"This is the best day ever!" giggled Pip. "Thank you for sharing with me, Squeaky."

"Thank you for being such a good friend," Squeaky replied. "Playing together is much better than playing alone."

When they heard footsteps approaching, both mice quickly and quietly returned the toys to their proper place by the bathtub. They followed the bathroom rules and made sure everything was clean and tidy.

Back in Squeaky's cozy hole, the two friends shared the rest of the cookie crumbs.

"Squeaky," said Pip, "you taught me something important today. When we share and are fair to others, everyone feels happy."

Squeaky nodded, his pink ears wiggling. "That's right, Pip! And when we're grateful for what we have and share it with friends, we end up having even more fun."

From that day on, Squeaky and Pip became the very best of friends. They always shared their food, took turns with toys, and helped each other. The bathroom became filled with the sound of their happy squeaks and giggles.

And whenever other little mice visited, Squeaky and Pip always remembered to be fair, share what they had, and include everyone in their fun.

The End.
