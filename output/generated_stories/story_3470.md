# Robo's Big Helper Day

Robo was a shiny blue robot dog who loved to help. His tail wagged with happy beeps, and his eyes glowed bright yellow when he was excited. Every morning, Robo rolled around the house on his little wheels, looking for ways to be helpful.

"Beep beep! Good morning!" Robo said cheerfully as he rolled into the bathroom. The warm sunlight streamed through the window, making the white tiles sparkle.

But today, something was different. Little Emma was sitting on the bathroom floor, looking very sad. Her bottom lip was wobbling, and her eyes were full of tears.

"What's wrong, Emma?" Robo asked gently, his voice making soft, caring sounds.

"I wanted to make a special craft for Mommy's birthday," Emma sniffled. "But I can't reach the pretty shells on the high shelf. And I dropped all my crayons, and they rolled everywhere!"

Robo's heart-light glowed warm pink. He wanted to help his friend feel better. "Don't worry, Emma! We can work together. Cooperation means helping each other!"

"Really?" Emma wiped her nose with her sleeve.

"Beep beep, yes!" Robo's tail wagged faster. "I'm tall when I stand up, and you have wonderful hands for crafting. Together, we make a great team!"

Robo stretched his robot legs up, up, up until he was standing tall. His mechanical arms reached high to the shelf. "I can reach the shells! Can you hold your hands out to catch them?"

Emma giggled and cupped her small hands. "Ready!"

One by one, Robo carefully passed down the beautiful pink and white shells. They felt smooth and cool in Emma's palms.

"Now for those runaway crayons," Robo said. "I'll use my special sensor eyes to find them, and you can pick them up with your quick fingers!"

Robo's eyes glowed brighter as he scanned the bathroom. "Beep! I see a red crayon behind the toilet!" His wheels whirred as he rolled over to point it out.

Emma crawled over and grabbed it. "Found it!"

"Blue crayon under the sink!" Robo announced.

"Got it!" Emma said, getting more excited.

They moved around the bathroom together like a fun dance. Robo would spot and point, Emma would crawl and collect. Soon, all the crayons were back in their box.

"Now we have everything we need," Robo said proudly. "What kind of craft are you making?"

"A special card with shells glued on it!" Emma explained. "But I need help holding things steady."

"I have very steady robot hands," Robo offered. "I can hold the card while you glue the shells."

Emma's face lit up with joy. "That would be perfect!"

They sat together on the soft bathroom rug. Robo held the colorful paper very still while Emma carefully placed each shell. She drew pretty flowers with her crayons around the shells.

"It's beautiful!" Robo said, his voice full of happiness. "Your mommy will love it!"

Emma hugged Robo's smooth metal body. "Thank you for helping me, Robo. We did it together!"

"That's what cooperation is all about," Robo said, his heart-light glowing the warmest pink. "When we work together and help each other, we can solve any problem!"

Emma smiled big. "And it's more fun too!"

"Beep beep! Exactly right!" Robo agreed, his tail wagging with joy.

The End.
