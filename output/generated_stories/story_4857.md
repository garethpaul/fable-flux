# Rocco's Clean-Up Song

Rocco the raccoon loved making music in his cozy treehouse. He had shiny drums made from old cans, a guitar with colorful strings, and bells that jingled like raindrops.

*Tap-tap-tap!* went his drums. *Ding-a-ling!* sang his bells.

But Rocco had a big problem. After each music session, he left his snacks everywhere. Apple cores rolled under his drums. Banana peels slipped down the tree trunk. Empty juice boxes scattered across the wooden floor.

"My treehouse is so messy," Rocco sighed, looking around at the trash.

One sunny morning, his friend Bella the bluebird flew to his window.

"Rocco!" she chirped sadly. "The baby birds can't fly safely. There's trash stuck in all the tree branches!"

Rocco peeked outside. Oh no! His old food wrappers were tangled in the leaves. A plastic bag fluttered dangerously near a bird's nest.

"I made this mess," Rocco whispered, his ears drooping. "The trees and animals are hurt because of me."

His tummy felt heavy with worry. How could he fix this big problem?

Then Rocco had an idea. Music always made him feel better. Maybe music could help him clean up too!

"I'll make a clean-up song!" he announced.

Rocco picked up his drums and started a happy beat. *Boom-boom-tap!*

"♪ Pick it up, pick it up, put it where it goes! ♪" he sang loudly.

The rhythm made cleaning feel like dancing. Rocco grabbed an apple core and tossed it in his compost bin. *Plop!*

"♪ Trash in the bin, not on the ground! Keep our forest safe and sound! ♪"

Soon, other forest friends heard his catchy tune. Bella flew down with her beak full of wrapper pieces. Two squirrels scampered over, carrying bottle caps in their tiny paws.

"Can we help?" squeaked the squirrels.

"Yes!" Rocco cheered. "Let's all sing together!"

The whole group sang Rocco's clean-up song while they worked. The forest friends made it into a game - who could find the most trash? Who could sort recycling the fastest?

*Ding-a-ling!* went Rocco's bells as he danced around, collecting every piece of litter.

After an hour of musical cleaning, the treehouse sparkled. The tree branches were clear and safe. The forest floor looked green and beautiful again.

"Look!" chirped Bella happily. "The baby birds can fly freely now!"

Three tiny bluebirds zoomed safely through the clean branches, singing their own little songs.

Rocco felt warm and proud inside. "I'm sorry I made such a mess," he told his friends. "I didn't think about how it hurt our home."

"But you fixed it!" said the squirrels. "And now we know how to keep it clean!"

From that day on, Rocco sang his clean-up song after every music session. He put his snacks in proper bins. He recycled his cans and bottles. He even taught other animals the special song.

"♪ Take care of our forest, take care of our trees! Keep them clean and healthy for you and me! ♪"

The treehouse stayed tidy and beautiful. The forest animals were safe and happy. And Rocco learned that taking care of his home was just as important as making beautiful music.

Whenever he played his drums now, he remembered: every small action matters when we work together to protect our world.

The End.
