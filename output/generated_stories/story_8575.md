# Leo's Big Block Tower Deal

The living room sparkled with afternoon sunshine streaming through tall windows. Colorful blocks were scattered across the soft carpet like rainbow jewels. Leo the lion cub sat in the middle, his golden fur glowing in the warm light.

"I want to build the BIGGEST tower ever!" Leo roared proudly, his tiny mane fluffy around his face.

Leo stacked red blocks, then blue ones, then yellow ones that smelled like vanilla. His tower grew taller and taller. His little paws worked carefully, placing each block just right.

*Thump, thump, thump!* Little footsteps came down the hallway.

"Leo! Can I play too?" called his sister Luna, a smaller lion cub with sparkly whiskers.

Leo's ears drooped. "No! These are MY blocks. I'm building something super important!"

Luna's bottom lip wobbled. "But I want to help. Please?"

"Absolutely not!" Leo huffed, turning his back to her. "Go find your own toys!"

Luna sat down with a sad *plop*. Tears made her big brown eyes shiny. "I just wanted to play with my big brother," she whispered.

Leo kept building, but something felt wrong in his tummy. The blocks didn't seem as bright anymore. His tower looked lonely, even though it was very tall.

Mama Lion padded into the room, her warm paws silent on the carpet. "What's happening here, cubs?"

"Leo won't share!" Luna sniffled. "I asked nicely, but he said no."

Leo's whiskers twitched. "But Mama, I was here first! And my tower is almost finished!"

Mama Lion sat down between them. "Leo, what if we could find a way for both of you to be happy? What would that look like?"

Leo thought hard, his little forehead wrinkled. "Well... maybe Luna could help, but only if she promises not to knock anything down."

"I promise!" Luna bounced excitedly. "I'll be super careful!"

"Wait," Leo said, holding up his paw. "And you have to let me be the tower boss. I decide where blocks go."

Luna nodded eagerly. "Okay! Can I pick the colors?"

Leo's eyes brightened. "That's actually a good idea! You're really good at making pretty patterns."

"And maybe," Luna added hopefully, "we could take turns being the boss for different parts?"

Leo considered this carefully. It felt fair in his heart. "Okay, but I get to be boss of the bottom part since I started it."

"Deal!" Luna giggled, reaching out her tiny paw.

Leo shook it solemnly. "Deal! But we have to be honest about whose turn it is, okay?"

"I promise to tell the truth," Luna said seriously.

Working together, their tower grew even taller than before. Luna chose beautiful color patterns while Leo made sure each block was perfectly straight. They took turns being the boss, and when Leo forgot once, he remembered his promise and let Luna have her turn.

"Look how amazing our tower is!" Leo marveled, stepping back to admire their work.

"It's the most beautiful tower in the whole world!" Luna agreed, her tail swishing happily.

Mama Lion smiled warmly. "When you work together and talk about what you both need, magical things happen."

Leo hugged his sister tight. "I'm sorry I didn't want to share at first. Our tower is much better with both of us!"

"And I'm glad we made a fair deal," Luna purred. "Negotiating is fun!"

Their tower stood tall and proud, sparkling with every color of the rainbow. But the best part wasn't the blocks at all – it was the warm, happy feeling of working together as a team.

The End.
