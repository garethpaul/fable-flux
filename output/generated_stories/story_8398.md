# Rocco's Clean-Up Song

Rocco the raccoon loved making music in his cozy garage workshop. Every morning, he would tap-tap-tap on his drums made from old buckets and strum his guitar made from a cardboard box. The garage smelled like sawdust and had tools hanging on every wall.

But today, something was different. Rocco woke up and his fur felt sticky and messy. His little paws were dirty from yesterday's adventure in the garden, and his tail had bits of leaves stuck in it.

"Oh my!" said Rocco, looking at himself in the shiny surface of his cymbal. "I'm too messy to make beautiful music today."

Rocco's tummy rumbled, but when he reached for his breakfast berries, he stopped. "Wait," he said to himself. "Mama always says clean paws make tasty food taste even better!"

So Rocco walked to his special washing station in the corner of the garage. He had made it himself from a big plastic container that used to hold birdseed. Now it caught rainwater from the roof - perfect for washing!

"Time for my clean-up song!" Rocco announced cheerfully. He began to sing as he dipped his paws in the cool, fresh water:

"Wash, wash, wash your paws,
Scrub them nice and clean!
Splish and splash and rinse them off,
The cleanest paws you've ever seen!"

The water felt wonderful on his little paws. Rocco rubbed them together, making tiny bubbles with his special soap made from gentle plants. He watched the dirt swirl away down the drain that led to his garden - nothing was wasted!

Next, Rocco brushed his fur with a soft brush made from recycled materials. "Brush, brush, brush your fur," he sang, "Make it soft and bright! When you take good care of you, everything feels just right!"

He carefully picked the leaves from his fluffy tail and combed it until it was perfectly clean. Then he brushed his tiny teeth with a small brush and minty toothpaste.

"There!" said Rocco, looking at his reflection again. "Now I feel fresh and ready for anything!"

His tummy rumbled again, so Rocco washed his paws one more time before eating his breakfast berries. They tasted extra delicious with clean paws!

Feeling wonderful, Rocco picked up his instruments. His music sounded more beautiful than ever! The drums went "boom-boom-boom" and his guitar went "strum-strum-strum." Even the birds outside seemed to sing along.

"When I take care of myself first," Rocco said happily, "everything else feels better too! My music sounds prettier, my food tastes yummier, and I feel ready for a whole day of fun!"

Just then, his friend Bella Bunny hopped into the garage. "Rocco, you look wonderful today! And your music sounds amazing!"

"Thank you, Bella!" said Rocco. "I learned that taking care of myself helps me take care of everything else better. Would you like to learn my clean-up song?"

Bella nodded excitedly, and together they sang and danced around the garage workshop, celebrating the joy of feeling clean, healthy, and ready for adventure.

From that day on, Rocco always started his mornings with his special clean-up routine. He knew that taking good care of himself was the best way to be ready for all the wonderful things each day would bring.

The End.
