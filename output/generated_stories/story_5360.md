# Nutkin's Helping Paws

In a cozy dining room with tall windows and a big wooden table, lived a little squirrel named Nutkin. Nutkin had fluffy brown fur and a bushy tail that twitched when he was excited. He was very good at solving problems and loved to help others.

One sunny morning, Nutkin heard sad crying sounds coming from under the dining room table. He scampered down from his favorite spot on the windowsill to see what was wrong.

"Hello?" Nutkin called softly. "Is someone there?"

A tiny field mouse peeked out from behind a table leg. Her whiskers were wet with tears, and her small paws were shaking.

"I'm Milly," she sniffled. "I'm lost and scared. I can't find my way back to my family."

Nutkin's heart felt warm and caring. He wanted to help right away! But then he remembered what his mama had taught him about being safe and setting good boundaries.

"I want to help you, Milly," Nutkin said kindly. "But first, let me think of the best way to do this safely."

Nutkin sat quietly for a moment, breathing slowly and thinking carefully. This was called being mindful - taking time to think before acting.

"I know!" Nutkin said, his tail twitching with excitement. "I can teach you some skills to help you find your way home, and we can work together as a team!"

First, Nutkin showed Milly how to climb up the table leg to get a better view of the room. "Use your claws like this," he demonstrated, "and take one step at a time."

Milly tried and tried. At first, she slipped down, but Nutkin cheered her on. "You're doing great! Try again!"

Soon, Milly could climb all the way to the top of the table. "I did it!" she squeaked happily.

Next, Nutkin taught Milly how to use her nose to smell for familiar scents. "Close your eyes and breathe in deeply," he said. "What do you smell?"

Milly sniffed and sniffed. "I smell... flowers! My home is near the flower garden!"

From their high spot on the table, they could see the beautiful flower garden through the dining room window. Nutkin helped Milly plan a safe path home, showing her landmarks to remember.

"But I'm still scared to go alone," Milly whispered.

Nutkin thought about this carefully. He wanted to help, but he also needed to stay safe and not go too far from his own home.

"I have an idea!" Nutkin said. "I'll walk with you to the edge of the dining room. From there, you can see the garden clearly, and you'll know exactly which way to go."

Together, they climbed down and walked to the dining room door. Nutkin pointed out the safe path through the hallway that led to the garden door.

"Remember," Nutkin said, "if you ever get lost again, find a safe, quiet place to sit and think. Take deep breaths, use your senses, and look for helpers like me."

Milly's eyes sparkled with confidence. "Thank you, Nutkin! You taught me so much. You're a wonderful friend!"

As Milly scampered toward the garden, Nutkin felt proud and happy. He had helped someone in need while staying safe and being mindful. He had built his own skills too - he was now even better at problem-solving and teaching others.

Back on his windowsill, Nutkin watched Milly reunite with her family in the garden. Her little mouse siblings danced with joy, and Milly pointed up at the dining room window, waving at her new friend.

Nutkin waved back, his heart full of the warm feeling that comes from true friendship and helping others.

The End.
