# Bunny's Big Bathroom Problem

Ruby the rabbit had a very big problem. Every morning, she tried to brush her teeth, but she was too short to reach the sink!

"Oh no!" Ruby said, standing on her tippy-toes. "I can't reach the faucet. How will I brush my teeth and keep them clean and sparkly?"

Ruby's mama heard her little sighs from the bathroom. "What's wrong, sweetie?" Mama called from the kitchen.

"I'm too little!" Ruby replied, her nose twitching with frustration. "The sink is too high, and I can't brush my teeth properly!"

Ruby sat on the cool bathroom tiles and thought hard. She could smell the minty toothpaste and see her colorful toothbrush sitting on the counter, but they felt so far away.

"There has to be a way," Ruby whispered to herself. "I need to think like a smart bunny!"

First, Ruby tried jumping. She hopped and hopped, her fluffy tail bouncing. But even her highest bunny hop wasn't high enough.

"Hmm," she said, tapping her paw on her chin. "Jumping doesn't work. What else can I try?"

Then Ruby had an idea! She remembered the wooden step stool in her bedroom. She hopped quickly to get it, her little feet pattering on the floor.

"This might work!" she said excitedly, dragging the step stool to the bathroom. It made a scraping sound on the floor.

Ruby climbed onto the step stool. She was closer, but still not quite tall enough to reach comfortably.

"I'm getting closer!" she said with determination. "But I need to think of something else."

Ruby looked around the bathroom with her bright, curious eyes. She noticed the thick phone book that Mama used to reach high shelves in the kitchen.

"Maybe I can use that too!" Ruby said, her ears perking up with excitement.

Carefully, Ruby placed the phone book on top of the step stool. She tested it with one paw to make sure it was safe and steady.

"Perfect!" she exclaimed, climbing up her new invention.

Now Ruby could reach the sink perfectly! The faucet was right at the perfect height. She could feel the warm water on her paws and easily reach her toothbrush.

"I did it! I did it!" Ruby sang happily as she squeezed the minty toothpaste onto her brush. The bathroom filled with the fresh smell of mint.

Ruby brushed her teeth carefully, making sure to clean all around. She could see herself in the mirror, smiling with her clean, white teeth.

"Ruby!" Mama called, coming to check on her. "Oh my! What a clever solution you created!"

"I couldn't reach the sink," Ruby explained proudly, "so I made my own special step! I used the step stool AND the phone book together!"

Mama smiled warmly. "That's wonderful thinking, sweetie! You saw a problem and found a creative way to solve it. That's what smart bunnies do!"

Ruby beamed with pride. "When something seems impossible, I just need to think of new ways to try!"

From that day on, Ruby used her special two-step solution every morning and night. She had learned that with creativity and determination, even big problems could have simple solutions.

"Every problem has an answer," Ruby would tell her friends. "You just have to think creatively and try different ideas!"

The End.
