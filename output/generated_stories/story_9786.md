# Gigi's Garden Goal

The morning sun warmed Gigi the giraffe's golden spots as she stretched her long neck high above the backyard fence. The grass felt soft under her hooves, and she could smell the sweet flowers blooming nearby.

"What a beautiful day!" Gigi said to herself, swishing her tail happily.

But then Gigi noticed something that made her frown. The vegetable garden looked messy and wild. Weeds grew everywhere between the tomato plants. The carrots were hidden under tall grass. Even the pretty sunflowers drooped sadly.

"Oh my," Gigi said, touching a wilted lettuce leaf with her nose. "This garden needs help!"

Gigi's friend Benny the bunny hopped over. "Hi Gigi! Want to play tag?"

"I'd love to play," Gigi said thoughtfully, "but look at this poor garden. The plants can't grow properly with all these weeds."

Benny wrinkled his nose. "That looks like a lot of work. It's too hot and dirty."

Gigi took a deep breath and counted to three, just like her mama taught her when she felt overwhelmed. "You're right, it is a big job," she said calmly. "But I think I can make a plan."

Gigi sat down carefully and thought hard. The dirt felt cool in the shade under her belly.

"First," she said out loud, "I'll pull weeds from just one small section. Then I'll water those plants. After a little rest, I'll do another small section."

"That sounds smart," said Benny, impressed.

Gigi started with the corner where the baby carrots grew. She wrapped her lips gently around each weed and pulled slowly. The roots made a satisfying *pop* sound as they came loose from the earth.

After fifteen minutes, Gigi felt tired and sweaty. Her neck ached a little from bending down so much.

"Time for a break," she said to herself. She drank some cool water from the garden hose. The water tasted fresh and made her feel much better.

"I'm proud of you for taking a break," said Benny. "You're being very smart about this."

Gigi smiled. "My mama says it's important to listen to your body and rest when you need to."

After her break, Gigi worked on the tomato section. She hummed a happy song while she worked. The sun felt warm on her back, and she could hear birds chirping in the trees.

When she felt tired again, she stopped and washed her hooves with the garden hose. The cool water felt wonderful on her feet.

"You know what?" said Benny. "This looks like fun. Can I help too?"

"Of course!" Gigi said excitedly. "You could help me water the plants I've already weeded."

Working together, they finished the whole garden by lunchtime. The vegetables stood tall and proud in neat rows. The sunflowers lifted their bright yellow faces toward the sky.

"Wow!" said Mrs. Robin from her nest in the apple tree. "You two did an amazing job! The garden looks beautiful."

Gigi felt proud and happy. Her muscles were tired, but it was a good kind of tired.

"I learned something important today," Gigi told Benny as they admired their work. "Big jobs feel easier when I break them into small pieces and take breaks when I need them."

"And asking for help made it even better," added Benny.

Gigi nodded wisely. "Next time I have a big goal, I'll remember to make a plan, take my time, and listen to my body."

The garden sparkled in the afternoon sun, and Gigi felt wonderful knowing she had helped it grow.

The End.
