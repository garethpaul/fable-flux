# Rocco's Dancing Day

In a cozy garage workshop filled with shiny tools and colorful paint cans, lived a special raccoon named Rocco. Rocco loved music more than anything in the whole world! His fluffy striped tail would wiggle whenever he heard a happy tune.

One sunny morning, Rocco woke up feeling very tired. His little paws were sore from building a birdhouse yesterday, and his tummy felt rumbly and grumbly.

"Oh my," said Rocco, stretching his furry arms. "I don't feel very good today."

Rocco looked around his workshop. Sawdust covered the floor like golden snow, and his tools were scattered everywhere. Even his favorite red toolbox looked messy and sad.

"Maybe some music will help," Rocco thought. He turned on his little radio, and a cheerful song began to play. *Dum-de-dum-dum!*

But instead of dancing like he usually did, Rocco just sat on his workbench and sighed. "I'm too tired to dance," he said softly.

Just then, Rocco's friend Bella the bluebird flew through the open garage door. Her bright blue feathers sparkled in the sunlight.

"Good morning, Rocco!" chirped Bella. "Why do you look so droopy today?"

"I feel yucky," Rocco explained. "My paws hurt, my tummy is hungry, and everything is messy."

Bella tilted her tiny head thoughtfully. "Oh, Rocco! It sounds like you forgot to take care of yourself. When did you last eat a good breakfast?"

Rocco's eyes grew wide. "Um... I can't remember!"

"And when did you last clean your cozy workshop?" asked Bella gently.

"Yesterday... no, maybe the day before..." Rocco mumbled.

Bella smiled kindly. "Taking care of yourself is just as important as taking care of your projects, dear friend. Let's help you feel better!"

First, Rocco washed his little paws with warm, soapy water. The bubbles felt soft and tickly! Then he ate a delicious breakfast of sweet berries and crunchy nuts. His tummy stopped grumbling and started feeling happy.

"Mmm, these berries taste like sunshine!" Rocco said, licking his lips.

Next, Rocco and Bella cleaned the workshop together. They swept up the sawdust, organized the colorful tools, and made everything neat and tidy. The garage smelled fresh and clean, like morning air.

"Now I can see my beautiful workshop again!" Rocco exclaimed, spinning around with joy.

As they finished cleaning, the radio played another happy song. This time, Rocco felt his paws start to tap and his tail begin to wiggle.

"I feel so much better!" Rocco laughed. "Thank you for reminding me to take care of myself, Bella!"

"You're very welcome," chirped Bella. "Now, shall we dance?"

Rocco and Bella danced together in the clean workshop. Rocco's paws felt strong, his tummy felt full and happy, and his heart felt grateful for his kind friend and his wonderful workshop.

*Tap-tap-wiggle-spin!* went Rocco's happy dance.

"I'm grateful for good food, a clean home, and caring for myself," Rocco sang as he danced. "Taking care of me helps me take care of everything else!"

From that day on, Rocco remembered to eat healthy meals, keep his workshop tidy, and rest when he felt tired. And every morning, he would dance a little grateful dance, feeling happy and healthy and ready for new adventures.

The End.
