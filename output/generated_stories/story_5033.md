# Leo's Big Voice

In a cozy basement lab filled with bubbling beakers and colorful bottles, lived a little lion cub named Leo. The lab belonged to Dr. Whiskers, a kind scientist mouse who loved to mix and create wonderful things.

Every day, Leo watched Dr. Whiskers work. The tiny mouse climbed tall ladders to reach high shelves. She mixed purple potions that smelled like grape juice. She stirred green mixtures that sparkled like stars.

But Leo felt too small to help. "I'm just a little cub," he whispered to himself. "My roar is tiny. My paws are small. I can't reach the tall shelves like Dr. Whiskers."

One busy Tuesday morning, Dr. Whiskers was making her special rainbow bubble solution. She needed the blue bottle from the very top shelf. Up, up, up she climbed on her little ladder.

"Almost there," she puffed, stretching her tiny paw.

Suddenly - CRASH! The ladder wobbled and fell! Dr. Whiskers tumbled down, landing safely in a pile of soft cotton balls. But now she couldn't reach the blue bottle at all.

"Oh my," said Dr. Whiskers, rubbing her head. "How will I finish my rainbow bubbles now?"

Leo's heart beat fast. He wanted to help, but he felt scared. "What if I'm too little?" he thought. "What if I can't do it right?"

Then Leo remembered what his mama always said: "Even little lions have big hearts and brave voices."

Leo took a deep breath. His chest puffed out. "Dr. Whiskers!" he called in his strongest voice. "I want to help you!"

Dr. Whiskers looked surprised. "Oh, Leo! That's very kind, but the bottle is so high up."

"I may be small," said Leo, standing tall on his hind legs, "but I'm taller than you! And I have an idea!"

Leo explained his plan. Dr. Whiskers could climb onto his back, and together they could reach the blue bottle. It was a compromise - they would work as a team!

"What a wonderful idea!" said Dr. Whiskers, clapping her tiny paws.

Carefully, Dr. Whiskers climbed onto Leo's fuzzy back. Leo stood very still and very brave. Step by step, he walked to the tall shelf. Dr. Whiskers reached up, up, up and grabbed the blue bottle.

"We did it!" cheered Dr. Whiskers. "Thank you for being so brave and speaking up, Leo!"

Leo's chest swelled with pride. His little roar grew a bit louder. "ROAR! I helped! I was brave!"

Together, they mixed the rainbow bubble solution. When they blew the bubbles, they shimmered with all the colors of the rainbow - red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple!

"You know what, Leo?" said Dr. Whiskers as they watched the beautiful bubbles float around the lab. "Being brave isn't about being big or loud. It's about caring enough to try, even when you feel scared."

Leo nodded, watching a purple bubble land on his nose. "And it's about asking for help and working together!"

From that day on, Leo became Dr. Whiskers' special helper. Whenever he felt scared or small, he remembered to use his brave voice and speak up. He learned that even little cubs could make big differences when they believed in themselves.

The basement lab filled with the sound of happy bubbles popping and Leo's proud little roars. And Dr. Whiskers always smiled, knowing she had the bravest assistant in the whole world.

The End.
