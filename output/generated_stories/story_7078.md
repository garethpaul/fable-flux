# Rocco's Brave Discovery

In a cozy basement lab filled with colorful bottles and bubbling beakers, lived a little raccoon named Rocco. Rocco loved music more than anything in the whole world. He wore tiny round glasses and carried a small tambourine wherever he went.

*Jingle, jangle, shake!* went his tambourine as he danced around the lab each morning.

"Good morning, lab!" Rocco would sing. "What wonderful things will we discover today?"

But there was one big problem. In the darkest corner of the basement sat a mysterious machine covered with a dusty white sheet. Strange humming sounds came from underneath, and sometimes colorful lights flickered around the edges.

*Hummmmm, buzz, whirrrr,* went the hidden machine.

Rocco was very curious about the machine, but he felt scared to look under the sheet. "What if it's something scary?" he whispered to himself. "What if it makes loud noises?"

Every day, Rocco would walk closer and closer to the covered machine, his tambourine shaking nervously in his paws. *Jingle, jingle,* it went, much quieter than usual.

One morning, Rocco's friend Dr. Owl flew down from the ceiling. "Good morning, Rocco! You look worried today."

"I am worried," Rocco admitted. "I want to see what's under that sheet, but I'm too scared. What if I don't like what I find?"

Dr. Owl's big, wise eyes twinkled. "You know, Rocco, being brave doesn't mean you're not scared. Being brave means you do something good even when you feel scared."

"Really?" Rocco asked, his ears perking up.

"Really! And here's a secret," Dr. Owl whispered. "When I feel scared about trying new things, I take three deep breaths. Want to try?"

Rocco nodded. Together, they breathed in slowly through their noses. *Whoooosh.* Then out through their mouths. *Ahhhhhh.* They did this three times.

"I feel a little better," Rocco said, surprised.

"Deep breathing is a healthy habit that helps our bodies feel calm," explained Dr. Owl. "Now, would you like to discover what's under that sheet? I'll be right here with you."

Rocco felt his heart beating fast, but he also felt excited. "Yes! Let's do it together!"

Step by step, Rocco walked toward the mysterious machine. His tambourine jingled softly, like a brave little song. When he reached the sheet, he took three more deep breaths.

"One, two, three!" he counted, and pulled off the sheet.

*WHOOSH!*

Underneath was the most beautiful music machine Rocco had ever seen! It had rainbow keys that glowed and made the most wonderful sounds when touched. Gentle melodies filled the air, and sparkly lights danced all around.

"It's a music maker!" Rocco gasped with joy. "It's not scary at all!"

"You were so brave, Rocco!" cheered Dr. Owl. "You tried something new even though you felt scared."

Rocco began to play the machine, and his tambourine joined in. *Ding, dong, jingle, jangle!* The basement lab filled with the most beautiful music.

"I learned something important today," Rocco said happily. "When I feel scared about trying something new, I can take deep breaths, ask a friend for help, and be brave anyway!"

From that day on, Rocco used his new music machine every morning, and he remembered to take deep breaths whenever he felt nervous about trying new things.

*Jingle, jangle, ding, dong!* went the happy sounds in the basement lab.

The End.
