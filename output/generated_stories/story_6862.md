# Miko's Garden Helper Adventure

Miko the monkey swung onto the sunny balcony garden. His brown fur felt warm in the morning light. He loved visiting this special place high up in the sky.

"Good morning, little plants!" Miko called out cheerfully.

The balcony was full of colorful pots. There were red tomatoes, green lettuce, and purple flowers. But something looked wrong today. Many plants were drooping. Their leaves looked thirsty and sad.

"Oh no!" said Miko. "The plants need help!"

Just then, Mrs. Chen came outside. She was an elderly lady who lived in the apartment. She carried a heavy watering can, but it looked too big for her.

"These poor plants," Mrs. Chen sighed. "I want to water them all, but this can is so heavy. My arms get tired."

Miko's heart felt warm. He wanted to help! He swung down from the railing.

"Mrs. Chen! Mrs. Chen!" Miko chattered excitedly. "I can help you!"

Mrs. Chen smiled when she saw the friendly monkey. "Oh, hello Miko! Would you really like to help me water the garden?"

"Yes! Yes!" Miko bounced up and down. "But how can we do it together?"

Mrs. Chen thought for a moment. Then her eyes twinkled. "I have an idea! You're good at climbing, and I know which plants need water. We can work as a team!"

Miko clapped his hands. "Tell me what to do!"

Mrs. Chen pointed to a small watering cup. "First, can you fill this little cup from the big can? It will be just right for you to carry."

Miko nodded. He carefully dipped the cup into the big watering can. The water made a gentle splashing sound. It felt cool and fresh.

"Perfect!" said Mrs. Chen. "Now, I'll tell you which plants to visit first. Start with the tomatoes in the red pots."

Miko followed her directions. He climbed carefully to each red pot. The water sparkled like tiny diamonds as he poured it slowly. The tomato plants seemed to perk up right away!

"You're doing wonderfully!" Mrs. Chen cheered. "Next, let's water the lettuce in the green pots."

Working together felt amazing. Mrs. Chen would point and give directions. Miko would climb and pour the water. They made a perfect team!

"The purple flowers need water too," Mrs. Chen said. "But be very gentle with these ones."

Miko moved extra carefully to the flower pots. He poured the water slowly and softly. The purple petals seemed to shimmer with happiness.

As they worked, other neighbors came out to watch. "What a helpful monkey!" said Mr. Kim from next door. "You two work so well together!"

Soon, all the plants were watered. The leaves stood up tall and green. The flowers bloomed brighter. The whole balcony garden looked healthy and beautiful.

"We did it!" Miko cheered, doing a little dance.

Mrs. Chen clapped her hands. "We certainly did! Miko, you were such a wonderful helper. By working together and following directions, we saved the whole garden!"

Miko felt proud and happy inside. His chest puffed out with joy. "It was fun to help! Can we do it again tomorrow?"

"Of course!" Mrs. Chen laughed. "Every garden needs good friends to take care of it."

From that day on, Miko came to help every morning. He learned that when friends work together and listen to each other, they can do amazing things. The balcony garden became the most beautiful garden in the whole building.

And Miko discovered that helping others made his heart feel as bright and warm as the sunshine on the garden leaves.

The End.
