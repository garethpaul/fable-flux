# Bolt's Big Helper Mission

Bolt was a shiny blue robot dog who lived in baby Emma's cozy nursery. His LED eyes glowed softly, and his metal tail wagged with gentle whirring sounds. Bolt's special job was helping take care of Emma and keeping the nursery tidy.

Every morning, Bolt would organize Emma's toys, fluff her soft pillows, and make sure everything was perfect. But today was different. Today, Emma had made the BIGGEST mess ever!

Toys were scattered everywhere. Building blocks covered the fluffy pink carpet. Stuffed animals sat in puddles of spilled juice. Books lay open with pages crumpled. The mess was so big that Bolt's sensors started blinking red.

"Oh no!" beeped Bolt in his cheerful robot voice. "This is too much mess for one robot dog!"

Bolt tried to clean up the blocks first. He picked up one block, then two, then three. But there were hundreds of blocks! His battery meter showed he was getting tired.

"Maybe I should give up," Bolt said sadly. His ears drooped down, and his tail stopped wagging.

Just then, Emma crawled over to Bolt. She patted his shiny head with her tiny hand. "Help! Help!" she babbled happily.

Bolt's eyes brightened. "You want to help me, Emma?"

Emma clapped her hands and giggled.

Bolt thought carefully. He took three deep breaths through his air vents to calm his circuits. "Okay, Bolt," he said to himself. "Let's try a different way. Small steps, one at a time."

First, Bolt showed Emma how to put blocks in their special box. Emma picked up one block and dropped it in. CLUNK! She laughed at the sound.

"Good job, Emma!" beeped Bolt encouragingly. "Let's do another one!"

Together, they put away block after block. When Emma got distracted by a butterfly outside the window, Bolt waited patiently. When she crawled away to play, Bolt gently guided her back with soft beeping sounds.

Next, they tackled the stuffed animals. Bolt picked up the big teddy bear while Emma hugged the small bunny. They made a game of it, with Bolt making silly robot sounds that made Emma giggle.

"Beep boop! All animals to the toy chest!" announced Bolt in his funniest voice.

For the spilled juice, Bolt used his special cleaning mode. His belly opened up, and out came a soft cloth. Emma watched with wide eyes as Bolt carefully wiped up every drop.

The books were trickier. Some pages were torn, and Bolt felt worried again. But he remembered his lesson about taking small steps.

"One page at a time," Bolt said calmly. He smoothed each page gently. Emma helped by pointing to the colorful pictures and making happy sounds.

Finally, after what felt like hours, the nursery was clean again! The carpet was soft and pink, the toys were organized, and everything smelled fresh and sweet.

Bolt's battery was low, but his heart circuits felt warm and happy. Emma clapped her hands and said, "Bolt! Bolt!"

"We did it together, Emma!" beeped Bolt proudly. "Even when it seemed too hard, we didn't give up. We took our time, worked as a team, and kept trying!"

Emma crawled over and gave Bolt the biggest hug her little arms could manage. Bolt's tail wagged so fast it made a happy humming sound.

From that day on, whenever there was a big mess or a hard job, Bolt remembered his lesson. Take small steps, stay calm, ask for help, and never give up. With persistence and teamwork, any problem could be solved!

The End.
