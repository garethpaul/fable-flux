# Toby's Clean Lab Adventure

In a cozy basement lab filled with colorful bottles and bubbling beakers, lived a wise old tortoise named Toby. His green shell sparkled clean, and his bright eyes twinkled with kindness. Toby loved making helpful inventions in his special underground home.

One sunny morning, Toby woke up and wrinkled his nose. "Phew! Something smells yucky down here," he said, looking around his messy lab. Empty food containers were scattered everywhere, his sink was full of dirty dishes, and trash overflowed from the corners.

"Oh my," Toby sighed. "I've been so busy inventing that I forgot to keep my lab clean!" His shell felt sticky, and his hands were covered in colorful stains from his experiments.

Just then, Toby heard tiny squeaking sounds. Three little mice peeked out from behind a pile of old newspapers. They looked sad and sniffly.

"Hello there, little friends," Toby said gently. "Are you okay?"

The smallest mouse stepped forward. "We're sorry, Mr. Toby. We came looking for food scraps, but everything smells so bad that it's making us sick. We don't want to bother you, but could you help us?"

Toby's heart filled with understanding. "Of course! Everyone deserves a clean, healthy place to live. Let's work together to fix this!"

First, Toby showed the mice to his special washing station. "The most important thing is to wash our hands and keep ourselves clean," he explained, pumping soap into his green hands. The soap smelled like fresh lemons and made lots of bubbly foam.

"Watch me!" Toby sang a little song: "Scrub, scrub, scrub for twenty counts, wash away all the germs that bounce!" The mice giggled and copied him, their tiny paws getting squeaky clean.

Next, they tackled the mess together. Toby sorted the trash into three colorful bins he had invented. "This blue bin is for things we can recycle, like these plastic bottles," he explained, holding up a clear container. "The green bin is for food scraps that will help plants grow, and the red bin is for things that need to go away."

The mice were amazing helpers! They scurried around, picking up small pieces of trash while Toby used his strong shell to carry the bigger items. Soon, they had three neat piles.

"Now for the fun part!" Toby exclaimed. He filled his sink with warm, soapy water that smelled like vanilla. Together, they washed all the dirty dishes, cups, and beakers. The mice splashed happily in the bubbles while Toby scrubbed everything until it sparkled.

As they worked, Toby learned that the mice were actually very smart and loved science too. "You know," he said thoughtfully, "everyone is different, but we all deserve respect and clean spaces to live in."

When they finished, the lab looked beautiful! The floors gleamed, the air smelled fresh like spring flowers, and everything had its proper place. Toby's shell shined bright green again, and the mice's fur was fluffy and clean.

"Thank you for teaching us about keeping clean and working together," squeaked the mice.

Toby smiled warmly. "And thank you for reminding me that taking care of our space and ourselves is just as important as any invention. From now on, I'll clean up every day!"

From that day forward, Toby and his mouse friends kept the lab spotless together. They washed their hands before every meal, sorted their recycling, and made cleaning feel like a fun game.

The End.
