# Fawn's Clean-Up Song

In a cozy quiet attic above Grandma's house, a gentle deer fawn named Melody loved to play with the old toys and dusty treasures. The attic smelled like old books and wooden boxes, and golden sunbeams danced through the small round window.

Melody had soft brown fur with white spots, and she loved making music with anything she could find. She would tap on tin cans, shake old jars with buttons inside, and hum sweet melodies that echoed softly in the peaceful space.

One sunny morning, Melody climbed up to her favorite attic to play. But when she got there, she wrinkled her little black nose. "Oh my!" she said, looking around. "Everything is so dusty and dirty!"

The toys were covered in thick gray dust. Spider webs hung in the corners like sticky curtains. Old boxes had muddy paw prints on them, and there were crumbs scattered everywhere from her snack yesterday.

Melody felt sad. "I can't play music in here when it's so messy," she sighed. "But this attic is so big, and I'm just one little fawn. How can I clean it all by myself?"

Just then, she heard tiny voices from behind a box. "We can help!" squeaked a family of field mice. "We heard your beautiful music yesterday, and we want to help you!"

"And us too!" chirped some sparrows who flew in through the window. "You always share your space with us. Now we want to help you!"

Melody's heart felt warm and happy. "That's so kind! But we need a plan so everyone can help fairly."

Being a natural leader, Melody thought carefully. "I know! Let's make cleaning into a musical game! We can sing and clean at the same time!"

She began to sing in her sweet voice: "♪ Dust away, dust away, let's clean up today! Sweep and wipe, sweep and wipe, make everything bright! ♪"

The mice scurried around, their tiny paws wiping dust from the small spaces. The sparrows flapped their wings to blow away cobwebs. Melody used her soft tail like a feather duster on the bigger toys.

"♪ Wash our paws, wash our paws, that's the cleaning laws! Clean and bright, clean and bright, everything's just right! ♪" they all sang together.

After they finished each section, Melody made sure everyone washed their paws in a little bowl of soapy water she had brought up. "Clean paws are happy paws!" she giggled, showing everyone how to scrub between their tiny fingers and toes.

The sparrows dipped their beaks in the water and splashed it on their feathers. "This feels so fresh and nice!" they chirped.

Soon, the whole attic sparkled and shined. The toys gleamed, the floor was spotless, and everything smelled fresh and clean.

"Look what we did together!" Melody said proudly. "When we all work as a team and keep ourselves clean while we work, we can do anything!"

The mice and sparrows cheered. "And it was fun because of your cleaning song!"

From that day on, Melody and her friends always remembered to clean up after themselves and wash their paws. They learned that keeping clean wasn't just about looking nice – it kept them healthy and made their special attic a wonderful place for everyone to enjoy.

Every day, they would sing Melody's cleaning song and work together, making sure their beautiful attic stayed sparkling clean for all their musical adventures.

The End.
