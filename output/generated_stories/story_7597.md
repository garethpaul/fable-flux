# Gigi's Bedtime Helper

The soft yellow lamp glowed warmly in the cozy bedroom. Gigi the giraffe stretched her long neck and looked around. Her spotted coat felt cozy in the warm room. The fluffy blue blanket on the bed looked so inviting.

"Time for bed, Gigi," said Mama Giraffe gently. She smoothed the pillow with her hoof.

Gigi yawned a big yawn. Her mouth opened wide like a cave. But then she heard something. A tiny sound. Sniff, sniff, sniff.

"What's that sound?" Gigi asked. She tilted her head to listen better.

The sniffing came from under the bed. Gigi bent her long neck down low. Her big brown eyes looked underneath. There sat a small gray mouse. The mouse had tears on his fuzzy cheeks.

"Hello, little mouse," Gigi said softly. "Why are you crying?"

The mouse looked up with sad, tiny eyes. "I'm Max," he squeaked. "I lost my favorite blankie. It's small and blue with white dots. I can't sleep without it."

Gigi's heart felt warm and caring. She wanted to help her new friend. "Don't worry, Max. I'll help you find it."

"Really?" Max wiped his eyes with his tiny paw. "You would do that for me?"

"Of course!" Gigi smiled. "Friends help friends."

Gigi used her long neck like a periscope. She looked on top of the tall dresser. No blankie there. She looked behind the rocking chair. The chair was wooden and smooth. Still no blankie.

"Let's check the toy box," Gigi suggested. The toy box was big and red. It had golden hinges that squeaked.

Max climbed onto Gigi's head. He felt safe up there. Together they looked inside the toy box. There were stuffed animals, blocks, and books. But no little blue blankie.

"I'll never find it," Max said sadly. His whiskers drooped down.

Gigi thought hard. She was very thoughtful. "Where did you last see your blankie?"

"I was playing by the window," Max remembered. "The curtains were blowing in the breeze."

Gigi walked to the window. The curtains were long and white. They moved gently like dancing ghosts. Gigi pushed them aside with her nose. Her nose felt the soft fabric.

"Look!" Max squeaked with joy. There was the little blue blankie with white dots. It had fallen behind the curtain.

"We found it!" Gigi said happily. She carefully picked up the blankie with her teeth. It was soft and smelled like flowers.

Max hugged his blankie tight. "Thank you, Gigi! You're the best friend ever!"

Gigi felt proud and happy inside. Her heart was full of joy. "I'm glad I could help you, Max."

"Can we be bedtime friends?" Max asked. "I promise to be quiet during your sleep."

"I would love that," Gigi said. "Good friends share their rooms and their kindness."

Mama Giraffe watched from the doorway. She smiled big. "I'm proud of you, Gigi. You showed true friendship tonight."

Max curled up in his tiny blankie near Gigi's pillow. Gigi lay down on her soft bed. The room felt peaceful and warm.

"Good night, Max," Gigi whispered.

"Good night, Gigi. Thank you for being so kind," Max whispered back.

As Gigi closed her eyes, she felt wonderful. Helping others made her heart feel light and happy. She learned that being a good friend means caring about others and helping when they need it.

The yellow lamp dimmed. The cozy bedroom became quiet and peaceful. Two new friends slept soundly together.

The End.
