# Benny Bear and the Shared Sunflower Seeds

Benny was a fluffy brown bear cub with soft, round ears. He lived on a sunny balcony garden filled with colorful flowers. The sweet smell of roses tickled his nose. Green leaves rustled gently in the warm breeze.

Every day, Benny loved to share sunflower seeds with his friends. He had a special blue bowl filled with crunchy, tasty seeds. The sparrows chirped happily when they saw him coming. The squirrels chattered with joy.

But this morning felt different. Benny walked to his seed bowl with excited paws. "Time for breakfast, friends!" he called out cheerfully.

But the bowl was empty! Not one single seed remained. Benny's brown eyes grew wide with surprise. "Oh no! Where did all the seeds go?" he wondered aloud.

Soon, his friends arrived. Chirpy the sparrow fluttered down first. "Good morning, Benny! Where are our seeds?" she asked, tilting her tiny head.

Squeaky the squirrel scampered up next. His bushy tail twitched nervously. "I'm so hungry," he said sadly. "Did you forget about us?"

Benny felt his heart sink. His friends looked disappointed. This wasn't fair at all! Someone had taken all the seeds without sharing.

"This isn't right," Benny said firmly. "Let's solve this mystery together!"

Benny put on his thinking cap. He looked around the balcony garden carefully. Near the tomato plants, he spotted tiny seed shells scattered on the ground. The shells made a crunchy sound under his paws.

"Look!" Benny pointed with his furry paw. "Someone was eating here!"

Following the trail of shells, they discovered Nibbles the chipmunk hiding behind the marigolds. His cheeks were puffed out like two fuzzy balloons. Seed shells stuck to his whiskers.

"Nibbles!" Benny called gently. "Did you eat all our seeds?"

Nibbles looked embarrassed. His ears drooped low. "I was so hungry," he whispered. "I couldn't help myself. I'm sorry."

Benny felt upset, but he also felt sorry for Nibbles. "Taking all the seeds without asking wasn't fair to our friends," Benny explained kindly. "But I understand you were hungry."

Chirpy nodded her head. "We all get hungry sometimes."

Squeaky's tail stopped twitching. "Maybe we can fix this together!"

Benny smiled warmly. "I have an idea! Let's make a sharing schedule. Everyone gets a turn, and no one goes hungry."

They all sat in a circle on the warm wooden deck. Benny drew a simple chart with colorful chalk. "Monday for Chirpy, Tuesday for Squeaky, Wednesday for Nibbles," he said, pointing to each day.

"And every day, we share some seeds together!" added Chirpy excitedly.

Nibbles smiled for the first time. "That sounds fair! I promise to wait my turn."

Benny ran inside and brought back a fresh bag of sunflower seeds. The seeds rattled like tiny maracas in the paper bag. He poured them carefully into the blue bowl.

"Let's start fresh," Benny said happily. "Today, we all share together!"

All the friends munched on the crunchy seeds. The balcony garden filled with happy sounds - chirping, chattering, and gentle munching. The afternoon sun warmed their backs like a cozy blanket.

From that day on, everyone followed their fair sharing schedule. Nibbles learned that taking without asking hurts friends. Benny learned that solving problems together makes everyone happy.

The balcony garden became an even more joyful place, where fairness and friendship bloomed like the beautiful flowers all around them.

The End.
