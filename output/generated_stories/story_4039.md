# Melody's Musical Gift

On a peaceful front porch covered with soft morning sunlight, lived a beautiful little bird named Melody. Her feathers sparkled like golden sunshine, and she had the most wonderful voice in the whole neighborhood. Every morning, Melody would perch on the white porch railing and sing the most beautiful songs.

"La-la-la! Tweet-tweet-chirp!" Melody sang happily, watching the warm sun peek through the green leaves above.

But Melody had a problem. She loved her beautiful voice so much that she didn't want to share her songs with anyone else. When other birds came to listen, she would fly away to a secret spot where only she could hear her music.

One sunny Tuesday morning, three little birds landed on the porch steps. There was Ruby the robin with her bright red chest, Charlie the chickadee with his tiny black cap, and Sunny the canary with her bright yellow feathers.

"Good morning, Melody!" chirped Ruby. "We heard your beautiful singing from far away. Could you please sing for us?"

Melody puffed up her chest and shook her head. "No, no, no! My songs are special. They're just for me!" She flew to the corner of the porch where a hanging flower basket created a cozy hiding spot.

The three little birds looked sad. Their shoulders drooped, and Charlie's tiny voice whispered, "We just wanted to hear something pretty."

From her hiding spot, Melody could see how disappointed her friends looked. She noticed Ruby's head hanging low, and Sunny's bright feathers seemed less shiny. Charlie was so quiet, he didn't make any sound at all.

Melody felt a funny feeling in her tummy - not a good feeling, but a heavy, uncomfortable one. She realized she felt sad too when she saw her friends looking so unhappy.

"Maybe," Melody thought to herself, "sharing my songs might make everyone feel better, including me."

Slowly, Melody flew back to the porch railing. She took a deep breath and looked at her three friends.

"I'm sorry," Melody said softly. "I was keeping my songs all to myself, and that wasn't very kind. Would you still like to hear me sing?"

Ruby, Charlie, and Sunny looked up with bright, hopeful eyes. "Oh yes, please!" they said together.

Melody opened her beak and began to sing the most beautiful morning song. "Good morning, sunshine! Good morning, trees! Good morning, flowers dancing in the breeze!"

The three little birds clapped their wings with joy. The music filled the peaceful porch with happiness, and even the flowers seemed to dance along.

"That was wonderful!" chirped Sunny. "Your voice makes everything feel so happy and bright!"

"Could we sing together?" asked Charlie shyly.

Melody's heart felt warm and full. "Yes! Let's all sing together!"

Soon, all four birds were singing in harmony on the peaceful front porch. Their voices blended together like a beautiful rainbow of sound. Melody discovered that sharing her music made it even more special and beautiful.

From that day on, Melody sang every morning with her friends. She learned that when we share the things we love, we don't lose them - we make them even better. The peaceful front porch became filled with friendship, music, and joy every single day.

"Sharing makes everything more wonderful!" Melody sang happily, surrounded by her dear friends.

The End.
