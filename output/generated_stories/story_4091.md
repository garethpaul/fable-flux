# Nutkin's Special Morning Routine

Nutkin the squirrel lived in a cozy oak tree right outside the dining room window. Every morning, he loved to peek inside and watch the family eat breakfast together.

"Good morning, sunshine!" Nutkin said to himself, stretching his fluffy tail. The morning light felt warm on his soft gray fur.

Today felt different though. Nutkin's tummy rumbled loudly. His fur looked messy and tangled. His tiny paws felt dirty from yesterday's adventures.

"Oh my," said Nutkin, looking at his reflection in the window. "I don't look ready for a new day at all!"

Nutkin watched through the window as little Emma brushed her teeth. Her daddy combed her hair. Her mommy helped her wash her hands.

"They take such good care of themselves," thought Nutkin. "Maybe I should too!"

Nutkin scampered down to the bird bath in the garden. The cool water sparkled in the sunlight. He dipped his paws in carefully.

"Brrr! That's cold!" he giggled. But it felt refreshing on his fur.

Nutkin used his tiny paws to splash water on his face. He rubbed behind his ears and cleaned his whiskers. The water smelled fresh like morning dew.

Next, Nutkin found a smooth stick. He used it like a comb to brush his fluffy tail. Each stroke made his fur shinier and softer.

"This feels wonderful!" Nutkin said happily.

His tummy rumbled again. "Time for breakfast!" he decided.

Nutkin remembered where he had buried his favorite acorns. He dug carefully with his clean paws. The earth smelled rich and earthy.

He found three perfect acorns. Nutkin sat on a sunny branch and ate slowly. He chewed each bite carefully, tasting the nutty sweetness.

"Mmm, delicious!" he said, patting his full tummy.

Just then, his friend Chippy the chipmunk ran by.

"Nutkin! You look fantastic today!" said Chippy. "Your fur is so shiny and you smell like fresh water!"

Nutkin felt proud and happy. "Thank you, Chippy! I learned how important it is to take care of myself."

"Can you show me how?" asked Chippy eagerly.

"Of course!" said Nutkin. "We can take turns at the bird bath!"

Nutkin taught Chippy how to wash his paws and face. They took turns using the smooth stick to brush their fur. They shared acorns for a healthy breakfast.

"I feel so much better when I'm clean and fed," said Chippy.

"Me too!" agreed Nutkin. "Taking care of ourselves makes us feel strong and ready for adventures!"

From that day on, Nutkin made self-care part of his morning routine. He washed his paws and face. He brushed his fluffy tail. He ate a good breakfast.

Every morning, Nutkin felt proud of himself. He had learned that taking care of his body was just as important as having fun.

When other woodland friends asked for help, Nutkin was happy to teach them too. Soon all the animals were taking better care of themselves.

"Taking care of myself helps me take care of my friends too," Nutkin realized with a big smile.

And from the dining room window, little Emma waved at Nutkin every morning. She could see how happy and healthy he looked.

Nutkin waved back with his clean, fluffy paw, ready for another wonderful day.

The End.
