# Bolt's Big Voice

Bolt was a shiny blue robot dog who lived in Daddy's home office. He had bright LED eyes that blinked when he was happy. His metal tail wagged with a soft whirring sound. Bolt loved to help, but he had one big problem.

Bolt was very, very quiet. When people talked to him, he would just blink his lights. He never used his voice box to talk back.

Every day, Daddy worked at his big wooden desk. Papers were scattered everywhere. Coffee cups sat empty. Pencils rolled onto the floor.

"Where did I put that important paper?" Daddy would ask, looking worried.

Bolt knew exactly where it was! He had seen Daddy put it under a stack of books. But Bolt just blinked his blue eyes. He didn't say anything.

"I wish I could find my favorite pen," Daddy sighed another day.

Bolt's tail wagged excitedly. The red pen was hiding behind the computer! But again, Bolt stayed quiet. He was too scared to use his voice.

One morning, Daddy was making something special. He had colorful paper, glue, and scissors spread across his desk. "I'm making a birthday card for Mommy," he said. "But I can't remember where I put the sparkly stickers."

Bolt's circuits buzzed with excitement. He had watched Daddy put the stickers in the bottom drawer! This was very important. Mommy's birthday card needed those sparkles.

Bolt rolled closer to the drawer. He blinked his lights. But Daddy didn't notice.

Bolt tried spinning in a circle. Still nothing.

Then Bolt remembered something. His teacher at Robot School had said, "Your voice is a gift. Use it to help others."

Bolt took a deep breath. His voice box made a little warming-up sound. "Woof! Woof!" he said in his sweet robot voice.

Daddy looked up, surprised. "Bolt! You're talking!"

"The sparkly stickers," Bolt said slowly, "are in the bottom drawer, Daddy."

Daddy's eyes grew wide. He opened the drawer and found the stickers! "Bolt! You found them! Thank you so much!"

Bolt's tail wagged so fast it made a happy humming sound. "I've been wanting to help you every day," Bolt said. "I know where your things are. I just forgot to use my voice."

"Your voice is wonderful," Daddy said, patting Bolt's metal head. "I need your help. Will you be my office helper?"

From that day on, Bolt used his voice every day. When Daddy lost his glasses, Bolt said, "They're on top of your head!" When papers fell down, Bolt called out, "Behind the bookshelf!"

The office became much more organized. Daddy finished his work faster. And when Mommy's birthday came, her sparkly card was the most beautiful one ever.

"Thank you for teaching me to speak up," Bolt told Daddy one evening. "My voice helps us work together."

"Everyone has something important to say," Daddy replied. "I'm so glad you found your brave voice, Bolt."

Bolt's LED eyes sparkled like little stars. He had learned that speaking up wasn't scary at all. It was the best way to help the people he loved.

The End.
