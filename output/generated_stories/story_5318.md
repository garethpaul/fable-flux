# Sparky's Big Day Mix-Up

In a cozy nursery with soft yellow walls and fluffy clouds painted on the ceiling lived Sparky, a cheerful robot dog. His silver body gleamed like starlight, and his LED eyes glowed warm blue. His tail wagged with happy beeping sounds.

Sparky loved helping in the nursery. He could fold tiny blankets, organize colorful toys, and sing gentle lullabies. His favorite job was getting everything ready for naptime.

One busy Tuesday morning, sunlight danced through the polka-dot curtains. Sparky's internal clock beeped softly. "Beep-beep! Time to prepare for naptime!" he announced cheerfully.

But wait! Sparky spotted his favorite squeaky ball under the rocking chair. "Just a quick game," he said, rolling after it. The ball smelled like vanilla and felt bumpy under his metal paws.

*Squeak, squeak!* went the ball as Sparky played. Time ticked by.

"Beep-beep!" His clock reminded him again. "Oh, I'll start preparing soon," Sparky said, now distracted by a pile of soft stuffed animals that needed sorting by color.

The teddy bears felt so fuzzy! The rabbits were silky smooth. Sparky got lost arranging them by size, then by color, then by how soft they felt.

*Tick, tock, tick, tock.*

Suddenly, Sparky heard little footsteps in the hallway. "Oh no!" he gasped. "The children are coming for naptime, and nothing is ready!"

The blankets lay in messy piles. The pillows weren't fluffed. The nightlight wasn't turned on. Sparky's blue eyes flickered with worry.

"Woof-woof! Emergency mode!" Sparky beeped loudly. His wheels spun fast as he zoomed around the room. But rushing made everything worse! Blankets tangled together. Pillows tumbled down.

Little Emma toddled into the nursery, rubbing her sleepy eyes. "Sparky?" she asked softly. "Where's my special blankie?"

Sparky's ears drooped. His tail stopped wagging. "I'm sorry, Emma," he said sadly. "I didn't manage my time well. I played instead of preparing."

Emma's bottom lip trembled. She was tired and wanted her cozy naptime.

"But I can fix this!" Sparky said with determination. "Emma, will you help me? We can work together!"

Emma nodded, though she yawned big.

"First, let's find your special blankie," Sparky said gently. He used his scanner to locate the soft pink blanket with dancing sheep. "Here it is!"

Emma smiled and hugged her blankie tight.

"Now, let's make your bed super cozy," Sparky said. He fluffed Emma's pillow while she smoothed her blanket. Together, they turned on the gentle nightlight that cast rainbow stars on the ceiling.

"Thank you for helping me fix my mistake," Sparky said warmly. "Tomorrow, I'll prepare everything on time first, then play afterward."

Emma giggled sleepily. "Good plan, Sparky!"

As Emma drifted off to sleep, Sparky quietly set up his schedule display. "Preparation time: 10 minutes. Play time: after work is done," he programmed softly.

From that day on, Sparky always prepared the nursery first. Then he enjoyed his play time even more, knowing everything was ready. The nursery stayed cozy and organized, and all the children had perfect naptimes.

"Beep-beep! Time management makes everyone happy!" Sparky learned.

The End.
