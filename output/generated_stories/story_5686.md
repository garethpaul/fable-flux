# Polly's Clean Workspace Discovery

Polly the parrot loved spending time in Daddy's home office. She had bright green feathers, a cheerful yellow beak, and the most curious eyes you ever did see. Every morning, she would perch on her special stand next to Daddy's big wooden desk.

"Good morning, Polly!" Daddy would say, gently scratching behind her feathers. "Ready for another day of work together?"

"Ready! Ready!" Polly would squawk happily, fluffing her beautiful feathers.

But one Tuesday morning, something felt different. Polly noticed a strange, yucky smell in the office. She wrinkled her little beak and looked around with her bright, curious eyes.

"Sniff, sniff," went Polly, turning her head this way and that. "What's that icky smell?"

The smell seemed to be coming from somewhere near her perch. Polly used her clever thinking skills to investigate. First, she looked up at the ceiling. No smell there. Then she looked at the clean bookshelf. The books smelled like paper - nice and fresh.

"Hmm," thought Polly, tapping her orange foot on her perch. "Where could this yucky smell be coming from?"

She looked down and gasped! Her food bowl was covered in old, moldy seeds. Some water had spilled and made everything soggy and gross. Green fuzzy mold was growing on the seeds, and tiny bugs were crawling around.

"Oh no!" squawked Polly. "My food bowl is very, very dirty! That's where the bad smell is coming from!"

Daddy looked over from his computer. "What's wrong, Polly?"

"Dirty bowl! Dirty bowl!" Polly called out, pointing with her wing toward the messy food area.

Daddy came over and saw the problem right away. "Oh my! You're absolutely right, Polly. Your food area is very dirty. That's not healthy for you at all!"

Together, they cleaned everything up. Daddy threw away all the old, moldy food. He washed Polly's bowl with warm, soapy water until it sparkled like new. The water dish got scrubbed clean too, and fresh, clear water was poured in.

"Clean is good!" chirped Polly, watching everything get nice and tidy.

"That's right," said Daddy, putting fresh, healthy seeds in the sparkling bowl. "Clean food and clean water keep us healthy and strong."

Polly discovered that when her eating area was clean, the food tasted so much better! The fresh seeds were crunchy and delicious. The clean water was cool and refreshing.

"Mmm! Tasty!" Polly said, happily munching her clean breakfast.

From that day on, Polly made sure to tell Daddy whenever her food area needed cleaning. She learned that keeping things clean wasn't just about looking nice - it was about staying healthy too!

"Clean every day keeps the germs away!" Polly would sing proudly.

Daddy was so proud of his smart parrot. "You used your thinking skills to solve a problem, Polly. You discovered something important about staying healthy!"

Every morning now, Polly would check her food area first thing. If it looked dirty, she would call out "Clean time!" and Daddy would help her make everything fresh and tidy again.

The office smelled wonderful now - like fresh air and clean spaces. Polly felt happy and healthy, and she had learned that being clean was one of the best ways to take care of herself.

"Smart and clean!" Polly would squawk cheerfully. "That's the way to be!"

The End.
