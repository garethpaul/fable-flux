# Gigi's Big Mistake

Gigi the Giraffe loved helping Mama work in the home office. The room smelled like fresh coffee and had soft sunlight streaming through the big window. Gigi's long neck made it easy to see everything on Mama's tall desk.

"Good morning, Gigi," said Mama, settling into her chair. "I have an important video call today. Can you be my quiet helper?"

"Yes, Mama!" Gigi whispered, her brown eyes sparkling. "I'll be super quiet like a mouse."

Mama smiled and patted Gigi's spotted head. "Thank you, sweetie. I'm presenting my special project to my boss today."

While Mama prepared her papers, Gigi noticed a beautiful plant on the windowsill. Its green leaves sparkled with tiny water drops. The plant looked so pretty in its white pot.

"I want to help make everything perfect for Mama," Gigi thought. She stretched her long neck toward the plant. "Maybe I should move this closer so Mama can see how nice it looks."

Gigi gently nudged the pot with her nose. But her neck was longer than she thought! The pot wobbled... and wobbled... and CRASH! It fell to the floor.

Dirt scattered everywhere. The pretty plant lay on its side. Water made a big puddle on the carpet.

"Oh no!" gasped Gigi. Her heart felt heavy and sad. "What did I do?"

Mama turned around quickly. "Gigi! What happened?"

Gigi's long neck drooped down low. She felt tears in her eyes. "I... I wanted to help make things pretty for your meeting. But I made a big mess instead."

Mama looked at the broken pot and the sad little plant. Then she looked at Gigi's worried face.

"I'm so sorry, Mama," Gigi said in a small voice. "I should have asked first. I made a mistake."

Mama knelt down and hugged Gigi's long neck. "Thank you for telling me the truth, sweetheart. That was very brave."

"But your meeting!" Gigi sniffled. "Everything is messy now!"

"You know what?" said Mama gently. "Mistakes happen. What matters most is that you're honest and you want to make things right. Can you help me clean up?"

Gigi nodded eagerly. "Yes! I want to fix my mistake!"

Together, they carefully picked up the plant and put it in a new pot. Gigi held the dustpan while Mama swept up the dirt. They used soft towels to dry the carpet.

"The plant looks happy again," said Gigi, watching Mama water it gently.

"It does," agreed Mama. "And you know what makes me happy? That you told me the truth and helped fix everything."

When Mama's video call started, Gigi sat quietly in the corner. She felt proud that she had been honest about her mistake. The room looked neat and clean again.

After the meeting, Mama gave Gigi a big hug. "My boss loved my presentation! And I'm so proud of you for making things right."

"Really?" asked Gigi, her eyes bright.

"Really," said Mama. "When we make mistakes, the best thing to do is tell the truth and help fix them. That's what good friends do."

Gigi smiled and nuzzled Mama's cheek. "I learned something important today. Next time I want to help, I'll ask first!"

"That sounds perfect," said Mama. "And Gigi? You're still my very best office helper."

From that day on, Gigi always asked before touching things in the office. And whenever she made a mistake, she remembered to be honest and help make it right.

The End.
