# Melody's Helpful Day

In a cozy guest room with soft yellow walls and a big fluffy bed, lived a little singing bird named Melody. She had bright blue feathers that sparkled in the morning sunlight, and she loved to wake up each day with a cheerful song.

"Tweet-tweet-tweetle-dee! Good morning, world!" Melody sang as she stretched her tiny wings.

But this morning was different. As Melody looked around the guest room, she noticed something was wrong. The room looked messy and sad. Dust covered the windowsill where she liked to perch, crumbs were scattered on the carpet, and the pretty flowers in the vase had wilted and turned brown.

"Oh my!" chirped Melody. "Grandma Rose is coming to visit today, and this room doesn't look welcoming at all!"

Melody knew that Grandma Rose was very special to the family who lived in this house. She was old and moved slowly, and she always brought the most delicious homemade cookies that smelled like cinnamon and love.

"I want to help make this room perfect for Grandma Rose," Melody decided. "She deserves a beautiful, clean place to rest."

First, Melody fluttered to the dusty windowsill. Using her soft wing feathers like a tiny duster, she swept away all the dust. "Whoosh, whoosh!" went her wings. The windowsill began to shine in the golden sunlight.

Next, she saw the scattered crumbs on the carpet. "These crumbs might make Grandma Rose slip!" worried Melody. She carefully picked up each crumb with her small beak and dropped them into the wastebasket. It was hard work, but Melody felt happy helping.

Then Melody noticed the wilted flowers. "Poor flowers," she sang softly. "You need fresh water to feel better." She flew to the bathroom and dipped her beak into a cup of cool water. Drop by drop, she carried water back to the thirsty flowers. After many trips, the flowers began to perk up and smell sweet again.

Just as Melody finished her cleaning, she heard footsteps in the hallway. The door opened, and there stood a kind lady with silver hair and twinkling eyes.

"Oh my goodness!" exclaimed Grandma Rose. "This room is absolutely beautiful! It's so clean and fresh, and these flowers smell wonderful!"

Melody felt warm and proud inside her little chest. She had worked hard, and now Grandma Rose had a lovely place to stay.

"Tweet-tweet-tweetle-dee!" Melody sang her happiest song.

Grandma Rose looked around and spotted the little blue bird. "Did you help make this room so special?" she asked with a gentle smile.

Melody nodded her tiny head and sang even louder. Grandma Rose reached into her bag and pulled out a small piece of her famous cookie.

"This is for you, little helper," said Grandma Rose, placing the cookie on the windowsill. "Thank you for being so thoughtful and kind."

As Melody nibbled the delicious cookie, she felt the best feeling in the world. She had helped someone she cared about, and it made her heart sing louder than her voice ever could.

From that day on, Melody always looked for ways to help others, because she learned that helping makes everyone feel happy – especially the helper!

The End.
