# Ping the Panda Learns to Share

Ping the panda loved bath time! Every morning, he would waddle into the cozy bathroom with his fluffy black and white fur bouncing. The bathroom had bright yellow walls, a big white bathtub, and shelves full of colorful bottles and towels.

"Time for my bath!" Ping would say happily, turning on the warm water. Splash, splash, splash! He loved the sound of water filling the tub.

But one morning, something was different. When Ping pushed open the bathroom door, he saw a small green turtle sitting by the sink.

"Oh!" said Ping, surprised. "Who are you?"

The little turtle looked up with big, worried eyes. "I'm Shelly," she whispered. "I'm new here, and I really need to brush my teeth. But I don't want to bother you."

Ping noticed that Shelly looked very different from him. She had a hard green shell instead of soft fur, and she moved much more slowly. Some of the other animals had told Ping that turtles were "weird" because they were so different.

"This is MY bathroom time," Ping said, not thinking about Shelly's feelings. "You'll have to wait until I'm done with my bath."

Shelly's eyes filled with tears. "But my teeth feel yucky, and my tummy hurts because I haven't brushed them," she said softly. "Could I please just brush quickly?"

Ping saw how sad Shelly looked. Her little flippers were shaking, and she kept touching her mouth like it really hurt. He remembered when his own teeth felt yucky - it was not fun at all!

"Hmm," Ping thought to himself. "Maybe I should think about how Shelly feels."

He looked at the big bathroom. There was plenty of room for both of them! The sink was on one side, and the bathtub was on the other side.

"You know what, Shelly?" Ping said with a warm smile. "We can both use the bathroom together! You can brush your teeth at the sink while I take my bath. There's room for both of us!"

Shelly's face lit up like sunshine. "Really? Thank you so much, Ping!"

As Shelly carefully climbed up to the sink and began brushing her teeth with her tiny toothbrush, Ping climbed into his warm, bubbly bath. The bathroom filled with happy sounds - Ping splashing and singing, and Shelly humming while she brushed.

"Your shell is such a pretty green color," Ping said, noticing how it sparkled in the morning light.

"And your fur looks so soft and fluffy!" Shelly replied. "We're different, but that makes us special!"

They both giggled and continued their morning routines. Ping realized that sharing the bathroom was actually more fun than being alone. They could talk and laugh together!

When they were both clean and fresh, they dried off with their towels.

"Thank you for sharing with me, Ping," said Shelly. "You're very kind."

"Thank you for teaching me that being different is wonderful," said Ping. "And that sharing makes everything better!"

From that day on, Ping and Shelly shared the bathroom every morning. They learned that respecting others and being kind made them both feel happy. They also discovered that celebrating their differences made their friendship extra special.

And they both always remembered to keep their teeth nice and clean!

The End.
