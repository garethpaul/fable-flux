# Rosie Rabbit's Rainbow Garden

Rosie Rabbit lived on a sunny balcony garden with her family. She loved the smell of fresh herbs and the feel of soft soil between her paws. But there was one big problem - all the plants looked the same! Green, green, everywhere green.

"I wish our garden had more colors," Rosie sighed, twitching her pink nose. "It's so plain and boring."

Her mama rabbit hopped over, her ears flopping gently. "What's wrong, little bunny?"

"Mama, our garden only has green plants. I want to see red and yellow and purple!" Rosie said, pointing her small paw around the balcony.

Mama Rabbit smiled warmly. "That sounds wonderful, Rosie. But remember, we need to talk nicely to our plant friends first. They've been growing here for a long time."

Rosie thought about this. She walked over to the big tomato plant and spoke softly. "Hello, Mr. Tomato. You're very nice and green, but could you help me make our garden more colorful?"

The tomato plant rustled in the breeze, as if saying yes!

"I have an idea!" Rosie exclaimed, her cotton-tail wiggling with excitement. "I'll be creative and find new ways to add colors!"

First, Rosie took a little rest in the shade. Mama always said taking breaks was important for thinking clearly. She drank some cool water and felt much better.

Then Rosie hopped to her art box and pulled out colorful ribbons. "I can tie these gently on the plant stems!" She carefully wrapped bright red ribbons around the tomato cages and yellow ones around the herb pots.

Next, she found her collection of smooth, painted rocks. There were purple ones, orange ones, and sparkly blue ones! Rosie placed them carefully around each plant, making sure not to hurt any roots.

"This is fun!" she giggled, her whiskers twitching with joy.

But Rosie wanted even more colors. She remembered her friend Carlos the Cardinal, who had beautiful red feathers, and Luna the Butterfly, who had orange and black wings.

"Maybe I can ask my friends to visit!" Rosie said.

She hopped to the edge of the balcony and called gently, "Carlos! Luna! Would you like to visit my garden? I'm making it more colorful!"

Soon, Carlos flew down with his bright red feathers gleaming in the sunshine. Luna fluttered over with her gorgeous orange wings dancing in the air.

"Wow, Rosie!" chirped Carlos. "Your ribbon idea is so creative!"

"And these painted rocks are beautiful!" added Luna, landing softly on a purple stone.

As they played together, something magical happened. The tomato plant began growing tiny green buds that would soon become red tomatoes! The marigold seeds Rosie had planted weeks ago started peeking through the soil with bright orange tops!

"Look!" Rosie squeaked with delight. "The plants are growing their own colors too!"

Mama Rabbit hopped over and hugged Rosie tight. "You used your creativity, took good care of yourself, spoke kindly to everyone, and helped make our garden beautiful. I'm so proud of you!"

Rosie looked around at her rainbow garden - red ribbons dancing, colorful rocks sparkling, friends with different colored feathers and wings, and plants beginning to bloom in many colors.

"Being creative and kind made everything more beautiful," Rosie said happily. "And it's even better when friends help!"

From that day on, Rosie's balcony garden was the most colorful, joyful place, filled with creativity, friendship, and love.

The End.
