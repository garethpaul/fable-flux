# Melody's Pantry Kindness

In the cozy pantry of a warm farmhouse, a little yellow bird named Melody perched on a high shelf. She loved to sing beautiful songs that echoed through the room filled with jars, boxes, and bags of food.

"Tweet-tweet-tra-la-la!" Melody sang happily. Her voice bounced off the glass jars and made the sweetest sounds.

But today, something was different. Down below on the floor, she heard tiny squeaking sounds. Melody stopped singing and listened carefully.

"Squeak... squeak... squeak..."

Melody fluttered down from her shelf to investigate. Behind a big bag of flour, she found three baby mice huddled together. They looked very hungry and sad.

"Oh my!" chirped Melody softly. "What's wrong, little ones?"

The smallest mouse looked up with big, worried eyes. "We can't reach any food," he squeaked. "Everything is too high up, and we're so very hungry."

Melody's heart filled with compassion. She wanted to help these little creatures right away. But how could a small bird help mice reach the tall shelves?

"Hmm," Melody thought out loud, tapping her tiny orange beak. Then her eyes sparkled with an idea! "I know! I can fly up and bring food down to you!"

Melody flew up to a shelf where crackers sat in an open box. She carefully picked up small crumbs in her beak and flew back down.

"Here you go!" she sang cheerfully, dropping the crumbs near the mice.

The baby mice's eyes grew wide with joy. "Thank you!" they squeaked together.

But Melody noticed there were only enough crumbs for two mice. The third little mouse stepped back politely.

"You two eat first," said the third mouse kindly. "I can wait my turn."

Melody was impressed by the mouse's thoughtfulness. "What a kind thing to do!" she chirped. "Let me get more food right away!"

She flew back up and gathered more crumbs, making sure each mouse had plenty to eat. Back and forth she went, her little wings working hard.

"This is fun!" Melody sang as she worked. "Let's take turns! First you eat, then I'll rest, then I'll get more food!"

The mice giggled at Melody's cheerful song. They took turns eating the delicious crumbs while Melody rested her wings.

When everyone had eaten enough, the grateful mice looked up at their new friend.

"Thank you so much, Melody," said the first mouse. "You shared your flying skills with us."

"And you taught us about taking turns," added the second mouse.

"You have the kindest heart," squeaked the third mouse. "We were so hungry and scared, but you helped us feel safe and full."

Melody's feathers puffed up with happiness. Helping others made her feel warm and joyful inside.

"You know what?" Melody chirped excitedly. "From now on, let's meet here every day! I'll bring food down, and you can tell me stories from around the farmhouse!"

The mice clapped their tiny paws together. "What a wonderful idea!"

From that day forward, the pantry became a place of friendship and sharing. Melody learned that using her special talents to help others made her songs even more beautiful. And the mice learned that kindness and taking turns made everything better.

Every morning, Melody's sweet songs filled the pantry as she shared food and friendship with her new friends.

The End.
