# Kody's Grateful Discovery

In a cozy home office filled with books and a big wooden desk, lived a calm koala named Kody. The office had tall shelves, a soft reading chair, and warm sunlight that streamed through the window each morning.

Kody loved his quiet home office. He spent his days reading picture books and taking peaceful naps on the fuzzy carpet. But lately, Kody felt a little sad.

"I wish I had more exciting things to do," Kody sighed, looking around the familiar room. "This place is so boring. I want something new and different!"

Just then, Kody heard a gentle tapping sound. *Tap, tap, tap.* He looked around, his round ears twitching with curiosity.

"What's that noise?" Kody wondered aloud.

The tapping came again. *Tap, tap, tap.* This time, Kody noticed it was coming from the window. He climbed onto the desk chair and peered outside.

There, on the windowsill, sat a tiny red bird. The bird was pecking at the glass with its small beak.

"Hello there, little bird," Kody said softly. "Are you trying to come inside?"

The bird chirped back, "Tweet, tweet! I'm looking for a safe place to rest. It's getting cold outside, and I'm very tired."

Kody's heart filled with warmth. He had been so focused on wanting something new that he hadn't noticed someone who needed help right outside his window.

"Oh my," Kody said. "You must be freezing! Let me open the window for you."

Carefully, Kody turned the window latch. The cool air rushed in, along with the grateful little bird.

"Thank you so much!" chirped the bird, fluffing her red feathers. "My name is Ruby. I've been flying for hours looking for shelter."

"I'm Kody," the koala replied. "Welcome to my home office. You can rest here as long as you need."

Ruby looked around the room with bright, curious eyes. "Wow! This is such a wonderful place! Look at all these colorful books! And that soft carpet looks so cozy! You're so lucky to have such a beautiful home."

Kody blinked in surprise. He looked around his office with fresh eyes. The books *were* colorful and interesting. The carpet *was* soft and warm. The sunlight *did* make everything glow beautifully.

"You know what, Ruby?" Kody said slowly. "I never really noticed how special this place is. I was so busy wishing for something different that I forgot to be grateful for what I already have."

Ruby settled down on the windowsill. "Sometimes we need someone else to help us see the good things around us," she said wisely.

Kody felt his heart grow warm and happy. "Ruby, would you like to stay and be my friend? We could read books together and enjoy this cozy office."

"I would love that!" Ruby chirped joyfully.

From that day on, Kody and Ruby shared the peaceful office. Kody learned to appreciate the simple pleasures around him - the soft morning light, the interesting books, and most importantly, the gift of friendship.

Every evening, as they watched the sunset through the window, Kody would say, "Thank you for helping me see how lucky I am, Ruby."

And Ruby would reply, "Thank you for opening your home and your heart to me, Kody."

Kody discovered that being grateful for what he had made everything more beautiful and meaningful. His home office wasn't boring at all - it was perfect just the way it was.

The End.
