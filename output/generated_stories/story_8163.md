# Benny's Big Energy-Saving Adventure

Benny the rabbit loved helping Daddy work in his home office. The room had a big wooden desk, a humming computer, and bright lights that made everything sparkle.

One morning, Benny hopped into the office. "Good morning, Daddy!" he said with his wiggly nose twitching.

"Good morning, little helper," Daddy said, turning on the computer. Click! On went the desk lamp. Click! On went the ceiling fan. Click, click, click!

Benny's ears perked up. "Daddy, why do you turn on so many things?"

"I need light to see and air to stay cool while I work," Daddy explained.

Later that day, Benny noticed something. When Daddy left for lunch, all the lights stayed on. The computer screen glowed bright blue. The fan spun round and round, making a whooshing sound.

"That's funny," Benny thought, his whiskers twitching. "Nobody's here, but everything is still on."

Benny remembered what Grandma Rabbit had taught him: "We should only use what we need, when we need it. That's how we take care of our Earth home."

The next morning, Benny had an idea! His cotton tail wiggled with excitement.

"Daddy, I want to be your Energy Helper!" Benny announced.

"Energy Helper? What's that?" Daddy asked, scratching his head.

"I'll help you remember to turn things off when you don't need them," Benny said proudly. "That way we can save energy for our Earth!"

Daddy smiled. "That sounds wonderful, Benny. Show me how."

Benny hopped around the office. "When you leave, we turn off the lights - click! When the computer sleeps, the screen goes dark - whoosh! And when it's not too hot, the fan can rest too!"

"But how will I remember?" Daddy wondered.

Benny's eyes sparkled. He had another brilliant idea! "We can make reminder notes!"

Together, they made colorful sticky notes. Benny drew pictures of a sleeping light bulb, a resting computer, and a quiet fan. They stuck the notes right where Daddy could see them.

"Turn me off when you leave!" Benny read in a squeaky voice, pretending to be the light.

Daddy laughed. "You're so creative, Benny!"

The very next day, Daddy got up for lunch. He started to walk away, then stopped. "Oh! Thank you, reminder note!" He turned off the desk lamp. Click!

Benny clapped his paws. "Hooray! You remembered!"

When Daddy finished his work that evening, he looked at Benny's computer note. "The computer can sleep now," he said, turning off the screen.

"And the fan can rest too!" Benny added, reaching up to flip the switch.

Days passed, and Daddy got better and better at remembering. Soon, he didn't even need the notes!

"Benny," Daddy said one afternoon, "do you know what you've done?"

Benny tilted his fuzzy head. "What?"

"You've helped us save energy! That means we're helping take care of our planet. You're a real problem-solver!"

Benny's chest puffed up with pride. His idea had worked! By thinking creatively and helping Daddy remember, they were making a difference.

"Can we put reminder notes in other rooms too?" Benny asked eagerly.

"Absolutely!" Daddy said. "We can help our whole family save energy!"

From that day on, Benny became the best Energy Helper in the house. He learned that even small rabbits can have big ideas that help the Earth. And the best part? It felt wonderful to work together as a team.

Benny snuggled up to Daddy in the now-quiet office. Outside, the birds chirped happily, and Benny knew they were chirping "thank you" for helping keep their world healthy and bright.

The End.
