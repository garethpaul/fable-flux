# Splash the Dolphin's Dancing Bath Time

Splash the dolphin loved bath time! Every evening, he would swim into his special bathroom in the coral reef. The walls sparkled with pretty shells, and warm water bubbled all around him.

"Time for my bath!" Splash said happily, diving into the bubbly water. The bubbles tickled his nose and made him giggle. "Pop, pop, pop!" went the bubbles.

As Splash splashed around, he heard beautiful music coming from outside his bathroom window. The music had a gentle rhythm that made his fins want to move.

"What's that lovely sound?" Splash wondered aloud. He peeked out the window and saw his friend Marina the seahorse dancing gracefully. Her movements were slow and flowing, like seaweed swaying in the current.

"Marina! Your dancing is so pretty!" Splash called out.

Marina smiled and swam closer to the window. "Thank you, Splash! This is a traditional seahorse dance my grandmother taught me. We call it the 'Ocean Wave Dance.' Would you like to learn?"

Splash's eyes lit up. "Oh yes! But I'm just a dolphin. Can I really dance like a seahorse?"

"Of course you can!" Marina said kindly. "Dancing is for everyone. We might move differently, but that's what makes it special!"

Marina showed Splash how to move his fins slowly, like gentle waves. "Feel the water around you," she said softly. "Let it guide your movements."

Splash tried to copy Marina's graceful moves, but his big tail made big splashes instead of gentle waves. Water splashed everywhere in his bathroom!

"Oh no!" Splash said sadly. "I'm making such a mess! I can't dance like you, Marina."

Marina swam into the bathroom through the window. "Splash, you don't need to dance exactly like me. Show me how a dolphin moves when you're happy!"

Splash thought for a moment. When he was happy, he loved to jump and spin! He began to leap out of the water and twist in the air. "Wheee!" he squealed with joy.

"That's it!" Marina clapped her tiny fins. "That's your dolphin dance! It's different from mine, but it's beautiful!"

Soon, other sea friends heard the splashing and music. Ollie the octopus came by, moving his eight arms in wiggly patterns. Shelly the crab scuttled sideways in her own special dance. Even Finn the fish joined in, swimming in zigzag patterns.

"Look!" Marina said happily. "We're all dancing together, but each of us moves in our own special way!"

Splash realized something wonderful. "We're all different, but we're all dancing! My jumping dance, your flowing dance, Ollie's wiggly dance - they're all beautiful!"

The friends spent the rest of bath time dancing together. Splash learned that being different made their dance even more magical. He also learned to be more careful with his big splashes so he wouldn't make too much of a mess.

"Thank you for teaching me about your seahorse dance, Marina," Splash said as he finished his bath. "And thank you for helping me find my own dolphin dance!"

"Dancing brings us all together," Marina replied with a warm smile. "Even when we're different, we can still share the joy of moving to music."

From that day on, Splash looked forward to bath time even more. He would dance his happy dolphin dance while getting clean, and sometimes his friends would join him for their special dancing bath time parties.

Splash learned that everyone has their own special way of dancing, and that's what makes the world so wonderful and colorful!

The End.
