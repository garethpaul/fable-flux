# Benny's Big Dining Room Workout

Benny the beaver waddled into the dining room with his big flat tail dragging behind him. The wooden table gleamed in the morning sunlight, and the chairs stood in neat rows like soldiers.

"Today I'm going to exercise!" Benny announced, puffing out his furry chest. "I want to be strong and healthy!"

Benny had heard his family talking about exercise. It sounded important and fun. He looked around the dining room with his bright brown eyes. The space seemed perfect for moving around.

First, Benny tried jumping jacks. He spread his short arms wide and jumped. But his webbed feet got tangled together. THUMP! Down he went, his bottom hitting the soft carpet.

"Ow!" Benny rubbed his tail. His whiskers twitched with frustration. "That didn't work at all!"

He felt his cheeks getting hot and red. Exercise was supposed to be easy, wasn't it? Benny's eyes started to fill with tears.

But then he took a deep breath through his little black nose. "Wait," he said to himself. "Maybe I need to try something different."

Benny looked at the dining room chairs. They were just the right height! He grabbed the back of one chair with his tiny paws.

"I can do push-ups against this!" he said excitedly.

Benny placed his paws on the chair seat and pushed his body away, then back again. Push, push, push! His muscles felt warm and tingly.

"One, two, three!" he counted proudly. "This feels much better!"

Next, Benny spotted the long dining table. It was smooth and shiny like a runway. He decided to crawl under it from one end to the other, like he was swimming through a tunnel.

"Whoosh!" Benny said as he crawled. His belly tickled against the carpet. "I'm like a furry submarine!"

The cool air under the table felt nice on his warm fur. Crawling used his arms and legs in a way that felt natural and fun.

After his table tunnel adventure, Benny tried marching in place. He lifted his knees high and swung his arms. "Left, right, left, right!" His webbed feet made soft patting sounds on the floor.

The rhythm made him feel happy and strong. His heart beat faster, and he could feel his blood pumping through his body like a gentle river.

"I'm getting the hang of this!" Benny cheered.

For his final exercise, Benny decided to do stretches. He reached his arms up high toward the ceiling, then bent down to touch his toes. His muscles felt long and loose, like warm taffy.

"Ahhhh," he sighed happily. "That feels so good!"

Benny stood in the middle of the dining room, breathing deeply. His fur was slightly damp with sweat, but he felt wonderful. His body hummed with energy, and his mind felt clear and bright.

"I did it!" Benny said, doing a little happy dance. "Exercise IS fun when you find the right way to do it!"

He realized that when something felt too hard or frustrating, he could try a different approach. There wasn't just one way to exercise. He could use chairs, tables, and even the floor to help him move his body.

Benny's tail wagged with pride. He had learned that being patient with himself and trying new things could turn a frustrating moment into a successful adventure.

"Tomorrow I'll try even more exercises," Benny promised himself. "My body feels so strong and happy!"

As he waddled out of the dining room, Benny's whiskers twitched with joy. He had discovered that exercise wasn't about being perfect – it was about moving, trying, and having fun with his amazing body.

The End.
