# Toby's Garden Helpers

Toby the tortoise loved his cozy balcony garden. Every morning, he would slowly walk between his colorful pots filled with bright red tomatoes, crunchy green lettuce, and sweet orange carrots. The warm sun felt wonderful on his shell, and the fresh smell of soil made him happy.

"Good morning, my beautiful vegetables!" Toby said cheerfully. "You're growing so big and strong!"

But today, something was different. Toby noticed his neighbor Mrs. Robin sitting sadly on her empty balcony next door. She looked very hungry.

"Hello, Mrs. Robin," called Toby in his gentle voice. "Why do you look so sad?"

Mrs. Robin fluffed her feathers sadly. "Oh, Toby, I'm so hungry, but I can't find any food. The ground is too hard for me to dig up worms, and there are no berries anywhere."

Toby's kind heart felt heavy. He looked at his garden full of fresh, healthy vegetables. "I have an idea!" he said slowly. "Would you like to share my vegetables? They're full of good vitamins to keep you strong!"

Mrs. Robin's eyes sparkled. "That's so kind, but your garden is too big for just you to take care of. How about I help you tend it? I can fly up high to check for bugs and water the tall plants!"

"What a wonderful idea!" said Toby, his wrinkled face breaking into a big smile.

Just then, Benny the bunny hopped onto the balcony. His nose twitched at the smell of fresh carrots. "Those carrots smell amazing!" he said. "I haven't eaten anything healthy all day."

"Would you like to join our garden team?" asked Toby warmly. "We're sharing the vegetables, but we need help taking care of them."

Benny's ears perked up with excitement. "Yes! I can dig holes for new seeds with my strong paws, and I know all about which vegetables are the most nutritious!"

Soon, little Squeaky the mouse appeared, attracted by the happy voices. "Can I help too?" she squeaked. "I may be small, but I can crawl into tiny spaces to plant seeds where no one else can reach!"

Toby clapped his flippers together slowly. "Of course! The more friends we have, the better our garden will grow!"

The four friends worked together all morning. Mrs. Robin flew high to sprinkle water on the tallest plants with her beak. Benny dug perfect holes for new carrot seeds with his powerful paws. Squeaky scurried into small corners to plant tiny herb seeds. And Toby used his wisdom to teach everyone which vegetables needed more sun and which needed more water.

"Working together makes everything easier!" puffed Benny as he finished digging.

"And more fun too!" chirped Mrs. Robin from above.

When lunchtime came, they all sat together sharing crispy lettuce, sweet carrots, and juicy tomatoes. The vegetables tasted extra delicious because they had grown them together.

"These vegetables are giving us energy and keeping us healthy," said Toby wisely. "And sharing them with friends makes them taste even better!"

Squeaky nibbled on a tiny cherry tomato. "I love how we all helped each other. My tummy is happy, and so is my heart!"

From that day on, Toby's balcony garden became the neighborhood's favorite place. The friends worked together every day, growing healthy food and sharing it with anyone who was hungry. They learned that when friends work as a team, they can do amazing things and help their whole community.

The End.
