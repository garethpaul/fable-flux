# Leo's Colorful Solution

Leo the little lion cub loved spending time in the creative art room at his school. The room smelled like sweet crayons and had big windows that let sunshine dance across the colorful tables. Leo's golden fur sparkled in the light as he looked around at all the wonderful art supplies.

"Good morning, Leo!" called Miss Bunny, his art teacher. "Today we're making special cards for the elderly animals at Sunny Meadow Home."

Leo's eyes grew wide with excitement. "I want to help make them happy!" he said, his little tail swishing back and forth.

But when Leo sat down at his table, he discovered a big problem. The red crayon was broken into tiny pieces, and red was his favorite color! All around him, his friends were drawing beautiful red hearts and roses for their cards.

"Oh no!" Leo said sadly. "How can I make a pretty card without red?"

His friend Bella the bunny looked over. "Maybe you could ask Miss Bunny for a new crayon?"

Leo checked, but Miss Bunny was all out of red crayons. The supply closet was empty too. Leo felt his whiskers droop with disappointment.

Then Leo had a wonderful idea! His eyes lit up like little golden stars. "I know what I can do!" he announced.

Leo carefully picked up a yellow crayon in one paw and an orange crayon in the other. He pressed them both gently on his paper at the same time. Like magic, they created a beautiful peachy-red color!

"Wow!" gasped Bella. "How did you do that?"

"I mixed the colors together!" Leo explained proudly. "Yellow and orange make a new kind of red!"

Soon, all of Leo's friends gathered around to watch. Leo showed them how blue and yellow made green, and how red and yellow made orange. The art room filled with excited giggles and "oohs" and "ahhs."

"Leo, you've discovered something amazing!" said Miss Bunny, clapping her paws together. "You used your imagination to solve a problem. That's called innovation!"

Leo felt warm and proud inside, like sunshine was glowing in his chest. He continued working on his card, using his new color-mixing technique to create the most beautiful rainbow he'd ever seen.

When it was time to visit Sunny Meadow Home, Leo carried his special rainbow card carefully in his paws. An elderly turtle named Mr. Shell smiled the biggest smile when he saw it.

"This is the most creative card I've ever received!" Mr. Shell said, his old eyes twinkling. "Tell me, young lion, how did you make such wonderful colors?"

Leo explained his discovery with excitement, and soon he was teaching Mr. Shell and all the other elderly animals how to mix colors too. The whole room buzzed with laughter and joy as everyone tried Leo's innovation.

That evening, Leo's mama asked him about his day. "I learned that when something doesn't work the way I want it to," Leo said thoughtfully, "I can use my imagination to find a new way! And when I help others, it makes my heart feel as bright as all the colors in my rainbow."

Leo had discovered that innovation means using your creativity to solve problems, and that helping others makes the best solutions even more special.

The End.
