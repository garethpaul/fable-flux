# Pippin's Colorful Song

Pippin the penguin waddled into the bright art room, his orange beak gleaming in the morning sunlight. The room smelled like fresh paint and crayons, and colorful pictures covered every wall. Pippin loved making art, but today something was very wrong.

"Oh no!" Pippin gasped, looking around the messy room. Paint tubes were squeezed empty and thrown on the floor. Broken crayons were scattered everywhere. Scraps of paper covered the tables like confetti after a party.

Mrs. Robin, the art teacher, looked very sad. "I don't know what to do, Pippin," she chirped softly. "The room is such a mess, and we don't have money for new supplies. How can we make beautiful art now?"

Pippin's heart felt heavy. He loved this special place where all the different animals came to create together. The tall giraffe painted high pictures, the tiny mouse made detailed drawings, and the elephant created big, bold masterpieces. Everyone was different, but they all loved art.

"Wait!" Pippin said, his black and white feathers ruffling with excitement. "I have an idea! What if we clean up AND make new art supplies from things that would be thrown away?"

Mrs. Robin tilted her head. "What do you mean, little one?"

Pippin began to waddle around the room, picking up the empty paint tubes. "Listen!" he said, shaking one. It made a soft rattling sound. "These could be shakers for music!" He picked up another and shook it too. "Shake, shake, shake!" he sang happily.

Soon, other animals heard Pippin's cheerful song and came to see what was happening. Ella the elephant used her strong trunk to gather the broken crayons. "We can melt these down and make new ones!" she trumpeted.

Gary the giraffe stretched his long neck to collect paper scraps from high shelves. "These pieces can become a beautiful collage!" he said in his deep voice.

Little Maya the mouse squeaked excitedly, "And I can organize all the tiny pieces by color!"

Together, they all started cleaning and singing Pippin's recycling song:

"Clean it up, clean it up, make it new!
Save the Earth, that's what we can do!
Different friends working as a team,
Making art is like a dream!"

The room filled with joyful sounds as they worked. The paint tubes became a whole orchestra of shakers. The broken crayons melted into beautiful new rainbow colors. The paper scraps transformed into a magnificent mural showing all their different animal friends holding hands around the Earth.

"Look what we did!" Pippin exclaimed, spinning in a happy circle. "We saved our art supplies AND helped take care of our planet!"

Mrs. Robin's eyes sparkled with joy. "You showed us that being different makes us stronger when we work together. And you taught us that taking care of the Earth can be creative and fun!"

All the animals cheered and played their new shaker instruments while admiring their recycled art. The room had never looked more beautiful or sounded more musical.

From that day on, Pippin's art room became known as the "Green Art Room," where every animal learned that taking care of the environment was just another way to create something wonderful together.

The End.
