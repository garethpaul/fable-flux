# Polly's Amazing World Map

Polly the parrot loved bright colors and making new things. Her rainbow feathers sparkled red, blue, green, and yellow in the morning sunlight that streamed through the guest room window.

"Good morning, world!" Polly chirped happily, flapping her colorful wings.

Today felt special. Polly's human family was expecting visitors from far away places. But the guest room looked so plain and empty. The white walls seemed sad, and the bed had boring gray sheets.

"Hmm," Polly tilted her head, thinking hard. "How can I make our guests feel welcome?"

Polly flew around the room, her sharp eyes noticing everything. She spotted some old magazines on the dresser, colorful crayons in a cup, and a big piece of white paper on the desk.

"I have an idea!" she squawked excitedly.

Polly carefully picked up a crayon with her strong beak. She began drawing a big circle on the paper. Then another circle. And another!

"This will be Earth," she said proudly, coloring one circle blue and green. "And this will be all the different countries where people live!"

As Polly worked, she remembered stories her family had told her about friends from other places. She drew a red circle for Japan, where their friend Yuki lived. She made a yellow circle for Brazil, where cousin Carlos came from. A purple circle represented Kenya, where their neighbor Amara was born.

"Each place is different and special," Polly sang as she worked. Her colorful feathers left tiny sparkles on the paper.

But then Polly stopped. Something was missing.

"Wait," she thought carefully. "How are all these places connected?"

Polly looked at her separate circles. They seemed lonely, floating by themselves on the paper. She tapped her beak thoughtfully.

"I know! We're all connected because we're all friends!"

Polly drew bright, happy lines connecting each circle to the others. She added little hearts along the lines. Then she drew tiny pictures: rice from Japan, soccer balls from Brazil, beautiful flowers from Kenya.

"Now everyone can see how we share wonderful things with each other!" Polly chirped with joy.

Just then, the doorbell rang. "Ring-a-ling-ling!"

Polly heard footsteps and voices speaking different languages. Her heart fluttered with excitement.

The guest room door opened, and in walked Yuki, Carlos, and Amara with Polly's family.

"Oh my!" gasped Yuki, seeing Polly's colorful creation. "What a beautiful map!"

"Look!" said Carlos, pointing with wonder. "She drew all our countries!"

Amara smiled warmly. "And see how she connected us all together? We really are one big family!"

Polly puffed up her feathers proudly. "Welcome to our home! I wanted to show that even though we come from different places, we can share and be friends!"

The visitors gathered around Polly's map, sharing stories about their homes. They talked about the foods they loved, the games they played, and the songs they sang. Polly listened carefully, learning something new about each place.

"Thank you, Polly," said Yuki gently. "You've shown us that the world is more beautiful when we share it together."

That night, Polly's world map hung proudly on the guest room wall. As she tucked her head under her wing to sleep, Polly felt happy and warm inside. She had learned that being a good friend to people from everywhere made the whole world feel like home.

The End.
