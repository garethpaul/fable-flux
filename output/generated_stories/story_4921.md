# Benny Bear's Splashy Discovery

Benny Bear was a curious little cub with soft brown fur and bright, twinkling eyes. He loved exploring new places and trying new things. Today, he was visiting his friend Lily Rabbit's house for the very first time.

"Come on, Benny!" called Lily from down the hallway. "Let's play hide and seek!"

Benny's nose twitched with excitement as he wandered through the cozy house. The walls smelled like fresh flowers, and everything looked so different from his own cave home. As he searched for the perfect hiding spot, he discovered a room he had never seen before.

"Wow!" Benny gasped, pushing open the bathroom door. His eyes grew wide as he looked around. There was a big white tub, a tall mirror that made funny reflections, and something very interesting - a sink with shiny handles that sparkled in the light.

Benny's curiosity got the better of him. "I wonder what these do?" he said to himself, reaching up on his tippy-toes to touch the handles.

WHOOSH! Water came gushing out of the faucet like a little waterfall. But oh no! Benny had turned it too hard, and water was splashing everywhere - on the floor, on the walls, and all over his fuzzy fur!

"Help! Help!" Benny called out, trying to turn the handle the other way. But his paws were slippery now, and he couldn't get a good grip. The water kept flowing, and the bathroom floor was getting very wet and slippery.

"Benny? Where are you?" Lily's voice echoed through the house.

"I'm in here!" Benny called back, his voice shaking a little. "I think I made a big mess!"

Lily hopped quickly to the bathroom and peeked inside. When she saw the water everywhere and Benny's worried face, she didn't get angry. Instead, she smiled kindly.

"Don't worry, Benny," Lily said in her gentle voice. "Everyone makes mistakes when they're learning something new. Let me help you!"

Lily hopped over to the sink and showed Benny how to turn the handle slowly and carefully. "See? Turn it this way to make it stop," she explained. "And this way to make it go."

Together, they turned off the water. Then Lily got some fluffy towels that smelled like sunshine from the closet.

"This is actually kind of fun!" Lily giggled as they wiped up the water together. "It's like we're playing a cleaning game!"

Benny felt much better. "Thank you for helping me, Lily. I was scared you'd be upset about the mess."

"That's what friends do," Lily said, giving Benny a warm hug. "We help each other, especially when we're trying new things. Besides, now you know how to use a sink properly!"

As they finished cleaning, Benny had an idea. "Hey Lily, want to play water sports outside instead? We could run through the sprinkler in your yard!"

"That sounds perfect!" Lily agreed. "And this time, we'll both get wet on purpose!"

They laughed together as they headed outside, hand in paw. Benny learned that making mistakes was okay, especially when you had a good friend to help you. And Lily learned that helping others made her feel happy inside.

From that day on, whenever Benny visited, he always remembered to ask for help when he was curious about something new. And Lily was always ready to lend a helping paw to her dear friend.

The End.
