# Milo Mouse and the Great Crumb Collection

In a cozy dining room with sunshine streaming through lace curtains, lived a kind little mouse named Milo. His tiny home was tucked behind the big wooden table, where he could smell the delicious aromas of breakfast, lunch, and dinner every day.

One bright morning, Milo peeked out from his mouse hole and saw something wonderful. Scattered across the shiny floor were breadcrumbs, bits of cheese, and tiny pieces of fruit from the family's breakfast.

"Oh my!" squeaked Milo excitedly, his whiskers twitching with curiosity. "What a feast!"

But as Milo scurried closer, he heard soft crying sounds coming from the corner of the room. Behind the tall chair sat Ruby, a smaller mouse with tears in her bright black eyes.

"What's wrong, Ruby?" asked Milo gently, his kind heart feeling worried.

"I'm so hungry," sniffled Ruby. "But I'm too little to reach the crumbs way up on the table, and there aren't many left on the floor."

Milo looked around the dining room. There were indeed many crumbs scattered about, but Ruby was right – she was much smaller than him and couldn't reach the good pieces.

"Don't worry, Ruby," said Milo with a warm smile. "I have an idea! Let's work together."

Milo's tiny paws got busy. First, he found a small bottle cap that had rolled under the cabinet. "This will be perfect!" he said, pushing it toward Ruby.

"What's it for?" asked Ruby, wiping her eyes.

"You'll see! Can you help me gather these crumbs?" Milo began collecting the scattered food bits, his nose twitching as he smelled the sweet apple pieces and savory cheese chunks.

Ruby's face brightened. "I can do that!" She began picking up the smaller crumbs with her tiny paws, dropping them into the bottle cap.

Together, they worked as a team. Milo climbed up the chair leg – his strong paws gripping the smooth wood – to reach the crumbs on the seat. "Catch!" he called down to Ruby, dropping the bigger pieces gently.

"Got it!" giggled Ruby, catching a piece of crust in her small paws.

When they had collected all the food, Milo said, "Now comes the best part. Let's share everything equally!"

He carefully divided all the crumbs into two piles. Ruby's eyes grew wide with surprise and joy.

"You're giving me half?" she asked in wonder.

"Of course!" said Milo. "Food tastes so much better when we share it with friends. And look – we collected much more working together than either of us could have alone!"

As they sat together on the warm wooden floor, munching their breakfast, Ruby said, "Milo, this is the most delicious meal I've ever had!"

"Mine too," agreed Milo, feeling the happiness that comes from helping others.

From that day forward, Milo and Ruby became the best of friends. Every morning, they would clean up the dining room together, collecting the fallen crumbs and sharing them equally. They even built a tiny storage area behind the table leg where they could save extra food for other hungry mice.

The dining room became a happier place because of their friendship and teamwork. Milo learned that sharing made everything more fun, and Ruby learned that even small mice could help with big jobs when they worked together.

The End.
