# Terry the Turtle's Tasty Adventure

Terry the turtle lived in a cozy house with his family. He was very shy and always hid inside his shell when meeting new people. But Terry had a special job - he was the family's pantry helper!

One sunny morning, Mama Turtle called out, "Terry! Can you please go to the pantry and pick healthy foods for our lunch?"

Terry peeked his little green head out just a tiny bit. "But Mama," he whispered, "the pantry is so big and dark. What if I pick the wrong foods?"

"Don't worry, sweetheart," Mama said gently. "Just remember our healthy food song: 'Colors bright make us strong, fruits and veggies all day long!'"

Terry slowly walked to the pantry door. *Creak!* The door opened wide. Inside, he could smell sweet apples and fresh carrots. Colorful cans and boxes lined the tall shelves like a rainbow.

"Oh my!" Terry gasped, pulling his head halfway into his shell. "There are so many choices!"

He spotted a box of chocolate cookies on a low shelf. They looked yummy and were easy to reach. Terry stretched out his little green arm, but then he remembered Mama's words: "Colors bright make us strong."

The cookies were brown - not very colorful. Terry looked around more carefully.

"What's that orange glow?" he wondered aloud. Following the bright color, he found a bag of carrots. "Orange is bright!" Terry said, feeling proud. He carefully picked up the bag. *Crunch!* The carrots sounded crisp and fresh.

Next, Terry saw something red and shiny on a higher shelf. "Red apples!" he exclaimed. But they were too high up. Terry felt scared. Should he give up?

"I can be brave," Terry told himself. "Mama is counting on me." He stood on his tippy-toes and stretched his neck as long as he could. *Stretch, stretch, reach!* Finally, his little claw touched the apple bag.

"Got it!" Terry cheered, surprising himself with how loud his voice was.

He continued exploring the pantry, finding yellow bananas that smelled sweet and green broccoli that looked like tiny trees. Soon, Terry had collected a rainbow of healthy foods.

"These colors are so pretty!" Terry said, admiring his collection. "And they all smell fresh and good."

When Terry returned to the kitchen, Mama Turtle clapped her flippers. "Terry! You did wonderfully! Look at all these nutritious foods - carrots for strong eyes, apples for healthy teeth, bananas for energy, and broccoli for growing big and strong!"

Papa Turtle peeked into the kitchen. "Who picked these beautiful foods?" he asked.

Terry felt shy again, but then he remembered how brave he had been in the pantry. He poked his head out proudly. "I did, Papa! I followed Mama's directions and picked colorful, healthy foods!"

"You were so brave and made such good choices," Papa said, giving Terry a gentle hug.

During lunch, Terry tasted each colorful food. The carrots were crunchy, the apples were sweet, the bananas were creamy, and the broccoli was surprisingly tasty!

"I feel strong already!" Terry announced, no longer hiding in his shell.

From that day on, Terry became the family's official healthy food picker. He learned that being brave and following directions helped him make good choices. And whenever he felt shy, he remembered his pantry adventure and how proud he felt picking nutritious, colorful foods for his family.

The End.
