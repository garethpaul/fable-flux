# Polly's Rainbow Rooftop

High up on a sunny rooftop, a bright green parrot named Polly was building something very special. She had colorful feathers that shimmered in the morning light, and her orange beak clicked excitedly as she worked.

"Perfect!" Polly squawked, arranging small twigs and shiny bottle caps. "My new clubhouse will be the most beautiful one ever!"

Polly had been working all week, using only green materials - green leaves, green stones, and green ribbons she had found. Everything matched her beautiful green feathers perfectly.

Just then, she heard tiny voices from below. "Excuse me," chirped a small blue jay. "May we come up?"

Polly looked down to see three birds waiting politely. There was the blue jay with bright blue feathers, a red cardinal with a cheerful crimson chest, and a yellow canary who sparkled like sunshine.

"Oh!" Polly said, feeling a bit surprised. "I... I suppose you can visit."

The three birds flew up and landed gently on the rooftop. Their eyes grew wide as they saw Polly's creation.

"Wow!" said Blue Jay. "This is amazing! But... why is everything green?"

"Because green is the best color," Polly replied, puffing up her feathers proudly. "Just like me!"

The little yellow canary tilted her head. "But what about other colors? I love yellow because it reminds me of warm sunshine!"

"And red makes me think of juicy apples and cozy fireplaces," added Cardinal softly.

"Blue is like the sky and the ocean - so peaceful and cool," said Blue Jay.

Polly felt confused. She had never thought about other colors being special too. "But... but my clubhouse is supposed to be perfect," she said, her voice getting smaller.

The three friends looked at each other, then at Polly's sad face. "May we help you make it even more perfect?" asked Blue Jay kindly.

"How?" Polly wondered.

"Well," said Canary, "what if we each brought our favorite colored things to add? Then everyone would feel welcome!"

Polly thought hard. Her green clubhouse was beautiful, but it did look a little... lonely. "You really think it would be better?"

"Let's try!" said Cardinal. "If you don't like it, we can change it back."

So the four friends worked together. Blue Jay brought soft blue flower petals that smelled like rain. Cardinal found shiny red berries that caught the sunlight. Canary gathered bright yellow dandelions that felt silky and smooth.

As they arranged everything together, something magical happened. The clubhouse began to look like a rainbow! The green, blue, red, and yellow created the most beautiful pattern Polly had ever seen.

"Oh my!" Polly gasped, her eyes sparkling. "It's... it's wonderful! It looks like the whole sky came down to visit!"

The four friends stepped back to admire their work. The clubhouse now welcomed everyone with its beautiful rainbow of colors.

"You know what?" Polly said, spreading her wings wide. "This is so much better than just green. Every color makes it more special!"

From that day on, Polly's Rainbow Rooftop became the most popular meeting place for all the neighborhood birds. Birds of every color felt welcome there - purple finches, orange orioles, black crows, and white doves.

And Polly learned that when everyone brings their own special gifts, everything becomes more beautiful and more fun. She discovered that differences don't divide us - they make us stronger and more colorful together.

"The best things happen," Polly would tell new visitors, "when everyone gets to shine!"

The End.
