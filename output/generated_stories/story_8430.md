# Joey's Safe Bathroom Adventure

Joey the kangaroo was a happy little joey who loved to hop around his family's cozy home. His soft brown fur bounced as he played, and his big dark eyes sparkled with curiosity about everything around him.

One sunny morning, Joey's mama called out, "Time for your bath, little one!"

Joey hopped excitedly toward the bathroom. He loved splashing in the warm, bubbly water! But as he bounced into the bathroom, he noticed something that made him stop and think.

"Mama," Joey said, his ears twitching with concern, "the floor looks very slippery and wet!"

Mama Kangaroo smiled proudly. "What a smart observation, Joey! You're absolutely right. Wet bathroom floors can be dangerous. What do you think we should do?"

Joey looked around the bathroom with his thoughtful eyes. He could see water droplets sparkling on the smooth tiles like tiny diamonds. The floor did look very slippery indeed!

"Maybe we need to make it safer?" Joey suggested, his little nose twitching as he sniffed the fresh, clean smell of soap in the air.

"Excellent thinking!" Mama said. "Let's work together to build a safer bathroom space. What do you see that might help us?"

Joey's eyes grew wide as he spotted something colorful near the bathtub. "Look, Mama! There's a pretty mat with rubber bumps on the bottom!"

"That's our non-slip bath mat," Mama explained. "When we place it on the wet floor, those little rubber bumps grip the tiles and help prevent slipping. Would you like to help me put it down?"

Joey felt brave and important. "Yes! I want to help keep us safe!"

Together, they carefully placed the bright blue mat on the bathroom floor. Joey could feel the soft, textured surface under his paws. It felt much more secure than the slippery tiles.

"Now," Mama said, "let's check for other safety helpers in our bathroom."

Joey hopped carefully on the mat, feeling proud of how steady he felt. He looked around and spotted a handle attached to the wall near the bathtub.

"What's that for, Mama?" he asked, pointing with his tiny paw.

"That's a safety grab bar," Mama explained. "If anyone feels unsteady, they can hold onto it for support. It's like having a helping hand always ready!"

Joey's understanding grew bigger and bigger. "So we're building a safe space by using all these helpful things?"

"Exactly right, my clever joey!" Mama said, giving him a gentle hug. "Safety isn't about being scared – it's about being smart and prepared."

As they filled the bathtub with warm, soapy water, Joey felt the steam rising and heard the cheerful sound of bubbles popping. The bathroom smelled like lavender soap, and everything felt cozy and secure.

"I understand now," Joey said happily. "Being safe means looking around, thinking carefully, and using the right tools to help us!"

During his bath, Joey splashed joyfully on the secure mat, knowing he had helped create a safe space. The grab bar was there if he needed it, and the non-slip mat kept him steady.

"Thank you for teaching me about bathroom safety, Mama," Joey said as he dried off with his fluffy towel.

"Thank you for being so thoughtful and brave, little one," Mama replied. "You helped build our safety together!"

From that day forward, Joey always remembered to look for safety helpers wherever he went, understanding that being careful and prepared made every adventure more fun.

The End.
