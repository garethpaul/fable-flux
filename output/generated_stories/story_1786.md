# Nutkin's Bathroom Sharing Adventure

Nutkin the squirrel lived in a cozy tree house with his family. Every morning, he brushed his fluffy tail and washed his tiny paws in their shared bathroom.

But today was different. Today, Nutkin's cousin Chippy was visiting for a whole week!

"Good morning, Nutkin!" chirped Chippy, bouncing into the bathroom. "I need to brush my teeth and wash my face!"

Nutkin frowned. The bathroom felt crowded with two squirrels. Chippy's toothbrush took up space next to his. Chippy's towel hung where Nutkin usually put his.

"This is MY bathroom," Nutkin squeaked. "I don't want to share!"

Chippy's ears drooped. "But I need to get ready too," he said softly.

The next morning, both squirrels rushed to the bathroom at the same time. They bumped into each other at the door.

"I was here first!" chittered Nutkin.

"No, I was!" replied Chippy.

They pushed and squeezed, trying to fit through the doorway together. Nutkin's tail got stuck. Chippy dropped his soap. The bathroom felt even more crowded and messy.

"This isn't working," sighed Nutkin, looking at the chaos around them. Toothpaste was splattered on the mirror. Water puddles covered the floor.

Nutkin sat on the edge of the bathtub and thought hard. His mama had always taught him to be kind to visitors. And Chippy was family too.

"Maybe," Nutkin said slowly, "we could make a plan together."

Chippy's eyes brightened. "A plan?"

Nutkin nodded. "You like to wake up early, and I like to sleep in. What if you use the bathroom first every morning?"

"That sounds fair," agreed Chippy. "And maybe I could use the left side of the sink, and you could use the right side?"

"Yes!" squeaked Nutkin excitedly. "And we could take turns choosing what bubble bath to use!"

The two cousins worked together to organize the bathroom. They made space for both their toothbrushes. They hung their towels side by side. They even drew a colorful schedule to remember their plan.

The next morning, Chippy quietly used the bathroom first. When Nutkin woke up, everything was clean and ready for him. The mirror sparkled. Fresh towels hung neatly.

"Thank you, Chippy!" called Nutkin. "This feels much better!"

At bath time, they took turns choosing bubbles. Chippy picked strawberry bubbles that smelled sweet and fruity. Nutkin chose vanilla bubbles that felt soft and creamy.

"Sharing isn't so bad," giggled Nutkin as they played with rubber ducks in the warm, bubbly water. "It's actually more fun with a friend!"

"And we both stay clean and happy," laughed Chippy, splashing gently.

From that day on, Nutkin and Chippy shared the bathroom peacefully. They learned that being tolerant and making plans together made everything work better. They even looked forward to their cousin visits!

When Chippy's week ended, Nutkin felt sad to see him go.

"Will you visit again soon?" asked Nutkin.

"Of course!" smiled Chippy. "And I know we'll share the bathroom perfectly!"

Nutkin learned that sharing space with others takes patience and planning, but it can lead to wonderful friendships and happy times together.

The End.
