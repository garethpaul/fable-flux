# Splash the Dolphin's Backyard Helper Day

Splash the dolphin lived in a big blue kiddie pool in the sunny backyard. The warm sunshine made the water sparkle like diamonds. Splash loved to jump and play, making happy splashing sounds all day long.

"Good morning, Splash!" called Emma, the little girl who lived in the house. She ran outside in her bright yellow dress. The grass felt soft and tickly under her bare feet.

"Good morning, Emma!" Splash clicked happily. "What a beautiful sunny day! Want to play?"

But Emma looked worried. Her face was sad, not smiley like usual. "I can't play today, Splash. Grandma is coming to visit, and our backyard is so messy! There are toys everywhere, and the flowers look thirsty."

Splash looked around with his big, kind eyes. Emma was right! Toy cars were scattered on the grass. A jump rope lay tangled by the fence. The pretty flowers drooped their heads sadly.

"I want to help Grandma feel welcome," Emma sighed. "But there's so much to do, and I'm just one little person."

Splash's fins wiggled with excitement. "Emma, I have an idea! What if we work together? I may live in water, but I can still help!"

Emma's eyes grew wide. "But how can you help from your pool?"

"Watch this!" Splash said cheerfully. He used his nose to push a beach ball toward Emma. "I can toss toys to you! And I can splash water on the flowers to help them drink!"

Emma clapped her hands. "That's so smart, Splash! You're a great problem-solver!"

They started working as a team. Splash gently tossed the scattered toys with his nose, and Emma caught them in a big basket. The toys made soft thuds as they landed safely inside.

"This is fun!" Emma giggled. "You make cleaning feel like a game!"

Next, Splash filled his mouth with cool, fresh water. He carefully sprayed it on the droopy flowers. The water sparkled in the sunshine like tiny rainbows. The flowers seemed to stand up taller and smile.

"The flowers are saying thank you!" Emma laughed, watching them perk up.

Soon, other backyard friends joined in. A friendly robin brought small sticks to the compost pile. A busy squirrel helped gather fallen leaves. Even Emma's cat, Whiskers, pushed a ball toward the toy basket with his paw.

"Look, Splash!" Emma said with wonder. "When we started helping, everyone wanted to help too!"

As they finished, the backyard looked beautiful. The grass was clear and green. The flowers stood tall and colorful. Everything was neat and welcoming.

Just then, Grandma arrived! She stepped into the backyard and gasped with joy. "Oh my! This is the most beautiful backyard I've ever seen!"

Emma hugged Grandma tight. "Splash helped me! He showed me that even big problems can be solved when we work together and think creatively!"

Splash did a happy flip in his pool. The water sparkled and splashed in the warm sunshine. "Every problem has a solution," he said wisely. "Sometimes we just need to look at it in a new way and ask our friends for help!"

Grandma smiled at both Emma and Splash. "What wonderful problem-solvers you are! And what kind hearts you have for helping others feel welcome."

As the sun shone down on their beautiful, clean backyard, Emma and Splash felt proud and happy. They had learned that working together, thinking creatively, and caring for others could solve any problem.

The End.
