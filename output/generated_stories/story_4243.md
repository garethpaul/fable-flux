# Pip the Penguin's Special Guest Room

Pip was a cheerful little penguin who loved making friends. He lived in a cozy house with his family, and they had a special guest room with a soft blue bed and fluffy white pillows that looked just like snow.

One sunny morning, Pip heard a gentle knock at the door. "Knock, knock, knock!"

"I'll get it!" chirped Pip, waddling quickly to the door with his orange feet making little pat-pat sounds on the floor.

When he opened the door, he saw three tired animals standing outside. There was Bella the bunny with droopy ears, Sam the seal with sleepy eyes, and Ollie the owl who was yawning.

"Hello," said Bella softly. "We've been traveling all day and we're very tired. Do you know anywhere we could rest?"

Pip's heart felt warm and happy. He wanted to help his new friends! "Of course! You can stay in our guest room!" he said with a big smile.

But then Pip looked at the guest room and frowned. The bed was only big enough for one animal, and there was just one pillow and one blanket.

"Oh no," Pip whispered to himself. "How can I help all three friends when there's only room for one?"

Pip sat down and thought very hard. He could hear his friends outside talking quietly about how tired they were. His tummy felt worried, but then he had a wonderful idea!

"I know what to do!" Pip said, clapping his flippers together.

First, Pip went to his own room and got his favorite soft blanket that smelled like fish and ocean breeze. Then he asked his mom for extra pillows from the closet that felt fluffy like clouds.

Next, Pip moved some chairs around to make the guest room bigger. He put his blanket on the floor to make a cozy sleeping spot. He arranged the pillows in a circle so everyone could rest their heads.

"This looks perfect!" said Pip, stepping back to admire his work.

But wait - Pip realized he had given away his own blanket and pillows. Where would he sleep?

Just then, his mom came in and saw what Pip had done. "Pip, that was very generous of you to share with your friends," she said, giving him a warm hug. "But you need to take care of yourself too. Let me help you."

Together, they found more blankets and pillows so everyone could be comfortable, including Pip.

Pip called his friends inside. "Come see your room!" he said excitedly.

Bella, Sam, and Ollie gasped with joy when they saw the cozy guest room.

"Pip, this is wonderful!" said Sam, clapping his flippers.

"You're so kind!" hooted Ollie.

"Thank you for sharing with us," said Bella, giving Pip a gentle hug.

That night, all four friends had a sleepover in the guest room. They told stories, shared snacks, and laughed together until their eyes grew heavy.

As they drifted off to sleep, Pip felt happy and warm inside. He learned that being generous and sharing with others made everyone feel good, including himself. And by taking care of himself too, he could help his friends even better.

"Sharing is caring," Pip whispered sleepily, and all his friends smiled in their dreams.

The End.
