# Splash's Mindful Morning

Splash the dolphin lived in a magical tide pool right next to Grandma Ocean's peaceful front porch. Every morning, the gentle waves would carry him up to visit the cozy wooden deck where wind chimes sang soft melodies.

Today, Splash felt wiggly and excited. He wanted to jump and spin and make big splashes! But when he arrived at the porch, he noticed something special.

"Good morning, Splash!" called Grandma Ocean from her rocking chair. Her voice was calm and soothing like gentle waves. "Would you like to try something new today?"

"Yes, yes!" Splash chirped, bouncing in his tide pool. The water sparkled in the morning sunlight.

Grandma Ocean smiled. "Let's practice being mindful. That means paying attention to right now - this very moment."

Splash tilted his head. "What does that mean, Grandma?"

"Close your eyes and listen," Grandma Ocean whispered. "What do you hear?"

Splash closed his big, bright eyes. At first, he wanted to wiggle and play. But then he listened carefully. "I hear the wind chimes! They sound like tiny bells. And... and the waves going 'whoosh, whoosh.' And birds singing in the trees!"

"Wonderful!" Grandma Ocean said. "Now, what do you feel?"

Splash took a deep breath through his blowhole. "The warm sun on my back feels nice. The water is cool and soft. The wooden porch smells like salt and sunshine."

"You're being mindful, dear Splash," Grandma Ocean said proudly. "When we pay attention to the present moment, we notice how beautiful our world is."

Splash opened his eyes slowly. Everything looked brighter somehow. The blue sky seemed bluer. The green plants looked greener. Even the old wooden boards of the porch seemed to glow.

"Grandma," Splash said thoughtfully, "when I'm mindful, I notice how clean and pretty everything is. I want to keep it that way!"

"That's being a good friend to our whole planet," Grandma Ocean said. "When we're mindful, we care about our Earth home and all the creatures who live here."

Splash nodded seriously. "I promise to always tell the truth about taking care of our ocean. And I'll be gentle with all the fish and coral and seaweed."

Just then, a small hermit crab scuttled across the porch, looking worried.

"What's wrong, little friend?" Splash asked kindly.

"I lost my shell," the crab said sadly. "I was rushing around and not paying attention."

Splash remembered his mindfulness lesson. "Let's look together - slowly and carefully."

They searched mindfully, noticing each board, each corner, each flower pot. Soon, Splash spotted the perfect shell hiding under the wind chimes.

"Here it is!" Splash said gently. "When we slow down and pay attention, we can help each other."

The hermit crab smiled big. "Thank you for being so kind and patient!"

As the morning sun climbed higher, Splash felt peaceful and happy. He had learned that being mindful helped him notice the beauty around him, care for his planet home, and be a good friend to others.

"Thank you, Grandma Ocean," Splash said, giving her a gentle splash-hug. "Being mindful makes everything more wonderful!"

Grandma Ocean rocked in her chair and smiled. "Remember, dear Splash - every moment is a chance to be mindful, caring, and true."

Splash nodded and settled into his tide pool, watching the clouds drift by slowly, feeling grateful for this beautiful day and all the friends who shared his ocean home.

The End.
