# Shelly's Super Sports Day

Deep in the cozy basement lab, Shelly the wise tortoise carefully arranged colorful bottles and books on her special table. The basement smelled like fresh mint and old paper - her favorite smells for thinking.

"Tomorrow is Sports Day at the forest!" Shelly said to herself, polishing her round glasses. "All my friends will be there."

Shelly loved Sports Day, but she had a worry. She was slow - much slower than Rabbit, who could hop-hop-hop super fast. She was smaller than Bear, who could throw balls really far.

"How can I help my team win?" Shelly wondered aloud.

She opened her big book of sports and read carefully. "Ah-ha!" she said, pointing with her green finger. "Preparation! That's the secret!"

Shelly began to prepare right away. First, she made special energy snacks. She mixed nuts and berries in a bowl. *Crunch, crunch, mix!* The sweet smell filled the basement.

"My friends will need good food for energy," she said happily.

Next, Shelly practiced cheering words in different languages. "¡Vamos!" she said in Spanish. "Allez-y!" she tried in French. "Go team!" she shouted in English. Each word felt bouncy and exciting on her tongue.

Then Shelly had her best idea yet. She created a special victory dance! She moved her arms like gentle waves and spun slowly in a circle. *Swoosh, swoosh, turn!* Her shell sparkled under the basement lights.

"This dance will make everyone feel proud, no matter who wins," Shelly smiled.

The next morning, Sports Day arrived with sunshine and bird songs. All the forest animals gathered in the big meadow. Rabbit stretched his long legs. Bear flexed his strong arms.

"Ready, Shelly?" asked Rabbit.

"I'm ready!" Shelly said confidently.

During the races, Shelly cheered in three languages. "¡Vamos, Bear!" she called when Bear ran. "Allez-y, Rabbit!" she shouted when Rabbit hopped.

When her friends got tired, Shelly shared her special energy snacks. "Mmm, these taste like sunshine!" said Bear, crunching happily.

During the ball games, Shelly couldn't throw far, but she had prepared something better. She knew exactly where each player should stand to catch the best throws.

"Rabbit, move left!" she called. "Bear, step back!" Her preparation helped everyone play their best.

At the end of Sports Day, Shelly taught everyone her victory dance. All the animals moved their arms like gentle waves and spun in slow, happy circles. Even the fastest rabbit and strongest bear loved the peaceful, joyful dance.

"Shelly," said Rabbit, "you didn't win the races, but you helped everyone have the best day ever!"

"How did you know exactly what to do?" asked Bear.

Shelly's eyes twinkled behind her glasses. "I prepared! I read books, I practiced words, I made snacks, and I planned how to help. Preparation is the best superpower of all!"

All the animals cheered. "Hooray for Shelly! Hooray for preparation!"

That night in her basement lab, Shelly wrote in her journal: "Today I learned that being prepared helps everyone succeed. When you plan ahead and practice, you can help your whole team shine!"

The End.
