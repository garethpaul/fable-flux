# Nutkin's Big Jump Challenge

In a cozy dining room, a small gray squirrel named Nutkin sat on the wooden table. His fluffy tail twitched with worry. The dining room had tall chairs, a big table, and shiny plates on the shelves high above.

"Oh no!" squeaked Nutkin. "I need to reach that acorn jar on the top shelf, but it's so very high!" The jar sparkled in the morning sunlight streaming through the window.

Nutkin tried jumping from the table. *Hop!* He reached up with his tiny paws, but the shelf was still too far away. His little legs felt tired already.

"I'm too small," Nutkin sighed. He sat down and felt his heart beating fast. *Thump-thump, thump-thump.* "Maybe I should give up."

But then Nutkin remembered what his mama always said: "Practice makes you stronger, little one!"

"I know what to do!" Nutkin said with a smile. "I need to exercise and get my jumping muscles ready!"

First, Nutkin did tiny hops around the dining room table. *Hop, hop, hop!* His paws made soft tapping sounds on the smooth wood. "One, two, three!" he counted out loud.

Next, he practiced stretching his legs. Nutkin reached his paws up high toward the ceiling. "Stretch, stretch, stretch!" he said. His muscles felt warm and good.

Then Nutkin did jumping jacks by the window. Up went his arms! Out went his legs! "This is fun!" he giggled. The warm sunlight made his gray fur feel cozy.

After that, he ran in circles around the dining room chairs. His little heart pumped faster. *Thump-thump, thump-thump!* "Running makes me feel strong!" Nutkin cheered.

For his last exercise, Nutkin practiced small jumps from chair to chair. Each jump felt easier than the last. His legs were getting stronger!

"Now I feel ready!" said Nutkin. He took three deep breaths. *In and out, in and out.* His body felt warm and energized.

Nutkin climbed onto the dining room table. He looked up at the high shelf with the acorn jar. "I can do this!" he said with confidence.

He bent his knees low. He swung his fluffy tail for balance. Then Nutkin jumped with all his might!

*WHOOSH!* Up, up, up he soared! His paws stretched high above his head. 

*Clink!* Nutkin's paws touched the jar! He grabbed it carefully and landed safely on the table with a gentle *thud*.

"I did it! I did it!" Nutkin danced around with joy. The acorn jar felt wonderful in his paws. "My exercises made me strong enough to reach the top shelf!"

Nutkin opened the jar and enjoyed a delicious acorn. *Crunch, crunch, crunch!* It tasted extra sweet because he had worked so hard.

"Tomorrow I'll exercise again," Nutkin said happily. "Moving my body makes me feel good and helps me do amazing things!"

From that day on, Nutkin exercised every morning in the dining room. He grew stronger and could jump even higher. And whenever other little animals needed help reaching high places, Nutkin would teach them his special exercises too.

The End.
