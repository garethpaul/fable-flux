# Ruby Rabbit's Sharing Room

Ruby Rabbit lived in a cozy burrow with her family. She had a special guest room with soft blue walls and a fluffy white bed. Ruby loved this room because it was where visitors could stay and feel welcome.

One sunny morning, Ruby's cousin Benny Bunny called. "Ruby, may I visit you today? My burrow is being painted, and I need somewhere to sleep tonight."

"Of course!" said Ruby happily. "You can stay in my guest room. It has the softest pillows!"

Ruby hopped to the guest room and fluffed the pillows. She put fresh lavender flowers on the nightstand because they smelled so sweet. Everything looked perfect.

But then Ruby remembered something. She had hidden her favorite toy carrot under the guest bed yesterday. It was orange and squeaky, and she played with it every day.

"Should I move my toy?" Ruby wondered aloud. "Benny might want to play with it, but it's my very favorite."

Ruby sat on the soft carpet and thought hard. Her whiskers twitched as she made a decision. She crawled under the bed and pulled out her beloved carrot toy.

When Benny arrived, he looked tired. His brown ears drooped, and his eyes seemed sleepy.

"Thank you for letting me stay, Ruby," Benny said softly. "I'm so tired from packing all my things."

Ruby's heart felt warm and fuzzy. She wanted to help her cousin feel better. She held up her orange carrot toy.

"Benny, would you like to play with my favorite toy? It squeaks when you squeeze it, and it always makes me feel happy when I'm tired."

Benny's eyes grew wide. "Really? But it's your favorite toy!"

Ruby nodded. "Yes, but sharing makes me feel even happier than playing alone. When we share, we both have more fun!"

Benny squeezed the carrot toy. *Squeak! Squeak!* The sound made both rabbits giggle.

"This is wonderful!" Benny laughed. "But Ruby, I want to be honest with you. I have something to share too." He opened his travel bag and pulled out a soft, purple blanket. "This is my comfort blanket. It helps me sleep. Would you like to snuggle with it while we play?"

Ruby's nose twitched with excitement. "Yes, please! It looks so cozy!"

They spread the purple blanket on the guest room floor and played together. Ruby showed Benny how to make the carrot toy squeak in rhythm, like music. Benny showed Ruby how his blanket was perfect for making blanket forts.

As the sun began to set, both rabbits felt sleepy. Ruby realized something important.

"Benny, I learned something today. When I take care of others by sharing, I feel good inside. And when I'm honest about my feelings, we can help each other even more!"

Benny yawned and smiled. "You're right, Ruby. Sharing and caring makes everything better. Thank you for being so generous."

Ruby tucked Benny into the guest bed with his purple blanket. She kept her carrot toy nearby, but she felt proud knowing she had shared it when Benny needed comfort.

"Good night, cousin," Ruby whispered.

"Good night, Ruby. You're the best friend ever," Benny replied sleepily.

Ruby hopped to her own room, feeling warm and happy. She had learned that being generous, taking care of others, and being honest about her feelings made her heart feel full of sunshine.

The End.
