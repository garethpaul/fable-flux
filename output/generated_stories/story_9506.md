# Splash the Dolphin's Garden Adventure

Splash the dolphin loved to swim and play. But today was different. A big storm had washed him far from the ocean. Now he found himself in a small puddle behind a garden shed.

"Oh no!" said Splash. "How will I get back to the water?" The puddle was so tiny. Splash could barely move his fins. The wooden shed felt strange and dry around him.

Splash heard soft crying sounds. "Sniff, sniff." The sounds came from inside the garden shed.

"Hello?" called Splash. "Is someone there?"

A little mouse peeked out. Her whiskers were wet with tears. "I'm Pip," she said. "I lost my acorn collection. The storm blew them all away!"

Next, a small bird hopped out. "I'm Tweet," he chirped sadly. "The wind broke my nest. My eggs are scattered everywhere!"

Then a tiny frog jumped forward. "I'm Hop," she croaked. "The rain washed away my lily pad home. I have nowhere to sit!"

Splash felt his heart grow warm. Even though he had his own big problem, he wanted to help his new friends.

"Don't worry," said Splash kindly. "Let's work together!"

Splash used his strong tail to splash water from his puddle. The water made the ground soft and muddy. "Pip, look in the soft mud. Your acorns might be buried there!"

Pip dug with her tiny paws. "I found one! And another!" Soon she had found all her acorns. "Thank you, Splash!"

Splash looked up at the shed's roof. "Tweet, I can see some twigs up there. But I can't reach them." 

Splash had an idea. He splashed water high into the air. The water knocked down the perfect twigs for Tweet's nest. "These are just right!" chirped Tweet happily.

For Hop, Splash shared some of his precious puddle water. He made a smaller puddle just for her. "This can be your new lily pad pond!" said Splash.

"You're so generous!" said Hop. "But what about you? Your puddle is getting smaller!"

Splash smiled. "Helping friends makes me happy. That's more important than having lots of water."

Just then, the three friends heard a rumbling sound. A big water truck was coming down the path!

"That's the farmer!" said Pip. "He fills the animal water troughs every day!"

The friends called out together. "Help! Help! Splash needs water!"

The kind farmer saw Splash in the tiny puddle. "Oh my! How did you get here, little dolphin?" 

The farmer gently scooped up Splash. He drove the water truck all the way to the ocean. "There you go," said the farmer. "Back where you belong!"

Splash splashed joyfully in the big blue waves. But he didn't forget his friends. Every day, he brought them gifts from the sea. Pretty shells for Pip. Soft seaweed for Tweet's nest. And special sea water for Hop's pond.

"Thank you for teaching me about sharing," Splash told his friends. "When we help others, we help ourselves too!"

From that day on, Splash visited the garden shed often. He learned that being generous and kind made him the happiest dolphin in the whole ocean.

The End.
