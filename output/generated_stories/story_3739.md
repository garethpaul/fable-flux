# Koko the Koala's Messy Lab Mystery

Deep underground in a cozy basement lab, Koko the koala worked quietly at her special table. She loved mixing colorful potions and watching them bubble and fizz. The lab smelled like sweet berries and fresh rain.

But today, something was very wrong. Koko's lab was a terrible mess! Test tubes rolled everywhere. Colorful bottles were scattered across the floor. Her important notebook was buried under a pile of papers.

"Oh my!" said Koko, scratching her fluffy gray head. "I can't find anything! Where did I put my purple potion?"

Just then, she heard tiny voices coming from behind a stack of books.

"Help us! Help us!" squeaked the voices.

Koko listened carefully with her round ears. She followed the sound and found three little lab mice trapped under a fallen box of supplies.

"Please help us get out!" cried the smallest mouse. "We were trying to clean up, but everything fell down!"

Koko's heart felt sad. "I'm so sorry! This mess isn't fair to you little friends. Let me help right away."

She gently lifted the heavy box. The three mice scurried out, shaking dust from their whiskers.

"Thank you, Koko!" they said together. "But why is your lab so messy?"

Koko looked around at the chaos. Her cheeks turned pink with embarrassment. "I forgot to put things back where they belong. I was so excited about my experiments that I left everything everywhere."

The biggest mouse twitched his nose. "We can help you organize! That would be fair since we live here too."

"That's very kind," said Koko. "But first, let me listen to what you need."

The mice explained they needed safe paths to walk and clean places to sleep. Koko realized her mess had made their home unsafe and uncomfortable.

"You're absolutely right," said Koko. "Everyone deserves a clean, safe place to live. Let's work together!"

Koko and the mice made a plan. They would organize everything properly so everyone could be happy and safe.

First, they sorted all the bottles by color - red ones on the red shelf, blue ones on the blue shelf. The bottles clinked softly as they found their homes.

Next, they organized the test tubes in special racks. Each rack had a label with pictures so everyone could find what they needed.

Then they created a special "mouse highway" - clear paths along the walls where the mice could walk safely without bumping into lab equipment.

Finally, they made a cozy corner with soft cotton for the mice to sleep, far away from any dangerous chemicals.

"Now this is much better!" said Koko, wiping her paws on her lab coat. The basement smelled fresh and clean.

The mice danced in circles. "Thank you for listening to us and making everything fair!"

Koko smiled warmly. "Thank you for teaching me something important. When we organize our spaces and listen to everyone's needs, we can all be happy together."

From that day on, Koko always put her supplies back where they belonged after each experiment. She listened carefully to her mouse friends and made sure her lab was safe and fair for everyone.

The basement lab became the most organized, happiest place underground, filled with the sounds of bubbling potions and cheerful mouse giggles.

The End.
