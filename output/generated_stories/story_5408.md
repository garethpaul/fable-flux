# Benny the Dancing Beaver Learns to Share

Benny the beaver loved to dance! Every morning in his cozy living room, he would wiggle his fluffy tail and tap his big flat feet on the soft carpet. The room was filled with colorful cushions, bouncy balls, and musical toys that made wonderful sounds.

"Tap, tap, wiggle, spin!" Benny sang as he danced around his favorite red cushion. The sunlight streamed through the window, making rainbow patterns on the walls. Benny felt so happy when he danced!

One sunny day, Benny heard a tiny knock at his door. "Knock, knock, knock!"

"Who could that be?" Benny wondered, stopping his morning dance. He opened the door and saw three little field mice looking very sad.

"Hello, Benny," squeaked the smallest mouse named Pip. "We heard beautiful music coming from your house. We love to dance too, but we don't have anywhere fun to practice."

Benny looked at his wonderful living room filled with all his special dancing things. He loved his space so much! But then he noticed how the mice's whiskers drooped sadly.

"Our burrow is too small and dark," said the second mouse, Dot. "And the meadow is too muddy from yesterday's rain."

Benny's tail twitched nervously. He had worked so hard to make his living room perfect for dancing. What if the mice messed up his neat piles of cushions? What if they moved his favorite bouncy balls?

"Um... well..." Benny started, but then he saw how hopefully the mice were looking at him.

The third mouse, Zip, added softly, "We promise we'll be very careful. We just want to dance and feel happy like you do."

Benny's heart felt warm and fuzzy. He remembered how wonderful it felt to dance and how sad he would be if he couldn't do it. These little mice just wanted to feel that same joy!

"You know what?" Benny said with a big smile, "Dancing is always more fun with friends! Come in, come in!"

The three mice squeaked with delight and scurried into the living room. Their tiny eyes grew wide as they saw all the colorful cushions and musical toys.

"This is amazing!" chirped Pip, doing a little spin on the soft carpet.

Benny showed them how to bounce gently on the cushions and how to make music with the special toys. Soon, all four friends were dancing together!

"Wiggle your whiskers!" laughed Benny.
"Tap your tiny toes!" giggled Dot.
"Spin around and around!" cheered Zip.

The living room filled with the most beautiful sounds - musical toys chiming, little feet pattering, and friends laughing together. Benny discovered that sharing his special space made dancing even more magical!

From that day on, every morning became a dance party. Benny taught the mice his favorite moves, and they taught him some tiny mouse dances too. The living room became a place where everyone felt welcome and happy.

"Thank you for sharing with us, Benny," said Pip one day after a particularly fun dance session.

Benny's chest puffed up with pride. "Sharing makes everything better," he said. "When we're generous with what we have, we all feel more joy!"

And from then on, whenever Benny danced in his playful living room, he always remembered that the best part wasn't the bouncy balls or colorful cushions - it was sharing the happiness with his friends.

The End.
