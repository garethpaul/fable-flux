# Bubbles and the Big Bath Plan

Bubbles was a playful dolphin who loved to splash, giggle, and sing little songs. One morning, Bubbles visited a bright, clean bathroom. The floor was warm, the tiles were shiny, and the air smelled like sweet soap. The mirror was foggy from warm steam, and tiny water drops tick-tick-ticked into the tub.

“Hello, bathroom!” Bubbles sang. “I’m ready for my big bubble bath!”

Bubbles peeked at the tub. It was empty and quiet. The silver faucet smiled like a shiny nose. On the edge sat a yellow duck named Ducky and a purple sponge named Squish. Ducky’s beak was a happy orange, and Squish felt soft and springy.

“Hi, friends!” Bubbles chirped. “Want to take a bath with me?”

Ducky squeaked, “Quack-yes! I love warm water!”

Squish wiggled. “I’m ready, but we need a plan!”

Bubbles blinked. “A plan?”

Squish nodded. “Yes! We prepare so bath time is safe and fun.”

Bubbles puffed a tiny spray. “Let’s do it together!”

They looked around the bathroom. The counter had a bottle of gentle soap, a cup, a small towel, and a blue toothbrush with friendly bristles. The window let in soft light. The room felt cozy like a hug.

“First,” said Squish, “we check the water.”

“How do we check it?” Bubbles asked.

Ducky bobbed. “Use our senses! We can see, we can touch, and we can listen.”

Bubbles turned the faucet a little. Water began to pour with a happy hush, like rain on leaves. “It sounds friendly,” Bubbles said.

“Careful,” Squish said. “Touch with just a fin tip.”

Bubbles dipped a fin and pulled back. “Too hot!” Bubbles squeaked.

“Good job checking,” Ducky said. “Try a little cold water.”

Bubbles turned the cold handle. The water changed. Steam slowly floated away. Bubbles tapped the water again. “Ahhh. Warm like sunshine. Just right.”

Squish clapped. “Step one: water is ready!”

“Step two?” Bubbles asked.

“Gather what we need,” said Squish. “Soap? Towel? Toys?”

Bubbles looked. “Soap is here. Towel is here. And you, Ducky, are the best toy!”

Ducky wiggled with pride. “Quack-quack!”

Bubbles thought for a moment. “What about the floor? It looks slippery.”

“Good thinking,” Squish said. “Let’s put the bath mat straight and dry.”

They smoothed the soft mat. Bubbles pressed a fin on it. “Not slippery now.”

“Step three,” Ducky said, “we get in slowly.”

Bubbles took a deep breath. “One fin… two fins… whoosh!” The warm water hugged Bubbles. It felt silky and cozy. Ducky hopped in and floated, making tiny ripples. Squish slid onto the side, ready to help.

“Time for bubbles!” Bubbles giggled, adding a little soap. Fluffy clouds of bubbles grew and grew. They smelled like sweet flowers. Bubbles blew softly. “Pfffff!” A bubble popped with a gentle “pop!” and a tickle on Bubbles’ nose.

Ducky laughed. “That tickles!”

They splashed, but not too big. “Small splashes keep water in the tub,” Squish reminded them.

Bubbles nodded. “We’re being careful. We prepared!”

Soon, the mirror fogged again like a soft gray blanket. Bubbles drew a smiley face on it with a wet fin. “Hello, smile!”

“Bubbles,” Ducky said, “let’s clean fins and flippers.”

“Okay!” Bubbles dipped under, humming a happy tune. “Swish, swish, swish.” Squish helped scrub gently. The soap made tiny stars of foam.

“Uh-oh,” Bubbles said, spotting a slippery soap bar sliding toward the tub edge. “If it falls, it could be messy.”

“What can we do?” Ducky asked.

Bubbles thought. “We can move it to the soap dish with bumps so it stays put.”

“Great plan,” Squish said. “You’re using your brain.”

Bubbles nudged the soap onto the bumpy dish. It stayed. “Problem solved!”

They rinsed with the cup. The warm water felt like a soft blanket. Drip, drip, drip went the last drops.

“All clean!” Ducky cheered.

“Now step four,” Squish said, “we turn off the water and tidy up.”

Bubbles turned the handles gently. The room became quiet. Bubbles placed the cup back, put the soap in the dish, and set the towel within reach.

“Step five,” said Ducky, “we get out slowly and dry.”

Bubbles climbed out carefully, one fin at a time, onto the dry mat. The towel felt fluffy and snug. “Mmm, warm and soft,” Bubbles sighed.

Ducky shook tiny drops off his head. Squish patted Ducky dry. They all felt fresh and happy.

Bubbles smiled at Ducky and Squish. “Thank you, friends. You helped me think, prepare, and stay safe.”

Ducky quacked softly. “We did it together.”

Squish beamed. “That’s what friends do!”

Bubbles looked around the shiny bathroom. It looked neat and calm. The mirror smiley face still grinned at them.

“I learned something today,” Bubbles said. “Friends help us make good plans. When we think first, we solve little problems. And when we prepare, bath time is easy and fun.”

Ducky bobbed. “And bubbles are extra fun with friends.”

Bubbles laughed, a happy dolphin giggle. “Let’s remember our steps next time: check the water, gather things, go slow, tidy up, and help each other.”

They all hugged—a dolphin, a duck, and a sponge. Warm, safe, and smiling, they felt proud of their big bath plan and their big hearts.

The End.
