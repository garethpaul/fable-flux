# Beep's Big Voice

Beep was a small robot dog with shiny silver fur and bright blue LED eyes. He lived in Mrs. Chen's balcony garden with the tomato plants and sunflowers. Beep loved helping water the plants every morning.

But Beep had a problem. He was very small, and his voice was very quiet. When he tried to help, nobody could hear him.

One sunny morning, Mrs. Chen's grandson Tommy came to visit. Tommy brought his soccer ball and started kicking it around the balcony.

"Be careful near the plants!" Beep said in his tiny robot voice. But Tommy couldn't hear him.

THUMP! The soccer ball knocked over a pot of baby carrots.

"Oh no!" Beep's lights flashed red with worry. He tried again. "Tommy, please watch the plants!" But his voice was still too quiet.

CRASH! Another pot fell over, spilling dirt everywhere.

Beep felt his circuits getting hot with frustration. His tail drooped down. "Nobody ever hears me," he whispered sadly. "Maybe I should just stay quiet."

But then Beep remembered something important. Mrs. Chen had a small radio in the garden that played music. Beep had special speakers in his chest!

"Wait!" Beep thought. "I don't have to use just my voice. I can make music too!"

Beep pressed a button on his side. Beautiful, loud music started playing from his speakers. The music was happy and bouncing, like a marching song.

Tommy stopped kicking his ball. "Wow! Cool music!"

Now that Tommy was listening, Beep spoke clearly and loudly over the music: "Hi Tommy! I'm Beep! I help take care of the garden. Can you please kick your ball away from the plants? They're very delicate!"

Tommy's eyes grew wide. "A talking robot dog! That's amazing! I'm sorry about the plants, Beep. I didn't see you there."

"That's okay!" Beep wagged his metal tail. "Want to help me replant the carrots? Then we can play soccer in the bigger space over there!"

Tommy nodded excitedly. "Yes! Can you teach me about the plants while we work?"

Beep turned his music to a gentle, soft tune. "Of course! This carrot plant needs loose soil and lots of sunshine. See how the leaves are bright green? That means it's healthy!"

Together, they carefully replanted the carrots. Beep showed Tommy which plants needed water and which ones were growing new flowers. Tommy was very gentle and listened carefully.

"Now can we play soccer?" Tommy asked.

"Absolutely!" Beep changed his music to an upbeat sports song. "But let's play over here, away from the plants!"

They kicked the ball back and forth in the safe area. Beep's music made everything more fun. When Tommy scored a goal, Beep played celebration music. When Beep made a good save, Tommy cheered loudly.

Mrs. Chen came outside and smiled. "I see you two are working together beautifully! Beep, I'm proud of you for speaking up about the plants."

Beep's LED eyes glowed bright blue with happiness. "I learned that when my regular voice doesn't work, I can find other ways to communicate! Music helped Tommy notice me."

Tommy patted Beep's shiny head. "And I learned to listen better and be more careful around growing things!"

From that day on, whenever Beep needed to get someone's attention, he used his music and spoke clearly. He never felt too small or too quiet again. He had found his big voice!

The End.
