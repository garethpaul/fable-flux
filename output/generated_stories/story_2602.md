# Ellie's Bubble Art Adventure

Little Ellie the elephant calf loved bath time. Her trunk would wiggle with excitement every evening when Mama said, "Time to get clean, little one!"

Tonight was extra special. As warm water filled the big bathtub, Ellie noticed something wonderful - rainbow bubbles floating everywhere! They sparkled like tiny soap jewels in the bathroom light.

"Ooh!" squeaked Ellie, reaching out with her little trunk. "Pretty bubbles!"

Just then, her friend Benny the bunny hopped into the bathroom. "Wow, Ellie! Look at all these bubbles! Can I play too?"

"Yes, yes!" trumpeted Ellie happily. "But how do we catch them? They keep popping when I touch them with my trunk!"

Benny's nose twitched thoughtfully. "Hmm, maybe we need to work together. What if we try something different?"

Ellie splashed gently in the tub. "I have an idea! But I need your help, Benny. Can you get the big bubble wand from the toy basket?"

"Of course!" Benny hopped over and brought back the colorful wand. "What's your plan?"

"Well," said Ellie, her eyes twinkling, "what if we make bubble art? You blow the bubbles, and I'll catch them on this special paper!"

Mama Elephant had given Ellie waterproof paper that changed colors when it got wet. It was perfect for their bubble adventure!

Benny dipped the wand in the soapy water. "Ready, Ellie?"

"Ready!" Ellie held the paper steady with her trunk.

Benny blew gently through the wand. Beautiful, shimmery bubbles floated toward Ellie. Pop! Pop! Pop! Each bubble left a colorful circle on the paper - pink, blue, yellow, and green!

"It's working!" giggled Ellie. "Look, Benny! We're making a rainbow picture together!"

"This is amazing!" said Benny, blowing more bubbles. "Your idea was so smart, Ellie!"

But then - oh no! - Benny accidentally blew too hard. Giant bubbles flew everywhere, and some landed on the bathroom mirror and walls.

"Oops!" said Benny, his ears drooping. "I made a mess. I'm sorry, Ellie."

Ellie looked around at the soapy bubbles covering everything. Instead of being upset, she started to laugh. "Don't worry, Benny! Let's clean up together. That can be fun too!"

"Really?" asked Benny, perking up.

"Really! You can help me reach the high spots with your hopping, and I can clean the low spots with my trunk. We make a great team!"

So Ellie and Benny worked together, giggling as they wiped away the bubbles. Benny hopped up to clean the mirror, while Ellie used her trunk like a gentle scrub brush on the walls.

"Teamwork makes everything easier!" said Ellie as they finished.

When Mama Elephant came to check on them, she found a sparkling clean bathroom and two very proud friends holding their beautiful bubble art.

"What lovely cooperation!" said Mama, admiring their colorful creation. "And what good communication! You worked together and talked through your problems."

Ellie and Benny beamed with pride. They had learned that when friends work together, share ideas, and help each other, they can create something wonderful - and have lots of fun doing it!

As Ellie settled into her warm, bubbly bath, she smiled at her friend. "Tomorrow, let's make more art together, Benny!"

"It's a deal!" laughed Benny. "Cooperation is the best!"

The End.
