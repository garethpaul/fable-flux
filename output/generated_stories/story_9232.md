# Milly Mouse's Art Room Adventure

Milly Mouse loved the bright art room at her school. The walls were covered with colorful paintings and drawings. Paint brushes stood in tall jars like flowers. The smell of crayons and paper filled the air.

Today, Milly bounced into the art room on her tiny paws. She was so excited to paint! But when she looked around, she saw something that made her whiskers twitch with worry.

In the corner sat Oliver Owl, all by himself. His feathers looked droopy, and his big eyes stared sadly at the floor.

"Hello, Oliver!" Milly squeaked cheerfully. "Want to paint with me?"

Oliver shook his head slowly. "I can't paint," he hooted softly. "My wings are too big. I knock things over. Everyone gets upset."

Milly's little heart felt heavy. She could see that Oliver was very sad. She looked at his beautiful brown and white feathers and his gentle eyes.

"That must feel really hard," Milly said kindly. She sat down next to Oliver. "Can you tell me more?"

Oliver's eyes grew watery. "Yesterday I tried to paint a flower. But my wing bumped the paint jar. Purple paint splashed everywhere! The other animals laughed."

Milly nodded and patted Oliver's wing gently with her tiny paw. "I understand why you feel sad. That sounds embarrassing."

"It was," Oliver whispered.

Milly's whiskers twitched as she thought. Then her eyes sparkled with an idea!

"Oliver, what if we paint together? I could help you, and you could help me!"

"How?" Oliver asked, tilting his head.

"Well," Milly said, hopping up excitedly, "your wings are perfect for making big, swooshy brush strokes! And I'm small, so I can do the tiny details. We could make the most beautiful painting ever!"

Oliver's eyes grew brighter. "Really? You think my big wings could help?"

"Yes!" Milly squeaked. She scurried over to get a large paintbrush and a big piece of paper. "Let's try!"

First, Milly showed Oliver how to hold the brush gently in his wing tip. "Now make big, happy circles," she encouraged.

Oliver carefully moved his wing in wide circles. Beautiful blue swirls appeared on the paper!

"Wow!" Oliver hooted with joy. "Look what I made!"

"It's wonderful!" Milly cheered. She jumped and did a little spin. "Now I'll add some tiny yellow dots for sunshine!"

Milly danced around the paper on her little paws, dabbing tiny spots of bright yellow paint. The exercise made her feel happy and energetic!

Together, they painted fluffy white clouds, green grass, and red flowers. Oliver made the big shapes with his wings, while Milly added small details with her tiny paws.

"This is the best painting ever!" Oliver said, flapping his wings happily.

Other animals gathered around to see their beautiful artwork.

"Oliver, your brush strokes are amazing!" said Bella Bunny.

"And Milly, those tiny details are perfect!" added Sam Squirrel.

Oliver's chest puffed up with pride. "Milly helped me see that being different can be wonderful!"

Milly smiled warmly. "And Oliver taught me that the best art happens when friends work together!"

They hung their painting on the wall where everyone could see it. Oliver and Milly became the best of art partners, creating beautiful pictures together every day.

From that day on, whenever someone felt sad or left out in the art room, Milly and Oliver remembered to listen with kind hearts and find ways to help.

The End.
