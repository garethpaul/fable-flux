# Oliver Owl's Wellness Plan

High up in a cozy, quiet attic, lived a wise old owl named Oliver. The attic smelled like old books and sunshine that streamed through a small round window. Oliver loved his peaceful home, but lately, he had been feeling very tired and grumpy.

"Hoot, hoot," Oliver sighed sadly. "I don't feel happy anymore. My feathers feel messy, and I'm always sleepy, even at night when I should be awake!"

Oliver looked at himself in an old mirror leaning against a dusty trunk. His beautiful brown and white feathers looked dull, and his big yellow eyes seemed droopy.

"Something needs to change," Oliver said to himself. "But what?"

Oliver thought and thought with his wise owl brain. Then he remembered something his grandmother owl had taught him long ago: "When you don't feel well, look at how you're taking care of yourself, little one."

"That's it!" Oliver hooted excitedly. "I need to make a wellness plan!"

Oliver found an old piece of paper and a pencil. With his wing, he carefully wrote down three important goals:

1. Eat healthy foods every day
2. Exercise my wings and body
3. Get good sleep

"These will be my healthy habits," Oliver said proudly.

The next morning, instead of eating old crackers he found in the attic, Oliver flew outside. The fresh air felt wonderful on his feathers! He gathered fresh berries, crunchy seeds, and clean water from the garden below.

"Mmm, these berries taste like sunshine!" Oliver said as he ate. The sweet, juicy berries made his tummy feel happy and full of energy.

After breakfast, Oliver decided to exercise. He stretched his big wings wide, feeling the muscles wake up. Then he flew around the attic, swooping and gliding. His heart beat faster, and he felt stronger already.

"Flying feels so good!" Oliver laughed as he landed softly on his favorite wooden beam.

When evening came, Oliver made his sleeping area extra cozy. He fluffed up some old, soft fabric and made sure his corner was quiet and dark. Instead of staying up worrying, he closed his eyes and thought about all the good things from his day.

"I ate healthy food, I exercised my body, and now I'm getting good sleep," Oliver whispered sleepily. "Tomorrow I'll do it all again!"

Day after day, Oliver followed his wellness plan. Soon, his feathers became shiny and smooth. His eyes sparkled bright and alert. Best of all, Oliver felt happy and energetic.

One morning, a little mouse named Maya peeked into the attic. "Oliver!" she squeaked. "You look wonderful! What happened?"

Oliver smiled warmly. "I learned that taking care of my body helps me feel good inside and out. I eat healthy foods, exercise every day, and get plenty of sleep. These healthy habits make me feel strong and happy!"

"Can you teach me?" Maya asked excitedly.

"Of course!" Oliver hooted joyfully. "When we take care of ourselves, we can take better care of others too!"

From that day on, Oliver shared his wellness wisdom with all his friends. He learned that setting goals and making healthy choices every day was the secret to feeling his very best.

The End.
