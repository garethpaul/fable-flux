# Miko's Morning Magic

Miko the monkey loved his playful living room. Colorful cushions scattered everywhere made perfect jumping spots. A big soft rug felt wonderful under his furry feet. Sunlight streamed through tall windows, making rainbow patterns on the walls.

But every morning, Miko had a problem. He felt grumpy and tired when he woke up. His brown fur stuck up in funny directions. His tummy rumbled loudly like a little drum.

"Ooh ooh, I don't feel good," Miko groaned. He flopped onto the squishy blue couch. The soft fabric felt nice, but his body still felt yucky.

Miko's wise grandmother had taught him something special. "Little monkeys need good habits to feel strong and happy," she had said. But Miko forgot her words when he felt so sleepy.

He looked around his bright living room. The morning sun made everything sparkle. "Maybe I can figure this out," Miko said, scratching his head with one finger.

First, Miko thought about what made him feel bad. "My tummy hurts," he said out loud. His voice echoed in the big room. "And I feel so tired."

Then Miko remembered something important. Yesterday, he had eaten candy for breakfast instead of healthy food. He had stayed up very late playing instead of sleeping. No wonder he felt awful!

"I need to make better choices," Miko decided. He jumped up from the couch with new energy.

Miko swung over to the kitchen area of his living room. He found fresh bananas in a bright yellow bowl. The sweet smell made his mouth water. "Bananas give me energy," he said happily. He peeled one carefully and took big, satisfying bites.

Next, Miko filled a tall glass with cool, clear water. The water tasted fresh and clean. "Water helps my body work better," he said, remembering grandmother's words. He drank it all up, feeling the coolness in his throat.

After eating, Miko felt much better already. But he knew he needed more healthy habits. He spread out his favorite exercise mat on the soft rug. The mat was bright red with fun pictures of fruits and vegetables.

"Time to move my body," Miko announced. He did jumping jacks on the bouncy mat. His arms and legs moved up and down like a happy dance. Then he stretched his long arms up high, reaching for the ceiling. His muscles felt warm and good.

"Exercise makes me strong," Miko panted, breathing hard but feeling wonderful.

Finally, Miko thought about bedtime. "Tonight, I'll go to sleep early," he promised himself. "Sleep helps my body grow and feel good."

Miko looked around his cheerful living room. Everything seemed brighter now. The colorful cushions looked more fun. The warm sunlight felt perfect on his fur.

"I figured it out!" Miko cheered, doing a little victory dance on the soft rug. "Healthy food, water, exercise, and good sleep make me feel amazing!"

From that day on, Miko followed his healthy routine every morning. He ate nutritious breakfast, drank plenty of water, exercised on his mat, and went to bed on time. Soon, he woke up feeling happy and energetic every single day.

"Being healthy is the best feeling ever," Miko sang, swinging joyfully around his beloved living room.

The End.
