# Miko's Garden Shed Adventure

Miko the monkey loved exploring, but today something was different. While swinging through the garden, he heard a soft crying sound coming from the old wooden shed.

"Hello?" called Miko, his furry ears twitching as he listened carefully. "Is someone in there?"

"Help me, please!" came a tiny voice from inside the shed.

Miko's kind heart wanted to help right away, but he remembered what Mama Monkey always told him: "Think before you act, little one. Use your smart monkey brain!"

Miko took three deep breaths, just like Mama taught him. "Hoo-haa, hoo-haa, hoo-haa." The cool morning air filled his lungs and helped him feel calm.

"Don't worry!" Miko called out. "I'm here to help. But first, can you tell me who you are?"

"I'm Pip the mouse," squeaked the voice. "I came into this shed to find seeds, but the door closed behind me! I can't reach the handle, and I'm scared!"

Miko looked at the tall wooden door. The rusty handle was indeed very high up, even for him. But Miko was resourceful – he knew how to solve problems by using what was around him.

"Pip, I'm going to help you, but I need to be safe too," said Miko. "Can you move away from the door? I'm going to look for something to help me reach the handle."

Miko's bright brown eyes scanned the garden. He saw flower pots, a watering can, and... perfect! A sturdy wooden crate sat near the shed.

"I have an idea!" Miko said excitedly. He pushed the wooden crate – it was heavy, but his strong monkey arms could move it. The crate made a scraping sound against the ground as he positioned it under the door handle.

Carefully, Miko climbed onto the crate. It wobbled just a little, so he spread his arms wide for balance, just like when he walked on tree branches.

"Almost there, Pip!" Miko called out. He stretched his long monkey arm up, up, up until his fingers touched the cold metal handle. Click! The door opened with a creaky sound.

Out scurried Pip, a tiny brown mouse with whiskers that twitched with relief.

"Oh, thank you, Miko!" squeaked Pip. "You saved me!"

"I'm so glad you're safe," said Miko, climbing down from the crate. "But Pip, next time, maybe ask a grown-up mouse to come with you to new places. It's always safer to explore with a friend."

Pip nodded his little head. "You're right! And thank you for being so calm and smart. You didn't just rush in – you thought about how to help safely."

Miko felt proud and warm inside. "My mama always says that being resourceful means using your brain and what's around you to solve problems. And it's important to stay calm so you can think clearly."

"You're the best helper ever!" said Pip, giving Miko's furry hand a tiny high-five.

As they walked out of the garden together, Miko felt happy knowing he had helped someone while keeping both of them safe. He had used his cleverness, stayed calm under pressure, and made sure to protect himself while helping others.

"Want to play together in the safe part of the garden?" asked Miko.

"Yes, please!" squeaked Pip, and off they went to have more fun adventures.

The End.
