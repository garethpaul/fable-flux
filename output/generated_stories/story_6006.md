# Splash the Leader

In a sunny guest room, something magical was happening. A small, shimmery blue dolphin named Splash had somehow found herself in a big fishbowl on the nightstand. The room smelled like fresh flowers and clean sheets.

Splash wiggled her fins and looked around with wonder. "Where am I?" she asked herself. The guest room was full of interesting things - soft pillows, colorful books, and toys scattered on the carpet.

Through the clear glass bowl, Splash could see other toy sea creatures on the floor. There was Shelly the starfish, Finn the little fish, and Ollie the octopus. They all looked sad and confused.

"Help! Help!" squeaked Finn from the carpet. "We're lost! We don't know how to get back to our toy box!"

Splash's heart filled with caring. She wanted to help her new friends, but she was stuck in the fishbowl. She took a deep breath and thought carefully.

"Don't worry, friends!" Splash called out bravely. "I'll help you! But first, I need to get down there."

Splash used her strong tail to rock the fishbowl gently. Rock, rock, rock. She was very patient, moving slowly and carefully. The bowl tilted just enough for the water to spill onto the soft carpet below.

"Wheee!" Splash slid down the water like a fun slide and landed safely on the fluffy rug.

"You're so brave!" said Shelly the starfish, her orange arms wiggling with excitement.

Splash smiled. "Now, let's work together to find your toy box. What does it look like?"

"It's big and blue with a picture of the ocean," said Ollie the octopus, his purple tentacles curling nervously.

Splash looked around the room with her bright eyes. She saw a closet, a dresser, and a chair. "Let's search step by step," she said kindly. "Finn, you check under the bed. Shelly, look by the window. Ollie, search near the dresser. I'll check the closet."

The friends spread out across the guest room. Splash opened the closet door with her nose. Inside, she saw clothes hanging and shoes lined up. But no toy box.

"Not here!" called Finn from under the bed.

"Nothing by the window!" said Shelly.

"Just books over here," sighed Ollie.

Splash didn't give up. She remembered seeing something blue behind the big chair. "Wait! Let's all look behind that chair together!"

The four friends gathered around the chair. They pushed with all their might. "One, two, three, push!" counted Splash.

Behind the chair was the big blue toy box with an ocean picture on it!

"Hooray!" cheered all the sea creatures. "You found it, Splash!"

"We all found it together," said Splash with a warm smile. "Good leaders help everyone work as a team."

One by one, the friends climbed into their cozy toy box home. The box smelled like ocean breeze and felt safe and warm.

"Thank you for helping us, Splash," said Finn gratefully. "You never gave up, even when it was hard."

"And you were so patient with us," added Shelly.

Splash felt proud and happy. "When we set a goal and work together, we can solve any problem!"

As the sun set through the guest room window, all the sea friends snuggled together in their toy box. Splash had learned that being a good leader meant being brave, patient, and kind. She had helped her friends discover they were stronger when they worked together.

"Tomorrow we'll have another adventure," yawned Splash contentedly.

The End.
