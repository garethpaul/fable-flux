# Rascal's Bathroom Band

Rascal the raccoon loved making music more than anything else in the whole wide world. Every morning, he would wake up in his cozy tree home and grab his favorite wooden spoons. *Tap-tap-tap!* he would play on anything he could find.

Today was special because Rascal was getting ready for the Forest Friends Talent Show. He wanted to practice his music, but his tree home was too small. Where could a little raccoon go to make beautiful sounds?

"I know!" Rascal said with a big smile. "The bathroom has the best echo sounds!"

Rascal scampered down to the bathroom with his wooden spoons tucked safely in his fluffy striped tail. The white tiles sparkled in the morning sunlight, and everything smelled fresh and clean like soap bubbles.

*Tap-tap-tap!* Rascal played his spoons on the bathtub. The sound bounced off the walls - *echo, echo, echo!* 

"Wow!" Rascal giggled. "That sounds amazing!"

Just then, he heard a tiny voice. "That's beautiful music! Can I listen?"

Rascal looked around and saw a little green frog sitting by the sink. The frog's eyes were wide with wonder.

"Oh! I'm sorry if I was too loud," Rascal said kindly. "I'm practicing for the talent show. My name is Rascal."

"I'm Lily," said the frog. "I love music, but I can't make any. I only know how to say 'ribbit' in frog language."

Rascal's heart felt warm and fuzzy. He could see that Lily looked a little sad. Then he had a wonderful idea!

"Lily, your 'ribbit' sounds are music too! Every language is special and beautiful. Would you like to make music together?"

Lily's face lit up like sunshine. "Really? You think my ribbits are musical?"

"Absolutely!" Rascal said with excitement. "Listen to this!"

Rascal began tapping his spoons on the bathtub: *Tap-tap-tap, tap-tap!* 

"Now you try your ribbit with my rhythm!" Rascal encouraged.

Lily took a deep breath and sang: "Ribbit-ribbit-ribbit, ribbit-ribbit!"

The bathroom filled with the most wonderful music! The tapping and ribbiting echoed off the tiles, creating a magical sound that made both friends smile from ear to ear.

"We sound fantastic together!" Rascal cheered. "Your frog language makes our music even more special!"

They practiced together for a whole hour. Rascal shared his wooden spoons, and Lily shared her beautiful ribbit songs. Soon, other forest friends heard their music and came to listen.

"This is the most beautiful music I've ever heard!" said Bella the bunny.

"You two make such a great team!" added Oliver the owl.

At the talent show that evening, Rascal and Lily performed their bathroom band music together. All the forest friends clapped and cheered. They won first place for "Most Creative Musical Team!"

After the show, Lily hugged Rascal tight. "Thank you for being so kind and for helping me see that my voice is special too."

Rascal hugged her back. "Thank you for sharing your beautiful language with me. Music is always better when we share it with friends!"

From that day on, Rascal and Lily practiced together every morning in the bathroom. They learned that when we are kind and share what we love with others, we create something even more wonderful than we could make alone.

The End.
