# Story story_1124

**Bouncy's Bath Time Adventure**

Bouncy was a bright red rubber ball. He lived in Tommy's toy box. All day long, Bouncy rolled and bounced around the house.

"Bath time!" called Mommy from upstairs.

Tommy scooped up Bouncy. "You're coming too," he said with a smile.

Bouncy felt worried. Water seemed scary. What if he sank? What if he got too slippery? Bouncy had never taken a bath before.

Tommy carried Bouncy up the stairs. The bathroom smelled like lavender soap. Warm steam floated in the air. The bathtub looked very big and deep.

"In you go," said Tommy. He placed Bouncy on the bathroom counter.

Bouncy watched Tommy climb into the tub. The water made gentle splashing sounds. It looked warm and bubbly. But Bouncy still felt nervous.

"Come on, Bouncy," Tommy called. "The water feels nice!"

Bouncy rolled closer to the edge. The water sparkled under the bright lights. Tiny bubbles danced on top. They looked like little clouds.

Tommy reached for Bouncy. "Don't worry. I'll hold you."

Bouncy took a deep breath. Tommy's hands felt safe and warm. Slowly, Tommy lowered Bouncy toward the water.

The first touch felt amazing! The water was perfectly warm. It tickled Bouncy's rubber surface. He didn't sink at all. Instead, he floated like a tiny boat!

"Look!" Tommy laughed. "You're floating!"

Bouncy bobbed up and down. The water felt soft and gentle. Bubbles stuck to his red surface. They made him look sparkly and new.

Tommy pushed Bouncy gently. Bouncy glided across the water. It felt like flying! He spun in happy circles. The bathroom filled with giggles and splashes.

"You're a boat!" Tommy said. "Sail away, Captain Bouncy!"

Bouncy loved his new game. He sailed between Tommy's knees. He rode the gentle waves Tommy made. The warm water made him feel relaxed and happy.

Bubbles covered Bouncy like fluffy snow. He could see his reflection in each tiny bubble. His red color looked brighter than ever. The soap made everything smell like flowers.

Tommy scooped up water in his hands. He let it pour over Bouncy like rain. Bouncy enjoyed the gentle shower. The water drops felt like tiny massages.

"This is fun!" Bouncy thought to himself.

They played submarine next. Bouncy dove under the water. Everything looked magical underwater. The light looked green and dreamy. Bouncy popped back up with a splash.

Tommy clapped his hands. "You're the best bath toy ever!"

Bouncy felt proud and happy. Bath time wasn't scary at all. It was the most fun he'd had all day! The warm water made his rubber feel soft and clean.

Soon, Mommy came to check on them. "Time to get out," she said gently.

Tommy lifted Bouncy from the water. Bouncy dripped and sparkled. He felt fresh and squeaky clean. His red color looked bright and shiny.

Mommy wrapped Tommy in a fluffy towel. She dried Bouncy with a soft cloth. The towel felt warm and cozy. Bouncy loved feeling clean and dry.

"Did you have fun, Bouncy?" Tommy asked.

If Bouncy could talk, he would say yes! Bath time had been wonderful. The water was warm and gentle. Playing boats was exciting. Getting clean felt good too.

Tommy carried Bouncy back to the toy box. "Tomorrow we'll take another bath," Tommy whispered. "Would you like that?"

Bouncy felt excited about tomorrow. He couldn't wait for bath time again! Maybe they could play different water games. Maybe he could meet other bath toys.

As Tommy tucked him into the toy box, Bouncy felt grateful. He had learned something important today. New things might seem scary at first. But trying new things can lead to wonderful adventures.

Bouncy closed his eyes and smiled. Tomorrow's bath time couldn't come soon enough. He dreamed of floating on warm water. He dreamed of bubbles and splashes and fun.

Bath time was now his favorite time of day.

The end.
