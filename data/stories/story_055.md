# Lily the Frog's Lost Treasure

Lily the little green frog loved the beach. She hopped across the warm, golden sand. Her webbed feet made tiny prints. The ocean waves sang a gentle song.

"Splash, splash, whoosh!" went the waves.

Lily carried her most special thing. It was her favorite book! The book had shiny pictures inside. It told stories about brave frogs. Lily read it every single day.

She found the perfect spot. A big rock made cool shade. Lily sat down and opened her book. The pages fluttered in the ocean breeze.

"I love this story," Lily said happily.

The wind grew stronger. It blew harder and harder. Suddenly - WHOOSH! The wind grabbed Lily's book! It flew high into the air.

"My book!" cried Lily. "Come back!"

The book tumbled and spun. It landed somewhere in the sand dunes. Tall grass waved everywhere. The book disappeared completely.

Lily's eyes filled with tears. "Where did it go?" she whispered. Her voice was very sad.

But Lily was a brave little frog. She took a deep breath. "I will find my book," she said firmly. "I won't give up!"

First, Lily hopped to the nearest sand dune. She looked behind every piece of grass. She moved shells and seaweed. No book anywhere.

"Maybe it's over there," Lily thought.

She hopped to the next dune. This one was bigger. Lily searched and searched. She looked under driftwood. She checked near tide pools. Still no book.

Lily felt tired. Her little legs were getting sore. But she remembered the brave frogs in her stories. They never gave up!

"One more place," Lily told herself.

She hopped to the tallest dune. This one had the most grass. Lily started at the bottom. She looked carefully everywhere. She moved slowly and checked each spot.

Halfway up the dune, something caught her eye. A corner of something colorful! It was peeking out from under some seaweed.

"Could it be?" Lily whispered excitedly.

She hopped closer. She gently moved the seaweed away. There it was! Her beautiful, special book! The cover was a little sandy. But it was perfectly safe.

"My book! My wonderful book!" Lily cheered. She hugged it tight against her chest. Happy tears rolled down her cheeks.

A friendly seagull landed nearby. "You found your treasure!" the seagull said kindly.

"Yes! I never stopped looking," Lily replied proudly. "Even when I felt tired. Even when I felt sad. I kept trying!"

The seagull nodded wisely. "That's what brave frogs do. They don't give up on important things."

Lily carefully carried her book back. She found a safer spot. This place had no wind at all. She sat against a smooth rock.

She opened her book gently. The pages were perfect! Not one was torn. The shiny pictures still sparkled. Lily smiled her biggest smile.

"Thank you, book, for waiting for me," she said softly.

As Lily read her favorite story, she felt very proud. She had solved her big problem! She didn't give up when things got hard. She kept trying until she succeeded.

The ocean waves still sang their gentle song. The warm sand felt cozy under Lily. The sun painted everything golden and beautiful.

"Tomorrow I'll be more careful," Lily thought. "But today I learned something important. I learned I'm braver than I knew!"

She read her book until the sunset. Pink and orange colors filled the sky. Lily felt happy and proud inside. Her special book was safe in her arms.

When it was time to go home, Lily hopped carefully. She held her book very tight. She would always remember this day. The day she learned that trying hard helps solve problems.

"I'm a brave, smart frog," Lily said to herself. And she truly was.

The end.
