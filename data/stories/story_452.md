# Truck's Garden Discovery

Beep beep! The morning sun peeked through the window. Truck yawned and stretched his big wheels. Mama called from the kitchen, "Truck! Time for garden time!"

Truck's engine made a grumbly sound. Garden time happened every day. Every single day! He rolled slowly to the back door. The garden looked the same as always. Green plants everywhere. Brown dirt paths. Nothing exciting at all.

"Do we have to?" Truck asked. His red paint looked dull in the morning light.

"Yes, little one," Mama smiled. "Plants need care every day. Just like you need breakfast every day!"

Truck rolled outside with heavy wheels. The grass felt bumpy under his tires. He parked next to the tomato plants. They hung heavy and red. Truck sighed a big, whooshy sigh.

Mama handed him a small watering can. "Start with the carrots, please."

Truck drove to the carrot patch. Orange tops poked through the soil. He tilted the watering can. Water splashed down. Drip, drop, splash!

Suddenly, something tickled his bumper. A tiny green caterpillar wiggled hello! "Hi there!" Truck giggled. The caterpillar crawled onto his hood.

"Look, Mama! I found a friend!"

Mama smiled. "Caterpillars love gardens too!"

Truck rolled to the next row. His new friend rode along. The lettuce leaves sparkled with water drops. They looked like tiny diamonds! Truck had never noticed before.

"Pretty!" he said, driving closer.

A butterfly landed on his antenna! Its wings were orange and black. It fluttered and danced. "Another friend!" Truck laughed.

The butterfly flew to a sunflower. The sunflower was taller than Truck! Its face was bright yellow. Like sunshine in flower form.

"Wow," Truck whispered.

He rolled between the bean plants. They climbed up tall poles. Green beans hung down like nature's decorations. Truck reached up with his crane arm. He picked a bean for Mama.

"Thank you, Truck!" she said. "You're such a good helper!"

Truck's engine purred with pride. His red paint seemed shinier now.

Near the strawberry patch, Truck discovered something amazing. Baby strawberries! Tiny white flowers would become red berries. "Magic!" Truck gasped.

A robin hopped nearby. It pecked at the ground. "Tweet, tweet!" it sang. Truck beeped back softly. "Beep, beep!"

The robin tilted its head. Then it sang again! They made music together. Bird songs and truck beeps!

Truck rolled to the herb garden. Mama showed him how to smell the mint. "Sniff gently," she said.

Truck's air intake breathed in. "Mmm! Fresh and cool!"

The basil smelled different. Sweet and strong. The rosemary was piney. Each plant had its own special smell!

"Gardens are like treasure boxes," Mama explained. "Full of surprises every day."

Truck nodded eagerly. He was finding treasures everywhere! A fuzzy bee buzzed past. Worms wiggled in the soil. Everything was alive and busy!

In the pumpkin patch, Truck found the biggest surprise. A tiny pumpkin! Just his size! It was green now, but Mama said it would turn orange.

"Can I watch it grow?" Truck asked.

"Every day," Mama promised. "That's the best part of garden time."

Truck drove around one more time. The garden looked completely different now. Not boring at all! It was full of friends and treasures. Growing things and flying things. Sweet smells and pretty colors.

"Time to go in," Mama called.

"Already?" Truck couldn't believe it. Garden time had gone so fast!

He parked his watering can carefully. "Thank you, garden friends!" he called. The caterpillar waved from a leaf.

That night, Truck dreamed about butterflies. He dreamed about his tiny pumpkin growing bigger. He dreamed about singing with robins.

When morning came, Truck's wheels were already moving. "Mama!" he called excitedly. "Is it garden time yet?"

Mama laughed. "Good morning to you too! Yes, it's almost time."

Truck beep-beeped happily. Today he would check on his pumpkin. Maybe find new treasures. Maybe make new friends.

Garden time wasn't just a daily chore anymore. It was a daily adventure! Full of magic and wonder. And the best part? Tomorrow would bring even more surprises.

Truck couldn't wait!

The end.
