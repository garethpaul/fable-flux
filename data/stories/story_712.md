# Story story_712

**Bella's Missing Doll**

Bella Bunny loved bath time. She loved the warm, bubbly water. She loved her yellow rubber duck. Most of all, she loved playing with Pretty Penny, her favorite doll.

Pretty Penny had soft pink hair. She wore a sparkly blue dress. Bella took Pretty Penny everywhere, even to the bathroom.

One morning, Bella hopped into the bathroom. She looked around for Pretty Penny. The doll was not on the sink. She was not by the bathtub either.

"Oh no!" cried Bella. "Where is Pretty Penny?"

Bella's whiskers twitched with worry. Her little heart felt sad. Pretty Penny was missing!

Bella looked under the fluffy bath mat. She peeked behind the toilet. She checked inside the laundry basket. No Pretty Penny anywhere.

"Mama!" called Bella. "I cannot find Pretty Penny!"

Mama Bunny hopped into the bathroom. Her ears drooped when she saw Bella's sad face.

"Let's look together, sweetie," said Mama gently.

Bella had an idea. "Maybe Pretty Penny fell in the bathtub!"

Bella climbed onto the edge of the big white tub. She leaned over to look inside. The tub was deep and empty.

"Be careful, Bella," warned Mama.

Bella reached her paw down into the tub. She stretched and stretched. Her little arms were too short. She could not reach the bottom.

"I cannot get down there," said Bella sadly. A tiny tear rolled down her furry cheek.

Mama gave Bella a warm hug. "Do not give up yet. Let's think of another way."

Bella wiped her eyes with her soft paws. She thought very hard. Her little nose wiggled as she concentrated.

"Wait!" said Bella. "I have another idea!"

Bella remembered something important. Last night, she gave Pretty Penny a pretend bath. She used the small pink basin from under the sink.

Bella hopped down to the cabinet. She opened the squeaky door. Inside were towels and soap. She moved them aside carefully.

There, behind a big bottle of shampoo, sat Pretty Penny!

"Pretty Penny!" squealed Bella with joy.

The little doll was safe and sound. Her pink hair was still soft. Her sparkly dress still shined. She smiled her painted smile at Bella.

Bella picked up Pretty Penny and hugged her tight. The doll felt warm and familiar in her arms.

"You were hiding, silly doll," giggled Bella.

Mama Bunny smiled proudly. "You did not give up, Bella. You kept trying until you found her."

Bella's ears perked up with happiness. "I looked in the tub first. That did not work. Then I remembered the basin!"

"That's right," said Mama. "Sometimes the first try does not work. But if we keep thinking and trying, we can solve our problems."

Bella nodded her little head. She felt proud and smart. She had found Pretty Penny all by herself!

"Can we take a real bath now?" asked Bella.

"Of course!" laughed Mama.

Mama filled the tub with warm water. She added sweet-smelling bubbles. The bathroom filled with the scent of lavender.

Bella and Pretty Penny splashed happily in the tub. The water felt wonderful on Bella's fur. The bubbles tickled her nose.

"I will never lose you again," Bella whispered to Pretty Penny.

After their bath, Bella dried Pretty Penny with a soft towel. She brushed the doll's pink hair until it was fluffy again.

That night, Bella put Pretty Penny in a special place. She set her on the nightstand by her bed. Now Pretty Penny would always be safe.

Bella learned something important that day. When something goes wrong, do not give up. Keep thinking of new ideas. Keep trying different ways. The answer might be hiding somewhere nearby.

As Bella drifted off to sleep, she hugged Pretty Penny close. Tomorrow would bring new adventures. But tonight, they were together and happy.

The end.
