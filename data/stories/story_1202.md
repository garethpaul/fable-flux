# Story story_1202

**Bella's Sleepy Time Adventure**

The sun was getting low in the sky. Golden light came through the window. It was almost nap time at Miss Sara's daycare.

"Time to get ready for naps," Miss Sara said softly. She smiled at all the children. "Everyone find your mats."

Bella hugged her favorite puppet tight. The puppet was soft and purple. It had a silly smile and floppy ears. Bella called her puppet Giggles.

"I don't want to nap," Bella whispered to Giggles. "Nap time is boring. I want to keep playing."

Giggles just smiled her sewn-on smile. Her button eyes looked sleepy.

All around the room, children were lying down. They had their blankets and stuffed animals. The room was getting quiet and peaceful.

Bella put her mat in the corner. She lay down but kept her eyes wide open. She stared at the ceiling. She wiggled her toes. She was not sleepy at all.

"Close your eyes, Bella," Miss Sara said gently. "Rest time helps you grow big and strong."

Bella squeezed her eyes shut. But she opened them right away. She looked at Giggles lying next to her.

Suddenly, something amazing happened. Giggles winked at Bella! Her button eye actually winked.

"Did you see that?" Bella whispered very quietly.

Giggles nodded her soft purple head. Then she sat up on the mat.

"Want to go on an adventure?" Giggles asked in the tiniest voice.

Bella's eyes grew wide with wonder. Her puppet was talking to her!

"Where can we go?" Bella asked.

"To Dreamland," Giggles said. "But you have to close your eyes first."

Bella closed her eyes tight. She felt herself getting smaller and smaller. Soon she was puppet-size too!

Giggles took her hand. They floated up like feathers. They went through the window and into the soft clouds.

"Look!" Giggles pointed with her paw.

Below them was a magical land. There were candy trees with gummy bear leaves. Rivers flowed with warm chocolate milk. Marshmallow flowers grew everywhere.

They landed on a bouncy cloud bed. It felt like the softest pillow ever. The cloud smelled like vanilla cookies.

"This is where tired children come to rest," Giggles explained. "Their dreams turn into beautiful things here."

Bella saw other children floating on clouds. They were all smiling in their sleep. Pretty butterflies made of rainbow colors flew around them.

"The butterflies are made from happy dreams," Giggles said.

A gentle breeze rocked their cloud bed. It felt so cozy and warm. Bella felt her eyelids getting heavy.

"I feel sleepy now," Bella said with a yawn.

"That's perfect," Giggles said. "When you sleep, you help make this place more beautiful."

Bella curled up on the soft cloud. Giggles snuggled close beside her. The marshmallow flowers sang a quiet lullaby.

"Sleep tight, little dreamer," the flowers sang sweetly.

Bella drifted off to sleep. She dreamed of flying with friendly dragons. She dreamed of tea parties with talking animals. All her dreams turned into colorful butterflies.

When Bella woke up, she was back on her mat. The room was quiet and peaceful. Other children were still sleeping softly.

Miss Sara was reading a book nearby. She looked up and smiled at Bella.

"Did you have a good rest?" Miss Sara asked quietly.

Bella nodded and hugged Giggles tight. The puppet felt extra special now.

"I went to Dreamland," Bella whispered.

"That sounds wonderful," Miss Sara said with a knowing smile.

Bella looked at Giggles. The puppet had her regular sewn-on smile. But Bella was sure she saw another tiny wink.

The next day at nap time, Bella was excited. She put her mat down quickly. She couldn't wait to lie down with Giggles.

"Are we going back to Dreamland?" she asked her purple friend.

Giggles just smiled. But Bella knew the answer.

She closed her eyes happily. Soon she was floating on clouds again. Nap time had become her favorite time of day.

And every day after that, Bella looked forward to her magical adventures in Dreamland with Giggles.

The end.
