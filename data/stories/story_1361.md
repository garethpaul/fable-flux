# Story story_1361

**Penny's Magical Bath Time**

Penny the pig rolled in the soft mud. She loved how it felt cool and squishy. The brown mud covered her pink skin completely.

"Bath time, Penny!" called Mama Pig.

Penny's ears drooped down. She did not want a bath. The mud felt so nice and warm. Why did she have to wash it off?

"I don't want to," Penny said sadly. She tried to hide behind the big oak tree.

Mama Pig smiled gently. "Come on, little one. Bath time can be fun too."

Penny walked very slowly to the farmhouse. Her little hooves made soft sounds on the wooden porch. She could smell the sweet lavender soap waiting for her.

The bathtub was big and white. Mama Pig turned on the warm water. It made a gentle splashing sound as it filled the tub.

"The water is too hot," Penny complained.

Mama Pig tested it with her hoof. "Just right," she said kindly. "In you go."

Penny stepped into the warm water. It felt different than the mud. The water was clean and clear. But something strange started to happen.

Tiny bubbles began to float up from the soap. They shimmered like little rainbows in the light. Penny's eyes grew wide with wonder.

"Look, Mama!" Penny squealed with delight. "Flying bubbles!"

More and more bubbles filled the air. They tickled Penny's nose when they popped. She giggled and tried to catch them with her hooves.

The bubbles made the bathroom smell like fresh flowers. Some bubbles were big and round. Others were tiny and quick. They danced all around Penny's head.

"This is magic!" Penny laughed happily.

Mama Pig smiled and added more soap. Even more bubbles appeared. They covered Penny like a fluffy white blanket. Penny felt like she was sitting in a cloud.

She splashed gently with her hooves. The warm water felt good on her skin. The bubbles made funny popping sounds. Pop, pop, pop!

"I can make bubble mountains!" Penny discovered. She piled the bubbles up high. They looked like snowy hills in her bathtub.

Mama Pig washed Penny's back with a soft cloth. It felt nice and gentle. The soap smelled like spring flowers and sunshine.

"You're getting so clean and pretty," Mama Pig said lovingly.

Penny looked at her reflection in the water. Her pink skin was bright and shiny. She felt fresh and new. The bubbles still danced around her.

"Can I play more?" Penny asked hopefully.

"A little bit longer," Mama Pig agreed.

Penny made bubble beards on her chin. She blew gently at the bubbles. They floated away like tiny balloons. Some stuck to the bathroom walls.

The warm water made Penny feel cozy. It was like sitting in a warm hug. She had never noticed how nice bath time could be.

"Time to get out now," Mama Pig said softly.

Penny stood up slowly. The water dripped off her clean pink skin. Mama Pig wrapped her in a big fluffy towel. It was warm and soft like a cloud.

"You smell wonderful," Mama Pig said. She dried Penny's ears gently.

Penny looked in the mirror. She sparkled and shined. Her skin felt smooth and soft. She smelled like beautiful flowers.

"I liked the magic bubbles," Penny said happily. "They made bath time special."

Mama Pig hugged her tight. "Every bath can be magical if you let it."

That night, Penny dreamed about floating bubbles. She dreamed about warm water and flower smells. She dreamed about feeling clean and fresh.

The next evening, Penny heard the water running. She knew what that sound meant. Bath time was coming again.

This time, Penny ran quickly to the bathroom. Her little hooves clicked excitedly on the floor.

"Can we make the magic bubbles again?" she asked with bright eyes.

Mama Pig smiled proudly. "Of course we can, my sweet girl."

Penny couldn't wait to see what magic would happen tonight. Bath time had become her favorite part of the day.

The end.
