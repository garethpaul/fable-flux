# Story story_1253

**Benny's Magical Nap Time**

The sun was high and bright. Mama Bunny looked at the clock. "Nap time, Benny!" she called softly.

Benny was playing with his toy cars. Zoom, zoom, zoom! The red car raced around the track. The blue car followed close behind.

"But Mama, I'm not sleepy," Benny said. His little nose twitched. "Can I play five more minutes?"

Mama Bunny smiled gently. "Your body needs rest to grow big and strong. Come, let's make your bed cozy."

Benny's ears drooped down. He walked slowly to his room. His feet felt heavy like rocks. Nap time was so boring!

Mama tucked the soft blue blanket around him. She fluffed his pillow just right. "Sweet dreams, little bunny," she whispered.

Benny closed his eyes tight. But sleep wouldn't come. He wiggled his toes. He turned left, then right. The room felt too quiet.

Suddenly, something sparkled near his window. Tiny golden lights danced in the air. They looked like fireflies, but brighter!

"Hello, Benny," said a tiny, musical voice.

Benny sat up quickly. A fairy no bigger than his thumb sat on his windowsill. She had silver wings that shimmered. Her dress looked like flower petals.

"I'm Luna, the Dream Fairy," she said with a smile. "I help little ones have wonderful naps."

Benny's eyes grew wide. "A real fairy?"

Luna giggled like tiny silver bells. "Would you like to visit the Dream Garden? It's where all the best dreams grow."

Benny nodded fast. His ears perked up with excitement.

Luna waved her tiny wand. Sparkles filled the air. They smelled like warm cookies and fresh flowers. The room began to spin gently.

When everything stopped spinning, Benny gasped. He stood in the most beautiful garden ever. Rainbow flowers swayed in the breeze. Crystal streams bubbled and sang.

"This is amazing!" Benny whispered.

Giant mushrooms dotted the grass like colorful umbrellas. Butterflies with wings like stained glass flew everywhere. The air tasted sweet like honey.

"Each flower holds a different dream," Luna explained. "The pink ones have adventure dreams. The yellow ones have happy dreams."

Benny touched a purple flower gently. Suddenly, he was flying through fluffy white clouds. The wind tickled his whiskers. Below, the world looked like a patchwork quilt.

"This is the best!" he laughed.

They visited dream after dream. Benny rode a friendly dragon. He swam with dolphins in a warm ocean. He built sandcastles that reached the sky.

In one dream, he was a brave knight. He saved a village from a grumpy dragon. But instead of fighting, he made the dragon laugh. Soon they were best friends.

"Dreams can take you anywhere," Luna said softly. "They help your mind rest and play."

Benny felt his eyelids getting heavy. The magical garden grew fuzzy around the edges. "I feel sleepy now," he yawned.

"That means the dream magic is working," Luna smiled. "Close your eyes, little bunny."

Benny snuggled into his pillow. It felt extra soft and warm. The Dream Garden faded away slowly. But the happy feelings stayed in his heart.

When he opened his eyes, golden sunlight filled his room. Mama Bunny sat in the rocking chair nearby.

"Did you have a good nap?" she asked.

Benny stretched his arms up high. "Mama, I had the most wonderful dreams! I flew with butterflies and rode a dragon!"

Mama's eyes twinkled. "Dreams are very special, aren't they?"

Benny nodded happily. His body felt rested and strong. His mind felt clear and bright.

That evening at bedtime, Benny asked, "Mama, will Luna visit again tomorrow?"

"Close your eyes and see," Mama whispered.

Benny smiled as he drifted off to sleep. He couldn't wait for tomorrow's nap time. Dreams were the best adventure of all!

From that day on, Benny never complained about naps again.

The end.
