# Story story_879

**The Hallway Friends**

Turtle sat alone in the long hallway. The smooth floor felt cool under his shell. Sunlight streamed through the big window. It made pretty patterns on the walls.

Turtle pushed his red ball slowly. It rolled and stopped. He pushed it again. The ball made a soft bouncing sound. But playing alone wasn't very fun.

"I wish I had a friend," Turtle whispered. His voice echoed in the quiet hallway.

Down the hall, Dog heard something. His ears perked up. He trotted over on his soft paws. His tail wagged when he saw Turtle.

"Hello!" Dog said with a friendly bark. "What are you doing?"

Turtle looked up. Dog had fluffy brown fur and kind eyes. "I'm playing with my ball," Turtle said. "But it's not much fun alone."

Dog's tail wagged faster. "Can I play too?" he asked. "I love balls!"

Turtle smiled for the first time all day. "Yes, please! That would be wonderful."

Dog gently nudged the red ball with his nose. It rolled to Turtle. The ball felt warm from Dog's touch.

Turtle pushed it back with his front foot. The ball rolled slowly across the smooth floor. Dog caught it and rolled it back.

"This is fun!" Turtle laughed. His laugh sounded like tiny bells.

But then the ball rolled too far. It went under a tall bookshelf. Both friends tried to reach it.

Dog's paws were too big. Turtle was too slow to crawl under. They both sat down and looked sad.

"What can we do?" Dog asked. His ears drooped down.

Turtle thought hard. His little brain worked like a puzzle. "I have an idea!" he said. "You can lift me up!"

Dog carefully picked up Turtle. Turtle felt safe in Dog's gentle mouth. Dog lifted him high enough to see the ball.

"I can reach it with my long neck!" Turtle said.

Turtle stretched his neck under the shelf. His neck was just long enough. He grabbed the ball with his mouth.

"Got it!" Turtle said proudly.

Dog set Turtle down gently. They both felt very happy. They had solved the problem together.

"You're so smart!" Dog said. His tail made a happy whooshing sound.

"You're so strong!" Turtle replied. "We make a great team!"

They played for a long time. The ball rolled back and forth. Sometimes Dog would run fast and catch it. Sometimes Turtle would hide it under his shell.

The hallway filled with sounds of joy. Dog's happy barks echoed off the walls. Turtle's giggles bounced around like music.

When they got tired, they sat together. Dog's warm fur felt cozy next to Turtle's smooth shell. The afternoon sun made them both sleepy.

"Thank you for playing with me," Turtle said. "I was lonely before."

"Thank you for letting me join," Dog said. "Friends make everything better."

They watched the sunlight dance on the walls. The hallway didn't feel empty anymore. It felt full of friendship and fun.

"Can we play again tomorrow?" Turtle asked hopefully.

"Every day!" Dog promised. "We're best friends now!"

Turtle's shell felt warm with happiness. Dog's tail wagged so hard his whole body wiggled.

From that day on, the hallway was never quiet. Every morning, Turtle and Dog met there. They played games and told stories. They helped each other when things went wrong.

Other animals heard their laughter. Soon, Cat and Rabbit joined them too. The hallway became the happiest place in the whole house.

But Turtle and Dog always remembered. They were each other's first friend in the hallway. That made their friendship extra special.

As the sun set that first day, they made a promise. "Friends forever," they said together.

And they kept that promise every single day.

The end.
