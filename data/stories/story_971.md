# Story story_971

**Tiger's Magical Nap Time**

Tiger stretched his orange paws wide. The sun was high in the sky. Birds chirped softly in the trees.

"Nap time, Tiger," called Mama from inside.

Tiger's ears drooped down. He did not want to nap. The grass felt so warm under his paws. Butterflies danced in the garden. A red ball sat nearby.

"But I'm not sleepy," Tiger said. His tail swished back and forth.

Mama smiled gently. "Come inside, little one. Your bed is waiting."

Tiger walked slowly to his room. His feet felt heavy like rocks. The blue blanket looked boring. His pillow seemed too fluffy.

He climbed onto his bed. The sheets felt cool and soft. Tiger closed his eyes tight. But sleep would not come.

"I wish nap time was fun," he whispered.

Suddenly, something tickled his nose. Tiger opened one eye. A tiny, glowing butterfly sat on his whiskers!

"Hello, Tiger," the butterfly said in a voice like tinkling bells. "I'm Luna."

Tiger's eyes grew wide. "You can talk!"

Luna's wings sparkled with silver dust. "I help children have magical naps. Would you like to see?"

Tiger nodded fast. His heart felt excited now.

"Close your eyes and count to three," Luna said.

Tiger squeezed his eyes shut. "One, two, three!"

When he opened them, his room had changed! Soft clouds floated near the ceiling. Stars twinkled like diamonds. His bed became a cozy boat.

"Welcome to the Dream Sea," Luna said.

The boat rocked gently on calm, blue water. Tiger felt warm and safe. Sleepy dolphins jumped nearby. They made soft, happy sounds.

"Look over there," Luna pointed with her tiny wing.

A family of sea turtles swam past. Their shells shone green and gold. Baby turtles rode on their parents' backs.

Tiger yawned. The rocking motion felt so nice. His eyelids grew heavy like soft curtains.

"The Dream Sea makes you sleepy," Luna explained. "But it also gives you wonderful dreams."

Tiger lay down in his boat-bed. The blanket felt like a warm hug. Gentle waves made quiet splashing sounds.

"Will you stay with me?" Tiger asked.

"Always," Luna said. She landed on his pillow.

Tiger's breathing became slow and deep. He dreamed of swimming with friendly whales. They sang beautiful songs together. Rainbow fish danced around him.

In his dream, Tiger rode on a seahorse. They visited a castle made of coral. Mermaids with kind faces waved hello.

The sun in his dream felt warm on his fur. Ocean breezes smelled like flowers. Everything felt peaceful and happy.

Tiger slept for a long, long time.

When he woke up, Luna was gone. His room looked normal again. But he felt wonderful! His body felt strong and rested.

Mama peeked in the door. "How was your nap, sweetie?"

"Amazing!" Tiger said. His voice sounded bright and cheerful. "I went to the Dream Sea!"

Mama sat on his bed. "Tell me about it."

Tiger shared his magical adventure. Mama listened with a big smile. Her eyes twinkled with joy.

"Dreams make nap time special," Mama said. She gave him a warm hug.

Tiger hopped out of bed. He felt full of energy now. The afternoon sun looked golden and pretty.

They went outside together. Tiger played with his red ball. But he felt different now. Nap time wasn't scary anymore.

At dinner, Tiger told Papa about Luna. Papa's eyebrows went up with surprise.

"Magic butterflies sound wonderful," Papa said.

That evening, Tiger brushed his teeth extra well. He put on his favorite pajamas. The stars outside looked familiar now.

"Tomorrow I want to nap again," Tiger told Mama.

"Really?" Mama asked. Her smile was huge.

"Yes! Maybe Luna will take me somewhere new."

Tiger climbed into bed happily. He pulled his soft blanket up to his chin. His pillow felt just right.

"Good night, Tiger," Mama said. She kissed his forehead.

"Good night, Mama. Good night, Luna," Tiger whispered.

He closed his eyes with a big smile. Tomorrow's nap time would be another adventure.

Sleep came easily now. Tiger dreamed of flying with Luna to candy cloud mountains.

Nap time had become his favorite time of day.

The end.
