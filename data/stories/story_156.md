# Benny and the Musical Swing

Benny sat on the big red swing. He pushed with his feet slowly. Back and forth, back and forth. The playground was very quiet today.

"I wish someone would play with me," Benny said softly. He looked around the empty playground. No friends were here today.

Benny tried to swing higher. But swinging alone wasn't very fun. He dragged his feet in the dirt. The swing stopped moving.

Just then, he heard a happy sound. *Boom-boom-tap!* *Boom-boom-tap!*

A small, shiny drum rolled toward the swings. The drum had bright blue stripes. Two wooden drumsticks bounced beside it.

"Hello!" called the drum cheerfully. "I'm Danny the Drum. Can I play here too?"

Benny looked surprised. "You can talk?"

"Of course I can!" Danny laughed. "I make music everywhere I go. What's your name?"

"I'm Benny," he said shyly. "I was just swinging by myself."

Danny rolled closer to the swing set. "Swinging looks like fun! But I don't have legs like you."

Benny thought for a moment. "Maybe we can play together anyway."

"How?" asked Danny, sounding curious.

Benny had an idea. "You could make music while I swing!"

Danny's drumsticks wiggled with excitement. "That sounds wonderful! Let's try it!"

Benny started swinging again. Danny began to play. *Boom-boom-tap!* went the drum as Benny swung forward. *Tap-tap-boom!* went the drum as Benny swung back.

"This is amazing!" Benny laughed. "Your music makes swinging feel like flying!"

"Your swinging gives my music a beat!" Danny replied happily.

They played together for a long time. Benny would swing fast, and Danny would play fast music. When Benny swung slowly, Danny played gentle, soft rhythms.

"I have another idea," said Benny. "What if we make up a song together?"

"Yes!" Danny exclaimed. "I love making new songs!"

Benny began to sing as he swung: "Swinging high, swinging low..."

Danny added the beat: *Boom-boom-tap!*

"Making music as we go!" Benny continued.

*Tap-tap-boom!* Danny played along.

"Friends can play in different ways..." they sang together.

*Boom-boom-tap!*

"Music and swinging fill our days!"

*Tap-tap-boom!*

Soon, other children heard their happy song. They came running to the playground.

"What a wonderful sound!" said a little girl.

"Can we join your game?" asked a boy.

Benny and Danny looked at each other. They both smiled big smiles.

"Of course!" said Benny. "There are swings for everyone!"

"And I can make music for all of you!" added Danny.

The children climbed onto the other swings. Danny played his drum for everyone. Each child swung to Danny's cheerful beat.

Some children swung in circles. Others pumped their legs high. One little girl swung very gently. Danny made different music for each style of swinging.

"This is the best playground music ever!" shouted the children.

Benny felt so happy. His heart was as light as a feather. He wasn't lonely anymore.

"Thank you for being my friend, Danny," Benny said during a quiet moment.

"Thank you for showing me how to play with others," Danny replied. "I used to roll around by myself. Now I have lots of friends!"

As the sun began to set, the children waved goodbye. But Benny and Danny stayed together.

"Will you come back tomorrow?" Benny asked.

"Every day!" promised Danny. "We make the best team!"

They decided to end their day with one more song. Benny swung gently as Danny played a soft, sleepy rhythm.

"Friends who share and play together," Benny sang quietly.

*Tap-tap-tap,* whispered Danny.

"Make each day so much better."

*Tap-tap-tap.*

From that day on, Benny and Danny played together every afternoon. They learned that friendship means sharing what you love. Benny shared his swinging, and Danny shared his music.

Together, they made the playground a happier place for everyone. And they were never lonely again.

The end.
