# Story story_1126

**Peppy Pig and the Missing Teddy**

Peppy Pig woke up in his cozy bedroom. The morning sun peeked through his yellow curtains. He stretched his little pink arms and yawned.

"Time to hug my teddy bear!" Peppy said happily.

He looked to his left. No teddy bear. He looked to his right. Still no teddy bear. Peppy sat up in his soft bed and looked around.

"Where is my teddy bear?" he wondered aloud.

Peppy's teddy bear was brown and fluffy. It had a red ribbon around its neck. The teddy bear's name was Cocoa. Peppy hugged Cocoa every morning and every night.

But this morning, Cocoa was nowhere to be seen.

Peppy felt worried. His little pig heart beat faster. His room felt too quiet without Cocoa.

"I need to find Cocoa!" Peppy declared.

He jumped out of bed. His bare feet touched the cool wooden floor. Peppy decided to look under his bed first.

He got down on his hands and knees. He peeked under the bed. It was dark and dusty under there. Peppy saw his red ball. He saw his toy truck. He saw one lonely sock.

But no Cocoa.

"Maybe Cocoa is in my toy box," Peppy thought.

He walked to his big blue toy box. The lid was heavy. Peppy lifted it with both hands. Inside, he saw many colorful toys. Building blocks tumbled around. His wooden train sat quietly. Three books were stacked in the corner.

Peppy moved toys around carefully. He looked behind his puzzle pieces. He checked under his coloring books. Still no brown, fluffy teddy bear.

Peppy's eyes started to feel watery. He really missed Cocoa. Where could his best friend be?

"Don't give up," Peppy told himself. "Think harder."

He sat on his bed and thought. Where did he last see Cocoa? Peppy remembered playing yesterday. He remembered reading a story. He remembered...

"The living room!" Peppy exclaimed.

Yesterday, Peppy had watched cartoons in the living room. He always brought Cocoa with him. Maybe Cocoa was still there!

Peppy ran out of his bedroom. His little hooves made soft sounds on the hallway floor. He hurried to the living room.

The living room was bright and cheerful. Peppy looked at the big green sofa. He looked behind the soft cushions. He checked under the coffee table.

Then Peppy saw something brown behind the rocking chair.

"Cocoa!" he squealed with joy.

There was his beloved teddy bear! Cocoa had fallen behind the rocking chair. The teddy bear looked a little dusty but perfectly fine.

Peppy picked up Cocoa and hugged him tight. The teddy bear felt warm and soft. It smelled like home. Peppy's heart felt happy again.

"I missed you so much!" Peppy whispered to Cocoa.

He carried his teddy bear back to his bedroom. Peppy sat on his bed with Cocoa in his lap. He felt proud of himself.

"I didn't give up," Peppy said to Cocoa. "I kept looking until I found you."

Peppy learned something important that morning. Sometimes things seem lost. But if you keep trying, you can solve the problem. It might take more than one try. That's okay.

"Next time, I'll remember where I put you," Peppy promised Cocoa.

He gave his teddy bear another big hug. Then Peppy got dressed for the day. He put on his favorite blue overalls. He brushed his teeth until they sparkled.

But first, he put Cocoa safely on his pillow.

"Stay here today," Peppy said with a smile. "I know exactly where to find you tonight."

Peppy felt ready for a wonderful day. He had solved his problem all by himself. His teddy bear was safe. And he had learned that trying again can lead to happy endings.

The sun shone brighter through his yellow curtains. It was going to be a perfect day.

The end.
