# Splash's Magical Cleanup Time

Splash the little yellow duck loved many things. She loved swimming in her bathtub. She loved playing with her toys. She loved eating crackers with Mama Duck.

But when Mama Duck said "Cleanup time!" every evening, Splash's wings drooped down. Her bright orange feet shuffled slowly. "Do I have to?" she quacked softly.

Tonight was just like every other night. Toys covered the living room floor. Blocks sat in corners. Books lay open everywhere. Splash's favorite ball had rolled under the couch.

"Yes, little one," Mama Duck said gently. "It's cleanup time again."

Splash sighed a big duck sigh. She waddled to her first toy. It was her red fire truck. As she picked it up, something wonderful happened.

The fire truck made a tiny "wee-ooo" sound! Splash blinked her bright eyes. Had she imagined it?

She picked up her blue blocks next. They clinked together like little bells. "Ting-a-ling!" they sang sweetly.

"Oh my!" Splash quacked with surprise.

Her purple stuffed elephant trumpeted softly. "Pfffrrrr!" Her green ball bounced and giggled. "Hee-hee-bounce!"

Every toy was making happy sounds! Splash's toys wanted to help with cleanup time.

"This is amazing!" Splash flapped her wings excitedly.

She picked up her yellow crayon box. It rattled like a maraca. "Shake-shake-shake!" She grabbed her pink doll. The doll whispered, "Thank you, Splash!"

Soon Splash was dancing around the room. She spun in circles. She wiggled her tail feathers. She quacked a happy cleanup song.

"Toys away, toys away! Let's clean up and play! Every toy has a special place. Cleanup time puts smiles on every face!"

Mama Duck watched from the doorway. Her heart felt warm and fuzzy. Splash was twirling and giggling. The little duck was having so much fun!

Splash carefully placed each toy. The blocks went in their wooden box. The books lined up on their shelf. The crayons marched into their container.

"Where do you belong?" Splash asked her fire truck.

"In the toy chest!" the truck seemed to whisper.

Splash gently put it away. She patted the toy chest lid. "Sleep well, little truck!"

The ball rolled happily to the basket. The stuffed elephant waddled to the bedroom. Even the puzzle pieces hopped into their box.

In just fifteen minutes, everything was clean! The room looked sparkly and neat. Splash felt proud and happy.

"I did it!" she quacked loudly. "Cleanup time was actually fun!"

Mama Duck gave Splash the biggest hug. "You were wonderful, sweetheart. You made cleanup time magical."

"The toys helped me," Splash giggled. "They sang and danced too!"

"Maybe they were happy to have such a caring friend," Mama Duck said with a smile.

Splash looked around the tidy room. Everything looked so nice and organized. She could find any toy easily tomorrow. The room felt peaceful and cozy.

"Mama," Splash said thoughtfully. "Can we have cleanup time again tomorrow?"

Mama Duck's eyes twinkled. "Of course, little one. Every evening at the same time."

"I can't wait!" Splash flapped her wings. "I wonder what sounds my toys will make tomorrow!"

That night, Splash dreamed about dancing toys. She dreamed about singing blocks. She dreamed about giggling balls and whispering dolls.

The next evening came quickly. When Mama Duck called "Cleanup time!" Splash jumped up immediately.

"Coming, Mama!" she quacked cheerfully.

She raced to the living room. Her toys were scattered around again. But now Splash saw them differently. They weren't just messy toys. They were her magical cleanup friends!

"Hello, friends!" Splash called happily. "Ready for another cleanup dance?"

The fire truck seemed to wink. The blocks seemed to shimmer. The ball seemed to bounce with excitement.

Splash started her cleanup song again. She danced and cleaned and giggled. Cleanup time had become her favorite time of day.

Because sometimes the most ordinary things can become the most magical adventures. All you need is the right attitude and an open heart.

And Splash's heart was very, very open.

The end.
