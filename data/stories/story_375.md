# Sam and Bunny's Tea Party

Sam sat at the big dining room table. He felt very lonely today. The table was so long and empty. Sam had set up his toy teacups. But playing tea party alone wasn't fun.

"I wish I had a friend," Sam sighed. He poured pretend tea into a tiny cup. The dining room felt too quiet. Too big for just one little boy.

*Hop, hop, hop.*

Sam heard soft sounds by the door. A small brown bunny peeked around the corner. The bunny's nose twitched with curiosity.

"Hello there, little bunny," Sam said softly. He didn't want to scare his visitor.

Bunny hopped closer to the table. She looked up at all the pretty teacups. Her eyes sparkled with interest.

"Do you want to play?" Sam asked hopefully. "I'm having a tea party. But it's not very fun alone."

Bunny's ears perked up. She loved tea parties! She hopped right up onto a chair. Her fluffy tail wiggled with excitement.

"Oh good!" Sam clapped his hands. "Now I have a friend to play with!"

Sam poured pretend tea for Bunny. "What kind of tea would you like?" he asked politely.

Bunny made happy little sounds. She pretended to sip from her tiny cup.

"I think you like carrot tea!" Sam giggled. "That's perfect for a bunny friend."

They played together beautifully. Sam served pretend cookies and cakes. Bunny shared her favorite nibbling games. She showed Sam how to twitch his nose like a bunny.

"This is so much better!" Sam laughed. "Playing together is way more fun."

Bunny agreed completely. She had been lonely too. Now she had found a wonderful friend.

Sam noticed something special happening. When they played together, everything felt magical. The dining room didn't feel big anymore. It felt cozy and warm.

"Let's set more places," Sam suggested. "Maybe other friends will come too!"

Together, they arranged more teacups. Bunny helped by pushing them with her nose. Sam carefully placed the tiny spoons.

"You're really good at helping," Sam told Bunny. "We make a great team!"

Bunny's whiskers twitched happily. She loved working together with Sam. Everything was easier with a friend.

Soon they heard more sounds. *Chirp, chirp!* A little bird landed on the windowsill. *Meow!* The neighbor's cat peeked through the door.

"Come join our tea party!" Sam called out. "There's room for everyone!"

Bunny hopped excitedly. She loved making new friends too!

The bird flew to the table. The cat padded over quietly. Now their tea party was full of friends!

Sam poured tea for everyone. Bunny shared her carrot cookies. The bird brought tiny seeds. The cat contributed gentle purrs.

"This is the best tea party ever!" Sam declared. His heart felt warm and happy.

All the friends agreed. Playing together was wonderful. They took turns telling stories. They shared their favorite games.

Sam learned that Bunny loved to garden. Bunny discovered that Sam loved to read. The bird enjoyed singing songs. The cat liked cozy naps.

"We all like different things," Sam noticed. "But we can enjoy them together!"

Bunny nodded wisely. Friends didn't have to be exactly the same. They just had to care about each other.

As the afternoon ended, all the friends felt grateful. They had found something precious today. They had found friendship and cooperation.

"Can we play again tomorrow?" Sam asked hopefully.

All his new friends said yes! Bunny would bring her favorite carrots. The bird would teach them new songs. The cat would show them how to nap perfectly.

Sam hugged Bunny gently. "Thank you for joining my tea party," he whispered. "You made everything better."

Bunny snuggled close to Sam. She felt the same way about her new friend.

From that day on, Sam never felt lonely. The dining room became their special meeting place. Every day brought new adventures with his dear friends.

And Sam learned something wonderful. The best games happen when friends play together. Cooperation and kindness make everything more fun!
