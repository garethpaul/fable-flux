# Leo's Musical Morning

The morning sun peeked through the curtains. It was time for music time! Leo the lion sat on his bedroom floor. His golden mane was still messy from sleep.

"Leo, come to the living room!" called Mama Lion. "It's music time!"

Leo's ears drooped down low. He didn't want to leave his toys. Music time felt too quiet and still. Lions were supposed to roar loudly! They were supposed to run and jump!

"Do I have to?" Leo asked softly.

"Come try it with me," Mama said gently. "Just for a little while."

Leo dragged his paws slowly. He walked to the sunny living room. Mama had set up their special music corner. Colorful instruments sparkled in the sunlight. There were drums and bells and shakers.

"I don't know what to do," Leo mumbled.

Mama smiled warmly at her little cub. "Let's start with something easy. Pick any instrument you like."

Leo looked around the room carefully. A small drum caught his eye. It was bright red with golden stripes. Just like his mane! He touched it with one paw.

*Tap, tap.*

The sound was soft and gentle. Leo tried again, a little harder.

*Boom, boom!*

"That sounds wonderful!" Mama cheered. She picked up silver bells. They jingled like tiny wind chimes.

Something magical began to happen. Leo's paws started moving by themselves. *Boom-tap-boom-tap!* The rhythm felt good in his chest.

Mama shook her bells in time. *Jingle-jingle-jingle!* The sounds danced together in the air.

"This is like a lion heartbeat!" Leo discovered. He played faster. *Boom-boom-tap-boom!*

"Yes! Feel that rhythm inside you," Mama encouraged.

Leo stood up on his hind legs. He swayed back and forth. The music moved through his whole body. His tail swished in perfect time.

"I'm making music with my tail too!" Leo laughed.

Mama grabbed colorful scarves from the basket. "Let's dance while we play!"

The scarves fluttered like butterflies. Red and blue and yellow silk flew around them. Leo drummed while he danced. The living room became their magical stage.

"Now let's try singing!" Mama suggested.

Leo had an idea. "Can I roar-sing?"

"Of course! Show me your musical roar!"

Leo took a deep breath. Instead of his usual loud roar, he made a gentle rumbling sound. "Roooooaaaar-la-la-la!"

It sounded like rolling thunder mixed with a lullaby. Mama clapped her paws together.

"That's the most beautiful roar-song ever!"

They played for twenty more minutes. Leo tried every instrument in the corner. The tambourine made happy jingly sounds. The wooden blocks clicked like dancing feet. The rainbow xylophone sang in different colors.

"Each instrument has its own voice," Leo realized. "Just like each lion has their own roar!"

"Exactly right, my musical cub," Mama agreed.

As music time ended, Leo felt warm and happy. His whole body buzzed with rhythm. He had discovered something amazing inside himself.

"Mama, can we do this again tomorrow?" Leo asked eagerly.

"Every morning, if you'd like," Mama promised.

Leo carefully put each instrument back. He touched the red drum one more time. *Tap-tap.* It seemed to whisper, "See you tomorrow!"

That night, Leo dreamed of dancing scarves. He dreamed of roar-songs and heartbeat drums. In his dream, he was the most musical lion in the whole world.

The next morning, Leo woke up early. Before Mama even called, he was already in the living room. He sat next to his favorite red drum.

"Good morning, drum," he whispered. "Ready for another adventure?"

When Mama found him there, she smiled the biggest smile. "Someone's excited for music time!"

"It's my favorite time now!" Leo announced proudly. "Music makes my heart feel like dancing!"

And from that day forward, music time became the most magical part of Leo's every day. He learned that the best adventures sometimes happen when you're sitting perfectly still, letting the music move through your soul.
