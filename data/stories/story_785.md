# Story story_785

**Reading Time Magic**

The big clock on the wall went tick-tock. Mama looked at baby Luna and smiled. "It's reading time!" she said softly.

Luna was playing with her colorful blocks. Red ones, blue ones, yellow ones too. She stacked them high like a tower. "No reading," Luna said. She shook her head. "Play blocks."

Mama sat in the big, cozy chair. It was soft and warm. The chair had fuzzy blue fabric. "Come here, little one," Mama said. She patted her lap gently.

Luna looked at her blocks. Then she looked at Mama. Reading time felt long and quiet. Luna liked moving and playing better. "Blocks fun," she said.

Mama picked up a special book. The cover was shiny and smooth. It had a picture of a friendly dragon. The dragon was purple with sparkly wings. "This book is magical," Mama whispered.

Luna's eyes got big. She loved magical things. Slowly, she walked to Mama. Her little feet made soft sounds on the carpet.

Mama lifted Luna onto her lap. Luna felt warm and safe. The chair rocked back and forth gently. Rock, rock, rock.

"Once upon a time," Mama began. Her voice was like music. Sweet and calm.

Suddenly, something amazing happened. The book started to glow! Soft, golden light came from the pages. Luna gasped. "Ooh!" she said.

The purple dragon on the page began to move. It stretched its sparkly wings. It winked at Luna with one bright green eye. "Hello, little friend," the dragon said in a tiny voice.

Luna giggled. The sound was like little bells. "Dragon talk!" she said excitedly.

As Mama turned each page, more magic happened. A fluffy white bunny hopped out of the book. It tickled Luna's nose with its whiskers. Luna laughed and laughed.

Next came a singing bluebird. It flew in circles around Luna's head. The bird sang a happy song. "Tweet, tweet, hooray!" it chirped.

A friendly bear appeared too. The bear was brown and cuddly. It gave Luna the softest hug. The bear smelled like honey and sunshine.

"More! More!" Luna clapped her hands together. Her eyes sparkled with joy.

Mama turned another page. A garden full of flowers bloomed before them. The flowers were every color Luna could imagine. Pink roses, orange marigolds, purple violets. They smelled so sweet.

Butterflies danced from flower to flower. Their wings looked like tiny rainbows. Luna reached out to touch one. It felt like silk on her finger.

The magical friends played with Luna. The dragon gave her rides on its back. The bunny played peek-a-boo behind the flowers. The bear told funny jokes that made Luna giggle.

But soon, the story came to an end. The magical friends waved goodbye. "See you tomorrow!" they called.

The golden light faded slowly. The book became quiet again. But Luna felt happy and excited inside.

"That was the best story ever!" Luna said. She hugged the book tight against her chest.

Mama kissed the top of Luna's head. Her hair smelled like baby shampoo. "Reading time is always special," Mama said.

Luna yawned. She felt sleepy and content. The magical adventure filled her dreams with wonderful thoughts.

"Mama," Luna said softly. "Can we read again tomorrow?"

"Of course, sweetie," Mama replied. "The magic is always waiting for us."

Luna smiled the biggest smile. She couldn't wait for tomorrow's reading time. Maybe they would meet new magical friends. Maybe they would visit exciting places.

Reading time wasn't quiet and boring anymore. It was full of adventure and wonder. It was the most magical time of all.

That night, Luna dreamed of purple dragons and singing birds. She dreamed of friendly bears and dancing butterflies. In her dreams, she was always reading with Mama.

The next morning, Luna woke up excited. "Is it reading time yet?" she asked.

And from that day on, reading time became Luna's very favorite time of day.

The end.
