# Grandpa's Wonderful Nap

It was two o'clock. The big clock went "tick-tock, tick-tock." Grandpa looked at his favorite blue chair. It was nap time.

"Oh my," Grandpa sighed softly. "I'm not very sleepy today." He rubbed his gray whiskers. The sun was shining bright outside. Birds were singing happy songs. 

Grandpa felt too awake for sleeping. His eyes were wide open. His mind was thinking busy thoughts. "Maybe I'll skip my nap," he said.

But Grandpa's chair looked so cozy. The soft cushions seemed to whisper, "Come sit down." So Grandpa walked over slowly. His slippers made quiet "shuffle-shuffle" sounds.

He sat down with a gentle "whoosh." The chair hugged him like a warm blanket. Grandpa closed his eyes. "Just for a minute," he whispered.

Suddenly, something magical began to happen. Behind his closed eyes, Grandpa saw wonderful colors. Swirls of purple and gold danced together. They moved like gentle waves.

The colors grew brighter and brighter. They painted beautiful pictures in his mind. Grandpa saw a peaceful garden. Butterflies fluttered on rainbow wings. Flowers swayed in a warm breeze.

"How lovely," Grandpa thought dreamily. The magical garden felt so real. He could smell sweet roses. He could hear water trickling softly. A friendly cat purred nearby.

In his dream, Grandpa walked down a winding path. The stones sparkled like tiny diamonds. Everything glowed with golden light. He felt young and happy again.

Grandpa met a wise old owl. "Welcome to the Nap Time Garden," hooted the owl. "This special place appears when you rest. Only peaceful hearts can find it."

The owl showed Grandpa amazing sights. There were trees that grew candy apples. A pond where fish painted with their tails. Clouds that looked like fluffy animals.

"Every day, this garden grows more beautiful," the owl explained. "The more you visit, the more wonders appear." The owl's eyes twinkled kindly.

Grandpa felt so peaceful and happy. All his busy thoughts melted away. His body felt light as a feather. His heart felt warm and content.

He explored the magical garden longer. He picked singing flowers that hummed lullabies. He watched tiny fairies dance on lily pads. Everything was perfect and calm.

Slowly, gently, Grandpa began to wake up. The magical garden faded softly. But the wonderful feelings stayed in his heart. He felt refreshed and joyful.

"Goodness me!" Grandpa said, stretching happily. "That was the most amazing nap ever!" His eyes sparkled with delight. He felt like he'd been on a grand adventure.

The afternoon sun felt warmer now. The bird songs sounded more beautiful. Even his cozy chair seemed extra special. Everything looked brighter and happier.

Grandpa stood up slowly. He felt wonderful and peaceful. His mind was clear and calm. His body felt rested and strong. "What a magical surprise," he chuckled.

He walked to the window. The real garden outside looked lovely too. But now Grandpa knew about the secret garden. The one that appeared during nap time.

"I can't wait for tomorrow," Grandpa said excitedly. He patted his chair gently. "Thank you for the magical journey," he whispered.

From that day on, Grandpa loved nap time. He looked forward to two o'clock. He couldn't wait to visit his dream garden. Each day brought new wonders and adventures.

The next afternoon, Grandpa hurried to his chair. "Time for another magical journey!" he said happily. He settled in with a big smile. His eyes closed peacefully.

Soon the beautiful colors appeared again. The magical garden welcomed him back. New flowers had grown overnight. A family of rabbits waved hello.

Grandpa learned something wonderful. Rest time wasn't boring at all. It was the most magical time of day. Dreams could take you anywhere. Peace brought the greatest adventures.

Now Grandpa treasures his daily naps. His chair is his magic carpet. His dreams are his greatest joy. Every afternoon brings new wonder and happiness.

The end.
