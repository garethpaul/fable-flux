# Story story_864

**The Little Red Plane and Ruby Doll**

In a cozy living room sat a little red plane. His name was Zoom. He had shiny silver wings and a bright blue propeller. Zoom loved to fly around the soft carpet. But today, he felt very lonely.

Zoom zoomed in circles by himself. He flew under the coffee table. He soared over the fluffy pillows. The house was quiet and still. "I wish I had a friend to play with," Zoom sighed softly.

On the bookshelf sat a beautiful doll named Ruby. She had curly brown hair and a yellow dress. Ruby watched Zoom fly around all by himself. Her button eyes sparkled with kindness. She wanted to help the sad little plane.

"Hello there!" called Ruby from up high. "You look lonely down there."

Zoom looked up in surprise. "Oh! I didn't see you there. I am feeling lonely today."

Ruby's face lit up with a warm smile. "Would you like to play together? I would love to be your friend!"

Zoom's propeller spun with excitement. "Really? You want to play with me?"

"Of course!" Ruby said cheerfully. "Can you help me get down?"

Zoom flew up to the bookshelf carefully. Ruby climbed onto his back. Her soft hands held on gently. Together they glided down to the carpet with a gentle whoosh.

"This is amazing!" Ruby laughed as they landed. "Flying feels like floating on a cloud!"

Zoom beamed with happiness. "What should we play first?"

Ruby clapped her hands together. "Let's explore the living room! You can fly, and I can walk beside you."

They started their adventure together. Zoom flew low and slow so Ruby could keep up. They discovered a forest of chair legs. The wooden legs felt smooth and cool. They found a mountain of couch cushions. The cushions were squishy and warm.

"Look!" Ruby pointed to a sunny spot by the window. "The sunlight makes everything golden and bright!"

They played hide and seek behind the curtains. The fabric was soft and tickled their faces. Ruby giggled when Zoom's propeller got tangled in the silky material.

Next, they built a fort with throw pillows. The pillows smelled like lavender and felt like marshmallows. Ruby arranged them in a circle. Zoom flew around the edges like a guard plane.

"This is our special place," Ruby declared proudly.

"Our friendship fort!" Zoom agreed, doing a happy loop in the air.

They pretended to have a tea party inside their fort. Ruby poured pretend tea from an invisible pot. Zoom made soft buzzing sounds as he "drank" his tea. They shared imaginary cookies that tasted like sunshine and giggles.

As the afternoon sun grew warmer, they played airplane and passenger. Ruby sat on Zoom's back again. He flew her gently around the living room. She pointed out all the wonderful sights below.

"There's the lamp that looks like a tall tree!" Ruby said with wonder.

"And there's the rug that looks like a grassy field!" Zoom added happily.

They played until the room grew dim and cozy. Ruby yawned a tiny yawn. Zoom's propeller spun more slowly.

"I had the best day ever," Ruby said sleepily. "Thank you for being my friend, Zoom."

"Thank you for playing with me, Ruby. I was so lonely before. Now I feel happy and warm inside."

They snuggled together in their pillow fort. Ruby's yellow dress was wrinkled from playing. Zoom's red paint gleamed in the soft evening light.

"Tomorrow we can play again," Ruby whispered.

"Every day!" Zoom whispered back. "Best friends play together every day."

The living room felt different now. It wasn't quiet and empty anymore. It was full of friendship and joy. The two friends had found something special together.

As stars began to twinkle outside the window, Zoom and Ruby drifted off to sleep. They dreamed of more adventures to come. In their dreams, they flew through cotton candy clouds and sailed on rivers of chocolate milk.

The little red plane was never lonely again. Ruby the doll had found her perfect playmate. Together, they filled every day with laughter, imagination, and the magic of true friendship.

The end.
