# Zara's Splashing Surprise

Zara the zebra heard the water running. Bath time was starting soon. She peeked around the corner. Steam rose from the warm tub.

"Zara, time for your bath!" called Mama Zebra. Her voice was gentle and kind.

Zara's ears drooped down low. She shuffled her hooves slowly. Bath time felt like work. The soap was slippery. Water got in her eyes sometimes.

"Do I have to?" Zara asked. She twisted her striped tail nervously.

"Yes, little one," Mama said. "But maybe tonight will be different."

Zara walked to the bathroom. Her steps were small and quiet. The bathtub looked very big. Bubbles floated on top.

"What are those?" Zara whispered. She had never seen bubbles before.

"Magic bath bubbles," Mama smiled. "They make bath time special."

Zara touched one bubble gently. POP! It disappeared in tiny sparkles. Her eyes grew wide with wonder.

"More!" Zara giggled softly.

She stepped into the warm water. It felt cozy around her hooves. The bubbles tickled her striped legs. They made soft popping sounds.

Pop! Pop! Pop!

Zara scooped up a handful. The bubbles danced on her nose. She blew them into the air. They floated like tiny clouds.

"Look, Mama! I'm making bubble rain!"

More bubbles landed on her ears. They felt light and fluffy. Zara laughed and shook her head. Bubbles flew everywhere!

The soap smelled like sweet strawberries. It made more bubbles appear. Zara rubbed it on her stripes. The black lines became sparkly white.

"I'm a magical zebra!" she announced proudly.

Mama poured warm water gently. It washed the soap away. Zara's stripes appeared again, clean and bright.

"Can I make more bubbles?" Zara asked excitedly.

"Of course," Mama said. "Blow through this special wand."

Zara took a deep breath. She blew slowly and carefully. One enormous bubble formed. It grew bigger and bigger.

"It's huge!" Zara gasped with delight.

The giant bubble floated upward. It reflected rainbow colors. Zara watched it dance in the air. Then it popped with a gentle splash.

"Again! Again!" Zara clapped her hooves together.

She made tiny bubbles next. They clustered around her like friends. Some stuck to the tub walls. Others floated to the ceiling.

Zara discovered bubble mountains too. She piled them on her head. She wore a bubble crown. She felt like a bubble princess.

"Look at me!" she called happily.

The warm water felt wonderful now. Zara splashed gently with her hooves. The bubbles bounced and danced. Everything sparkled in the bathroom light.

Mama washed behind Zara's ears carefully. It felt like a gentle massage. Zara closed her eyes peacefully.

"This feels so nice," she sighed contentedly.

When bath time was over, Zara felt sad. The bubbles were almost gone. The warm water was draining away.

"Can we do this tomorrow?" she asked hopefully.

"Every night," Mama promised. "Bath time is our special time."

Mama wrapped Zara in a fluffy towel. It was soft and warm. Zara snuggled close to Mama's side.

"I love bubble baths," Zara whispered sleepily.

"I love you," Mama whispered back.

That night, Zara dreamed of bubbles. They danced in her dreams. They sang quiet lullabies. They promised to return tomorrow.

When morning came, Zara thought about bath time. She couldn't wait for evening. She wanted to make bubbles again.

"Mama," Zara said at breakfast. "Can tonight's bath have extra bubbles?"

"Absolutely," Mama smiled. "What color bubbles today?"

"Rainbow bubbles!" Zara exclaimed joyfully.

All day long, Zara smiled. Bath time wasn't scary anymore. It was magical and fun. It was her favorite time.

Now Zara counts the hours. She waits for bubble time. Every bath brings new surprises. Every splash brings more joy.

Bath time became Zara's daily adventure. And adventures, she learned, happen everywhere. Even in the bathtub at home.

The end.
