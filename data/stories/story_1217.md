# Story story_1217

**Whiskers and the Magic Snack Time**

The kitchen clock went "tick-tock, tick-tock." It was three o'clock. Time for snack time!

Mama called from the kitchen. "Whiskers! Come eat your snack!"

Whiskers the gray cat sat by his sunny window. He watched birds hop in the garden. He did not want to leave his warm spot.

"I'm not hungry," Whiskers said softly.

But his tummy made a little rumble sound. Maybe he was hungry after all.

Whiskers walked slowly to the kitchen. His paws made soft "pat-pat" sounds on the floor. He saw his blue food bowl waiting for him.

"Here's your favorite fish treat," Mama said. She smiled at Whiskers.

Whiskers sniffed the bowl. It smelled like ocean and sunshine. But he still felt lazy. Eating seemed like too much work.

He sat down and stared at his food. The little fish shapes looked boring. They just sat there doing nothing.

Then something wonderful happened.

One tiny fish treat started to wiggle! It jumped up and did a little dance. Then another fish joined in. Soon all the fish treats were dancing in his bowl!

"Come play with us, Whiskers!" the fish treats sang in tiny voices. "We're having a party!"

Whiskers blinked his big green eyes. Was this really happening?

The fish treats made a conga line. They danced around the edge of his bowl. They sang a happy song: "Snack time, snack time, come and play! Make your tummy happy today!"

Whiskers giggled. The dancing fish looked so silly and fun. One fish wore a tiny hat. Another had a little bow tie.

"How do I play?" Whiskers asked.

"Catch us if you can!" laughed the fish treats.

Whiskers reached out with his paw. He gently touched one dancing fish. It tasted crunchy and delicious! The fish treat giggled as Whiskers ate it.

"That tickles!" said the fish treat happily.

Soon Whiskers was playing and eating. He pounced on the dancing treats. They laughed and cheered each time he caught one. The bowl became empty very quickly.

"More! More!" Whiskers meowed. He licked his whiskers clean.

But the magic fish treats were all gone. His bowl was empty and shiny. Whiskers felt full and happy.

"Did you enjoy your snack?" Mama asked. She petted his soft fur.

Whiskers purred loudly. "It was the best snack ever! The fish danced and played with me!"

Mama smiled. "That sounds magical, little one."

Whiskers cleaned his face with his paw. His tummy felt warm and satisfied. He wasn't tired anymore. The dancing fish had given him energy!

He played with his toy mouse. He chased dust specks in the sunlight. He felt strong and playful.

When dinner time came, Whiskers ran to the kitchen. He hoped his food would dance again. But his dinner kibble just sat quietly in the bowl.

That was okay. Whiskers ate every bite anyway. He remembered how fun snack time had been.

At bedtime, Whiskers curled up in his cozy bed. He thought about the dancing fish treats. They had made eating so much fun!

"Tomorrow I won't be reluctant," Whiskers promised himself. "I'll eat my snack right away!"

He dreamed of fish treats wearing funny hats. In his dream, they taught him new dance moves. They spun in circles and did jumping jacks.

The next morning, Whiskers woke up excited. His tummy rumbled for breakfast. He ran to his bowl and ate every bite.

When three o'clock came, Whiskers was ready. He sat by his bowl and waited.

"Snack time, Whiskers!" Mama called.

"I'm already here!" Whiskers meowed happily.

Maybe the fish would dance again. Maybe they wouldn't. But Whiskers didn't mind anymore.

He had learned that snack time was always special. Sometimes magic happened. Sometimes it was just yummy food.

Both ways made his tummy happy. And that was the most important thing of all.

Whiskers ate his snack with a big smile. Tomorrow would bring another wonderful snack time adventure!

The end.
