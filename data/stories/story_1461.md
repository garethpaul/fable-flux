# Story story_1461

**Buddy and the Dancing Kite**

Buddy was a golden puppy who lived on Farmer Joe's farm. His fluffy tail wagged as he walked through the green grass. The sun felt warm on his fur.

But Buddy felt lonely today. The chickens were busy pecking for bugs. The cows were eating hay in the field. The pigs were rolling in cool mud.

"I wish I had a friend to play with," Buddy said sadly. His ears drooped down. He sat by the old red barn and watched the clouds.

High above in the bright blue sky, a colorful kite danced in the wind. The kite had red and yellow stripes. Long purple ribbons fluttered from its tail.

The kite saw Buddy sitting alone. "That puppy looks sad," thought the kite. "Maybe I can cheer him up!"

The wind carried the kite lower and lower. Soon it was floating right above Buddy's head. The purple ribbons tickled Buddy's nose.

"Achoo!" sneezed Buddy. He looked up and saw the pretty kite.

"Hello there!" called the kite in a gentle voice. "I'm Kira the kite. Why do you look so sad?"

Buddy's brown eyes grew wide. "You can talk!" he barked with excitement. His tail started wagging again.

"Of course I can talk," laughed Kira. "I've been watching you from up high. You looked lonely."

"I am lonely," Buddy admitted. "All the farm animals are busy. I have no one to play with."

"Well, now you have me!" said Kira cheerfully. "Would you like to play together?"

Buddy jumped up on his hind legs. "Yes! But how can we play? You're up there and I'm down here."

"Watch this!" said Kira. She swooped down low. Her ribbons trailed on the ground like colorful snakes.

Buddy chased the dancing ribbons around the yard. He jumped and barked with joy. His paws made soft thumping sounds on the dirt.

"This is fun!" panted Buddy. "What else can we do?"

"Follow me!" called Kira. She glided toward the big oak tree. Buddy ran underneath, his tongue hanging out happily.

They played tag around the chicken coop. Kira would dip down and touch Buddy's head with her ribbons. Then Buddy would chase her around the fence posts.

The chickens clucked and watched the new game. Even the sleepy cows looked over to see what was happening.

"Let's play hide and seek!" suggested Buddy. He ran behind the water trough and crouched down low.

Kira pretended she couldn't find him. "Where could Buddy be?" she called out. "I wonder if he's behind that trough?"

"You found me!" laughed Buddy, popping up. His fur was dusty but his eyes sparkled with happiness.

They played until the sun began to set. The sky turned orange and pink. Farmer Joe came out to feed the animals.

"Well hello there, Buddy," said Farmer Joe. He noticed Buddy's happy face. "You look like you've had a good day."

Buddy barked and wagged his whole body. He looked up at Kira floating above.

"I made a new friend today," Buddy told Kira. "Thank you for playing with me."

"Thank you for being my friend too," said Kira. "I was lonely up in the sky all by myself."

As the stars began to twinkle, Kira settled down in the soft grass next to Buddy. The wind grew gentle and quiet.

"Will you play with me again tomorrow?" asked Buddy hopefully.

"Every day," promised Kira. "Friends always stick together."

Buddy curled up next to his new friend. The farm felt peaceful and happy. The crickets sang their nighttime songs.

From that day on, Buddy was never lonely again. Every morning, he would run outside to find Kira dancing in the sky. They would play until sunset, filling the farm with laughter and joy.

The other animals loved watching their games. Soon the whole farm felt like one big happy family.

And whenever the wind blew just right, you could hear Buddy barking with delight and see Kira's colorful ribbons dancing in the breeze.

The end.
