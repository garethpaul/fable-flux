# Splash Friends

Little Emma sat on the soft bathroom rug. She held her yellow rubber duck. The bathtub was empty and quiet. Emma felt lonely today.

"Quack, quack," she whispered softly. Her duck didn't answer back. Emma sighed and looked around. The bathroom felt too big. Too quiet for just one baby.

*Tap, tap, tap.* Paws clicked on the tiles. Max the golden dog peeked around the corner. His tail wagged slowly. His brown eyes looked curious.

Emma looked up at Max. "Woof?" Max asked gently. He tilted his fuzzy head. His ears flopped sideways.

Emma giggled. "Duck!" she said proudly. She held up her yellow toy. Max stepped closer. His nose twitched with interest.

Max sat down carefully. He was much bigger than Emma. But he moved very gently. His tail made soft *thump, thump* sounds. Emma wasn't scared at all.

"Woof, woof!" Max said happily. Emma clapped her hands. "Duck, duck!" she replied. They were talking together!

Emma stood up on wobbly legs. She toddled to the bathtub. Max followed behind her. His paws made gentle clicking sounds. Emma pointed at the empty tub.

"Bath!" Emma announced. She dropped her duck inside. It landed with a small *plop*. Max wagged his tail faster.

Emma tried to climb into the tub. But her little legs couldn't reach. She made frustrated baby sounds. "Uh, uh, uh!"

Max understood immediately. He moved closer to the tub. He lay down flat. Now Emma could use him. Like a furry step stool!

"Good doggy!" Emma giggled. She patted Max's soft fur. Max's tail wagged proudly. He stayed very still. Emma climbed up carefully.

Soon Emma sat in the empty tub. She picked up her duck again. "Lonely duck," she said sadly. The duck looked small. All by itself.

Max stood up tall. He put his front paws. Right on the tub's edge. His nose almost touched Emma's duck. "Woof?" he asked softly.

Emma's eyes got big and bright. "Play!" she shouted with joy. She held out her duck. Max sniffed it gently. His wet nose tickled.

Emma giggled and pulled back. Max play-bowed down low. His bottom stayed up high. His tail wagged like crazy. Emma laughed louder.

"More!" Emma demanded happily. She reached for Max's nose. Max let her touch it. His nose was cold and wet. Emma squealed with delight.

They played peek-a-boo next. Emma hid behind her duck. "Where Emma?" Max would whine. Then Emma popped up. "Here!" she would shout.

Max learned the game quickly. He hid his head. Behind his big paws. "Where Max?" Emma giggled. Up popped his furry face. "Woof!" he barked softly.

Emma clapped her hands together. "Again, again!" she demanded. Max's tail never stopped wagging. His pink tongue hung out. He looked so happy too.

Next, they played catch. Emma threw her duck gently. Max caught it carefully. His teeth didn't hurt it. He brought it right back.

"Good boy, Max!" Emma praised. She patted his head lovingly. Max's eyes sparkled with joy. His whole body wiggled happily.

Emma tried to pat Max. But she lost her balance. She started to tip over! Max quickly moved closer. His warm, furry side. Caught Emma safely.

"Safe!" Emma giggled. She hugged Max's neck. Max stood very still. He let her snuggle. His fur was so soft. So warm and comforting.

Emma felt different now. Not lonely anymore. Max felt happy too. They were perfect friends. Playing together was wonderful.

"Friends!" Emma announced proudly. She pointed at herself. Then at Max. "Emma! Max! Friends!"

Max barked one happy bark. His tail wagged so hard. His whole bottom wiggled. Emma laughed until her belly hurt.

From that day forward. Emma and Max played together. Every single day. The bathroom became their. Special friendship place.

Emma learned something important. Playing with friends. Was much more fun. Than playing all alone.

And Max learned too. Little humans needed. Gentle, caring friends. Just like him.

Their friendship grew stronger. Every single day. Through splashes, giggles, and. Lots of happy play.

The end.

The end.
