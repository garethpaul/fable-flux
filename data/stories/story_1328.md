# Story story_1328

**Maya Mouse and the Magic Snack Time**

The big clock on the wall went tick-tock, tick-tock. "Snack time!" called Teacher Rabbit from across the classroom. All the little animals put down their crayons and blocks.

Maya Mouse sat very still at her tiny desk. Her whiskers twitched nervously. She watched her friends hop and skip to the snack table. But Maya's little paws stayed folded in her lap.

"Come on, Maya!" squeaked her friend Benny Bunny. "Today we have cheese crackers!"

Maya's tummy made a quiet rumble. She loved cheese crackers. But eating in front of everyone made her feel shy. What if she dropped crumbs? What if she made crunchy sounds?

Maya shook her little head. "I'm not hungry," she whispered.

Teacher Rabbit noticed Maya sitting alone. She walked over with gentle, quiet steps. Her soft ears flopped as she bent down low.

"Hello, sweet Maya," Teacher Rabbit said softly. "Would you like to help me with something special?"

Maya's dark eyes looked up curiously. "What kind of special?"

"Follow me to the snack table. I'll show you."

Maya's tiny paws trembled a little. But she trusted Teacher Rabbit. Slowly, she slipped off her chair and followed.

At the snack table, Teacher Rabbit handed Maya a small, sparkly bell. "This is our Magic Snack Bell," she whispered. "When you ring it, something wonderful happens."

Maya held the bell carefully. It felt warm in her paws. "What does it do?"

"Ring it and see!"

Maya gave the bell a gentle shake. *Ting-a-ling!* The sweet sound filled the air.

Suddenly, all her friends started making funny eating sounds. Benny Bunny crunched his crackers loudly. "CRUNCH, CRUNCH, CRUNCH!" Penny Pig made silly slurping sounds with her apple juice. "SLURP, SLURP!"

Maya giggled. The sounds weren't scary anymore. They were funny!

"The Magic Bell makes snack time extra fun," explained Teacher Rabbit. "Everyone gets to make happy eating sounds together."

Maya's friend Sophie Squirrel made chomping sounds with her nuts. "CHOMP, CHOMP!" All the animals laughed and made their own silly snack sounds.

Maya felt her tummy rumble again. This time, she felt excited instead of worried. "Can I have a cheese cracker now?" she asked.

"Of course!" Teacher Rabbit smiled.

Maya picked up a golden cheese cracker. It smelled nutty and delicious. She took a small bite. *Crunch!* The sound mixed with all her friends' happy munching.

"Make it louder, Maya!" called Benny Bunny.

Maya took a bigger bite. *CRUNCH!* She giggled at the sound. Her friends cheered and made their own loud, happy eating sounds.

The cheese cracker tasted wonderful. It was salty and crispy. Maya licked her lips and reached for another one.

"This is the best snack time ever!" Maya squeaked.

She rang the Magic Bell again. *Ting-a-ling!* All her friends made even sillier sounds. They giggled and munched together.

Maya tried making different sounds with each bite. *Crunch, munch, chomp!* Her friends copied her sounds. Soon everyone was laughing and eating happily.

When snack time was over, Maya helped Teacher Rabbit clean up. She carefully put the Magic Bell in a special box.

"Thank you for showing me the Magic Bell," Maya said. "I felt brave today."

"You were very brave," Teacher Rabbit agreed. "And tomorrow, you can ring the bell again."

Maya's whiskers twitched with excitement instead of worry. "Really? Tomorrow too?"

"Every snack time can be magical when we're together."

Maya hugged Teacher Rabbit's soft, fluffy leg. Then she skipped back to her desk with a full, happy tummy.

As she picked up her crayons, Maya thought about tomorrow. She couldn't wait for snack time to come again. She would ring the Magic Bell and munch loudly with all her friends.

Maya Mouse had learned something important. Snack time wasn't scary when everyone was together. It was magical, delicious, and full of giggles.

The big clock kept going tick-tock, tick-tock. But now Maya was counting the time until tomorrow's magical snack time would come.

The end.
