# Splish-Splash Magic

The clock on the bathroom wall said seven o'clock. Mom called from downstairs, "Tommy, bath time!"

Tommy was playing with his toy cars. He made them zoom around the carpet. "Vroom, vroom!" he said loudly. He didn't want to stop playing.

"Just five more minutes?" Tommy called back. His red race car was winning. His blue truck was catching up. This was the best part!

"Come on, sweetheart," Mom said gently. She appeared in his doorway. "Bath time helps you get ready for bed."

Tommy's shoulders drooped down low. Bath time felt so boring. Just soap and water. Just sitting still. He wanted to keep playing instead.

"Do I have to?" he asked. His bottom lip stuck out. His eyes looked sad and tired.

Mom smiled her warm, understanding smile. "What if we make it special tonight?" she asked. "I have a surprise for you."

Tommy perked up a little bit. He loved surprises very much. "What kind of surprise?" he wondered.

"You'll see," Mom said mysteriously. She winked one brown eye. "Bring your imagination with you."

Tommy followed Mom to the bathroom. The bathtub looked the same. White and clean and ordinary. Nothing looked special or different.

Mom turned on the warm water. It splashed and gurgled loudly. She poured in some bubble bath. Sweet lavender smell filled the air.

"Close your eyes," Mom whispered softly. Tommy squeezed his eyes shut tight. He heard Mom moving around. Something clinked and splashed gently.

"Open them now," she said.

Tommy gasped with pure delight! The bathtub sparkled like magic. Tiny LED lights floated everywhere. They glowed blue and green. They looked like underwater stars!

"Wow!" Tommy exclaimed with joy. His eyes grew round and wide. "It's like the ocean!"

Mom had added special bath lights. They made the water shimmer. The bubbles looked like sea foam. The whole bathroom felt magical.

Tommy climbed in very carefully. The warm water felt wonderful. The lights danced all around him. He was an ocean explorer!

"Look, Mom!" he said excitedly. "I'm swimming with mermaids!" He moved his arms slowly. The lights swirled and twirled.

Mom sat on the edge. She watched Tommy play happily. "What do you see down there?" she asked with a smile.

"Treasure chests!" Tommy announced proudly. He dove his hands underwater. The lights scattered like fish. "And seahorses and dolphins too!"

Tommy forgot all about his cars. This was much more fun! He made whooshing sounds underwater. He pretended to catch fish. The lights made everything magical.

Mom helped him wash his hair. Even shampooing felt like adventure. The bubbles were sea creatures. The washcloth was a friendly octopus.

"This is the best bath ever!" Tommy said happily. Water dripped from his chin. His cheeks were pink and rosy.

Tommy played until his fingers wrinkled. The water stayed perfectly warm. The lights kept twinkling brightly. He never wanted it to end.

Finally, Mom brought his fluffy towel. It was warm from the dryer. Tommy climbed out very reluctantly. The lights still glowed softly.

"Can we do this tomorrow?" Tommy asked hopefully. His eyes sparkled with excitement. "Please, please, please?"

Mom wrapped him up tight. The towel felt like a hug. "Of course we can," she promised. "Bath time can be special."

Tommy brushed his teeth quickly. He put on his pajamas. But he kept thinking about tomorrow. About the magical underwater world.

As Mom tucked him in, Tommy smiled widely. "I can't wait for bath time!" he said with pure joy.

"Sweet dreams, my little explorer," Mom whispered softly. She kissed his forehead.

Tommy closed his eyes peacefully. He dreamed of glowing fish. He dreamed of treasure hunts. He dreamed of tomorrow's magical bath.

Bath time wasn't boring anymore. It was the best adventure. Tommy couldn't wait to splash again.

The lights would be waiting. The magic would return tomorrow.

The end.
