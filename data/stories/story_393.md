# The Drum and Sister's Playground Song

At the sunny playground, a little red drum sat alone on a wooden bench. The drum had shiny silver sides and a bright white top. His name was Danny.

Danny loved to make music. *Tap-tap-tap!* went his drumsticks. *Boom-boom-boom!* went his round belly. But today, Danny felt sad and lonely.

"I wish someone would play with me," Danny sighed. He watched children swing on swings and slide down slides. They laughed and played together. Danny played his drum softly. *Tap... tap... tap.*

Just then, a little girl with curly brown hair skipped over. She wore a yellow dress with dancing flowers. Her name was Lily, and she was someone's big sister.

"Hello!" Lily said with a bright smile. "I love your drum music!"

Danny's drumsticks wiggled with excitement. "Really? Do you want to hear more?"

"Yes, please!" Lily clapped her hands together.

Danny played a happy rhythm. *Tap-tap-BOOM! Tap-tap-BOOM!* The music bounced around the playground like jumping beans.

Lily's eyes sparkled. "That sounds wonderful! Can I help somehow?"

"How can you help?" Danny asked curiously.

Lily thought for a moment. Then she grinned. "I know lots of songs! I can sing while you drum!"

"That sounds amazing!" Danny said. His drum top gleamed in the sunshine.

Lily began to sing a sweet song about friendship. Her voice was clear and happy like morning birds. Danny played along with gentle rhythms. *Tap-tap-tap, boom-boom-boom!*

The music sounded even better together! Other children stopped playing to listen. They smiled and nodded their heads to the beat.

"This is so much fun!" Lily laughed. "Let's try another song!"

Danny played faster rhythms. Lily sang louder melodies. They discovered they both loved silly songs about animals and dancing.

"Let's make up our own song!" Lily suggested.

"Yes!" Danny agreed excitedly.

Together, they created a special playground song. Lily sang about swings that touched the clouds. Danny drummed rhythms that sounded like children laughing. They sang about slides that felt like rainbows and sandboxes full of treasure.

Soon, other children gathered around them. A boy with freckles started clapping along. Twin sisters began dancing to the music. Everyone joined their playground concert!

"This is the best day ever!" Danny said, his drumsticks dancing in the air.

"I'm so happy we're friends!" Lily said, spinning in her yellow dress.

They played song after song together. When Lily sang high notes, Danny played soft taps. When Danny played loud booms, Lily sang strong melodies. They listened to each other carefully and made beautiful music.

As the sun began to set, painting the sky orange and pink, Danny and Lily sat together on the bench.

"Thank you for playing with me," Danny said. "Music is so much better with a friend."

"Thank you for sharing your drum," Lily replied. "Singing is more fun when someone plays along!"

They watched the other children head home. The playground grew quiet, but Danny didn't feel lonely anymore.

"Can we play together again tomorrow?" Lily asked hopefully.

"Every day!" Danny said with joy. "We make the best team!"

Lily gave Danny a gentle hug. "Best friends forever!"

"Best friends forever!" Danny agreed.

The next morning, Danny waited excitedly on the bench. When Lily arrived, she brought something special - a small tambourine!

"Now we have even more music to make!" she said.

Danny's drumsticks practically danced with happiness. Together, they filled the playground with the most wonderful sounds. Their friendship had grown into something magical.

From that day on, Danny and Lily met every morning at the playground. They created new songs, made other children smile, and discovered that everything was more fun when shared with a true friend.

*Tap-tap-BOOM!* went Danny's drum.

"La-la-la!" sang Lily's sweet voice.

And their friendship song echoed happily across the playground, bringing joy to everyone who heard it.
