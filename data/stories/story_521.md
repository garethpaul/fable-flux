# Bella's Musical Morning

The morning sun peeked through the curtains. It painted golden stripes across Bella Bunny's bedroom floor. From downstairs came a familiar sound. *Ding-dong-ding!* Mama's music bell was ringing.

"Music time, Bella!" called Mama's cheerful voice.

Bella's ears drooped down low. She pulled her soft pink blanket over her head. Music time happened every morning after breakfast. But today felt different somehow.

"I don't want to today," Bella whispered. Her whiskers twitched with worry. "What if I can't remember the songs?"

Yesterday, Bella had forgotten the words to "Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star." Her cheeks had turned bright pink. Music time felt scary instead of fun.

*Ding-dong-ding!* The bell rang again.

Bella's little paws clutched her favorite stuffed carrot. She took three deep breaths. Mama always said deep breaths help with big feelings.

Slowly, Bella hopped down the stairs. Her fluffy tail bounced with each step. In the living room, Mama sat by the piano. Colorful instruments covered the coffee table. Shakers, bells, and drums waited quietly.

"Good morning, my musical bunny," said Mama. Her eyes sparkled with love. "Ready for our special time?"

Bella shook her head. "I might forget again."

Mama patted the piano bench. "Come sit with me. Let's try something new today."

Bella climbed up beside Mama. The piano keys looked like a big smile. Black and white teeth grinning up at her.

"Close your eyes," whispered Mama. "Just listen."

Mama's fingers danced across the keys. Soft, gentle notes filled the air. They sounded like raindrops on flower petals. Like butterfly wings fluttering. Like happy sighs.

Bella's ears perked up. The music felt warm and cozy. Like being wrapped in Mama's hug.

"What do you hear?" asked Mama.

"It sounds like... like sunshine," said Bella. Her eyes stayed closed. "And maybe birds singing good morning."

"That's beautiful, Bella. Music isn't about remembering perfectly. It's about feeling happy inside."

Mama played another melody. This one bounced and giggled. It reminded Bella of hopscotch and jumping in puddles.

"Can I try?" asked Bella.

Her small paws touched the keys. *Plink-plonk-ping!* The sounds were wobbly and silly. But they were hers.

"Listen to your beautiful music!" said Mama.

Bella giggled. Her music did sound beautiful. Not perfect, but perfectly hers.

Mama reached for the shakers. "Let's make music together."

*Shake-shake-shake!* went Bella's shaker. *Plink-plonk-ping!* went the piano. Together they created something magical. Something that made Bella's heart dance.

"I'm making sunshine music!" laughed Bella. She shook her instrument high above her head. Golden morning light caught the colorful beads inside.

Mama picked up the small drum. *Boom-tap-boom!* It sounded like Bella's happy heartbeat.

Soon they were making the most wonderful noise. Shakers shaking, piano playing, drum drumming. Bella's giggles mixed with all the sounds. The living room filled with joy.

"Let's sing our own song," suggested Mama.

"About what?" asked Bella.

"About this perfect morning. About music time. About you!"

Bella thought for a moment. Then she began to sing. The words came from her heart. "Music time is fun for me. La-la-la and tee-hee-hee. Shakers shake and pianos play. Music makes a happy day!"

Mama clapped her paws. "That's the most beautiful song ever!"

Bella's chest puffed out with pride. She had made her very own song. No forgetting, no worrying. Just pure, happy music.

They played three more songs together. Each one felt like a little celebration. When the music bell rang to signal the end, Bella felt surprised.

"Is it over already?" she asked.

"Time goes fast when we're having fun," said Mama.

Bella hugged her shaker tight. "Can we do this again tomorrow?"

"Every single day," promised Mama. "Music time is our special time."

That night, Bella fell asleep humming her sunshine song. She dreamed of dancing piano keys and laughing drums. In her dreams, music time lasted forever.

When morning came again, Bella woke up smiling. She couldn't wait to hear Mama's music bell. Today would bring new songs and new joy.

*Ding-dong-ding!* There it was!

"Music time!" called Bella, hopping toward the stairs. Her heart sang with excitement.

Music time had become magical again.

The end.
