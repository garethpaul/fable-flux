# Papa's Magical Nap Time

Papa looked at the big clock. The little hand pointed to two. The big hand pointed to twelve. "Oh my," Papa said with a yawn. "It's nap time already."

Papa rubbed his tired eyes. He had been playing blocks with little Emma. He had been reading three whole books. He had been making silly faces in the mirror. Papa felt very, very sleepy.

But Papa didn't want to nap. "Nap time is boring," he mumbled. "I want to keep playing. I want to stay awake." Papa's shoulders drooped down low. His feet dragged across the soft carpet.

Emma giggled and pointed to Papa's bedroom. "Go nap, Papa! Sweet dreams!" she said with a big smile.

Papa walked slowly to his cozy room. The afternoon sun peeked through the curtains. It made golden stripes on his blue blanket. Papa sat on the edge of his bed. He still didn't want to sleep.

Papa pulled off his fuzzy slippers. He fluffed his pillow twice. He pulled the soft blanket up to his chin. The pillow felt like a fluffy cloud. The blanket smelled like fresh flowers.

Papa closed his eyes tight. But something wonderful began to happen.

First, Papa heard gentle music. It sounded like tiny bells dancing. The music came from the sunbeams streaming through his window. Each golden ray sang a different note.

Then Papa felt his bed begin to float. Up, up, up it went! His bed became a magic carpet. It sailed through puffy white clouds. Papa giggled with delight.

"This is amazing!" Papa whispered to himself.

Papa's flying bed soared over rainbow mountains. He saw purple elephants having tea parties. He watched orange butterflies paint pictures with their wings. Blue dolphins jumped through cotton candy clouds.

Papa met a friendly dragon who breathed bubbles instead of fire. The dragon had sparkly green scales. "Hello, Papa!" said the dragon. "Welcome to Dreamland!"

"This place is wonderful!" Papa said. His eyes sparkled with joy.

Papa and the dragon played hide-and-seek behind silver stars. They slid down moonbeams like playground slides. They had a picnic on a cloud made of marshmallows.

The dragon showed Papa a magical garden. Flowers sang lullabies in the gentle breeze. Trees grew cookies instead of leaves. A chocolate river bubbled and gurgled nearby.

"Can I come back tomorrow?" Papa asked hopefully.

"Of course!" the dragon said. "Dreamland is always here during nap time. Sweet dreams, Papa!"

Papa felt his bed floating back down. Down, down, down through the clouds. The music grew softer and softer. Papa landed gently in his cozy room.

Papa stretched his arms wide. He felt refreshed and happy. His body felt light as a feather. His mind felt clear and bright.

"Nap time was magical!" Papa said with wonder.

Papa put on his fuzzy slippers. He walked to the living room. Emma was playing with her dolls.

"How was your nap, Papa?" Emma asked.

"It was the best nap ever!" Papa said. His face glowed with happiness. "I went on an amazing adventure!"

Papa told Emma about the singing sunbeams. He described the bubble-breathing dragon. He explained about the marshmallow clouds. Emma's eyes grew wide with excitement.

"Tomorrow I want to nap too!" Emma said.

Papa smiled the biggest smile. "Nap time is actually wonderful," he realized. "It's not boring at all!"

That evening, Papa could hardly wait. He kept looking at the clock. He wanted tomorrow to come quickly.

"I can't wait for nap time!" Papa told Emma at bedtime. "I wonder what new adventures await!"

Emma hugged Papa tight. "Sweet dreams tonight, Papa. And sweet nap dreams tomorrow!"

Papa tucked Emma into bed. He gave her a gentle kiss. Papa felt so grateful for magical nap times.

From that day on, Papa loved nap time. Every afternoon brought new adventures. Every nap brought new friends. Every dream brought pure joy.

Papa learned that the most ordinary things can become extraordinary. Sometimes magic happens when we least expect it. And sometimes the best adventures happen when we close our eyes.

Nap time became Papa's favorite time of day.

The end.
