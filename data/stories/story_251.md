# Melody's Music Time

Melody the little horse lived in a cozy red barn with her mama and papa. Every morning after breakfast, Mama would call out, "Melody, it's music time!"

But today, Melody felt different. She shuffled her hooves slowly. Music time seemed boring. All that singing and dancing. All those silly sounds.

"Do I have to?" Melody asked softly.

Mama smiled warmly. "Come try, little one. Just for a few minutes."

Melody dragged her hooves across the wooden floor. *Clip-clop, clip-clop.* She sat down with a big sigh. The piano looked so quiet. The drums seemed sleepy too.

Mama began to play a gentle tune. *Plink, plink, plink.* The notes danced in the air like tiny butterflies. But Melody just stared at her hooves.

"Would you like to try?" Mama asked.

Melody shook her head. "I don't feel musical today."

Mama nodded and kept playing. The melody was soft and sweet. Like raindrops on flower petals. Like wind through tall grass.

Then something wonderful happened. Melody's hoof began to tap. *Tap, tap, tap.* She didn't even notice at first. The music was pulling her along.

*Tap, tap, tap.* Her other hoof joined in. Soon both front hooves were dancing to the beat.

"Oh!" Melody gasped. "My hooves are dancing by themselves!"

Mama laughed like tinkling bells. "Music has magic, doesn't it?"

Melody stood up slowly. Her whole body wanted to move. She swayed left and right. The music flowed through her like warm sunshine.

"Can I play too?" Melody asked excitedly.

"Of course, my dear!"

Melody trotted to the little drums. She tapped them gently. *Boom, boom, pat!* What a wonderful sound! Deep and round and happy.

Mama played the piano while Melody drummed. Together they made beautiful music. The notes swirled around the barn like colorful ribbons.

"Let's sing too!" Melody neighed joyfully.

They sang about sunshine and flowers. They sang about friendship and love. Melody's voice was clear and sweet. It mixed perfectly with Mama's gentle melody.

Melody galloped around the barn while singing. Her mane flew behind her like a golden flag. Her hooves kept perfect time with the music.

*Clip-clop, boom-boom, la-la-la!*

The barn filled with their happy sounds. Even the birds outside began chirping along. The whole world seemed to be making music together.

Melody tried the triangle next. *Ting!* Such a bright, shiny sound! Then she shook the maracas. *Shake-shake-shake!* They sounded like rain on leaves.

"I'm a music horse!" Melody declared proudly.

She danced with the tambourine. She marched with the wooden sticks. Every instrument had its own special voice. And Melody loved them all.

Time passed quickly. The music made everything feel magical. The ordinary barn became a concert hall. Melody felt like a real musician.

"That was wonderful!" Mama said as they finished their last song.

Melody's eyes sparkled with joy. "Can we do more music tomorrow?"

"Every day, if you'd like," Mama promised.

Melody hugged her mama tight. "I love music time! It makes my heart feel happy and light."

That evening, Melody hummed softly as she ate her dinner. She hummed while brushing her teeth. She even hummed herself to sleep.

In her dreams, she danced on clouds. She played drums made of thunder. She sang with the stars themselves.

When morning came, Melody woke up excited. She galloped downstairs for breakfast. She ate quickly and cleaned her bowl.

Then she heard the most wonderful words: "Melody, it's music time!"

"Hooray!" Melody neighed happily. She pranced to the music corner with joy in her heart.

Music time wasn't boring anymore. It was magical. It was special. It was the best part of every day.

And from that morning on, Melody always looked forward to making beautiful music with Mama in their cozy red barn.

The end.
