# Story story_782

**Teddy's Magical Cleanup Time**

"Cleanup time, Teddy!" called Mama from the kitchen.

Teddy looked around his playroom. Toy cars covered the blue carpet. Building blocks made tall towers everywhere. His stuffed animals sat in funny places. Books lay open like sleeping birds.

Teddy's shoulders dropped down low. His bottom lip stuck out. "But I don't want to clean up," he whispered. "It's too much work."

The playroom felt so messy and big. Where should he start? Teddy sat down on the soft carpet. He put his chin in his hands.

Just then, something sparkly caught his eye. A tiny golden light danced near his toy box. It twirled and spun like a happy firefly.

"Hello, Teddy," said a sweet, tiny voice. "I'm Sparkle, the Cleanup Fairy!"

Teddy's eyes grew wide and round. "A real fairy?" he asked.

Sparkle nodded her glittery head. "I help children make cleanup time magical. Want to try?"

"How?" asked Teddy, feeling curious now.

"First, we'll make it a game," Sparkle said. "Can you find all the red toys? Let's count them together!"

Teddy jumped up. He spotted a red fire truck. "One!" he called out happily. Then he found red building blocks. "Two, three, four!"

His feet moved faster now. The red toys seemed to sparkle as he picked them up. "Five, six, seven!" Teddy giggled as he placed them in the toy box.

"Now try the blue toys," Sparkle suggested. Her tiny wings made a gentle humming sound.

Teddy's eyes searched everywhere. Blue cars, blue books, blue puzzle pieces. "Look how many I found!" he said proudly.

"You're doing wonderful work," Sparkle cheered. "Now let's make the blocks dance!"

Sparkle waved her tiny wand. The colorful blocks seemed to float gently into neat stacks. Teddy clapped his hands with joy. "This is like magic!"

"Everything has a special home," Sparkle explained. "Books live on shelves. Cars park in the toy box. Stuffed animals rest on beds."

Teddy nodded. He picked up his brown teddy bear. "Mr. Cuddles lives on my pillow," he said. The bear felt soft and warm in his arms.

Together, they made cleanup into a wonderful game. Teddy sorted toys by colors. He lined up his cars like a parade. His books stood tall like brave soldiers.

"What about this?" asked Teddy. He held up a puzzle piece.

"Puzzle pieces love being together," Sparkle said. "They feel happy when they're complete."

Teddy found the puzzle box. He placed the piece inside carefully. "There you go, little piece. Now you're home."

Soon the playroom looked neat and tidy. The blue carpet showed again. Toys sat in their proper places. Books stood straight and proud.

"Wow!" said Teddy, spinning around slowly. "My room looks beautiful!"

Sparkle's light grew brighter. "You did all the work, Teddy. I just helped you see the magic."

Teddy felt proud and happy inside. His chest puffed out like a superhero. "Cleanup time is actually fun!"

"The magic happens when you try," Sparkle explained. "Every toy feels loved when it has a home."

Mama peeked into the room. "Oh my! What a wonderful job, Teddy!"

Teddy beamed with pride. "I made cleanup time magical, Mama!"

Sparkle winked at Teddy. "Remember, the magic is always inside you. You just need to believe."

The tiny fairy's light grew softer. Then she disappeared with a gentle twinkle.

Teddy looked around his clean, cozy room. Everything looked perfect and peaceful. He felt calm and proud.

"Tomorrow I'll clean up again," Teddy announced. "Maybe Sparkle will visit!"

Mama gave him a big, warm hug. "You're my special helper," she said.

That night, Teddy fell asleep quickly. He dreamed of dancing toys and sparkling lights. In his dreams, cleanup time was always magical and fun.

The next morning, Teddy woke up excited. He couldn't wait to play and clean up again.

The end.
