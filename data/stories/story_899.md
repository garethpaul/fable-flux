# Story story_899

**Mimi's Magical Nap Time**

The sun was high in the sky. Birds sang soft, sleepy songs. It was nap time in the jungle.

"Mimi!" called Mama Monkey. "Time for your nap!"

Little Mimi swung from branch to branch. Her brown fur was warm from playing. She did not want to stop.

"But I'm not sleepy," Mimi said. She hung upside down from her tail. "I want to play more!"

Mama Monkey smiled. "Come here, little one. Your body needs rest."

Mimi's shoulders drooped. She slowly climbed down to their cozy tree home. The nest was made of soft leaves and fluffy moss.

"I don't like naps," Mimi grumbled. She flopped onto her leaf bed. The moss felt cool against her cheek.

Mama tucked a banana leaf over Mimi like a blanket. "Close your eyes, sweet pea. Try to rest."

Mimi squeezed her eyes shut tight. She wiggled her toes. She scratched her ear. Sleep felt so far away.

Then something strange happened. Tiny sparkles of light danced behind her eyelids. They twinkled like stars.

"What's that?" Mimi whispered. She opened one eye.

A gentle voice spoke. "Hello, Mimi. I'm the Dream Fairy."

A tiny fairy with butterfly wings floated near Mimi's nose. She glowed with silver light. Her voice sounded like wind chimes.

"Would you like to go on an adventure?" asked the fairy.

Mimi's eyes grew wide. "An adventure? During nap time?"

"The best adventures happen when you sleep," said the fairy. "Close your eyes and I'll show you."

Mimi felt curious now. She closed her eyes again. The sparkles grew brighter.

Suddenly, Mimi was flying! She soared above the jungle trees. The wind tickled her fur. Below, she saw colorful flowers and winding rivers.

"This is amazing!" Mimi laughed. She did loops in the fluffy white clouds.

The Dream Fairy giggled. "Where would you like to go?"

"To the rainbow!" Mimi pointed at a bright rainbow in the distance.

They flew to the rainbow together. Mimi slid down each colored stripe. Red felt warm like sunshine. Blue felt cool like water. Yellow tasted like sweet bananas.

Next, they visited a field of giant flowers. Each flower was bigger than Mimi! She bounced from petal to petal like soft trampolines. The flowers smelled like vanilla and honey.

"Can we visit the ocean?" asked Mimi.

"Of course!" said the fairy.

They dove into crystal clear water. Mimi could breathe underwater in her dream. Friendly dolphins swam with her. Colorful fish danced around her fingers.

She rode on a sea turtle's back. The water felt silky smooth. Coral reefs sparkled like jewels below.

"This is the best day ever!" Mimi said.

The Dream Fairy smiled. "Dream adventures are always special. But now it's time to rest."

The magical world began to fade. Mimi felt herself floating back to her cozy nest. The sparkles grew dimmer and dimmer.

"Will you come back tomorrow?" Mimi asked sleepily.

"Every nap time," promised the fairy. "Sweet dreams, little one."

Mimi felt so peaceful now. Her body was warm and relaxed. The soft moss cradled her like a hug. She drifted into the deepest, most wonderful sleep.

When Mimi woke up, the sun was lower in the sky. Mama Monkey was picking fresh fruit for snack time.

"Did you have a good nap?" Mama asked.

"The best nap ever!" Mimi stretched her arms wide. "I went flying and saw rainbows and swam with dolphins!"

Mama smiled knowingly. "Dreams can be magical, can't they?"

Mimi nodded eagerly. She felt refreshed and happy. Her energy was back, but now she understood something new.

"Mama, can we have nap time again tomorrow?" Mimi asked.

"Of course, sweetheart. Every day."

Mimi hugged her mama tight. She couldn't wait for tomorrow's nap time adventure. Maybe she would visit the moon or dance with butterflies.

From that day on, Mimi loved nap time. She learned that rest could be just as exciting as play. And the Dream Fairy kept her promise, visiting every single day.

The end.
