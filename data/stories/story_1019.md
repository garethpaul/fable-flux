# Story story_1019

**Nutkin's Magical Cleanup**

The sun was setting behind the tall oak tree. Mama Squirrel looked around their cozy tree house. Toys were scattered everywhere. Acorns rolled across the floor. Books lay open on the soft moss carpet.

"Cleanup time, Nutkin!" called Mama Squirrel.

Little Nutkin was playing with his favorite pinecone car. He pushed it around in circles. The car made quiet whooshing sounds on the wooden floor.

"But I'm still playing," Nutkin said. His fluffy tail drooped down. "Cleanup is boring."

Nutkin looked at all his toys. There were so many! His wooden blocks were in one corner. His acorn collection was spread under the window. His soft leaf pillow was on the floor.

"This will take forever," Nutkin sighed.

Mama Squirrel smiled gently. "Let me show you something special," she said.

She picked up a shiny acorn from the floor. As she held it up, the acorn began to glow! Soft golden light danced around it.

"Wow!" Nutkin's eyes grew wide. "It's magic!"

"Every toy has a special home," Mama said. "When you put things where they belong, they become happy. Happy toys glow!"

Nutkin reached for his pinecone car. He placed it carefully in the toy box. Suddenly, the car sparkled with warm yellow light!

"It worked!" Nutkin giggled. His whiskers twitched with excitement.

He picked up his wooden blocks next. Each block felt smooth and cool in his paws. One by one, he stacked them in their special corner. The blocks glowed bright blue!

"Look, Mama! They're dancing!" Nutkin pointed at the twinkling lights.

The room filled with gentle, colorful glows. Red light came from the book shelf. Green light shimmered from the acorn jar. Purple sparkles floated around the art supplies.

Nutkin scurried around the room. His little paws moved quickly. He hummed a happy tune as he worked. "This toy goes here. That book goes there!"

The leaf pillow glowed soft pink when Nutkin fluffed it. He placed it perfectly on his tiny bed. The pillow felt warm and cozy under his paws.

"Everything looks so pretty!" Nutkin said. He spun around in a circle. His bushy tail swished behind him.

Mama Squirrel clapped her paws together. "You did wonderful work, sweetie!"

The magical lights slowly faded away. But the tree house looked beautiful. Every toy was in its place. The floor was clear and clean. Everything smelled fresh like pine needles.

Nutkin climbed onto his glowing pillow. He felt proud and happy. His chest puffed out with joy.

"Mama, will the toys glow again tomorrow?" he asked.

"Only if you take good care of them," Mama said. She tucked a soft moss blanket around him.

Nutkin yawned a tiny yawn. "I can't wait for cleanup time tomorrow!"

"Why is that?" Mama asked.

"Because making things happy is fun!" Nutkin said. His eyelids felt heavy and sleepy.

Outside their window, fireflies blinked in the dark forest. The moon cast silver light through the leaves. A gentle breeze made the tree branches sway.

"I love you, little one," Mama whispered.

"I love you too, Mama," Nutkin said softly.

As Nutkin drifted off to sleep, he dreamed about glowing toys. In his dream, he was the best cleanup helper in the whole forest. All the woodland animals came to see his magical tree house.

The next morning, Nutkin woke up excited. He looked around his neat, tidy room. Everything was still in its special place.

"Today I'll be extra careful with my toys," he promised himself. "Then cleanup time will be magical again!"

Nutkin jumped out of bed with a big smile. He couldn't wait to play and later watch his toys glow. Cleanup time wasn't boring anymore. It was the most magical part of his day!

The end.
