# Story story_852

**The Lonely Doll and the Friendly Squirrel**

Ruby was a pretty doll with soft yellow hair. She sat alone on the big playground swing. The sun felt warm on her pink dress. But Ruby felt sad inside.

"I wish I had a friend to play with," Ruby said quietly. She watched other toys playing together. They laughed and ran around the colorful playground. Ruby pushed herself gently on the swing. Back and forth, back and forth.

The metal chains made a soft creaking sound. Ruby's shiny black shoes touched the sandy ground. She looked at the tall slide. She looked at the spinning merry-go-round. Everything looked fun. But playing alone wasn't very fun at all.

High up in an oak tree, a small gray squirrel watched Ruby. His name was Nutkin. He had a fluffy tail and bright black eyes. Nutkin saw Ruby sitting all by herself. She looked very lonely.

"Maybe she wants to play," Nutkin thought. He scampered down the rough tree bark. His tiny claws made scratching sounds. Nutkin's heart beat fast. He was a little nervous.

Nutkin hopped closer to Ruby's swing. "Hello," he said in a squeaky voice. "I'm Nutkin. What's your name?"

Ruby looked up with surprise. "I'm Ruby," she said softly. "Are you talking to me?"

"Yes!" Nutkin's tail twitched with excitement. "Would you like to play together? I know fun games we can play."

Ruby's blue eyes grew bright. "Really? You want to play with me?"

"Of course!" Nutkin did a little flip. "Want to go down the slide first?"

Ruby jumped off the swing. Her feet landed with a small thump. "That sounds wonderful!"

They ran to the tall red slide together. Ruby climbed up the ladder carefully. Her hands gripped each rung. Nutkin scrambled up the outside. His claws helped him climb fast.

"This is so high!" Ruby said at the top. She could see the whole playground. The swings looked tiny below.

"Don't worry," Nutkin said kindly. "I'll go first to show you."

Nutkin whooshed down the smooth slide. "Wheeeee!" he called out. He landed in the soft sand with a tiny puff.

Ruby took a deep breath. She pushed off and slid down fast. "This is amazing!" she laughed. The wind made her yellow hair flutter. She felt so happy and free.

They played on the seesaw next. Ruby sat on one end. Nutkin jumped on the other. Up and down they went. The seesaw made happy squeaking sounds.

"Higher!" Ruby giggled. "This is the best day ever!"

Next they tried the merry-go-round. Nutkin pushed while Ruby held on tight. Round and round they spun. The world became a blur of colors. Green trees, blue sky, yellow flowers all mixed together.

"Faster, faster!" Ruby called out. Her laughter filled the air like music.

When they got dizzy, they flopped on the cool grass. Fluffy white clouds drifted overhead. A gentle breeze made the leaves whisper.

"Thank you for playing with me," Ruby said. "I was so lonely before."

"I was lonely too," Nutkin admitted. "I watched other animals play together. But I was too shy to ask."

Ruby smiled her biggest smile. "We're friends now, right?"

"Best friends forever!" Nutkin declared. He did three happy flips in a row.

They spent the rest of the day exploring together. They found smooth pebbles and pretty flowers. They made up silly songs and told funny jokes. The playground felt magical with a friend to share it.

As the sun began to set, they sat on the swing together. Ruby held Nutkin gently in her lap. His fur felt soft and warm.

"Same time tomorrow?" Ruby asked hopefully.

"Absolutely!" Nutkin's eyes sparkled. "We have so many more games to play!"

Ruby wasn't lonely anymore. She had found something wonderful. A true friend who made every day an adventure. The playground would never feel empty again.

From that day on, Ruby and Nutkin played together every single day. Their friendship grew stronger with each laugh they shared.

The end.
