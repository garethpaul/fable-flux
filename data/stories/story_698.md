# Story story_698

**Maya's Musical Day**

The morning sun painted golden stripes through the jungle trees. All the animals gathered in the sunny clearing. It was music time!

"Come on, Maya!" called Elephant, waving his trunk. "Music time is starting!"

Maya the monkey sat high in her favorite mango tree. She wrapped her long tail around a branch. Her tummy felt funny and twisted.

"I don't want to come down," Maya whispered. She watched her friends below. They looked so excited.

Parrot flew up to Maya's branch. His bright feathers sparkled in the sunlight. "What's wrong, little friend?"

"Music time is scary," Maya said quietly. "What if I'm too loud? What if I mess up?"

Down below, the other animals began to play. Elephant tapped his big feet. THUMP, THUMP, THUMP! Rabbit shook colorful maracas. SHAKE, SHAKE, SHAKE!

The sounds drifted up to Maya. They made her ears tingle. Her toes started to wiggle.

"Just listen for now," Parrot said gently. "You don't have to play yet."

Maya closed her eyes. The music felt warm like sunshine. Turtle played a wooden drum. TAP, TAP, TAP! Frog croaked a happy tune. RIBBIT, RIBBIT, RIBBIT!

Something magical was happening. The music seemed to dance in the air. Maya could almost see it floating like colorful butterflies.

Her foot began to tap on the branch. Then her other foot joined in. Soon her whole body was swaying.

"The music is calling to you," Parrot smiled. "Can you feel it?"

Maya nodded. The scared feeling in her tummy was melting away. The music felt like a warm hug.

Slowly, Maya climbed down from her tree. Her friends saw her coming. They all smiled big, welcoming smiles.

"Maya!" cheered Rabbit. "We saved a special spot for you!"

In the center of the circle sat a small drum. It was just the right size for monkey hands. The drum was painted with swirls of blue and yellow.

Maya touched the drum gently. It felt smooth and warm. She tapped it once with her finger. BONG! The sound was soft and pretty.

"Try both hands," suggested Turtle kindly.

Maya placed both hands on the drum. She tapped a gentle rhythm. BONG, BONG, BONG! The sound mixed perfectly with her friends' music.

Her heart began to sing. This wasn't scary at all! This was wonderful!

Elephant trumpeted a joyful tune. Maya added her drum beats. BONG, BONG, BONG! Parrot whistled high notes. Frog croaked low notes.

All the sounds swirled together like a musical rainbow. Maya felt like she was flying even though her feet stayed on the ground.

"You're a natural musician!" called Elephant happily.

Maya's tail swished with excitement. She tried different rhythms. Fast beats, slow beats, loud beats, soft beats. Each one sounded perfect with her friends.

The music filled the whole jungle. Birds in distant trees began to chirp along. Even the leaves seemed to dance in the breeze.

When music time ended, Maya didn't want to stop. Her hands tingled from playing. Her heart felt light and happy.

"That was amazing!" Maya giggled. "I felt like the music was magic!"

"Music is magic," agreed Turtle wisely. "It brings friends together."

All the animals hugged Maya. She felt so proud and happy. The scared feeling was completely gone.

As the sun climbed higher, the animals said goodbye. Maya helped put away the instruments. She touched her special drum one more time.

"See you tomorrow, little drum," she whispered.

Walking home through the jungle, Maya hummed her favorite tune from today. Her steps had a bouncy rhythm. Everything felt musical now.

That night, Maya dreamed of colorful music floating through the trees. In her dream, she played her drum under a sky full of singing stars.

When morning came, Maya woke up excited. She looked toward the clearing where they played music.

"I can't wait for music time!" she said out loud.

Maya swung through the trees toward breakfast. But in her heart, she was already making beautiful music with her friends.

Tomorrow would bring another magical music day.

The end.
