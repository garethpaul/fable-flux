# Story story_1154

**Buddy's Bubble Adventure**

Buddy the golden dog loved many things. He loved running in the yard. He loved chasing butterflies. He loved eating his favorite treats. But Buddy did not love bath time.

"Come here, Buddy!" called Mom. "Time for your bath!"

Buddy's ears drooped down. His tail stopped wagging. He could smell the warm, soapy water from the bathroom. The sound of water filling the tub made him want to hide.

"I don't want a bath," Buddy whimpered softly. He tried to squeeze under the couch. Only his fluffy tail stuck out.

Mom laughed gently. "Oh, Buddy. Baths help you stay clean and healthy."

She carefully lifted him up. Buddy's paws felt heavy as they walked to the bathroom. The white bathtub looked so big and scary.

Mom placed Buddy in the warm water. It felt nice on his paws. But he still didn't like it much.

"Let's add some bubbles," Mom said with a smile.

She squeezed a bottle of special dog shampoo. Sweet-smelling bubbles began to form. They grew bigger and bigger. Soon the whole tub sparkled with rainbow bubbles!

Buddy's brown eyes grew wide. He had never seen so many pretty bubbles before. One landed on his black nose. It tickled and made him giggle.

"The bubbles are dancing!" Buddy barked happily.

More bubbles floated around him. Some were tiny like pearls. Others were big like balloons. They shimmered in the bathroom light.

Buddy tried to catch a bubble with his paw. Pop! It disappeared with a tiny splash. He tried to catch another one. Pop! That was fun!

"Look, Buddy!" Mom pointed up high. "The bubbles are flying!"

Hundreds of bubbles floated toward the ceiling. They looked like tiny clouds. Buddy watched them spin and twirl through the air.

Mom began washing his golden fur. The warm water felt like a gentle hug. The soap smelled like fresh flowers. Buddy's muscles relaxed as Mom's hands massaged his back.

"This feels really good," Buddy said, closing his eyes.

A big bubble landed on his ear. Another one sat on top of his head like a crown. Buddy looked in the bathroom mirror. He looked like a bubble king!

"I'm the bubble king!" he barked with joy.

Mom laughed as she rinsed his fur. Clean, warm water ran down his sides. All the dirt from playing outside washed away. Buddy felt fresh and light.

More bubbles kept appearing. They covered his paws like little mittens. They decorated his tail like party balloons. The whole bathroom became a magical bubble land.

"Can we make more bubbles?" Buddy asked excitedly.

"Of course!" Mom squeezed the bottle again.

Even more bubbles filled the air. Buddy tried to pop them all. He splashed happily in the warm water. Bath time wasn't scary anymore. It was the best game ever!

Soon it was time to get out. Mom wrapped Buddy in a big, fluffy towel. It felt soft and cozy. She dried his fur until it was clean and shiny.

Buddy looked at himself in the mirror. His golden fur sparkled. He smelled wonderful. He felt amazing.

"I love being clean!" Buddy said proudly.

Mom gave him his favorite treat. It tasted extra delicious after his bubble bath.

That night, Buddy dreamed about dancing bubbles. They floated around him like magic stars. In his dream, he was still the bubble king.

The next morning, Buddy woke up excited. His fur still smelled like flowers. He remembered all the fun bubbles from yesterday.

"Mom!" Buddy barked happily. "When is my next bath?"

Mom smiled and hugged him tight. "Tomorrow, my clean little bubble king."

Buddy's tail wagged fast. He couldn't wait for another bubble adventure. Bath time was now his favorite time of day!

From that day on, Buddy always ran to the bathroom when Mom called. He knew magical bubbles were waiting for him.

The end.
