# Story story_1491

**Tiger and Ben at the Playground**

Tiger sat alone on the big red swing. His orange and black stripes looked bright in the sunny playground. But Tiger felt sad inside. He pushed the swing back and forth with his paws.

"I wish I had a friend to play with," Tiger whispered softly.

The playground was full of wonderful things. There were tall slides that sparkled in the sun. There were monkey bars that gleamed silver. There was a sandbox with soft, warm sand. But Tiger played by himself every day.

Tiger's ears drooped down. His tail dragged on the ground. Playing alone was no fun at all.

Just then, a little boy walked into the playground. His name was Ben. Ben had curly brown hair and wore a bright blue shirt. He carried a small red ball in his hands.

Ben saw Tiger sitting alone on the swing. Tiger looked very sad. Ben's heart felt warm and kind. He wanted to help.

"Hi there," Ben said gently. "I'm Ben. What's your name?"

Tiger looked up with surprise. His yellow eyes grew wide. "I'm Tiger," he said quietly. "Are you talking to me?"

"Yes!" Ben smiled big. "Would you like to play with me?"

Tiger's heart jumped with joy. "Really? You want to play with me?"

"Of course!" Ben bounced his red ball. "Playing is more fun with friends."

Tiger hopped off the swing. His paws made soft sounds on the rubber ground. "What should we play?"

Ben thought for a moment. "Let's play catch!"

Ben threw the red ball gently to Tiger. Tiger caught it with his big paws. The ball felt smooth and bouncy. Tiger threw it back to Ben.

"Good catch!" Tiger said happily.

Back and forth they threw the ball. Ben laughed when Tiger made funny faces. Tiger giggled when Ben did a silly dance. The warm sun shone down on them.

"Let's try the slide!" Ben suggested.

They ran to the tall blue slide together. Their feet pounded on the ground. Tiger climbed up first. His claws helped him grip the ladder. Ben followed right behind.

"Wheee!" Tiger slid down fast. His stripes blurred together.

"That looks fun!" Ben slid down next. "Wheee!"

They climbed up and slid down again and again. Each time felt more exciting than the last.

Next, they played in the sandbox. The sand felt cool and soft between their fingers and paws. They built a castle together. Ben made the walls. Tiger dug a moat around it.

"Our castle is beautiful," Ben said proudly.

"We make a great team," Tiger agreed.

Then they swung on the swings side by side. Tiger pushed Ben's swing gently with his paw. Ben pushed Tiger's swing back. They swung higher and higher. The wind felt cool on their faces.

"This is the best day ever!" Tiger roared happily.

"I think so too!" Ben laughed.

As the sun began to set, they sat together on a bench. The sky turned pretty colors of pink and orange. Tiger's stripes looked golden in the soft light.

"Thank you for playing with me," Tiger said. "I was very lonely before."

"Thank you for being my friend," Ben replied. "You're the nicest tiger I know."

"Will you come back tomorrow?" Tiger asked hopefully.

"Yes!" Ben promised. "We can play every day if you want."

Tiger's whole body felt warm and happy. His tail swished back and forth with joy. He had found something very special today.

"I've never had a best friend before," Tiger said softly.

"Well, now you do," Ben said with a big smile.

They hugged goodbye. Tiger's fur was soft and warm. Ben's hug was gentle and kind.

From that day on, Tiger and Ben played together every single day. The playground was filled with their laughter and joy. Tiger never felt lonely again.

And they both learned that the very best adventures happen when you have a good friend to share them with.
