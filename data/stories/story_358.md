# Bella Ball's Big Search

Bella Ball loved rolling around the sunny dining room. The wooden floor felt smooth under her bright red surface. She bounced softly between the tall chair legs, making gentle *thump-thump* sounds.

Bella's favorite thing was playing with her pretty doll, Rosie. Rosie had yellow yarn hair and a blue dress. Every morning, Bella would roll to find Rosie for their special playtime together.

But this morning was different. Bella rolled to Rosie's usual spot by the big table. No Rosie! She rolled to the corner by the window. Still no Rosie!

"Oh no!" said Bella Ball. "Where is my pretty doll? We always play together after breakfast!"

Bella felt worried. Her red surface seemed to dim a little. But she took a deep breath and rolled herself straight.

"I will find Rosie," Bella said firmly. "I won't give up!"

First, Bella rolled under the dining table. It was dark and dusty underneath. She bumped into table legs and chair legs. *Bonk! Bonk!* No Rosie there.

"Maybe Rosie is hiding," thought Bella. She rolled to check behind the tall bookshelf. She squeezed into the narrow space. Dust tickles made her want to sneeze! But no pretty doll was hiding there.

Next, Bella rolled to the corner where the big plant sat. The green leaves rustled softly above her. She looked all around the brown pot. Still no Rosie!

Bella started to feel sad. She sat still for a moment. The morning sun made warm golden squares on the floor. She watched the light dance and sparkle.

"Think, Bella, think!" she said to herself. "Where could Rosie be?"

Then Bella remembered yesterday's playtime. They had been rolling around together near the window seat. The cushions were soft and bouncy!

Bella rolled quickly to the window seat. She looked up at the tall cushions. They were too high for her to see over!

"How can I look up there?" wondered Bella.

She thought hard. Then she had an idea! Bella rolled backward, then forward very fast. She bounced up high! For just a moment, she could see the top of the cushions.

"There!" she gasped. "I see yellow hair!"

But how could she reach Rosie? Bella looked around the room. She spotted something helpful - the footstool! It sat near the big chair.

Bella rolled to the footstool. She pushed it with all her might toward the window seat. *Scrape, scrape, scrape* went the footstool across the floor.

"Almost there!" puffed Bella.

When the footstool was close enough, Bella rolled up onto it. Now she could reach the window seat cushions!

She rolled carefully onto the soft cushions. They felt squishy and warm from the sunshine. And there, tucked between two cushions, was Rosie!

"Rosie!" called Bella happily. "I found you!"

Rosie's blue dress was a little wrinkled, but she looked perfect. Her yellow yarn hair caught the sunlight and sparkled.

"I must have rolled too hard yesterday," said Bella. "You got stuck between the cushions!"

Bella gently nudged Rosie free. Together, they rolled down from the window seat onto the smooth floor. The dining room felt bright and cheerful again.

"I'm so happy we're together!" said Bella, spinning in a little circle. Rosie's dress fluttered in the breeze.

They spent the rest of the morning playing their favorite games. They rolled around the table legs together. They practiced gentle bounces by the bookshelf. The *thump-thump* sounds filled the dining room with joy.

As the sun climbed higher, Bella felt proud and happy. She had felt worried when Rosie was lost. She had felt tired when searching was hard. But she never gave up!

"When something important is lost," Bella said to Rosie, "we keep looking until we find it. We think of new ideas. We try different ways!"

Rosie seemed to smile in the golden sunlight.

From that day on, Bella always remembered to be extra gentle during playtime. And whenever something seemed lost or hard to find, she remembered her big search. She would take a deep breath, think of new ideas, and never give up.

The dining room echoed with happy *thump-thump* sounds as Bella and Rosie played together, the best of friends once again.

The end.
