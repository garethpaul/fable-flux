# Mama's Musical Morning

It was music time at home. The clock showed nine o'clock. Little Emma bounced on her toes. "Music time, Mama! Music time!"

Mama looked at the piano. She felt very tired today. Her shoulders drooped down low. "Oh sweetie," Mama said softly. "Maybe we can skip today?"

Emma's face fell like a wilted flower. Her bottom lip trembled slightly. "But Mama, we always have music time. It's our special thing!"

Mama sighed deeply and slowly. She rubbed her sleepy eyes. The piano keys looked cold. The music books felt heavy. "I just don't feel musical today."

Emma tugged on Mama's soft sweater. "Please, Mama? Just one tiny song?" Her eyes sparkled like morning stars.

Mama's heart felt warm suddenly. She couldn't say no anymore. "Okay, little bird. One song only." She walked to the piano. Her feet felt like lead.

Mama sat on the piano bench. The seat felt hard today. She pressed one key gently. The note rang out clearly. *Ding!* It sounded lonely by itself.

"What song should we play?" Mama asked quietly. She still felt sleepy.

"The happy dancing song!" Emma clapped her hands together. She started wiggling her whole body.

Mama found the music sheet. Her fingers touched the keys. She played the first note. *Ding!* Then the second note. *Dong!* The melody began to flow.

Something wonderful started to happen. The music filled the cozy room. The notes danced in the air. Mama's fingers felt lighter suddenly. Her heart began to flutter.

Emma started dancing around the room. She spun like a beautiful butterfly. She giggled with pure joy. "It's working, Mama! The music magic!"

Mama felt the magic too. Her fingers moved more quickly. The melody grew stronger and brighter. The piano seemed to smile. The whole room felt alive.

"Let's add some clapping!" Mama said happily. Her voice sounded musical now. She played and clapped together. *Clap, clap, ding, dong!*

Emma clapped along with Mama. Their hands moved in perfect time. The rhythm filled their hearts. The music surrounded them completely.

"Now let's sing!" Mama called out. Her eyes sparkled with joy. She began to sing loudly. "La la la, we love music!"

Emma sang along very loudly. "La la la, music time!" Their voices blended together beautifully. The sound was pure happiness.

They played three more songs. Then four more songs after that. The piano keys felt warm. The music books felt light. Time flew by like birds.

Mama's whole body felt different now. Her shoulders stood up tall. Her smile stretched ear to ear. Her heart felt full of sunshine.

"That was the best music time!" Emma hugged Mama tightly. "You played so beautifully today!"

Mama hugged Emma back warmly. "You're right, sweet pea. That was truly magical." She looked at the piano. It seemed to wink back.

The room still hummed with music. Happy notes seemed to float. The air felt sparkly and bright. Everything looked more colorful now.

"Can we play more tomorrow?" Emma asked hopefully. She bounced on her toes again.

Mama laughed a musical laugh. "Of course we can! I can hardly wait." She meant every single word.

That night, Mama hummed softly. The melodies filled her dreams. She dreamed of dancing notes. She dreamed of Emma's bright smile.

The next morning came quickly. Mama woke up feeling excited. She looked at the piano. It called to her sweetly.

"Emma!" Mama called out cheerfully. "Are you ready for music time?"

Emma ran down the stairs. "You remembered, Mama! You remembered our magic!"

Mama sat at the piano. Her fingers felt ready to dance. Her heart felt ready to sing. Music time had become magical again.

From that day forward always, Mama looked forward to music time. She learned that joy lives inside. Sometimes it just needs music to wake it up.

The piano smiled every morning. Emma danced every single day. And Mama's heart sang with happiness.

Music time was magical indeed.

The end.
