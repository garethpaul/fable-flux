# The Bathroom Band

## Chapter 1: Splash, Splash, Lonely

Henry the horse stood in the big, sunny bathroom. Water dripped from the shiny faucet. *Drip, drop, drip, drop.* 

Henry loved bath time. He splashed his hooves gently. He made little waves. The warm water felt wonderful.

But something was missing. Henry sighed a big, sad sigh. "Playing alone isn't very fun," he whispered.

The bathroom felt too quiet. Too empty. Henry wished someone would come play.

## Chapter 2: A Tiny Visitor

*Tap, tap, tap!* 

Tiny paws scurried across the tile floor. A little gray squirrel peeked around the corner.

"Hi there!" squeaked Stella the squirrel. "I'm Stella! What are you doing?"

Henry's ears perked up. "I'm Henry! I'm taking a bath. But it's kind of lonely."

Stella's fluffy tail twitched with excitement. "Can I play too? I love water games!"

Henry smiled his first real smile. "Yes! Come join me!"

## Chapter 3: Making Music Together

Stella climbed onto the bathtub edge. She was small but very brave.

"Listen to this!" Henry said. He tapped his hoof on the tub. *Tap, tap, tap!*

"Ooh!" Stella clapped her tiny paws. "That sounds like music!"

Stella found a plastic cup. She filled it with water. Then she poured it out slowly. *Splash, trickle, splash!*

"We're making a song!" Henry laughed.

Together they created beautiful bathroom music. Henry kept the beat. Stella added the melody.

*Tap, splash, tap, trickle!*
*Tap, splash, tap, trickle!*

## Chapter 4: The Best Discovery

"I have an idea!" Stella said. Her eyes sparkled with excitement.

She found two rubber ducks. One was big. One was small.

"These can be our backup singers!" she giggled.

Henry pushed the big duck gently. *Squeak!* Stella made the little duck dance. *Squeak, squeak!*

"We both love making music!" Henry said happily.

"And we both love being silly!" Stella added.

They discovered they both loved:
- Splashing games
- Funny sounds
- Making friends laugh
- Sharing toys

Working together felt magical.

## Chapter 5: The Perfect Team

Henry and Stella played for a long time. They took turns. They shared ideas. They helped each other.

When Henry couldn't reach the soap, Stella helped. When Stella slipped on the wet edge, Henry caught her gently.

"You're the best friend ever!" Henry said.

"You are too!" Stella replied. "Everything is more fun together!"

They made up songs. They told jokes. They splashed carefully so no one got too wet.

The bathroom filled with laughter and joy.

## Chapter 6: Celebration Time

As the sun began to set, Henry and Stella sat together. The bathroom glowed with warm, golden light.

"Should we have a concert?" Stella asked.

"Yes!" Henry neighed happily.

They performed their bathroom song one more time. The rubber ducks squeaked along. The water sparkled like diamonds.

*Tap, splash, tap, trickle!*
*Squeak, squeak, hooray!*

"We make beautiful music together," Henry said.

"And we make even better friends," Stella added.

They hugged carefully. Henry was gentle with his new tiny friend. Stella felt safe and happy.

## Chapter 7: Friends Forever

From that day on, bath time was never lonely. Henry and Stella met every afternoon. They played water games. They sang songs. They shared stories.

Sometimes other animal friends joined them. The bathroom became the happiest place in the whole house.

Henry learned that sharing made everything better. Stella learned that big friends could be gentle and kind.

"Thank you for asking to play," Henry said one day.

"Thank you for saying yes," Stella replied.

They splashed together in the warm water. Their laughter echoed off the bathroom walls. The sound was better than any music.

Two friends had found each other. And that was the most wonderful thing of all.

*The End*

**What Henry and Stella Learned:**
- Friends make everything more fun
- Cooperation creates beautiful things
- Asking to play takes courage
- Sharing makes us all happier
- Different sized friends can play together perfectly
