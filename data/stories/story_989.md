# Story story_989

**Papa's Magical Bath Time**

"Bath time, Papa!" called Mama from the bathroom.

Papa Bear sat in his big chair. He loved reading his book. The chair felt so cozy and warm.

"Do I have to?" Papa asked with a sigh.

Papa's fur was dusty from working outside. His paws were muddy too. But baths seemed like so much work.

"Yes, you do," said Mama with a smile. "Come on, sleepy bear."

Papa walked slowly to the bathroom. His feet felt heavy on the floor. The bathtub looked very big and empty.

Mama turned on the water. It made a rushing sound. Steam began to rise up like little clouds.

"The water is too hot," Papa said.

Mama added some cold water. She tested it with her paw.

"Perfect now," she said. "In you go."

Papa put one paw in the water. It felt nice and warm. Then he put his other paw in. The warmth tickled his toes.

"This isn't so bad," Papa thought.

Mama poured water over Papa's back. The warm water felt like sunshine. Papa's muscles began to relax.

Then something amazing happened.

Mama added bubble soap to the water. Tiny bubbles started to form. They grew bigger and bigger.

"Look at all the bubbles!" Papa said with wonder.

The bubbles sparkled in the light. They looked like tiny rainbows. Some bubbles popped with soft little sounds.

Papa scooped up bubbles in his paws. He blew them gently. They floated through the air like magic.

"I'm a bubble wizard!" Papa laughed.

He made a bubble beard on his chin. Mama giggled at how silly he looked. Papa made bubble horns on his head too.

"Now I'm a bubble monster!" he roared playfully.

Mama splashed water gently at Papa. He splashed back with a big grin. Water droplets danced in the air.

The bathroom filled with happy sounds. Splashing, laughing, and bubble pops.

Papa found a yellow rubber duck. He made it swim through the bubbles. The duck bobbed up and down in the soapy water.

"Quack, quack," Papa said in a funny voice.

Mama washed Papa's fur with sweet-smelling soap. It smelled like fresh flowers and sunshine. The soap made his fur feel soft and clean.

Papa closed his eyes and enjoyed the gentle scrubbing. His whole body felt relaxed and happy.

"This feels wonderful," Papa said with a content sigh.

Mama washed behind Papa's ears. She was very gentle and careful. Papa felt loved and cared for.

The warm water made Papa feel sleepy in a good way. Like being wrapped in a cozy blanket.

"Time to rinse off," Mama said softly.

She poured clean water over Papa's head. The soap bubbles swirled down the drain. Papa's fur felt squeaky clean.

Papa stood up in the tub. Water dripped from his fur. He felt fresh and new all over.

Mama wrapped him in a big, fluffy towel. The towel was warm and soft against his skin.

"There's my clean bear," Mama said with love.

She rubbed the towel gently over Papa's fur. Soon he was dry and fluffy again.

Papa looked at himself in the mirror. His fur was shiny and clean. He smelled like flowers and felt wonderful.

"I look handsome," Papa said with pride.

He put on his cozy pajamas. They felt extra soft against his clean fur.

Papa gave Mama a big, warm hug.

"Thank you for the magical bath," he said.

"You're welcome, sweet bear," Mama replied.

Papa climbed into his comfortable bed. The clean sheets felt amazing against his fresh fur.

As he closed his eyes, Papa smiled big.

"Tomorrow I want another bubble bath," he said sleepily.

Mama kissed his forehead goodnight.

"Sweet dreams, my clean little bear," she whispered.

Papa drifted off to sleep. He dreamed of floating bubbles and warm, magical water.

Bath time wasn't scary anymore. It was the best part of his day.

Tomorrow couldn't come soon enough.

The end.
