# Story story_1235

**Lily's Special Snack Time**

The kitchen clock chimed three times. "Snack time, Lily!" called Mama from the kitchen. Lily sat on her bedroom floor with her favorite doll, Rose. Rose had curly brown hair and a soft pink dress.

"Come on, Rose," Lily whispered. "Time for snacks." But Lily felt tired. She didn't want to leave her cozy room. Playing with dolls was much more fun than eating.

Lily picked up Rose and walked slowly to the kitchen. Her feet dragged on the wooden floor. "I'm not very hungry," she told Mama. Mama smiled and set out apple slices and crackers. The apple pieces looked shiny and sweet.

"Rose looks hungry," said Mama, winking. Lily looked down at her doll. Rose's painted smile seemed bigger than before. How strange!

Lily sat at the small table. She placed Rose in the chair next to her. "Would you like some apple, Rose?" Lily asked. She pretended to give Rose a tiny bite. 

Suddenly, Rose's eyes seemed to sparkle. Lily blinked hard. Was she imagining things? She offered Rose another pretend bite. This time, Rose's head nodded just a little bit!

"Mama, did you see that?" Lily gasped. But Mama was washing dishes and humming softly. The warm sunlight streamed through the window. It made everything look golden and magical.

Lily took a bite of apple. The juice was sweet and cold. "Mmm, Rose, this tastes like sunshine!" she said. Rose seemed to smile even wider. Her pink dress shimmered in the light.

"Let's have a tea party," Lily decided. She got her tiny plastic cups from the toy box. Mama helped her pour water into the little cups. Lily set one cup in front of Rose.

"Cheers!" Lily said, lifting her cup. Rose's cup wobbled slightly. Lily's eyes grew wide with wonder. "You really are magic, aren't you?" she whispered.

As Lily ate her crackers, she told Rose about her day. "We painted butterflies at school. And Tommy shared his blocks with me." Rose listened carefully. Her button eyes seemed to twinkle with each word.

The apple slices crunched between Lily's teeth. They tasted fresh and crisp. She saved the sweetest piece for last. "This one's for you, Rose," she said, holding it near the doll's painted lips.

A warm breeze came through the open window. It made Rose's curly hair flutter gently. Lily giggled with delight. "You like apples too!"

Mama came over and sat down. "How's your snack, girls?" she asked. Lily beamed up at her. "Rose loves apples! And she's helping me eat everything!"

"That's wonderful," said Mama. "Good friends make everything better." She kissed the top of Lily's head. Her kiss felt warm and safe.

Lily finished her last cracker. Her tummy felt happy and full. "Thank you for sharing snack time with me, Rose," she said. She hugged her doll tight. Rose felt soft and warm in her arms.

"Tomorrow we can have snacks together again," Lily said. "Maybe we'll try strawberries!" Rose's smile seemed to grow even brighter. The afternoon sun made her dress glow like a real princess.

Lily helped Mama clean up the cups and plates. She carefully carried Rose back to the bedroom. "That was the best snack time ever," she told her doll. She tucked Rose into the tiny doll bed.

As Lily played quietly, she thought about tomorrow. She couldn't wait for snack time again. Having Rose there made everything more special. Even eating felt like an adventure.

The clock in the kitchen chimed four times. Lily smiled and patted Rose's head. "See you tomorrow for snacks, my magical friend," she whispered. Rose seemed to wink back, ready for their next delicious adventure together.

From that day on, Lily always looked forward to snack time. With Rose by her side, every bite was filled with magic and wonder.

The end.
