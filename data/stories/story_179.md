# Benny's Bubble Bath Magic

Benny the bunny sat on his bedroom floor. He was building a tall tower with blocks. The blocks were red, blue, and yellow. They made a perfect castle.

"Bath time, Benny!" called Mama Bunny from downstairs.

Benny's ears drooped down. His whiskers twitched sadly. He didn't want to stop playing. Bath time meant leaving his wonderful block castle. Bath time meant getting all wet and soapy.

"Do I have to?" Benny asked softly.

"Yes, little bunny," Mama said gently. "You've been playing outside all day. Your paws are muddy. Your fur is dusty."

Benny looked at his paws. They were indeed very muddy. Brown dirt was stuck between his toes. His white fur had gray smudges everywhere.

He sighed a big bunny sigh. "Okay, Mama."

Benny hopped slowly to the bathroom. Each hop felt heavy and sad. The bathroom was warm and steamy. Mama had already filled the bathtub. The water looked deep and inviting.

"I added something special today," Mama said. Her eyes twinkled with a secret.

She poured something from a purple bottle. Tiny bubbles started forming immediately. Then more bubbles appeared. Soon, hundreds of bubbles danced on the water.

Benny's eyes grew wide with wonder.

The bubbles weren't ordinary bubbles. They shimmered like tiny rainbows. Each bubble reflected the bathroom lights. Some bubbles were big as oranges. Others were small as pearls.

"Wow!" Benny whispered in amazement.

He dipped one paw carefully. The water felt perfectly warm. It tickled his toes nicely. A cluster of bubbles stuck to his paw. They felt soft and silky.

Benny climbed into the tub. The bubbles surrounded him completely. He felt like he was sitting in a cloud. A fluffy, magical cloud that sparkled.

"Look, Mama!" Benny giggled happily.

He scooped up bubbles carefully. He made a bubble beard. It felt tickly and funny. Mama laughed at his silly appearance.

Then Benny discovered something amazing. When he blew gently, the bubbles floated away. They danced through the air. They landed on the mirror. They stuck to the walls.

"I'm a bubble wizard!" Benny announced proudly.

He blew more bubbles everywhere. The bathroom became a magical kingdom. Bubbles floated like tiny balloons. They popped with soft sounds. Pop! Pop! Pop!

Mama helped wash Benny's fur. The soap smelled like lavender flowers. It made more beautiful bubbles. Benny's fur became clean and soft. The dirt washed away completely.

"Can I make bubble mountains?" asked Benny.

"Of course!" Mama smiled warmly.

Benny piled bubbles into tall peaks. He made bubble castles and towers. He pretended tiny bubble people lived there. They had bubble adventures together.

The warm water relaxed his muscles. His paws felt clean and fresh. His fur was fluffy and sweet-smelling. Benny felt wonderful all over.

"This is the best bath ever!" he declared.

Time passed quickly in the magical bathroom. Soon Mama brought a fluffy towel. It was soft and warm. She wrapped Benny up carefully.

"All clean, little bunny," she said lovingly.

Benny felt cozy and content. His fur was perfectly clean. He smelled like flowers and sunshine. The bath had been truly magical.

"Mama, can we have bubbles tomorrow?" Benny asked hopefully.

"We'll see," Mama said mysteriously.

That night, Benny dreamed about bubbles. He dreamed about floating in clouds. He dreamed about being a bubble wizard. In his dreams, every bath was magical.

The next evening came quickly. Benny was playing with puzzles. His paws got messy with crayons. His fur collected dust again.

"Bath time, Benny!" called Mama.

This time, Benny's ears perked up. His whiskers twitched with excitement. He hopped quickly to the bathroom. He couldn't wait for bubble magic.

"Can we make bubble castles again?" he asked eagerly.

Mama smiled and reached for the purple bottle. "Let's make this bath even more special," she said.

Benny clapped his paws together. Bath time had become his favorite time. Every day brought new bubble adventures. Every bath was filled with magic and joy.

The end.
