# Story story_833

**Finny's Garden Adventure**

The morning sun peeked through the water. It was garden time at the underwater school. All the little fish swam to their special growing patch.

"Time for garden time!" called Mrs. Seahorse cheerfully.

Finny the goldfish stayed behind a big rock. His fins drooped down sadly. "I don't want to garden today," he whispered.

Finny watched his friends swim away. Bella the blue fish loved planting seeds. Tommy the turtle enjoyed watering plants. But Finny felt different.

"Gardens are too hard," Finny said quietly. "My fins are too small. I can't dig like the others."

Mrs. Seahorse swam over gently. Her kind eyes sparkled like pearls. "What's wrong, little Finny?" she asked softly.

"I'm not good at gardening," Finny said. His voice was very quiet. "Everyone else knows what to do."

Mrs. Seahorse smiled warmly. "Would you like to try something special today?"

Finny's eyes grew wide with curiosity. He nodded his head slowly.

Mrs. Seahorse led him to a magical corner. Tiny glowing seeds floated in the water. They sparkled like stars.

"These are singing seeds," Mrs. Seahorse explained. "They grow when they hear beautiful sounds."

Finny gasped with wonder. The seeds danced and twirled around him. They made soft tinkling music.

"But I can't sing very well," Finny said worried.

"Everyone has their own special song," Mrs. Seahorse said kindly. "Even you, dear Finny."

Finny took a deep breath. He made a small humming sound. The water bubbled gently around him.

Something amazing happened! The glowing seeds began to glow brighter. They swayed back and forth happily.

"They like my humming!" Finny said excitedly.

He hummed a little louder. The seeds spun faster and faster. Tiny green shoots started growing from them.

Bella swam over to watch. "Finny, that's beautiful!" she said with big smile.

Tommy the turtle clapped his flippers. "Your humming is magical!" he said happily.

More fish gathered around Finny's corner. They watched the singing seeds grow taller and taller.

Finny felt warm and proud inside. His humming grew stronger and sweeter. The plants danced to his special song.

Colorful flowers bloomed on the stems. Pink ones and yellow ones. Purple ones and orange ones too.

"I did it!" Finny cheered joyfully. "I grew a singing garden!"

The flowers chimed like little bells. They made the most beautiful music ever.

Mrs. Seahorse beamed with pride. "You found your special gardening gift, Finny."

All afternoon, Finny hummed to his singing garden. The plants grew bigger and more beautiful. Other fish came to listen too.

"Your garden sounds like heaven," said Bella sweetly.

"It smells like sunshine," added Tommy with a big grin.

Finny's heart felt full of happiness. The water around him felt warm and cozy.

When garden time ended, Finny didn't want to stop. His singing flowers glowed softly in the dim water.

"Can I come back tomorrow?" Finny asked hopefully.

"Of course!" said Mrs. Seahorse. "Your singing garden needs you."

That night, Finny dreamed of music and flowers. He dreamed of colorful petals dancing. He dreamed of his friends' happy faces.

The next morning, Finny woke up early. The sun was just starting to shine through the water.

"Time for garden time!" he called out excitedly.

This time, Finny swam quickly to the garden patch. He couldn't wait to hum to his singing seeds.

His friends were already there waiting. They loved listening to Finny's special songs.

"Good morning, singing garden!" Finny said cheerfully.

The flowers chimed back happily. They remembered his voice.

Finny had learned something wonderful. Everyone has their own special way to help things grow. His way was through music and kindness.

From that day on, Finny loved garden time. He looked forward to it every single day.

His singing garden became the most beautiful spot in the whole underwater school.

The end.
