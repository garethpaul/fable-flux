# Story story_842

**Ducky's Magical Cleanup Day**

The sun was setting orange and pink. Mama Duck looked around their cozy pond home. Toys floated everywhere in the water. Lily pads were scattered about. Acorns rolled on the muddy shore.

"Cleanup time, Ducky!" called Mama Duck sweetly.

Little Ducky was playing with his favorite red boat. He pushed it around the shallow water. The boat made tiny waves that tickled his orange feet.

"Do I have to?" Ducky asked with a big sigh.

His shoulders drooped down low. Cleanup time felt so boring. There were so many toys to gather. The lily pads needed sorting too. It would take forever and ever.

"Yes, little one," Mama said gently. "Our home needs tidying up."

Ducky's bottom lip stuck out. He looked at all the mess around him. His toy boat bobbed sadly in the water. His building blocks sat crooked on a log. Even his favorite yellow ball was stuck under some weeds.

"I don't want to clean," Ducky mumbled quietly.

He picked up one small acorn slowly. Then he dropped it in the bucket. The acorn made a soft *plunk* sound. Ducky picked up another acorn. *Plunk.* Then another. *Plunk.*

Suddenly, something wonderful happened! The bucket began to glow with sparkly golden light. The acorns inside started to jingle like tiny bells. They sounded like beautiful music!

"Oh my!" gasped Ducky with wide eyes.

Each toy he picked up started glowing too. His red boat shimmered and sparkled. The building blocks hummed a happy tune. His yellow ball bounced with magical rainbow colors.

"The cleanup magic is here!" laughed Mama Duck.

Ducky grabbed his boat excitedly. It felt warm and tingly in his wings. He placed it in the toy box. The boat sang a sweet little song! Ducky giggled with pure joy.

Next, he gathered his colorful blocks quickly. Each block glowed brighter as he stacked them. They hummed louder and louder together. The sound made Ducky feel happy and proud.

"This is amazing!" Ducky quacked with delight.

He splashed through the water faster now. His yellow ball glowed like a tiny sun. When he tossed it in the toy chest, it made beautiful chiming sounds. The lily pads sparkled green and gold as he arranged them neatly.

Ducky worked harder and harder. He wanted to see what would happen next! Every single thing he cleaned up became more magical. The acorns jingled sweetly in their bucket. The toys hummed gentle lullabies together.

Soon, their pond home looked perfect again. Everything was clean and organized. The magical lights danced all around them. The sweet music filled the evening air.

"You did wonderful work, Ducky!" said Mama proudly.

Ducky's chest puffed out with pride. He had cleaned up everything all by himself! Well, almost by himself. The cleanup magic had helped make it fun.

"I loved the glowing and singing!" Ducky said happily.

As the stars began twinkling overhead, the magical lights slowly faded away. But Ducky still felt the warm, happy feeling inside his heart. Their home looked so beautiful and peaceful now.

Mama Duck tucked Ducky into his soft, cozy nest. She smoothed his fluffy yellow feathers gently. The clean toys sat quietly in their proper places. Everything smelled fresh and nice.

"Will the magic come back tomorrow?" asked Ducky hopefully.

"Maybe it will," Mama whispered with a knowing smile. "Magic often comes to those who try their best."

Ducky closed his sleepy eyes. He dreamed about glowing toys and singing acorns. In his dreams, cleanup time was the most wonderful part of every day.

The next morning, Ducky woke up excited. He looked around at their tidy home. Even without the magical lights, everything still looked beautiful. He couldn't wait for cleanup time to come again.

"Good morning, Mama!" Ducky called cheerfully. "Can we clean up something today?"

Mama Duck smiled her biggest, happiest smile. Her little Ducky had learned that cleanup time could be fun. Sometimes the best magic happens when we try our hardest and keep a happy heart.

The end.
