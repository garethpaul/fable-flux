# Story story_1312

**Finny's Lost Teddy Bear**

Finny was a bright orange fish. He lived in a pretty pond. The pond was in Emma's backyard. Finny loved his little teddy bear. The teddy bear was soft and brown. It had tiny button eyes.

Every morning, Finny hugged his teddy bear. Every night, he slept with it. The teddy bear made Finny feel safe. It made him feel happy too.

One sunny morning, Finny woke up. He looked around his cozy spot. His teddy bear was gone! Finny's heart felt heavy. His eyes filled with tears.

"Where is my teddy bear?" Finny said. His voice was very quiet. He looked under the lily pad. No teddy bear there. He looked near the rocks. Still no teddy bear.

Finny swam around the pond. The water felt cool on his fins. He searched high and low. He checked every corner. The pond seemed so big now. It felt empty without his special friend.

"I must find my teddy bear," Finny said. He was very worried. The sun was getting higher. The water sparkled like diamonds. But Finny didn't notice the pretty sparkles.

Finny had an idea. Maybe his teddy bear fell down deep. He swam to the bottom of the pond. The water was darker down there. It felt colder too. Finny moved slowly. He pushed aside some old leaves. They felt slimy on his fins.

He found a shiny bottle cap. He found some smooth pebbles. He even found a lost toy car. But no teddy bear anywhere. Finny's heart sank lower. This wasn't working at all.

"Maybe I should ask for help," Finny thought. He swam back up to the top. The warm sunlight felt good again. Finny saw his friend Bella. She was a pretty blue fish.

"Bella!" called Finny. "Can you help me? I lost my teddy bear." His voice shook a little.

"Of course!" said Bella kindly. "Let's look together. Two fish are better than one."

Bella was very smart. She had a good idea. "Let's check the waterfall," she said. "Things sometimes get stuck there."

They swam to the small waterfall. The water bubbled and splashed. It made a gentle whooshing sound. Behind the falling water was a little cave. Finny had never looked there before.

"I'll check inside," said Bella bravely. She swam through the misty water. It tickled her scales. A moment later, she came back out.

"Finny! Come see!" Bella called excitedly. Her voice was full of joy.

Finny swam through the waterfall too. The water felt like tiny bubbles. Inside the cave, he saw something wonderful. There was his teddy bear! It was sitting on a small rock ledge. The teddy bear looked perfectly dry and safe.

"My teddy bear!" Finny shouted happily. He hugged it tight. The teddy bear felt soft and warm. It smelled like home. Finny's heart felt full again.

"How did it get here?" Finny wondered.

"The current must have carried it," said Bella. "The water flows toward the waterfall. Your teddy bear went on an adventure!"

Finny laughed. His laugh sounded like tiny bubbles. "Thank you, Bella," he said. "I couldn't have found it alone."

"That's what friends are for," said Bella. She smiled a big fish smile.

They swam back home together. The water felt perfect now. The sun made rainbow colors in the water drops. Everything looked beautiful again.

That night, Finny held his teddy bear extra tight. He learned something important. When you have a big problem, don't give up. Try different ways to solve it. Ask friends for help too. Working together makes everything better.

Finny closed his eyes. He felt grateful and happy. His teddy bear was safe in his fins. Tomorrow would be another wonderful day in the pond.

The moon shone down on the quiet water. All the fish were sleeping peacefully. Finny's teddy bear kept him cozy all night long.

The end.
